A Deeply Mystic Music Collection Supplement ~ Touhou Shinpiroku First Pressing Special CD (深秘的楽曲集・補 東方深秘録初回特典CD, Shinpiteki Gakkyokushuu - Ho ~ Touhou Shinpiroku Shokai Tokuten CD) is an official doujin album by Twilight Frontier and Team Shanghai Alice released on 2016-12-08. It is a follow-up to the original soundtrack of the 14.5th Touhou Project game Urban Legend in Limbo, released as a bonus CD with the first pressing edition of the PlayStation 4 version of the game.
