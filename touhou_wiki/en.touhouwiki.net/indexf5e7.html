Shunsatsu sare do? (瞬殺サレ道？) is a Japanese game development organization that are the creator of the Seihou Project series, starting with Shuusou Gyoku. Similar to Team Shanghai Alice, they branched apart from Amusement Makers after they created Kioh Gyoku. Every game "Shunsatsu sare do?" has developed from its creation has been a shooting game of some kind, and it seems to have been influenced by the Touhou Project.
