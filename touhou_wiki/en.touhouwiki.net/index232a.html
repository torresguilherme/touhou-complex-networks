Touhou Shikoubana (Incense of Violet Flowers)  ~ Seasonal Dream Vision (東方紫香花　～ Seasonal Dream Vision) is a semi-official Touhou fanbook. It is quite similar to Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red, as it features comics, a music CD, and a story by ZUN, all about Gensokyo. It ranges from drama to comedy, all focused on the strange world Touhou takes place in.
