History of Yougakudan 4 ~ Akyu's Untouched Score vol.4 (幺樂団の歴史４　～ Akyu's Untouched Score vol.4, Yougakudan no Rekishi 4 ~ Akyu's Untouched Score vol.4) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2007-12-31 (Comiket 73). It is the forth volume of the Akyu's Untouched Score collection, presented by Hieda no Akyuu. It contains the full soundtrack from the third PC-98 game Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream. On the cover along with Akyuu, Reimu Hakurei, Marisa Kirisame and Mima can be seen; this is making a reference to the cover of the original game.
