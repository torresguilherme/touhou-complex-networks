The Grimoire of Usami Hifuu Kurabu Ikai Satsuei Kiroku (Secret Sealing Club's Otherworld Photography Record) (The Grimoire of Usami 秘封倶楽部異界撮影記録) is an official Touhou book, released April 27, 2019. The book features a comic written by ZUN and drawn by Aya Azuma, and shows a large selection of Spell Cards which are commented and judged by various characters during a firework festival.

Plot[edit]

Sumireko Usami's stories of fireworks festivals in the outside world give Reimu the idea to host one in Gensokyo too, but with danmaku instead of fireworks. Partway through, the festival is crashed by some unexpected guests who plan to liven things up.
