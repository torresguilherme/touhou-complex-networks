Description

A doujin music circle notable for various Touhou arrangements such as つるぺったん and ケロ⑨destiny.

In May 2012, due to the poor health of vocalist Sayuri the group temporarily disbanded. [1]

In 2013, they reformed with a new vocalist called maicoro-phone and released a new album in Comiket 85[2]
