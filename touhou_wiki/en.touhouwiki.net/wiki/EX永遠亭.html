Description

Ex Eientei is a side circle of 1569 for collaborative works.


Discography
夢十夜 a waking ten dream
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy and Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey.

Happy Birthday Renko
Release date: 2013-02-03 (Dai-Kyuu-Shuu Touhousai 7)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

百音語
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
Two albums featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey, Trojan Green Asteroid, Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

千終楽
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
Two albums featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy and Trojan Green Asteroid.

Happy Birthday Merry
Release date: 2015-02-21 (Kyoukai kara Mieta Keshiki -Musubu-)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy and Trojan Green Asteroid.

零 -原点怪奇-
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

神獣之宴 -the Grateful BEASTS-
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Hopeless Masquerade and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

妖怪道六四七次
Release date: 2018-07-15 (Kanasawa Touhousai 7)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ten Desires and Hopeless Masquerade.
