Description

TAMUSIC is ...
 TAM is an amateur player & President of TAMUSIC
TAM is 100% impromptu rendition. no score.
TAMUSIC is an amateur classic group of the maximum scale in Japan.
200 Members, 50 Lives, 50 CDs, 500 Movies
We play the music of Game & Anime.
TOUHOU, Higurashi, Kanon, Air, CLANNAD, Haruhi, FF, DQ, miku, niconico, and more

Most of the circle's discography has been divided into categories such as Violin+Piano, Piano Solo, Violin×Vocal or Violin Rock.
