Profile

Dates
2004 - 2017
Website
CROW'SCLAW official website
Genre
Instrumental, Vocal, Metal, Rock, Guitar

Description

Hard rock doujin circle led by guitarist Taka (鷹).

Album usage

Taka doesn't permit any usage of his works, and there is currently (September 2012) no way to legally get his works, but buying the CDs, if they are sold. However, if there are enough requests, he will upload his works to YouTube and/or iTunes.
