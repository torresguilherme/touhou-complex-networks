Discography
Elementary Particle
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Double Dealing Character.

Just Cause
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

Silent Majority
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Hopeless Masquerade Double Dealing Character and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

Even a worm will turn.
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Shoot the Bullet, Double Dealing Character and Shuusou Gyoku

Irreversible reaction
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Fairy Wars and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

Escape From a Sky
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

Art CUBE

Originally ゆうね formed a circle under the name Art CUBE in 2014 featuring their arrangements and vocals by 葛城ユイ as well as contributions by several other musicians, in 2015 the circle's name was changed to No Gimmick No Life.

三寒四温
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Perfect Cherry Blossom.

情動Skyscraper
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.
