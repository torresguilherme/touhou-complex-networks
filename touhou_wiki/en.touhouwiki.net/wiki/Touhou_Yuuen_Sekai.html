Touhou Yuuen Sekai (東方遊宴世界) is a fan-made Touhou Project action platforming game released by 激辛spice. It is the predecessor to The Mystery of Gensokyo, which was released in 2012.

Gameplay[edit]

Main article: Gameplay, Characters

Travel all around Gensokyo while fighting bosses that will join your team. You start with Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame in your party, and gradually gather 8 more party members as you progress. Different characters have different attacks, jump height and stats, so you must choose the character you want to use wisely.

Story[edit]

The residents of Gensokyo are suddenly unable to fly. Reimu and Marisa depart to find out the cause of this incident.
