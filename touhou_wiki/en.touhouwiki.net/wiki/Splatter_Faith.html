Splatter Faith (スプラッターフェイス) is a Touhou Project fan game made by Mitsume Shobo (ミツメ書房 3-me). This is a parody of the classic horror arcade game Splatterhouse.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay and Strategy

Splatter Faith features Sanae Kochiya as the main protagonist.

Guide Sanae through corridors filled with aliens, plant zombies, headcrabs, phantoms, space invaders, and traps along the way. There is a boss at the end of every level.

To unlock Extra Mode, clear Story Mode without continuing. Please Note: Knowledge in Japanese will be required at a certain boss fight during Extra Mode.

Story[edit]

Main articles: Story, Translation, and Characters

Kanako Yasaka has been abducted by a Red UFO. Suwako Moriya entrusts Sanae Kochiya with her hat and sends Sanae towards the Red UFO to try and rescue Kanako.
