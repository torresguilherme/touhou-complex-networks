Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ This phrase is a parody of Matarou Urami's phrase of "Matarou ga kuru!!", "ウラミハラサデオクベキカ！！" (Urami harasade okubekika!!, "Shouldn't I keep myself from avenging?!").
  2. ↑ It's unclear what this phrase really means, but it's a joke of "All roads lead to Rome" (全ての道はローマに通ず).
  3. ↑ This phrase can be taken literally, but really is a parody of Tarou Okamoto's popular remark: "Art is explosion." (芸術は爆発だ).
  4. ↑ Clubs or Circles belonging to Japanese school or university can be briefly classified into the two groups; Indoor Type (lit. cultural style, 文化系) and Outdoor Type (lit. physical activity style, 体育会系). The stereotype of the former is smart and weak, while that of the latter is active and violent.) Possibly, it has some.
  5. ↑ Speaking as if she sent them to the afterlife.
  6. ↑ From Mōri Motonari's parable of three arrows.
  7. ↑ 死ぬがよい (Shinu ga yoi) — catchphrase from CAVE Shoot 'em Up series DoDonPachi. 1 2
