Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A word play coming from the similarity of "パワープレイ" (power play, pawaa purei) and "パープル霊" (purple ghost, paapuru rei)
  2. ↑ "泣く子も凍る" (freeze crying babies, naku ko mo kooru) is a wordplay of a idiom "泣く子も黙る" (babies stop crying, naku ko mo damaru). The original idiom refers to Zhang Liao, whose prowess in battle became so feared that it was said babies in the enemy's territory would stop crying upon hearing his name.
  3. ↑ The proverb goes "勝てば官軍、負ければ賊軍" (winners will be the government army, losers will be the rebel army); in other words, "might is right", "winning is everything".
