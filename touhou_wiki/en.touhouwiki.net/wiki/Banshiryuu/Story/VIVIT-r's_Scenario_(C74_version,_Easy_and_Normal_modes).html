< Prologue   Story   VIVIT-r's Extra >

Note: the C74 version of this game is different with the dialogues compared to any other game related to Touhou. You cannot skip the dialogues as the dialogues are automatic. The game doesn't pause for the dialogues as they play with the game and they disappear in a matter of seconds, so you need to be a quick reader (while dodging fast bullets).
