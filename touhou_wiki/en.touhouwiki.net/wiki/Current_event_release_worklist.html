Also see: Current event release worklist/Archive

The Current even release worklist is an attempt to better coordinate editing efforts and better keep track of albums & games released per event. The goal is to ensure fewer albums are forgotten about per event, see the the archive for albums from previous events that didn't receive pages.

  • current event: Reitaisai 15
  • add missing albums: albums (for the current event) without pages on the wiki should be listed here so add them if you notice any missing.
  • division of labor: if you plan to create pages for a number of circles post your worklist here to better coordinate editing efforts.
  • please be mindful of official titles: if you are unsure of an albums official title then please leave it as black text instead of a redlink.
  • resources: Reitaisai 15 official website, Reitaisai 15 circle participation list
  • deadline: 2018-08-XX (Shortly before Comiket 94), at that time all unfinished tier 2 pages (including voice dramas and games) will be moved to the archive while all unfinished tier 1 albums will be moved to a "Priority leftovers" section.
