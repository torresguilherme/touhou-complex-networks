The Grimoire of Marisa (グリモワールオブマリサ, Gurimowāru obu Marisa) is the third official Touhou book. Its focus is mainly on spell cards of many characters with screen shots of that spell card from the games, and comments written in the perspective of Marisa Kirisame, as she has made this album or scrapbook. It has art by Moriki Takeshi and Genji Asai.

It also comes with a CD containing two original music tracks and some desktop wallpapers.
