Level 4 Spell Card Strategy


Spell Card 4 - 1

Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Level 4 — 1
Aya's Comment
河原で遊んでいた河童を激写しました
ネタを探しに行くのが面倒なときは、こうやって近場で取材を
済ますのが楽で良いですね
Aya's Comment Translation
I took rolls of photos of a kappa playing on the river bed.
Whenever you find that looking for story material is a pain,
it's wonderfully easy to just cover local stuff like this.
Hatate's Comment
私のカメラを作ったのは河童の皆さんなのよねー
河童は愛想が悪いけど、技術力は高いわ
それに河童の作る機械は、完全防水な所が素晴らしい！
Hatate's Comment Translation
The kappas were the ones who made my camera, y'know~
They can be kinda rude, but they're really good at high-tech stuff.
Another great thing about machines that kappas create, is that they're completely waterproof!
Spell Card 4 - 2

Owner
Momiji Inubashiri
Level 4 — 2
Aya's Comment
山の見張り役、白狼天狗です
ちゃんと仕事しているのかちょっと調査してみました
どうも顔を合わせると喧嘩になりやすいんですけど……
Aya's Comment Translation
The white wolf tengu who is in charge of the mountain security.
I came to check if she has been actually working.
Though we get into fights easily whenever we meet....
Hatate's Comment
ああ、白狼天狗の椛ねー
文が苦手としているみたいだけど何かあったのかな
単純に犬猿の仲って事なのかな
Hatate's Comment Translation
Ah, it's Momiji the white wolf tengu, huh~?
It looks like she can't get along with Aya, I wonder why...
Maybe they just have a cat-and-dog relationship.
Spell Card 4 - 3
水符「ウォーターカーペット」 (Suifu "Wootaa Kaapetto")
Water Sign "Water Carpet"
Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Level 4 — 3
Aya's Comment
河童が水遊びをしているところを撮影しました
無邪気に遊んでいるように見えて、良く見ると人間が溺れて
いたりするんですよね。あー怖い
Aya's Comment Translation
I photographed the kappa playing in the water.
She appears to be playing innocently, but if you look closely
you tend to see a human drowning. Ahh, so scary.
Hatate's Comment
水中では河童に敵う者は殆ど無いのよねー
その傍若無人っぷりを記事にすれば河童の自由を奪えるかな？
ペンは水より強しってね
Hatate's Comment Translation
There's almost nobody who can keep up with kappa underwater, y'know~
If I capture that arrogance in an article, maybe that would be like taking away the kappas' freedom?
As they say, the pen is mightier than the water.
Spell Card 4 - 4
狗符「レイビーズバイト」 (Inu-fu "Reibiizu Baito")
Dog Sign "Rabies[1] Bite"
Owner
Momiji Inubashiri
Level 4 — 4
Aya's Comment
案の定、噛みつかれました
こいつは大天狗様には従順な癖に、私達鴉天狗を見下している
節があるんですよねぇ。ぷんぷん
Aya's Comment Translation
As usual, I got bitten.
This mutt is so obedient towards the Great Tengu,
but you can tell that she is looking down on us crow tengu. *fume, fume*
Hatate's Comment
人間が進入してきたら、こうやって噛みついて追っ払うのかー
しかも恐水病のおまけ付きと、この辺り水だらけだからねぇ
河童と相性良い理由がわかるよ
Hatate's Comment Translation
When humans come all the way here, I wonder if she chases them away by biting them like this...?
And I guess adding the hydrophobia would be because this place is full of water?
I can totally see how that goes together with the kappa.
Spell Card 4 - 5
河符「ディバイディングエッジ」 (Kawa-fu "Dibaidingu Ejji")
River Sign "Dividing Edge"
Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Level 4 — 5
Aya's Comment
河童の豪快な水浴びの様子ですね
これはイジメなんじゃ無いかと思ってしまいますが違います
河童を見かけたら勢いよく水を吹きかけてあげましょう
Aya's Comment Translation
It looks like the kappa is bathing happily.
You might think this is harassment, but it's not.
If a kappa spotted you, You'll be blown with lots of water with gusto.
Hatate's Comment
服のまま水浴びして寒くないのかなぁ
両生類の気持ちは判らないや
山椒魚とかただの食料だしなぁ
Hatate's Comment Translation
Isn't it cold playing in water with her clothes on like that?
I just don't get amphibians.
I mean, salamanders and stuff are, like, just food.
Spell Card 4 - 6
山窩「エクスペリーズカナン」 (Sanka "Ekusuperiizu Kanan")
Mountain Nomad "Expellee's Canaan"
Owner
Momiji Inubashiri
Level 4 — 6
Aya's Comment
身内ですが折角の取材なので、侵入者の追っ払い方を見せて
頂きました。小さい自分を大きく見せる技。流石ですね！
これからもずっと見張りをして頂きたいです
Aya's Comment Translation
She's a colleague, but I need materials for my article, so I had her show me how she chases off intruders.
Her skill that makes her look bigger than she actually is, that is impressive!
I hope she would always be in charge of guard duty.
Hatate's Comment
文は何か苦手みたいだけど、白狼天狗は眼が良いんだよねー
それに鼻も利くし、実は凄いんだ
まあ、そういう長所を記事に書いた方が良くない？　身内だし
Hatate's Comment Translation
Aya doesn't seem to get along with her, but this white wolf tengu sure has good eyes.
Plus, she has a good nose too, really amazing.
Well, wouldn't it be better if I don't write an article about that kind of strong points? Being my colleague and all.
Spell Card 4 - 7
河童「乾燥尻子玉」 (Kappa "Kansou Shirikodama")
Kappa "Dried Shirikodama[2]"
Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Level 4 — 7
Aya's Comment
あの鼻が曲がりそうな程臭い尻子玉も、乾燥させればそんなに
酷くないですね。それにしてもこれだけの数の尻子玉……
何人の人間が犠牲になったのでしょう。興味が尽きないです
Aya's Comment Translation
The shirikodama, with its eye-watering stench, isn't that bad
if it's dried out. Even so...how many humans were sacrificed to
create this many shirikodama? This is something of endless interest.
Hatate's Comment
うわ、尻子玉だー、これくっさいんだよねー
実はグルメなのかな。こんな珍味を好んで食べるなんて
そんなホルモンより肉の方がずっと美味しいと思うんだけど
Hatate's Comment Translation
Eek, it's shirikodama~ These are way stinky~
I wonder if she's actually a gourmet. I mean, she eats delicacies like these.
But I think meat is totally better than that offal kinda stuff.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Rabies is sometimes referred to as hydrophobia because late-stage symptoms include difficulty swallowing, insatiable thirst, and panic attacks in response to drinks.
  2. ↑ A shirikodama is a mythical ball in the anus that kappa typically feed on humans for in old legends.
