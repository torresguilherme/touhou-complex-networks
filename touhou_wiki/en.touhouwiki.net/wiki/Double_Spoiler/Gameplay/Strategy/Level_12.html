Editor's Comment
Take a shot when Byakuren has fired all of her red bullets, then retreat to the bottom center of the screen. If there are too many bullets, take a defensive shot of just the red cluster in front of you; this will clear most of the yellow and blue orbs too and give you enough charge to take an offensive shot next time.
