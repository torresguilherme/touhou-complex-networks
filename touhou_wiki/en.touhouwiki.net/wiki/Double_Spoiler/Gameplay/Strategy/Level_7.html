Level 7 Spell Cards


Spell Card 7 - 1

Owner
Shou Toramaru
Level 7 — 1
Editor's Comment
A three-part attack. For the first part, stay just below Shou, after she fires the first group of laser, run above her, snap a photo removing the second group of lasers and then find a suitable place to avoid the round bullets. After that you might want to run to the bottom to charge for the next round. The second part is relatively easy, just keep running around Shou and snap photos when the spell circle appears. For the third part, either stay at the bottom and rush up to take a picture, or stay at the top corners where lasers are less troublesome.
Spell Card 7 - 2

Owner
Nazrin
Level 7 — 2
Editor's Comment
All the bullets go straight up and down, making them easy to dodge. Avoid standing directly under Nazrin, as it's highly likely that two bullets are generated side by side, and their effective hitbox will become a lot larger. Since the bullets accelerate, staying on the upper half screen might help, where you have more time to move to a safe location. Move as little as you can, or you might run into a stray bullet.
Spell Card 7 - 3
寅符「ハングリータイガー」
Tiger Sign "Hungry Tiger"
Owner
Shou Toramaru
Level 7 — 3
Editor's Comment
Don't run recklessly, or you'll get trapped. Try and have Shou move in a pentagon or hexagon pattern around the stage. Take defensive photos actively to remove the slow-moving bullets blocking your way.
Spell Card 7 - 4
棒符「ナズーリンロッド」
Rod Sign "Nazrin Rod"
Owner
Nazrin
Level 7 — 4
Editor's Comment
Take a photo when the purple tracers are about to intersect and crush you. The timing's more generous if you use portrait-oriented photos and stay near the bottom. More coins appear next to Nazrin. You have to charge most of the time, so watch out when dodging the coins.
Spell Card 7 - 5
天符「焦土曼荼羅」
Heaven Sign "Scorched Earth Mandala"
Owner
Shou Toramaru
Level 7 — 5
Editor's Comment
When taking photos of different lasers, the gaps move towards different directions. This makes going to certain directions safer than others. A tested strategy is to start in the left grid, and when Shou comes, take a photo and get to the bottom grid, then the right one, then the top one, then the left one and repeat.
Spell Card 7 - 6
財宝「ゴールドラッシュ」
Treasure "Gold Rush"
Owner
Nazrin
Level 7 — 6
Editor's Comment
This is probably the easiest spell card in this level. Just stay in charge mode and take photos whenever you're ready. Those slow small bullets shoudln't be a problem. Be sure to remove as many gold nuggets as possible, as they spawn bullets and are worth a lot of points.
Spell Card 7 - 7
宝符「黄金の震眩」
Treasure Sign "Dazzling Gold"
Owner
Shou Toramaru
Level 7 — 7
Editor's Comment
Dodging the lasers isn't as hard as it seems. The lasers aren't random; the pattern is always the same for each photo. If you had a hard time dodging the last few waves, you can try to finish this card earlier by charging and taking photos every time when Shou begins firing lasers.
