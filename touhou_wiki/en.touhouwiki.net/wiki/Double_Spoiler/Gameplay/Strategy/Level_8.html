Level 8 Spell Cards


Spell Card 8 - 1

Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Level 8 — 1
Editor's Comment
This is a rather simple card. Don't cross two lines of bullets at once if you had a hard time dealing with them. Simply stay near Orin and take photos whenever you can.
Spell Card 8 - 2
熔解「メルティングホワイト」
Fusion "Melting White"
Owner
Utsuho Reiuji
Level 8 — 2
Editor's Comment
No real tricks here, just take your time charging, take a photo after Okuu released a new wave of nuclear bullets, run down, charge and repeat. Beware of the blue bullets obscured by the nuclear bullets, as they might take you by surprise. The directions of the nuclear bullets of each turn are fixed, so plan ahead. It's also possible to capture 2 or even 3 waves of nuclear bullets for a high score if you can position yourself correctly and accurately.
Spell Card 8 - 3
死符「ゴーストタウン」
Death Sign "Ghost Town"
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Level 8 — 3
Editor's Comment
The faster bullets are aimed. Just use the streaming technique and watch out for the random dot bullets. It's almost impossible to perform a cutback, so make use of the whole stage and go in circles around Orin. Be careful of the fairies spawning near the edge which might be blocking your way.
Spell Card 8 - 4
巨星「レッドジャイアント」
Giant Star "Red Giant"
Owner
Utsuho Reiuji
Level 8 — 4
Editor's Comment
This spell card will be a lot easier if Okuu is made constantly running out of the stage and generating less blue bullets. Here's a specific strategy:

1. Stand to the right of Okuu, and when she dashes at you, run to the bottom right corner. Now Okuu should be out of the stage.

2. When she goes for you again, run to the bottom left corner, then run to some place near where you were in step 1 and begin charging.

3. When she comes near you, take a photo of her which will cause her to stop near the middle of the stage. You can then repeat step 1.
Spell Card 8 - 5
「死体繁華街」
"Corpse Shopping District"
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Level 8 — 5
Editor's Comment
The purple bullets poses little threat because one of them is aimed, so streaming works well. This time the fairies no longer follows you, instead they go in straight lines and can leave the screen. However they will still be overwhelming if you aren't quick enough. One practical way is to snap a photo just after when new fairies become alive so you have enough space to do streaming/cutting back.
Spell Card 8 - 6
星符「巨星墜つ」
Star Sign "Fall of Great Stars"
Owner
Utsuho Reiuji
Level 8 — 6
Editor's Comment
Remember to take defensive photos often. A photo of just one giant star means a half-clear screen and an already half-charged camera. The stars are roughly aimed, so you might want to stay in corners to guide them to the sides.
Spell Card 8 - 7
酔歩「キャットランダムウォーク」
Drunkard's Walk "Cat Random Walk"
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Level 8 — 7
Editor's Comment
This is not as hard as it seems. The bullets are always arranged in a spiral pattern, and one of the fastest bullets is aimed, so they are quite predictable. Try to move slowly and in one direction so that the fast bullets can pass without problems, just like streaming. Slow bullets are the real threat here. Keep dodging and take shots when you can, trying to get as many bullets on the other side of Orin as possible, then move quickly over there.
Spell Card 8 - 8
七星「セプテントリオン」
Seven Stars "Septentrion"
Owner
Utsuho Reiuji
Level 8 — 8
Editor's Comment
The direction of the seven (plus one) stars is dependent on the relative position of Aya/Hatate and Okuu. Make use of this and always try to guide the stars to a fixed position so the situation becomes more manageable. A specific strategy would be standing directly below Okuu each time so that the stars are roughly aligned vertically. Then approach Okuu from the left and take a photo, which will remove at least 4 main stars and sometimes the small star as well. If something went wrong, just take a defensive photo, and the camera should be fully charged before the next round.
