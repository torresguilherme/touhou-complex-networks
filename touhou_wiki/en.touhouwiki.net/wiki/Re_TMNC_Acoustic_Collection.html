Re:TMNC Acoustic Collection is a doujin album by TUMENECO released on 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15). It features vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey, Neo-traditionalism of Japan, Dateless Bar "Old Adam", and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense.
