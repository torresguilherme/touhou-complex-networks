Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Likely refers to Hello World. Aside from that, "hello" could also be a pun on "halo", and the word 暈 (halo) and 傘 (umbrella) are both pronounced "kasa".
  2. ↑ "Parasol Stars" is a video game by Taito Corporation released in 1991. "Symphony" is named after "Bubble Symphony", which is the sequel of "Parasol Stars."
  3. ↑ "Memories" is named after "Bubble Memories", which is the sequel of "Bubble Symphony."
  4. ↑ This spell card's name is a play on words; "bone-dry" ("kasakasa") is a repetition of the word "umbrella" ("kasa").
