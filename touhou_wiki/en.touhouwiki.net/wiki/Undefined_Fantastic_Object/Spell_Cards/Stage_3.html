Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Tenmou (天網) is the net cast by the gods to correct the wrongs of the world.
  2. ↑ The word for barrage here, rather than "danmaku", is "renda", indicating a flurry of blows rather than of bullets.
  3. ↑ Kraken are legendary giant octopi. Unzan is a nyuudou. Octopus nyuudou (蛸入道, tako-nyuudou) is a term to tease bald-headed people. And Octopus strike (タコ殴り) means to strike again and again violently.
  4. ↑ Oo-Medama (大目玉) literally means a giant eyeball (hence his eye beams), and metaphorically means a harsh scolding.
  5. ↑ Medama-yaki (目玉焼き) means sunny-side up eggs.
  6. ↑ Kuuzen zetsugo (空前絶後) means "so horrible that it might be the first and last of its kind."
