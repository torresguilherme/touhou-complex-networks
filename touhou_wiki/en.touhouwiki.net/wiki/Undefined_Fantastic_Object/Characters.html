Playable Characters[edit]

In Undefined Fantastic Object, there are three playable characters to choose from, each with two shot types.

楽園の巫女
Shrine Maiden of Paradise

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character

The shrine maiden of the Hakurei Shrine launches off to the sky in search of treasure... or, perhaps, simply to exterminate the youkai causing the ruckus.


普通の魔法使い
Ordinary Magician

Marisa Kirisame
Playable Character

Curiosity grips the ordinary magician as the strange flying object soars off in the sky. Perhaps there is some monetary gain at hand?


山の新人神様
Newbie Goddess of the Mountain

Sanae Kochiya
Playable Character

Directed by the gods of her shrine, this miracle worker heads into the air to chase the flying object. It's her exciting first adventure in Gensokyo.


Enemy Characters[edit]

 ダウザーの小さな大将
The Little Dowser General

Nazrin
Stage 1 Midboss and Stage 1 Boss; Stage 5 Midboss

A mouse youkai who seems to be looking for something important.


愉快な忘れ傘
The Cheery Forgotten Umbrella

Kogasa Tatara
Stage 2 Midboss and Stage 2 Boss; Extra Stage Midboss

An umbrella youkai who approaches the player to try and scare her out of boredom.


守り守られし大輪
The Great Wheel, Having Guarded and Been Guarded

Ichirin Kumoi
Stage 3 Midboss and Stage 3 Boss

A youkai that controls a giant cloud named Unzan. Irritated by the attention the flying object has caused, she attempts to wrest the important fragments away from the player.


守り守られし大輪
The Great Wheel, Having Guarded and Been Guarded

Unzan
Stage 3 Midboss and Stage 3 Boss

An old nyuudou controlled by Ichirin. A shy cloud who only talks to Ichirin, he imforms her that the player has got the important fragments.


水難事故の念縛霊
The Ghost Left From the Shipwreck Accident

Minamitsu Murasa
Stage 4 Boss

The spirit of the captain of the palanquin ship. She is aiding the others in trying to revive Byakuren Hijiri.


毘沙門天の弟子
Disciple of Bishamonten

Shou Toramaru
Stage 5 Boss

The tiger youkai and Nazrin's superior. She is leading the effort to unseal Byakuren Hijiri.


封印された大魔法使い
The Sealed Great Magician

Byakuren Hijiri
Final Stage Boss

The living saint, brought back through the player's efforts. A former human friend to all humans and youkai.


未確認幻想飛行少女
The Unidentified Fantastic Flying Girl

Nue Houjuu
Stage 4 Midboss (Unknown Form); Final Stage Midboss (Unknown Form); Extra Stage Boss (True Form)

A strange, unknown youkai who has interfered with your quest time and time again. Is she an alien?


Trivia[edit]

  • UFO is the first Windows game (excluding the spinoffs) in the series to have no midboss-only characters.
  • This is the only game where every character art is holding (or has) an inanimate object.
  • Almost every boss has a unique item in their danmaku: Nazrin's pendulums, Kogasa's umbrellas, Unzan, Murasa's anchors and Nue's UFOs.
