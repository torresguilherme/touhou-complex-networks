C-CLAYS

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

C-CLAYS (Celestian Clays) is a doujin circle that does Touhou arrangements.　Closely related to K2 SOUND in addition to sharing some core members with 舞音KAGURA.
