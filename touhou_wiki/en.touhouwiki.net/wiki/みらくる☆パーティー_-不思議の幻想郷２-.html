Miracle Party -Mystery Gensokyo 2- (みらくる☆パーティー -不思議の幻想郷２-, Mirakuru pāteī) is a roguelike game starring Sanae as the main character, created by AQUA STYLE. It is the fourth title of the Fushigi no Gensokyo series and has many new features such as a complete new user-interface, a new party system (you can pick two from Marisa, Suwako, Kanako, and Reimu to join your team), a higher screen resolution (1280px to 720px), implement of the "i-dash" (iダッシュ, short for "instant dash"), and the dialogues are voice acted by professional seiyuu.
