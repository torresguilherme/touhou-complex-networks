Touhou Illusionary Ridge and the Fastest Legend ~ Project Downhill Queen (東方幻想峠最速伝説 ～ プロジェクトDownHill Queen, Touhou Gensoutouge Saisoku Densetsu ~ Project DownHill Queen) is a doujin album by とらのあな released on 2010-12-29 (Comiket 79). It's a package containing a music album with Touhou arrangements, illustrations by various artists drawing Touhou characters and a small comic book.
