○ Marisa's Touhou Fuumaroku Strategy　===================================================

Heya, there.  Marisa here.  Can everyone manage No Continue before beating me?
But of course, there's no way Mima-sama can be beaten.
So here's a strategy guide and tips for you taught by Marisa~.

　●About Reimu's Selection Type
　　 We start with Reimu's selection of battle type.
   From the left selection, it is ordered: high-mobility type, defensive type, and attack type.
   Brief explaination about these.

   High-mobility type: The fastest type that is out there, so it's difficult for you to miss items.
                       And you'll have the easiest time dodging bullets.
                       Though there's no shot option even if you see one on the screen(laugh).
                       Instead, Reimu's charm has a wide spread range, and can cover a lot of field.
                       With power-ups, you'll feel comfortable from increased number of shots you shoot.
                       Of course, the next important thing to talk about is about its destructive power.
                       I guess it's kinda low.  So expect the boss fight to be a bit lengthy.

                       But, if you get the Max Power, the story is completely different.
                       The two charms shooting in front are penetrating shots.
                       You cannot argue about it's power.
                       So the point of this form is to maintain your Max Power mode.

   Defensive Type: You may ask, even if you call a form a defense type, what's so different about it if you die in one shot?
                   It's about the bomb.  The bomb's power, and the duration of invincibility is high.
                   The movement speed is around in the middle, and the shot is pretty balanced out as well.
                   In other words, it's a balance-type.  

                   The performance of this type, as shown in the option menu screen, is arguably better than that of offense type.
                   At Max Power, the homing shots are included, which greatly increases its power.
                   It's probably a type that you can use to easily clear the game.

   Offensive Type: As the name says, the most powerful type of all.
                   It's the slowest type as well, so there's danger of losing lots of item.
                   But this game has a rather high setting of movement speed, so it's not much of a loss.
                   
                   It's also a type that's really weak with small Power Ups, but become stronger as you go.
                   There's no real difference in Max Power, so it's not much of a loss if you die.
                   The beauty of this type is that you'll defeat a boss very quickly.

   It was a weird explanation about the types, but to summarize:

The best way to get rich is through high-mobility type.
The best way to clear the game is through defense type.
The best way to be the least troubled by the boss is through offensive type.

Conversely, for myself, you'll have the easiest time if you use the high-mobility type.

　●About Bombs
    Know that the bombs aren't actually bombs, but spiritual attacks.
    Then, here's some additional explanation about the bombs (I know not).

    Once pressing for bombs, you become invincible.
    In other words, if you die despite pressing the bomb, you died between the duration of your brain signal to your finger action.
    
    Even if the bomb's power itself may be weak, the invincibility is worthwhile.
