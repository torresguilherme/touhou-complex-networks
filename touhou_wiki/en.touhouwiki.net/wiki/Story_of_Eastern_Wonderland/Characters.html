Playable Character[edit]

 主人公
Main Character

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character

The shrine maiden, using her spiritual abilities and the powers of the Yin-Yang Orb, launches into the sky with Genjii to find the culprit behind the mess of spirits.


亀
Turtle

Genjii
Secondary Character

The old turtle living at the Hakurei Shrine. It is with his help that Reimu is able to fly.


Weapon[edit]

 博麗神社最大の秘宝
Greatest Treasure of the Hakurei Shrine

Yin-Yang Orb
Attack Option



Enemy Characters[edit]

Shrine Tank
Stage 1 Midboss

Official name unknown. A tank created by Rika.


Rika
Stage 1 Boss, Extra Stage Boss

An engineer who happens to be the mastermind behind the appearance of the spirits at the shrine.


Noroiko
Stage 2 Midboss

Official name unknown. A Youkai(?) who attacks Reimu.


Meira
Stage 2 Boss

A samurai who appears to desire the legacy of the Hakurei family's power.


Two Red Dots
Stage 3 Midboss

Official name unknown. Two strange, but magical tools attacked Reimu in the World of Fantasies.


Five Magic Stones
Stage 3 Boss

Official name unknown. A strange group of magically-imbued stones(?) that appears to block Reimu's path.


Matenshi
Stage 4A and 4B Midboss

Official name unknown. An Angel(?) who attacks Reimu twice in Reimaden.


Marisa
Stage 4 Boss

A magician, and servant of Mima, who faces Reimu before her master.


Mima
Final Stage Boss

A ghost out for vengeance on humanity with an unknown reason.
