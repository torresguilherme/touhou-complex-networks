The Grimoire of Marisa is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2009-07-28. This music CD accompanied the The Grimoire of Marisa fanbook.

Tracks

  • 01. 魔法使いの憂鬱 (04:30)
    • Magician's Melancholy
  • 02. スプートニク幻夜 (04:34)
    • Illusionary Sputnik Night
    • original title: スプートニク幻夜
    • source: 秋霜玉

Notes

  • This CD-R has not only the two musics above, but also some wallpapers.
