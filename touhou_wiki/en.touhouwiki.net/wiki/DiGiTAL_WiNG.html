Description

Currently they are friends with Halozy, LiLA'c Records, NJK Record, A-One and macaroom.
Sometimes you will see ベジミックス☆ビーツ as an arranger of DiGiTAL WiNG album. That means the track was arranged by katsu and すみじゅん (Halozy).
