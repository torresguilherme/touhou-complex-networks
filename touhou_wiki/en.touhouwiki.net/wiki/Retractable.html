Description

つきいち is notably a fan of Touhou project characters Koishi Komeiji and Flandre Scarlet, album covers for his circle Retractable almost exclusively feature the two, and various arrangements of their themes have been featured within their discography numerous times.

Associated circles include pikapika.


Discography
mooncalves
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler.

Paragramme
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism.

Awakening World
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.

GREAT Re:SET
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Double Spoiler, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise and Retrospective 53 minutes. The album primarely features remasters of tracks the circle contributed to other circles and re-arrangements of older tracks in addition to 4 new arrangements.

4 tracing heart
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism, Hopeless Masquerade and Magical Astronomy.

Restless/Resetless
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of a theme from Magical Astronomy. It is a short 2 track EP done in collaboration with Satellite Himawari.

contact
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An short 2 track single featuring arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Subterranean Animism. The album's tracks would later appear on murder focus.

Retractable BEST
Release date: 2017-06-17 (APOLLO A-06)
A best-of compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Hopeless Masquerade and Magical Astronomy. The album is themed around Koishi Komeiji and largely features arrangements of themes associated with her.

murder focus
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Subterranean Animism.
