Chapter 22 magazine notes[edit]

Two magazine covers are shown on page 6; the first one mentioned appears to be Time Magazine, the American weekly newsmagazine.

The second magazine Kosuzu and Mami-nee discuss, "Fourteen Times", is a parody of The Fortean Times; it's a magazine dedicated to reports of objects and events similar to those collected by writer Charles Fort in his Book of the Damned (1919) and subsequent works. Things like rains of frogs, sightings of strange things in the sky or at sea, modern myths and urban legends, or, y'know, Slenderman, star jelly, and snakes in India that swallow humans. I don't know that this needs to be added to the summary at all, but it's an interesting easter egg. Taper (talk) 03:32, 9 July 2015 (UTC)
