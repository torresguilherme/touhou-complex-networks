Midboss Spell Card[edit]

Spell Card 023
夢符「緋色の悪夢」 (Mufu "Hiiro no Akumu")
Dream Sign "Scarlet Nightmare"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
A terrifying, oppressive nightmare.
Here, it is only scare tactics, so do not be fazed.
Spell Card 024
夢符「緋色の悪夢」 (Mufu "Hiiro no Akumu")
Dream Sign "Scarlet Nightmare"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
A terrifying, oppressive nightmare.
Standard approaches will end in suffering. Unorthodox dodging is required.
Spell Card 025
夢符「緋色の圧迫悪夢」 (Mufu "Hiiro no Appaku Akumu")
Dream Sign "Scarlet Oppressive Nightmare"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
A night terror of worrisome proportions.
You are fully surrounded, so frantic movements are needed at all times.
Spell Card 026
夢符「緋色の圧迫悪夢」 (Mufu "Hiiro no Appaku Akumu")
Dream Sign "Scarlet Oppressive Nightmare"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
A night terror of worrisome proportions.
Nightmares this terrifying are the result of your losses in real life danmaku battles.

Boss Spell Card #1[edit]

Spell Card 027
夢符「藍色の愁夢」 (Mufu "Aiiro no Shuumu")
Dream Sign "Indigo Dream of Anxiety"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
A dream born of constant stress.
Here, said stress is represented by repetitive, plodding danmaku movements.
Spell Card 028
夢符「藍色の愁夢」 (Mufu "Aiiro no Shuumu")
Dream Sign "Indigo Dream of Anxiety"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
A dream born of constant stress.
You must brave this repetitive danmaku to rid yourself of your anxiousness.
Spell Card 029
夢符「藍色の愁三重夢」 (Mufu "Aiiro no Shuusanjuumu")
Dream Sign "Indigo Three-Layered Dream of Anxiety"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
A dream born of weekly stress.
You will eventually figure it out this confusing danmaku, whether your want to or not.
Spell Card 030
夢符「愁永遠の夢」 (Mufu "Shu Eien no Yume")
Dream Sign "Eternally Anxious Dream"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
An endlessly stressful dream, brought upon by the monotony in one's life.
One must brave this torrid danmaku before they become void of their sense of self.

Boss Spell Card #2[edit]

Dream Sign "Ochre Confusion"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
An unorthodox, unexplainable dream.
This maze-like danmaku may have meaning to some people.
Spell Card 032
夢符「刈安色の迷夢」 (Mufu "Kariyasuiro no Meimu")
Dream Sign "Ochre Confusion"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
An unorthodox, unexplainable dream.
Many lucid dreamers spin around this maze of danmaku until they become afflicted with vertigo, and then fall out of bed.
Spell Card 033
夢符「刈安色の錯綜迷夢」 (Mufu "Kariyasuiro no Sakusou Meimu")
Dream Sign "Ochre Labyrinthine Confusion"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
A bizarre dream with unknown origins.
The more clear one's dream is, the more terrifying the dream can become, as represented with this danmaku.
Spell Card 034
夢符「刈安色の錯綜迷夢」 (Mufu "Kariyasuiro no Sakusou Meimu")
Dream Sign "Ochre Labyrinthine Confusion"
Owner
Doremy Sweet
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
A bizarre dream with unknown origins.
Perhaps, this dream has been seen in alternative ways by other dreamers; a rotating hallway in zero-gravity, for example.
