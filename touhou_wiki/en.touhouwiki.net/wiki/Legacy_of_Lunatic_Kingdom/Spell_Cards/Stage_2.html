Boss Spell Card #1[edit]

Spell Card 011
兎符「ストロベリーダンゴ」 (Tofu "Sutoroberii Dango")
Rabbit Sign "Strawberry Dango"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Strawberry jam leaks out of the dango as they are thrown about.
Much like dumplings, the best parts of the food are filled into it.
Spell Card 012
兎符「ストロベリーダンゴ」 (Tofu "Sutoroberii Dango")
Rabbit Sign "Strawberry Dango"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Strawberry jam leaks out of the dango as they are thrown about.
Certainly, artificial flavors and preservatives would hamper the use of this danmaku.
Spell Card 013
兎符「ベリーベリーダンゴ」 (Tofu "Berii Berii Dango")
Rabbit Sign "Berry Berry Dango"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Various flavors of jam leak out of the dango as they are rapidly thrown about.
When eating and/or dodging dango, be sure to not get fruit puree on your odango.
Spell Card 014
兎符「ベリーベリーダンゴ」 (Tofu "Berii Berii Dango")
Rabbit Sign "Berry Berry Dango"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Various flavors of jam leak out of the dango as they are rapidly thrown about.
Where does one produce this much dango, anyways?

Dango is pounded out from walls of rice flour.
Here, the rice flour is certain to get in your eyes.

This flour could be used for better purposes, but mochi is all what is on the minds of Moon Rabbits.

Ringo is getting greedy with all of this flour; it's too much for her to handle on her own.

Boss Spell Card #3[edit]

Spell Card 019
月見「セプテンバーフルムーン」 (Tsukimi "Seputenbaa Furumuun")
Moon-Viewing "September Full Moon"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Dorodango of various colors break apart into a fine dust.
Due to being tsukimi dango, instead of hanami dango, more dyes are used.
Spell Card 020
月見「セプテンバーフルムーン」 (Tsukimi "Seputenbaa Furumuun")
Moon-Viewing "September Full Moon"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Dorodango of various colors break apart into a fine dust.
Certain types of danmaku can be used to make colorful pigments.
Spell Card 021
月見「セプテンバーフルムーン」 (Tsukimi "Seputenbaa Furumuun")
Moon-Viewing "September Full Moon"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Dorodango of various colors break apart into a fine dust.
Moon Rabbits care way too much about mochi products, sadly.
Spell Card 022
月見酒「ルナティックセプテンバー」 (Tsukimi-zake "Runatikku Seputenbaa")
Moon-Viewing Sake "Lunatic September"
Owner
Ringo
Stage 2 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Dorodango break apart into fine dust under the banquet of a full moon.
Here, the Moon Rabbits are sure to fight with a certain fervor in the name of their home planet.
