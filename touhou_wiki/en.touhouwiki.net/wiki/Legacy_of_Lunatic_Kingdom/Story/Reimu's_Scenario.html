Additional Information[edit]

  1. ↑ "The Dream of Kaian" (槐安の夢) is a story in which a man falls asleep by a tree and is whisked away to become ruler of a kingdom, only to wake and discover that the kingdom was an anthill at the base of the tree.
  2. ↑ Sabi is a part of the term "wabi-sabi" with "sabi" referring to the appreciation of the old, faded, and obtrusive.
  3. ↑ A Chinese and Japanese idiom, meaning to hate someone so much that you cannot even stand the two of you existing together under the same heaven (i.e. living in the same world). Usually meaning wishing that the other person was dead.
