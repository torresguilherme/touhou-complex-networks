REVIVAL BEST III is a doujin album by SOUND HOLIC released on 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92). It features vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Shoot the Bullet, Double Spoiler, Fairy Wars, Ten Desires, Eastern and Little Nature Deity and Strange and Bright Nature Deity. It is a best-of album featuring songs from previous SOUND HOLIC feat. 709sec. albums, as well as two new songs.
