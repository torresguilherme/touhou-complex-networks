Touhou Cultural Album ~ Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red (東方文花帖　～ Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red, Touhou Bunkachou ~ Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2005-08-11. The CD accompanied the Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red fanbook. These tracks are remixes of the Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

The CD-R has not only the three following music tracks, but also a wallpaper and trial version 0.02a of Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

Tracks

  • 01. 風神少女 (06:12)
    • Wind God Girl
    • source: 東方花映塚　～ Phantasmagoria of Flower View
  • 02. おてんば恋娘の冒険 (04:51)
    • Adventure of the Lovestruck Tomboy
    • source: 東方花映塚　～ Phantasmagoria of Flower View
  • 03. 花映塚　～ after Higan Retour (03:24)
    • Flower Reflecting Mound ~ after Higan Retour
    • source: 東方花映塚　～ Phantasmagoria of Flower View

Notes

  • Track 1 is the original version of this theme. A shortened version appears in Phantasmagoria of Flower View, renamed as "Wind God Girl (Short Version) " (風神少女　(Short Version)).
