Yougakudan no Rekishi ~ Akyu's Untouched Score (幺樂団の歴史　～ Akyu's Untouched Score, lit. "History of Yougakudan") is a collection of official Touhou Project soundtracks by Team Shanghai Alice's ZUN. The soundtrack collection is represented by Hieda no Akyuu and the music is in FM Synthesis format because the source to the tracks are from the PC-98 series of Touhou Project, one of the few indications of the PC-98 era within the Windows canon.

Works[edit]

Currently, there are five pieces from volume 1 up to volume 5. Volume 1 is Hieda no Akyuu's first début in the series, where she also appears on the CD jacket on the other four volumes. Reimu Hakurei also appears on the vol.4 and 5 jacket, along with Marisa Kirisame and Mima only appearing on vol.4. Even though a few extra tracks have been added, even existing tracks had a little rework.

  • Volume 1, soundtracks from Lotus Land Story (contains two CDs to accommodate the amount of tracks, and includes two new unused tracks)
  • Volume 2, soundtracks from Mystic Square
  • Volume 3, soundtracks from Story of Eastern Wonderland
  • Volume 4, soundtracks from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream
  • Volume 5, soundtracks from Highly Responsive to Prayers (also contains an original track)

Concept[edit]

幺樂（ようがく）、それは今にも消え入りそうな音楽、幻樂の前身。ここではFM音源の事を指す。

その昔幻想郷には、この幺樂を好んで演奏した幺樂団（ようがくだん）という演奏団がいた。
――幺樂団演奏の記録を、一人の少女が歴史に残す。

効果音に割り当てられて使用不能だった機能も解放し、本来の曲に戻ったFM幻想郷のサントラです。
上海アリス幻樂団（公式HP）音楽CD紹介より

Background comment[edit]

This particular comment appears on the back cover of each CD.

幺樂（ようがく）とは、今にも消え入りそうな音楽、ここではFM音源を指す。

その昔、幻想郷には幺樂団と呼ばれるFM音源をこよなく愛した楽団がいた。

この幺樂団が残した曲の歴史を、録音し、幻想郷縁起に残す。		 "Yougaku" refers to a type of music that is in danger of disappearing from today's world, FM Synthesis.

Many years ago in Gensokyo, there was a band known as the Yougakudan which dearly loved FM synthesis.

Here, I shall record the history of the music the Yougakudan has left us that it forever remain in the Gensokyo Chronicle.
九代目阿礼乙女　阿求 Akyuu, the Ninth Are Maiden
