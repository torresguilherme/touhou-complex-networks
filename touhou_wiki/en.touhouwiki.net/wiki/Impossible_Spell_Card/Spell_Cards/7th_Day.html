Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Marisa wrote in the Grimoire of Marisa that she was studying how to shoot the Diffusional Master Spark.
  2. ↑ A parody of Jean-Pierre Polnareff's memetic speech about Dio's Stand in Jojo's Bizarre Adventure. Many aspects of Sakuya's design are related to that work.
  3. ↑ Japanese idiom which means "to use the tactics that sb. (almost 100% the samurai) will be very severely damaged but denifitely bring death to the opponent".
  4. ↑ 4.0 4.1 A reference to Shopan Igari's catch phrase: Hey, Red Snake!! Come On!!, which was popular with people born approximately from '60s to '80s.
