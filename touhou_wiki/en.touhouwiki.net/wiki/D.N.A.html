Description

Variably known as "D.N.A.Softwares" or "D.N.A. Softwares", as both are used together; the logo implies the former. Also known as "D.N.A. Software Creations" for some older works.

Doujin group that creates various Touhou and non-Touhou games. Many of their games parody some existing game. Possibly their most well known works are the ZUN-backed 神魔討綺伝 ～ Magus in Mystic Geometries. and Touhou Unreal Mahjong.

Discography
東方幻想麻雀オリジナルサウンドトラック
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Highly Responsive to Prayers, Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Changeability of Strange Dream and Uwabami Breakers as well as several original compositions. It is the original soundtrack to Touhou Unreal Mahjong and notably features several compositions by ZUN.

Musicale Jubilance
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires, Perfect Memento in Strict Sense, Uwabami Breakers and Touhou Unreal Mahjong.
