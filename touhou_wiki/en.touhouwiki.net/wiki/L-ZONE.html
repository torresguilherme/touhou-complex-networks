Description

L-ZONE/翠工房 is the personal circle of vocalist and arranger 翠(MIDORI), releases by the circle regularly feature guest arrangements by UG of Naturanotes and lyrics by 翔月 瞳. Releases by the circle are primarily mini albums/EPs often featuring 2 vocal arrangements as well as off vocal versions.


Discography
Crossroad Fantasia
Release date: 2014-03-30 (Touhou Meikasai 8)
A short demo featuring a vocal arrangement of theme from Perfect Cherry Blossom, the track would be featured on Boader Of Misty Night.

Boader Of Misty Night
Release date: 2015-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Undefined Fantastic Object.

迅風神舞 -迅キ風ニ神ハ舞ウ-
Release date: 2014-10-12 (Touhou Kouroumu 10)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangement of themes from Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith.

燈桜-hiou-
Release date: 2015-04-19 (Touhou Meikasai 9)
An album featuring vocal and off vocal arrangement of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

Artifact Princess
Release date: 2015-10-18 (Autumn Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring vocal and off vocal arrangement of themes from Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

七色の彼方
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and off vocal arrangement of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

AnswerSky　-空の彼方-
Release date: 2016-10-16 (Autumn Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring vocal and off vocal arrangement of themes from Imperishable Night and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

Lies a Rise -ライズ ア ライズ-
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
