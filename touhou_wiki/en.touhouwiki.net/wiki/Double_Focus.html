Double Focus -Aya and Momiji Bullet Coverage Travelogue- (ダブルフォーカス -文と椛の弾丸取材紀行-) is a Metroidvania-style Side Scrolling Action Adventure Game, developed and published by AQUA STYLE.

Originally released for PC on December 29, 2014, at Comiket 87, the game was released on January 28, 2016 in Japan for the PlayStation Vita, as part of Play, Doujin!. A PlayStation 4 port was announced alongside the localization of the game by NIS America, under the shortened title of "Touhou Double Focus".

Plot[edit]

A mysterious magical book made a commontion inside Suzunaan and the shop keeper was in shock and panic.

Hearing the shop keeper, Aya Shameimaru arrives at the book store with Momiji Inubashiri, who was carrying Kisume, hoping for an interesting story to cover.

Aya suddenly approaches the mysterious book but was warned by Kosuzu not to open the book, to which the crow tengu already opened. The mysterious book suddenly sucks everyone inside Suzunaan into the Book World.

Intrigued by the events that occured, Aya tries to get out of the Book World, with the help of Momiji, while possibly covering one of the biggest news yet.

Release[edit]

Limited Edition of Genso Wanderer with Double Focus included (Art not final)

The localized version of the game was bundled for free if the Limited Edition of Touhou Genso Wanderer for PlayStation 4 was bought. The Limited Edition contains both games, a reversible cover, soundtracks and artbooks (hardcover for Genso Wanderer while softcover for Double Focus), and an acrylic standee. Double Focus will be packaged in physical form in North America while a digital voucher will be for Europe.

The game is also packaged for free if the Standard version of Genso Wanderer, which contains the artbook for Genso Wanderer and a reversible cover, was bought.

In May 21, 2017, a standalone version of the port was released in PSN, allowing people who prefer to directly buy the game instead.
