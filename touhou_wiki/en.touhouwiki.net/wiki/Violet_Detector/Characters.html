From Touhou Wiki

This article lists the Characters that appear in Violet Detector. There are no new characters that first appear in this game, being the fourth game to do this after Shoot the Bullet, Fairy Wars and Impossible Spell Card in which all characters make a return from previous Touhou Project games of the Windows era or the supplementary manga.

Playable Characters[edit]

Sumireko Usami
Playable Character, Nightmare Diary Final Boss
