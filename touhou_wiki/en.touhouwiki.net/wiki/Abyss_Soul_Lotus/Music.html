Music List[edit]

Title Screen theme
 盛开在净土的莲花 Lotus Blooming in the Pure Land ♪ 11

标题画面的主题曲。

舒缓得不像样的标题曲。

想要给人一种听着曲子就感受到“天气真冷”的感觉。

今天的东方标题曲主旋律，也是面目全非尸骨无存了呢（误）。

Title Screen Theme

An uncharacteristically relaxed title theme.

I wanted to give people a feeling of "It's really cold" as soon as they hear this.

Nothing remains of the main Touhou theme of today (w).

Stage 1 theme
 Icy Blossom, Icy Dream Icy Blossom, Icy Dream ♪ 11

第一面的主题曲。

心目中的冰封的感觉。

乐器选取上，加入了一点类似冰晶和水晶叮咚碰撞的音色。

又用回音钢琴增强了一下空灵感。

果然提到冰和雪也就只能想到这些东西而已啊……

Stage 1 Theme

Has the feeling of a freeze.

On top of the selected instruments, I added tinkling sounds that are kind of like ice crystals and crystals.

And I also used an echo piano to strengthen the feeling of expansiveness and magic.

Of course, these were the only things I could think of when ice and snow were mentioned...

Stage 1 Boss - Shekle's theme
 冰与雪的灵动 Cleverness of Ice and Snow ♪ 11

雪珂的主题曲。

活泼，灵动，又有点蠢的雪妖精，大概和琪露诺比较像吧？

但是曲风完全不像嘛。（扶额）

给一面Boss这种杂鱼曲就用上了电吉他Solo的话，待遇会不会有点太优厚了啊哈哈哈。

Shekle's Theme

An ice fairy that's lively, clever, but a bit dumb, kind of like Cirno?

But the theme is completely different. *thinking*

Is it a bit too nice to give a Stage 1 Boss's small fry theme an electric guitar solo? lol

Stage 2 theme
 悔恨凝结之路 The Road Where Remorse Condenses ♪ 11

第二面的主题曲。

用吉他作为了宁静的开场。以后可就没机会这么安静了啊。

二面也是与主线有关的一面呢。

悔恨的话是不是应该拿小提琴渲染一下悲伤感啊，这么想着。

我终于会用小提琴了啊啊啊！（老泪纵横）（倒地）

Stage 2 Theme

I used guitar to have a calm and quiet introduction, after this I won't have a chance to be calm and quiet anymore.

Stage 2 is a stage relevant to the plot.

Speaking of remorse, shouldn't a bit of violin be used to colour in a sense of sorrow?

I finally can use violins now hahaha! (*tears streak down face*) (*falls down*)

Stage 2 Boss - Miyaka Yamika's theme
 暗之花，吹来夜之香 The Flower of Shadow Brings the Fragrance of Night [1] ♪ 11

暗华 美夜香的主题曲。

于激烈的战斗感中点缀着华丽和冷艳的曲子。

掺进了交响乐队的铙钹，好像还是蛮有意思的。

毕竟是来自地狱的使者，曲子也应该有点地狱的感觉了。

Miyaka Yamika's Theme

Within a feeling of intense battle is woven a beautiful, icy, and elegant song.

Adding some symphonic cymbals seems to be quite appropriate.

Since she is an emissary from Hell, the song should have a feeling of Hell as well.

Stage 3 theme
 开不尽的雪莲华 Endlessly Blooming Snow Lotus Flowers[2] ♪ 11

第三面的主题曲。

随心所欲地写了几段高速钢琴和跳音和弦，一下子就把曲风变得活泼起来了。活泼过后，也有高潮部分的旷阔与华丽。

顺便一提，高潮旋律的前两句和姜夔的《鬲溪梅令》可以完美契合，就像谱曲一样的契合度呢。

Stage 3 Theme

Since I did as I pleased and wrote several sections of fast piano and jumping chords, the mood of the track suddenly became lively. After the liveliness, there's also the vastness and beauty of the climax.

As an aside, the two phrases before the climax melody matches perfectly with Jiang Kui's "Gexi Meiling", as if it was composed to be like that.

Stage 3 Boss - Lepus Timidys Snowy's theme
 雪兔的踪迹　～ Nowhere but Everywhere Tracks of the Snow Rabbit ~ Nowhere but Everywhere ♪ 11

莉帕斯·缇米蒂丝·白雪的主题曲。

活泼又狡黠，让人难以捉摸的雪兔贼。

——贼什么贼，幻想乡的事，能叫贼吗？

不过话说回来，请雪兔小姐马上来偷我家的东西吧。

那样就可以顺便抓回家养起来了！（不是）

Lepus Timidys Snowy's Theme

Lively yet cunning, a hard to grasp snow rabbit thief.

--thief? what thief? Considering Gensokyo, can she even be called a thief?

Anyways, Miss Snow Rabbit, please come steal my house's stuff right away.

So I can conveniently catch you and raise you as a pet! (jk)

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ This is a reference to the boss's name, Miyaka (美夜香, "beautiful night fragrance") Yamika (暗华, "shadow flower")
  2. ↑ This is a parallel to Shining Shooting Star's stage 3 theme, The Kodama's Summer Night Festival. In both cases, the title of the track ends with the Chinese name of the game: "Summer Night Festival" and "Snow Lotus Flower".
