Ponchi (ぽんち, pen-name) is a member of the circle "Shunsatsu sare do?" and is one of the many who created the Seihou Project. Ponchi also participated in the development of the sixth Touhou Project game the Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, and is credited as Program Support. He also participated in two Project Blank games (Samidare and RefRain), both credited as Program Support.
