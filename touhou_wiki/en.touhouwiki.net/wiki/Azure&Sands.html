Discography
東方 JAZZ 2005
Release date: 2005-05-01 (M3-15)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

西行寺事件簿
Release date: 2005-12-29 (Comiket 69)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.
This album was released along with a picture and a short story collection.

東方 JAZZ 2006
Release date: 2006-05-21 (Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View, as well as one original intro track and an arrangement from Super Marisa Land.

東方 JAZZ 2007
Release date: 2007-05-20 (Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

戯曲幻想 -cherry garden project-
Release date: 2007-11-04 (Touhou Kouroumu 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith, as well as arranges from Tales of Phantasia, Tales of Eternia, Tales of Destiny and Valkyrie Profile, with one original extra track.

マサミカウトガリア
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith.

東方 JAZZ 2008
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power and Super Marisa Land.

holy night's Dong
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Subterranean Animism.

八雲紫の多世界解釈
Release date: 2009-03-08 (Reitaisai 6)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet.

CASINO聖輦船
Release date: 2010-05-05 (M3-25)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object.

十六夜咲夜の時間旅行
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet, as well as an original intro track.
