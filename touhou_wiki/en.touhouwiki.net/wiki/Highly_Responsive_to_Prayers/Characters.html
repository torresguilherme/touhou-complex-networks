Playable Characters[edit]

In Highly Responsive to Prayers, there's only one playable character to use.

巫女
Shrine Maiden

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character

"Well, whatever. Things will turn out just fine as long I use the yin-yang orb!"
The shrine maiden sets out to avenge her shrine, armed with her heavenly treasure—
Ah, that's right.
She lacks experience.


Weapon[edit]

 博麗神社最大の秘宝
Greatest Treasure of the Hakurei Shrine

Yin-Yang Orb
Attack Option

The great treasure of the Hakurei. It seems like a mere touch will purify any Youkai energy and fully exterminate them. Too bad the shrine maiden lacks discipline.
