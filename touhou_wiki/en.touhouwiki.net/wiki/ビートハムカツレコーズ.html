Discography
ST WORKZ 東方紅魔郷
Release date: 2016 (APOLLO 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. The album was distributed online for free by the circle.

PUSH START BUTTON
Release date: 2016-10-16 (Autumn Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom.

バトルマリサ大吟醸
Release date: 2017-04-30 (M3-39)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Fairy Wars and Shuusou Gyoku.

ロービット・ゲンソウキョウ
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.
