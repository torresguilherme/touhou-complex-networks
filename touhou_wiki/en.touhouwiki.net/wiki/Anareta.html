Anareta is the Stage 2 boss of Samidare. It's the automated guardian of Tetra-Biblos.

Character Basis[edit]

Name[edit]

It's name is Anareta. Anareta is Greek for "destroyer". In every official piece of information on Samidare, it's name is spelled as Anareta. However, it's name is spelled as Anareto in-game.

Additional Information[edit]

  • Anareta bears a strong resemblance to the RX-78-2 Gundam. In addition, during the battle with it, Anareta sustains noticeable damage after each phase, losing one arm, a leg, and finally its head, leaving it in a very similar condition to the Gundam after its final battle.
