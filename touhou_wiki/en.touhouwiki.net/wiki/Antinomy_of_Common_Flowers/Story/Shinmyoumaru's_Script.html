Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Shinmyoumaru's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Shinmyoumaru defeats Reimu[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

巫女に勝てれば最強だね！

If I can win against a shrine maiden, I'll be the strongest!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Marisa[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

レーザーなんか
お椀でガードしちゃえ！

The bowl will protect me from the laser!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Ichirin[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

下っ端僧侶なんて
ガンチューにないよ！

A training monk is nothing!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Byakuren[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

武闘派僧侶に勝った！
私のコンビ候補に入れようっと

I defeated an armed monk!
I'll keep you in mind as a potential partner.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Futo[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

頭痛いのか？
叩き過ぎちゃったかな

Does your head hurt?
Maybe I hit too much.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Miko[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

よーし、こいつに勝てば
もう最強じゃん？

Alright, if I defeat you, I think I'll be the strongest.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Nitori[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

こいつの水の所為で
お椀の中がビチョビチョだよ！

Aw man, my bowls are all wet because of your water!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Koishi[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

地底の妖怪にも勝ったぞー！
もう地底でも最強！

I even defeated a youkai from the Underworld!
Strongest of the Underworld!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Mamizou[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

狸の大将……はないか
余り組みたくない奴だなー

A Tanuki Leader……nah.
I don't really want to work with you.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Kokoro[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

この気持ち悪い妖怪とは
組みたくないなぁ

I don't want to team up with this gross youkai.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Kasen[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

仙人に勝った！
目をついちゃえ！

I defeated a hermit!
Poke her in the eyes!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Mokou[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

何かおっかないし
コンビは組みたくない奴ね

Seems scary.
I don't want to team up with her.

Shinmyoumaru defeats herself[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

偽物だー！ 殺せー！

An imposter! Kill it!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Sumireko[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

こいつと組むと
言うのも有りなのか……？
前の事を思うと気が進まないが

Maybe it's ok to work with this one……?
Hmm... if I remember the past, I'm not so sure.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Reisen[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

何かこいつと組んでも
強くなれなさそう

I don't think I'll be strong teaming up with you.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Doremy[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

夢の世界？
もしかして、身体が大きい自分に
会える夢のような世界!?

The Dream World?
Like a magical world where I can meet a bigger me?

Shinmyoumaru defeats Tenshi[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

この出鱈目なオーラ！
この人こそ
私のコンビに相応しいかも！

What an aura!
This person might be the best for me!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Yukari[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

嫌だ嫌だ
この不気味な人から早く逃げたい

No way, no way.
I want to get away from this creepy person.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Joon[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

直感でわかる
こいつは近寄らない方が良い奴

I just know.
I shouldn't get close to this guy.

Shinmyoumaru

直感でわかる
こいつは近寄ってはいけない奴

I just know.
This is a bad guy.

Generic Victory[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

勝てたぞー
この組み合わせに決めようかな

We won!
Should I decide on this team then...?

Shinmyoumaru

勝てたけど
自分がスレイブの方が
得意かもなー

We won,
but I think I'm best as the slave, huh?
