Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Mokou's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Mokou defeats Reimu[edit]

Mokou

暫くは異変解決しなくて良いぜ
楽しいからな

You don't have to solve this
incident right away, you know.
Isn't it fun?

Mokou defeats Marisa[edit]

Mokou

今度は私と組もうな
最強の火力でぬるま湯の奴らを
焼き尽くそう！

We should partner up next time.
Our ultimate firepower would fry any of
our half-assed opponents!

Mokou defeats Ichirin[edit]

Mokou

お前に憑依したスレイブに
念仏でも唱えてやれよ

The slave possessing you needs to chant a prayer too.

Mokou defeats Byakuren[edit]

Mokou

パワー勝負してみたいと
思ってたんだ

I was thinking about having a
match of power against you.

Mokou defeats Futo[edit]

Mokou

元気を出せ
私が勝つのは当たり前なんだから

Cheer up!
It's completely natural for me to win,
after all.

Mokou defeats Miko[edit]

Mokou

さすがの大物も
組み合わせが悪ければ
見る影もないな

Even a big-shot like you ends up
being nothing if you have a bad pairing.

Mokou defeats Nitori[edit]

Mokou

そんな水量じゃあ
私の怨嗟の炎は消せないな

Not even all that water can
extinguish the flames of my resentment.

Mokou defeats Koishi[edit]

Mokou

地底にはもっと強い奴も
居るだろうに、なんでこんな奴と
戦わなきゃいけないんだ

I'm sure there are lots of stronger foes underground,
so why do I have to fight someone like this?

Mokou defeats Mamizou[edit]

Mokou

その大胆かつ飄々とした
戦い方は、私と組むのに
ピッタリじゃないか？

Those bold, but swift moves of yours...
Aren't they a perfect match for me?

Mokou defeats Kokoro[edit]

Mokou

表情から感情が読めないって
不気味なんだな

It's a little ominous when you can't
read someone's emotions from their face.

Mokou defeats Kasen[edit]

Mokou

ヘタな仙人より
長く生きているからな

I've lived much longer than sloppy hermits like you.

Mokou defeats herself[edit]

Mokou

狸の旦那だろ？
悪ふざけも大概にしな

Tanuki chief, it's you, right?
Enough with the practical jokes.

Mokou defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Mokou

小さくても、見所あるな
中々楽しかったぞ

Even though you're small,
you show a lot of promise.
I had lots of fun.

Mokou defeats Sumireko[edit]

Mokou

お前に完全憑依をしたら
外の世界にいけるのか？

If I perfect possess you,
will I be able to go to the outside world?

Mokou defeats Reisen[edit]

Mokou

お前何か知ってそうだな
やっぱりこの異変も
月の民の仕業なのか？

You seem like you'd know something.
So is this incident also the work of the Lunar Capital?

Mokou defeats Doremy[edit]

Mokou

夢の支配者だと？
そんな子供騙しの話
信じられるもんか

The ruler of dreams?
There's no way I can believe all that kid stuff.

Mokou defeats Tenshi[edit]

Mokou

ふざけた奴だが、力は本物だな
生まれつきの才能なのか
出鱈目な力を持っている

You like to mess around,
but your power is the real deal.
Were you talented from the start?
You have some crazy power.

Mokou

天人にも寿命は無いのか

その上で、その底抜けの軽さ
尊敬するべきかもな

Celestials are also immortals, huh?
Maybe that ridiculously casual mindset
of yours is something to respect.

Mokou defeats Yukari[edit]

Mokou

お前が出てくると
何だか気持ち悪くなるんだよ

I always get a bad feeling whenever you show up.

Mokou defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Mokou

いけ好かない奴だ

What a creepy person.

Against Shion

Mokou

私の服の継ぎ当ては
請求書じゃないぞ？

The patches on my clothes aren't bills, you know?

Generic Victory[edit]

Mokou

完全憑依の原因はよく判らないが 暴れるチャンスが二倍に なったってことだな

I may not get what's causing this Perfect Possession thing,
but it means I have twice the chance to go wild.

Mokou works together with Marisa for a slave[edit]

Mokou

私の火と魔理沙（おまえ）の火は どうやら別の火みたいだな

組んでも火力が上がらん これでは意味が無い

My fire and your fire seem to be different kinds of fire, Marisa.

If our firepower doesn't go up when we team up, there's no point.
