Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Ichirin's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Ichirin defeats Reimu[edit]

Ichirin

この異変は面白いわー
もう暫く遊んでみない？

This incident sure is interesting.
Can't we just play a bit more like this?

Ichirin defeats Marisa[edit]

Ichirin

トリッキーな戦い方は
苦手そうね

Looks like you're no good at dealing
with these tricky fighting styles.

Ichirin defeats herself[edit]

Ichirin

やっぱり憑依で
入れ替わってしまうと
雲山に声が届かなくなるか

Nope. My voice just doesn't reach Unzan
when I switch places by possession.

Ichirin defeats Byakuren[edit]

Ichirin

是非、私をスレイブに起用して
頂けませんかね

Won't you please appoint me to be your slave?

Ichirin defeats Futo[edit]

Ichirin

ほら、私の方がスレイブの扱いに
慣れているわ

Hey, I'm much better at dealing with you as a slave.

Ichirin defeats Miko[edit]

Ichirin

聖様と喧嘩していない
貴方を見る日が来るとは感激です

To think I'd see the day you and Master
Hijiri would stop fighting... I'm impressed.

Ichirin

失礼を承知で進言しますが

今日は一段と寝癖が凄いですよ
整えた方が格好良いと思いますよ

With all due respect, I would like to give some advice.

Your bedhead is terrible today.
I think you'd look much cooler if you took care of it.

Ichirin defeats Nitori[edit]

Ichirin

完全憑依の相手って
その機械で洗脳して
操ったりしてない？

Wait, your perfect possession partner...
Are you sure you're not just brainwashing them with that machine?

Ichirin defeats Koishi[edit]

Ichirin

無心と何も考えていない
は別物だって
聖様が言ってました

There's a difference between
"being free of thought" and "not thinking anything".
That's what Master Hijiri said.

Ichirin defeats Mamizou[edit]

Ichirin

戦場までお酒を持ってくるなんて
よっぽど好きなんでしょうねぇ

Carrying liquor to the battlefield...
You must really like drinking.

Ichirin defeats Kokoro[edit]

Ichirin

割と感情豊かになったみたいだけど
表情は能面のままなのねぇ

Though it looks like you've gotten more emotional,
your expressions are still blank.

Ichirin defeats Kasen[edit]

Ichirin

霞って美味しいんです？

Does mist taste good?

Ichirin defeats Mokou[edit]

Ichirin

死にたがりだと思っていたけど
随分と楽しそうだねぇ
良かった良かった

I thought you wanted to die,
but it seems like you're having a lot of fun.
Good for you.

Ichirin defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Ichirin

小人って身体も小さければ
中身も小さいね！

Your inchling mind is as small as your body!

Ichirin defeats Sumireko[edit]

Ichirin

仏教ではね
超能力とか自慢する事は
徳の低い人間のやる事なんだって

In Buddhism, those who boast of their
powers are people without virtue.

Ichirin defeats Reisen[edit]

Ichirin

気持ち悪い弾幕ね
貴方と組む人の気が知れないわ

Your danmaku is pretty unsettling.
I don't get anyone who'd want to team up with you.

Ichirin defeats Doremy[edit]

Ichirin

貴方を見てると眠くなる……

I'm getting sleepy just looking at you...

Ichirin defeats Tenshi[edit]

Ichirin

え？
その頭の桃は食べられるの？

What?
You can actually eat those peaches on your head?

Ichirin defeats Yukari[edit]

Ichirin

入道と海坊主の境界は何かって？
そりゃ海岸線なんじゃない？

The boundary between a nyuudou and an umibouzu?
Isn't that the coastline?

Ichirin defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Ichirin

そんなチャラチャラしたもんに
お金をかけてはいけないって
聖様が言ってました

You should not be spending your money on such showy things.
That's what Master Hijiri said.

Against Shion

Ichirin

その乞食のような格好……
もしかして托鉢の修行中ですか？

That beggar-ish look...
Are you perhaps aiming to be a mendicant?

Generic Victory[edit]

Ichirin

あースッキリした！
やっぱり決闘は最高ね！

Phew! That feels good!
Nothing's better than a good duel!

Ichirin

入れ替わっている時に雲山が
もっと自由に動いてくれたら
良いのになー

You know, Unzan, it'd be nice if
you could just move on your own when I'm swapped out.

Ichirin works together with Byakuren for a slave[edit]

Ichirin

聖様をスレイブにする
なんて緊張するわー

でも、ちょっと快感？

It makes me a little nervous having Master Hijiri as my slave.
But it's also a bit nice.
