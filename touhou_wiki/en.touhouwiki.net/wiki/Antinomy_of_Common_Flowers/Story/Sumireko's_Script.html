Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Sumireko's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Sumireko defeats Reimu[edit]

Sumireko

えー？　御朱印やってないのー？
そんなんじゃ人気でないよ？

Whaat, you're not giving out seal stamps?
You won't get popular that way!

Sumireko defeats Marisa[edit]

Sumireko

一周まわって
箒で飛ぶ魔法使いも新鮮かもね

A witch riding a broom might actually
go all the way around and seem fresh again.

Sumireko defeats Ichirin[edit]

Sumireko

プチ妖怪ブームもあったけど
入道はからっきしだったわね

I heard there was a boom for tiny youkai,
but I guess that's useless to a nyuudou.

Sumireko defeats Byakuren[edit]

Sumireko

今や法事だって
アマゾンの時代よ？
お寺も変化しないと滅びるわ

Did you know? Nowadays, you can even order
memorial services on Amazon. Temples will
fall behind if they don't change things up.

Sumireko defeats Futo[edit]

Sumireko

お皿を回す人を
見なくなったわねぇ
昔は正月によく見た気がするけど

It's been a while since I've seen a performer
that spins plates. I feel like I used to see
a lot of them during New Year's, though.

Sumireko defeats Miko[edit]

Sumireko

そのヘッドホン
メーカーは何処ですか？

Those headphones...
Can you tell me who the manufacturer is?

Sumireko defeats Nitori[edit]

Sumireko

ぷぷっ 今どき河童って無いわよね
どうしても笑っちゃう

Pfft. These days, there are no such things as kappa.
I just can't help but laugh.

Sumireko defeats Koishi[edit]

Sumireko

地底にもお邪魔しましたよ
いやー幻想郷は奥が深い

I've even visited the depths of the earth!
Wow, Gensokyo sure goes deep.

Sumireko defeats Mamizou[edit]

Sumireko

都市部でも、意外と狸って
いるんですよ
本当に化けてたりして

There are a surprising number of tanuki
in urban places, too! Really, they're
transforming and everything.

Sumireko defeats Kokoro[edit]

Sumireko

秦、秦氏、秦……
うーん、日本史で聞いたような
期末テストに出るかなぁ

Hata, Hata... Hmm, I feel like I've heard
about it in history class. I wonder if
it'll be on the final exam.

Sumireko defeats Kasen[edit]

Sumireko

カセンちゃんに勝っちゃった
今度は何処に連れて行って
貰おうかな

Yay, I managed to beat Kasen-chan!
Wonder where I'll have you take me this time.

Sumireko

カセンちゃんに勝っちゃった

魔法の森ってところには
入ったことないなぁ
今度案内してよ

Yay, I managed to beat Kasen-chan!

I've never been inside the Forest of Magic.
Could you show me around later?

Sumireko defeats Mokou[edit]

Sumireko

相変わらず強いわね！
でも、私も負けられないわよ！

You're as strong as ever!
But I won't lose, either!

Sumireko defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Sumireko

捕まえたー！
これどうにかして
外の世界に連れ出せないかなぁ

I've got you now! I wonder if there's some way
I can take you to the outside world with me.

Sumireko defeats herself[edit]

Sumireko

ドッペルゲンガー？
いや、何か変ねぇ

A doppelganger? No, something's off.

Sumireko defeats Reisen[edit]

Sumireko

月の都って場所もあるんだってね
こっちの世界の地理は
一体どうなってるのかしら

So there's a place called the Lunar Capital, too.
I wonder what the geography is like in this world?

Sumireko defeats Doremy[edit]

Sumireko

私の夢の世界って
幻想郷の事だよね？

For me, the Dream World is Gensokyo, right?

Sumireko defeats Tenshi[edit]

Sumireko

仙人の上に
天人ってのも居たのね

So above hermits, there were celestials, too.

Sumireko defeats Yukari[edit]

Sumireko

その裂け目は何？
空間が斬れてるの？

What's that crack?
Is space being cut open?

Sumireko defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Sumireko

自分の財力以上に
ついつい課金してしまう能力？
疫病神ってそういう奴なの？

A power that makes you spend more than
just your assets before you realize?
Was a plague god something like that?

Against Shion

Sumireko

うわぁ、貧乏神だー
あっちいけカードとか
あったら良いな

Aaah, it's a poverty god!
If only I had a Get Away From Me Card!

Generic Victory[edit]

Sumireko

幻想郷が変な事になっているわね
楽しいけどさ

Gensokyo has turned into something really weird.
But it's still fun!
