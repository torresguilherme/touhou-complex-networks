Kasen defeats Reimu[edit]

Kasen

完全憑依異変には謎が多すぎる
これは解決には苦労しそうね

The are too many mysteries to the Perfect Possession Incident.
This will be difficult to resolve.

Kasen

完全憑依異変には謎が多すぎる
これは解決には苦労しそうね

守矢神社の索道 (ロープウェイ) だけどね
あれ多分河童と天狗が組んで
仕掛けてるよ

このままじゃあ
商売面では博麗神社に
勝ち目はないかも

The Moriya Shrine has that ropeway,

but it was most likely planned alongside the Kappa and the Tengu.

At this rate, the Hakurei Shrine
has no chance of commerical victory.

Kasen defeats Marisa[edit]

Kasen

最近、大人しいじゃない
本業の泥棒稼業が

You've been well-behaved lately, haven't you?
Even though your main occupation is thief.

Kasen defeats Ichirin[edit]

Kasen

みんな、貴方じゃなくて
後ろの人達ばかり見ていて
可哀想ねぇ

Everyone always looks at the nyuudou behind you,
instead of you, don't they?
How pitiable.

Kasen defeats Byakuren[edit]

Kasen

貴方も完全憑依異変の調査を
始めたのね、でもこの異変は
一筋縄ではいかないわよ

So you've already begun your own investigation
of the Perfect Possession Incident.
But ordinary methods won't work here.[1]

Kasen defeats Futo[edit]

Kasen

常に私が鬼門に来るように
動く戦い方は見事だわ
それでも勝っちゃうけど

The way you fight by always coming
after my weak point is marvelous.
Although I did end up winning.

Kasen defeats Miko[edit]

Kasen

貴方も完全憑依異変の調査を
始めたのね、でもこの異変は
一筋縄ではいかないわよ

So you've already begun your own investigation
of the Perfect Possession Incident.
But ordinary methods won't work here.[2]

Kasen defeats Nitori[edit]

Kasen

完全憑依が人工的に再現出来る
ですって？

You say you can can artificially reproduce
Perfect Possession?!

Kasen defeats Koishi[edit]

Kasen

お姉ちゃん元気してる？
たまには地上に来ても良いのに

How's your sister been?
She does realize it's alright to
come to the surface from time to time, right?

Kasen defeats Mamizou[edit]

Kasen

無限に酒が湧く瓢箪を借りてきて
呑み比べしましょうか？

Should I borrow that gourd of limitless sake
so we can have another drinking match?

Kasen defeats Kokoro[edit]

Kasen

感情豊かになったというか
軽くなった気がするわねぇ

I see you're full of emotions now,
or should I say you've become rather lighthearted?

Kasen defeats herself[edit]

Kasen

もしかして、夢の世界の私!?

Could this be the dream me?!

Kasen defeats Mokou[edit]

Kasen

パワーだけで押し切っている
様に見えて、実は無駄のない
身のこなしをしているね

At first glance you seem to be pushing through with pure force,
but actually you have no wasted movements, don't you?

Kasen defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Kasen

身体が小さいのに
気だけが大きくなってるねぇ

Your body is small,
but your feelings have gotten large, huh?

Kasen defeats Sumireko[edit]

Kasen

そうか、外の世界は
また大きく変化するみたいねぇ

I see. So the outside world
is undergoing another major change.

Kasen defeats Reisen[edit]

Kasen

都市伝説異変は月の民の仕業
らしいわね
永遠亭の連中は動かないの？

Apparently, the Urban Legend Incident
is the work of a Lunarian
Is Eientei not going to take action?

Kasen defeats Doremy[edit]

Kasen

夢の世界
本当に存在するみたいね

The Dream World.
So it really does exist.

Kasen defeats Tenshi[edit]

Kasen

貴方を見てもなお
天人を目指すべきか否か……

I'm even more conflicted after seeing you.
Should I aim to become a celestial or not?

Kasen defeats Yukari[edit]

Kasen

貴方は、やり方が少し乱暴ね
本当の賢者ってのは
他人からは愚者に見られるものよ

As I thought, your methods are a little crude.
A true sage will sometimes
appear the fool to others.

Kasen defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Kasen

こいつに貢ぐ理由が判らない

I don't understand why I should give you anything.

Against Shion

Kasen

貧相でも品があるのなら
良いんだけどねぇ

If only you'd had grace in poverty.

Generic Victory[edit]

Kasen

常に自然体であれ
それが最強への近道よ

Always keep a relaxed stance.
That is the shortcut to being the strongest.

Kasen works together with Miko for a slave[edit]

Kasen

なる程、仙人同士組むと
仙術が使いやすくなるのね
気のせいかも知れないけど

I see. Hermit arts are easier to perform when
paired with another hermit.
Or maybe that's just my imagination.
