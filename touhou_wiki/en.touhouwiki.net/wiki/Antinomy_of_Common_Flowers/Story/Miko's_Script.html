Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Miko's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Miko defeats Reimu[edit]

Miko

完全憑依異変は私が解決する！

I shall be the one to resolve the Perfect Possession Incident!

Miko defeats Marisa[edit]

Miko

前々からお前は見所のある奴だと
思っていた、どうだ？
私のスレイブにならないか？

You've always shown promise.
How about it?
Won't you become my slave?

Miko defeats Ichirin[edit]

Miko

入道に頼りっきりだな
私の元で修業すれば
強くなると思うんだが、どうだ？

You're too reliant on your nyuudou.
How about this?
If you train by my side, you can become stronger.

Miko defeats Byakuren[edit]

Miko

正直言って
この異変の解決は困難を
極めると思う

To be honest,
the difficulty of resolving this incident is at its limits.

Miko defeats Futo[edit]

Miko

お前、最近、どんどん俗っぽく
なってるような

You...
Recently, it feels like you've become a bit vulgar.

Miko defeats herself[edit]

Miko

むむ、何故私と戦っているんだ？

Hmm, how did I end up fighting myself?

Miko

こいつがもしかして……

最近噂になっている

夢の世界の自分と言う奴なのか

Don't tell me...

That rumor that's been going around...

Could this be the dream version of me?

Miko defeats Nitori[edit]

Miko

機械的に完全憑依を
解析できるだと!?

You can analyze perfect possession with machines!?

Miko defeats Koishi[edit]

Miko

望むのならその三番目の瞳
開眼させてやろうか？

If you wish,
shall I force that third eye of yours open?

Miko defeats Mamizou[edit]

Miko

お前も口が達者の様だが
実力が伴っていないところが
私と違うところだな

You, like me, have a way with words,
but you don't have the strength to back them up.
That's what makes you different from me.

Miko defeats Kokoro[edit]

Miko

お前はすぐに
お面を壊してしまうな

You break your masks rather quickly, don't you.

Miko defeats Kasen[edit]

Miko

お前は不思議な奴だ
仙人なのに常人に近い
オーラを出す必要が何処にある？

You're quite an unusual person.
Even though you're a hermit, why do you feel the need
to broadcast the aura of just a normal person?

Miko defeats Mokou[edit]

Miko

不老不死なら
もっと賢く生きられそうな
気がするが

If you really are immortal,
I feel like you could live your life a little more wisely.

Miko defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Miko

私の元で鍛え直してみないか？
お前は身体以上の能力を
持っているぞ

Why don't you retrain yourself by my side?
You possess a power that exceeds your physical capabilities.

Miko defeats Sumireko[edit]

Miko

ほう、私の生きた時代は
飛鳥時代と言うのか
中々良い響きだ

Oh, so the period I lived in is
called the Asuka period.
It has a nice ring to it.

Miko defeats Reisen[edit]

Miko

月の都とは
いつか決着を着けねばならぬな

At some point,
we shall have to settle things with the Lunarians.

Miko

そうだ、この完全憑依異変も
都市伝説異変の影響だったな

稀神サグメという奴について
知っていることを教えよ

That's right, the Perfect Possession
Incident occurred due to the Urban Legend Incident.

Tell me everything you know about
this person named Sagume Kishin.

Miko defeats Doremy[edit]

Miko

夢の世界という物が
本当に存在するとはな

To think something like the
Dream World would actually exist.

Miko defeats Tenshi[edit]

Miko

天人ならば教えて欲しい
私が天界に行くには
後は何が必要だ？

If you really are a celestial, then answer me this.
What more do I need now in order to reach Heaven?

Miko defeats Yukari[edit]

Miko

完全憑依異変について
何か情報を握っているようだな

It seems like you're about to grasp
some vital information regarding the Perfect Possession Incident.

Miko defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Miko

悔しいがよく判っているな
人間というのは派手な奴に
つい貢いでしまうもんだ

It's frustrating, but I understand the feeling.
Humans are always quick to support the flashiest one among them.

Against Shion

Miko

貧しさは判断を誤らせる
僅かだが恵んでやろう

The poor will err in their judgment.
I shall forgive you, if only for a bit.

Generic Victory[edit]

Miko

我のスレイブになりたくなったら
いつでも言うがいい！

If you wish to become my slave,
feel free to say so at any time!

Miko works together with Kasen for a slave[edit]

Miko

仙人コンビというのも悪くない

見た目は違えどっ居z根本にある仙術は
同じだから相性が良いな

A hermit team is not bad at all.

Though it may look different on the outside,
our hermit arts come from the same roots,

so we're rather compatible.
