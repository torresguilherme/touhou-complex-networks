Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Reimu's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Reimu defeats herself[edit]

Reimu

憑依しすぎなのか私が見える……

Am I getting possessed too much? I'm starting to see myself...

Reimu defeats Marisa[edit]

Reimu

異変解決は私に任せて！
あんたは大人しく
スレイブしてれば良いのよ

Leave the incident solving to me!
You'd better just stay
as an obedient slave.

Reimu defeats Ichirin[edit]

Reimu

あんたのスレイブは
その入道だけじゃなかったの？

Wasn't that nyuudou your only slave?

Reimu defeats Byakuren[edit]

Reimu

あんたも異変解決の調査
してるんだって？
商売の邪魔よ

I heard you were investigating
to resolve this incident as well?
You're interfering with my business.

Reimu defeats Futo[edit]

Reimu

あんた、どんどん
馬鹿っぽくなってない？

Are you...
actually getting dumber?

Reimu defeats Miko[edit]

Reimu

そうなのね、貴方も異変解決の
ライバルなのねぇ

Yeah, yeah, sure,
another rival of incident solving...

Reimu defeats Nitori[edit]

Reimu

この異変って、機械的な仕掛けで
出来ているって事はないかしら

This incident... I wonder if
it could be caused by mechanical means.

Reimu defeats Koishi[edit]

Reimu

完全憑依の所為なのか
あんたがハッキリと認識出来るわ

Dunno if it's because of perfect possession, but
I can clearly recognize you.

Reimu defeats Mamizou[edit]

Reimu

完全憑依だったのか
ただ単に化けただけかと思ったわ

So that was perfect possession?
I thought you had just disguised yourself.

Reimu defeats Kokoro[edit]

Reimu

あれから結構経つけど
中々表情豊かにならないわねぇ

It's been a while since then,
but you're as expressionless as ever.

Reimu defeats Kasen[edit]

Reimu

あんたの腕の怪我って
治らないの？

That injury on your arm...
Is it ever going to get better?

Reimu defeats Mokou[edit]

Reimu

そういえば光る竹を見つけた
という噂を聞いたんだけど……

Come to think of it, I've heard rumors
about people finding glowing bamboo...

Reimu defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Reimu

あんたに完全憑依した奴は
そのお椀には入ったままだと
思ってたのに

Aww. I thought the one who
perfect possessed you was
hiding in that bowl all the time.

Reimu defeats Sumireko[edit]

Reimu

超能力って、手品と何が違うの？

What's the difference between ESP and sleight of hand?

Reimu defeats Reisen[edit]

Reimu

奴隷根性が染み付いてて
上手くスレイブを扱えてない
みたいね

You're really used to serving people,
so it seems you're not so good at
handling your own slave.

Reimu defeats Doremy[edit]

Reimu

手に持っている
ぷよぷよしてるやつ
それ何？

That jelly-ish thing you are holding...
What IS that exactly?[1]

Reimu defeats Tenshi[edit]

Reimu

久しぶりね
また天界で暴れてたのかしら

Long time no see.
I wonder if you've made a lot of
trouble back in Heaven as well.

Reimu

完全憑依ばっかで
オカルトの存在を
忘れていたけどさー

あんたのオカルト
マジで何なの？

Because of all this perfect possession
I'd forgotten about urban legends, but...
Really, what IS
your urban legend all about?

Reimu defeats Yukari[edit]

Reimu

完全憑依異変、マジで厄介ね

This Perfect Possession Incident is seriously getting on my nerves.

Reimu defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Reimu

お金持ってそうね
私と何かやらない？

Hey, you look rich.
Wanna do some business with me?

Reimu

ところでさ

あんたらは二人組なのに
更に完全憑依出来る
っておかしくない？

By the way...
You're already a team of two,
but you can perfect possess on top of that.
Isn't that a little weird?

Generic Victory[edit]

Reimu

多分、私一人でも勝ててたわね

I probably could've won all on my own, though.

Reimu

やだ、この異変は楽しい……
いやいや解決しないとね

Oh dear... This incident is so fun...
No no no, I need to resolve it..

Reimu works together with Marisa for a slave[edit]

Reimu

意外と魔理沙 (あんた) とコンビを組んで
戦った事って殆ど無かったけど

練習次第では最強の二人に
なれるかも？

Surprisingly, we hardly ever
team up and fight together, do we, Marisa?

Depending on how hard we practice,
maybe we could become the strongest duo.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A dream soul.
