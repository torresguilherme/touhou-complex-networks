Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Marisa's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Marisa defeats Reimu[edit]

Marisa

こんな面白い異変も珍しいな
私が調べてやるよ

Incidents this interesting are pretty rare.
I'll look into it for you.

Marisa

ところで

プリズムリバーのライブって
何であんなに人気あるんだ？

By the way,

Why are the Prismrivers' concerts
so popular all of a sudden?

Marisa defeats herself[edit]

Marisa

な、何故私が居るんだ？

Wh-why is there another me?

Marisa defeats Ichirin[edit]

Marisa

お前は最初からタッグだったな
それはハンデだと思っていたが

You were a tag team to begin with, weren'tcha?
Always thought of it as a handicap, personally.

Marisa defeats Byakuren[edit]

Marisa

確実に檀家を増やしているらしいな
命蓮寺は博麗神社と比べると
商売上手だな

Seems like your following is steadily growing.
Compared to the Hakurei Shrine,
business is booming at Myouren Temple.

Marisa defeats Futo[edit]

Marisa

皿洗いのバイトは終わったのか？

Are you done with your dishwashing job?

Marisa defeats Miko[edit]

Marisa

我が強い奴だから
お前一人の方が
強いんじゃないか？

Since you're so egoistic,
wouldn't you be stronger on your own?

Marisa defeats Nitori[edit]

Marisa

技術者のくせにこんな
非科学的な異変を利用するとはな

I'm surprised an engineer like you is able to utilize
an unscientific incident like this.

Marisa defeats Koishi[edit]

Marisa

気が付いたら勝っていたぜ
相変わらずお前は不思議な奴だ

By the time I noticed, I'd already won.
You're as mysterious as ever.

Marisa defeats Mamizou[edit]

Marisa

煙草の火が尻尾に
着火しそうなのが気になってな

I'm curious...
It feels like you could easily
light your tail on fire with your pipe.

Marisa defeats Kokoro[edit]

Marisa

お前は取り憑くスレイブ側の方が
似合うと思うぞ

I think you'd fit better on the possessing side,
as a slave, y'know.

Marisa defeats Kasen[edit]

Marisa

動物達が戸惑っているぜ
飼い主が完全憑依されて

The animals get all confused, you know.
When their master gets perfect possessed.

Marisa defeats Mokou[edit]

Marisa

お前昔にフェニックスに
憑依されてなかったか？

Weren't you possessed by a phoenix in the past?

Marisa defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Marisa

小さくてもいっちょ前に
完全憑依出来るのか

So you can still properly do perfect
possession even if you're small, huh.

Marisa defeats Sumireko[edit]

Marisa

その手に持ってる奴
何か高価そうだな
ヒビは入ってるけど

That thing you're holding looks pretty expensive.
Although there are some cracks on it.

Marisa defeats Reisen[edit]

Marisa

この完全憑依異変
もしかして月の技術じゃないよな

Don't tell me this Perfect Possession Incident
comes from lunar technology?

Marisa defeats Doremy[edit]

Marisa

散髪しない主義なのか？

Is it a rule of yours to never cut your hair?

Marisa defeats Tenshi[edit]

Marisa

何でお前が地上にいるんだよ

What are you doing on Earth?

Marisa defeats Yukari[edit]

Marisa

最近、お前が出てこなくて
平和だったのにな

It had been peaceful recently since you weren't showing up.

Marisa defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Marisa

金遣い荒そうだなー

Wow, you seem like the extravagant type.

Against Shion

Marisa

うわー、貧乏神だー
逃げろー

Uwah! Poverty god!
Run away!

Generic Victory[edit]

Marisa

憑依と言っても
身体を乗っ取られるという
感覚とはちょっと違うな

They call it "possession",
but it's a little different from
someone taking over your body.

Marisa works together with Koishi for a slave[edit]

Marisa

やった勝てたぜ

扱いづらいこいし (おまえ) だけど……
翻弄役のスレイブとしては
優秀かもな

Yay, we did it!
You're a little hard to handle, Koishi...
But you're probably the best at being a
slave that messes around with others.
