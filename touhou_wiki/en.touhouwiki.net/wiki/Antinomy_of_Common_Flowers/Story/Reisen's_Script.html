Antinomy of Common Flowers/Story/Reisen's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Antinomy of Common Flowers‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Reisen defeats Reimu[edit]

Reisen

この異変は私が調べます
元、月の兎としての負い目も
ありますので

I'll look into this incident.
I still have a debt to pay as a former Moon Rabbit,
after all.

Reisen defeats Marisa[edit]

Reisen

あんたがマスターだったんだ
見た目からてっきりスレイブかと

Oh, so you were the master?
Just from appearances,
I could've sworn you were the slave.

Reisen defeats Ichirin[edit]

Reisen

入道は憑依では無く
リアルスレイブなのねぇ

So if that nyuudou isn't possessing you,
I guess he's a REAL slave.

Reisen defeats Byakuren[edit]

Reisen

僧侶って
完全憑依みたいな
胡散臭い力使っていいの？

Is it okay for a monk like you to use a shady power
like perfect possession?

Reisen defeats Futo[edit]

Reisen

尸解仙とはゾンビの事だって
お師匠様に教えて貰いましたよ

I've learned a lot from my master
about shikaisen and zombies as well.

Reisen defeats Miko[edit]

Reisen

貴方も完全憑依異変の調査を
しているのですね

So you are also investigating
the Perfect Possession Incident.

Reisen

サグメ様はですねぇ……

月の都の政治的な要人なんですよ
無口だけど大胆で、ちょっと……
おっちょこちょいなんですけどね

About Lady Sagume...

She is an influential politician in the Lunar Capital.
Reticent but bold, and also...
She can be a bit scatterbrained.

Reisen defeats Nitori[edit]

Reisen

そんなオモチャで月の技術に
勝とうなんて
ちゃんちゃらおかしいわ！

You think you can win against
lunar technology with those toys of yours?
What a joke!

Reisen defeats Koishi[edit]

Reisen

その無意識の身のこなし
みんなには効いているけど
私の眼には丸見えよ

Your unconscious movements may work on everyone else,
but they're completely visible to my eyes.

Reisen defeats Mamizou[edit]

Reisen

まだカチカチ山事件を
引きずっているんですか？

Are we reenacting the fire-crackle mountain story?
Is that it?

Reisen defeats Kokoro[edit]

Reisen

いつまでも表情が硬いのは
お面の妖怪だからって事？

The reason your face is always so stiff...
Does it have to do with you being a mask youkai?

Reisen defeats Kasen[edit]

Reisen

動物は操れても
私は操れないようね！

You may be able to control animals,
but it doesn't look like you can control me!

Reisen defeats Mokou[edit]

Reisen

最初に完全憑依を見たのは
霊夢と貴方が入れ替わった
時なのよねぇ

The first ever Perfect Possession
I saw was when Reimu switched places with you, wasnt it?

Reisen defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Reisen

小人族には失礼無いように
ってお師匠様に言われたんだけど
ま、いっか

Master told me to be more respectful to inchlings, though...
Well, whatever.

Reisen defeats Sumireko[edit]

Reisen

うさぎカフェ？
外の世界では兎が
持て囃されてるんですか？

Rabbit Café?
So the rabbits in the outside world
are treated extravagantly?

Reisen defeats herself[edit]

Reisen

見つけたぞー！
偽物だー！

I found you... faker!

Reisen

ふっふっふ
我こそが本物だー！

Hahaha! I'm the real Reisen!

Reisen defeats Doremy[edit]

Reisen

何で、貴方 (バク) が幻想郷に……!?

Why is a baku like you in Gensokyo...!?

Reisen defeats Tenshi[edit]

Reisen

お久しぶりね
あんまり会いたくなかったけど

Long time no see.
Didn't really want to see you though.

Reisen defeats Yukari[edit]

Reisen

貴方が出てきたって事は
この異変も貴方がらみなのね

The fact that you showed up means that this
incident has something to do with you, right?

Reisen defeats Joon[edit]

Against Joon

Reisen

いくら豪勢に着飾ったって
下賤さが滲み出ているわ！

No matter how extravagantly you dress up,
your lowly origins still show through!

Against Shion

Reisen

頼むから
うちに来ないで下さいね……

I'm begging you, stay away from me...

Generic Victory[edit]

Reisen

完全憑依かー
これも都市伝説異変の影響って
ことよねぇ

So that's Perfect Possession...
Another effect of Urban Legend Incident, I see.

Reisen works together with Doremy for a slave[edit]

Reisen

ふっふっふ
私は夢の世界の鈴仙だ

たまに現 (うつつ) の世界で暴れるのは
気持ちいいねぇ

Hahaha!
I am the Reisen from the Dream World!

It feels good to run wild in the real world once in a while!
