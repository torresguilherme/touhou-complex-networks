Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A play on the phrase "一寸の虫にも五分の魂" ("even a one-sun bug has a five-bu soul," or less literally "even the smallest beings have their own will". One sun, roughly equivalent to an inch, includes ten bu; this unit of measurement is the namesake of the 'one-inch samurai' Issun-Boushi.)
