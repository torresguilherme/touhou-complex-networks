Spell Card Last Word
＊宇宙機密漏洩！直ちに処置せよ＊
*Space Security Leak! Deal With the Situation Promptly*
Owner
Mamizou Futatsuiwa

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Bunbuku refers to Bunbuku Chagame, an old Japanese folktale
  2. ↑ The word youkai (妖界) in its name is a pun combining the words youkai (妖怪) and world (界, kai).
  3. ↑ This is a parody of "Royal Judgment" or "Pharaoh's Curse" (王家の裁き), the special attack of Anakaris, a character from the Darkstalkers series.
