Description

Liz Triangle is a doujin music circle led by kaztora.

The circle was originally called ShinRa-Bansho, but after their first release under that label, the circle's name was changed to their current one. The label Shinra-bansho lay unused until 2012, when it became a sub-circle of Liz Triangle for alternate releases.

It was announced in July of 2016 by vocalist lily-an that the circle would be disbanding the following year due to the her time constraints and health.[1] kaztora has stated that after Liz Triangle disbands he plans to make ShinRa-Bansho his main focus. Though the circle originally planned to disband after C92, vocalist lily-an later confirmed that it had been postponed until C93.[2]
