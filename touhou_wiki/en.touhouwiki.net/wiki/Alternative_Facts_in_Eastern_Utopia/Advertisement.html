Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ The word in the original text "公司" is "company/firm/enterprise" in Chinese. Here it is temporarily translated in French, with the group "Cirque du Soleil" in mind; the name is French while its headquarter is placed at Quebec, Canada.
  2. ↑ This and various other phrases in the advertisement (such as "wages negotiable, experience preferred") are notorious as warning signs of exploitative businesses.
    "reliable employer" - autocratic president
    "kind superior" - boss imposing too many penalties
    "tons of partners" - bad air without tenderness among co-workers
    "wages negotiable with employers" - they take every chance to look for little mistakes of employees and to make their wage down
    "filled with smiles" - they force employees to be always positive and obedient
    "at-home" - they don't promote cooperative environment, a boss always shouts to scold people.
  3. ↑ Spoof on the Beppu Jigoku-meguri hot spring sightseeing tour.
