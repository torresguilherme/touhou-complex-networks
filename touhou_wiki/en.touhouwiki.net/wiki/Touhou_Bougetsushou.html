Touhou Bougetsushou

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Front image from the Touhou Bougetsushou webpage.

Touhou Bougetsushou (東方儚月抄) is a loosely interconnected storyline about the Lunarians that includes Silent Sinner in Blue, Cage in Lunatic Runagate, and Inaba of the Moon and Inaba of the Earth. Bougetsushou (儚月抄) can be translated as "Ephemeral Moon Vignette"; compare with the main title of its predecessor 東方永夜抄　～ Imperishable Night (Touhou Eiyashou), where "永夜抄" translates to "Eternal Night Vignette".

Works[edit]

  • 東方儚月抄　～ Silent Sinner in Blue, a manga drawn by Aki★Eda and written by ZUN, serialised in Comic Rex.
  • 東方儚月抄　～ Cage in Lunatic Runagate, a novel written by ZUN and illustrated by TOKIAME, serialised in Chara☆Mel.
  • 東方儚月抄　～ 月のイナバと地上の因幡 (Inaba of Moon & Inaba of Earth), a series of comedic 4koma strips drawn by Toshihira Arata and setting provided by ZUN, serialised in Manga 4koma Kings Palette
  • Silent Sinner in Blue (CD), a music CD included with the first volume of the Silent Sinner in Blue tankoubon.
