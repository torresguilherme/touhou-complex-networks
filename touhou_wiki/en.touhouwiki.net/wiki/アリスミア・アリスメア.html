Dates

アリスミア・アリスメア official website
Genre
Math rock, Rock
Members
麻疹 (hacica) — Arrangement, Bass, Lyrics (Twitter, Soundcloud)
真歩 (Miho) — Vocals (joined the circle in 2017)

Description

The official English name of this circle has a typo, the correct spelling is Arrhythmia. Before Miho joined the circle, all vocal arrangements were preformed by Hatsune Miku.
