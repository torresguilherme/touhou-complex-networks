Description

Kishida Kyoudan & The Akeboshi Rockets (岸田教団＆The明星ロケッツ) is a rock music group that does both Touhou arrangements and original songs, including anime and visual novel soundtracks. They're frequent guests on Flowering Night concerts where they often preform with T-tsu as a support guitarist.

Formerly known as Studio K2 (not to be confused with K2 SOUND).


Discography
SUPERNOVA
Release date: 2005-12-30 (Comiket 69)
An instrumental and vocal album featuring arrangements from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night, as well as two original compositions, two arrangements from The Legend of Heroes: Trails in the Sky and one arrangement from Melty Blood.

明星ロケット
Release date: 2006-05-21 (Reitaisai 3)
An instrumental album featuring arrangements from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet.

SuperSonicSpeedStar
Release date: 2006-12-31 (Comiket 71)
An instrumental album featuring arrangements from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story and Mystic Square.

幻想事変
Release date: 2007-05-20 (Reitaisai 4)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

星空ロジック
Release date: 2007-12-31 (Comiket 73)
A vocal album featuring original compositions.

Electric blue
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith.

Literal World
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
A vocal album featuring original compositions.

ROLLING★STAR
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

セブンスワールド
Release date: 2012-04-30 (M3-29)
A vocal album featuring original compositions.

.JP
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Ten Desires and Magical Astronomy.

ロックンロール ラボラトリー
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
A vocal album featuring an arrangement from Shoot the Bullet and four original compositions.

スルメイカー
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night and Undefined Fantastic Object.

2007-2010 東方アレンジベスト
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
A vocal compilation album featuring arrangements from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet and Subterranean Animism. It features re-recordings of the circle's earlier works from 2007-2010.

SUPER SONIC APPLE RABBIT
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
A vocal album featuring arrangements from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Subterranean Animism.

ROCK'N'ROLL LABORATORY 2
Release date: 2016-04-24 (M3-37)
A vocal album featuring mostly original compositions in addition to arrangements from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet. It features 2 new original compositions, 4 re-recordings of early original compositions, 3 re-released original visual novel openings (2 of which are unique to the album) and and 3 re-released Touhou arrangements.

ANCIENT FLOWER
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism and Fairy Wars.
