Therianthrope

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Therianthropes (獣人, Juujin/Kemonobito[1], lit. "beastmen") are human hybrids with other animals, sometimes classified as a type of beast youkai. Wolf theriantropes (werewolves) are specifically referred to as Lycanthropes.

Therianthropes in Touhou[edit]

Characters under this Bestiary[edit]

  • Kagerou Imaizumi (Werewolf)
  • Keine Kamishirasawa (Were-Hakutaku)
