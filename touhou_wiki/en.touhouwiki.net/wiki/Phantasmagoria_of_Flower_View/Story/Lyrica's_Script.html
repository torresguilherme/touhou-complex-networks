Phantasmagoria of Flower View/Story/Lyrica's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Phantasmagoria of Flower View‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Lyrica defeats Reimu[edit]

Lyrica

神社には死んだ音って余り無いのよねー。

There's not a lot of dead sounds in the shrine, huh.

Lyrica defeats Marisa[edit]

Lyrica

魔法の森では耳を澄ますとキノコの生長する
音が聞こえるって知ってた？

Did you know if you strain your ears in magical forests that you can hear the sound of mushrooms growing?

Lyrica defeats Sakuya[edit]

Lyrica

紅色はこの世の重低音。
紅色を超えると幻想の音。私の出番ね。

Rainbows are the bass sounds of this world.
Exceeding the rainbows are illusionary sounds. That's where I come in.

Lyrica defeats Youmu[edit]

Lyrica

幽霊の音って、実際何の音なのかしら？

I wonder what kind of sound the sound of a ghost is?

Lyrica defeats Reisen[edit]

Lyrica

波を弄ることが出来るのなら、シンセサイザー
みたいなもんよね。

When you're able to tamper with the waves, you're like a synthesizer.

Lyrica defeats Cirno[edit]

Lyrica

物が凍ると音も凍るのよー。
氷を割ったときの音はその音が溶けた時の音。

You freeze sound as well as things.
The sound of the ice breaking is the sound of those sounds melting.

Lyrica defeats herself[edit]

Lyrica

あら、そっちのキーボードの方が良いなぁ……

Huh, that keyboard is better...

Lyrica defeats Merlin[edit]

Lyrica

ほら、私が一番偉いでしょ？

See, aren't I the greatest?

Lyrica defeats Lunasa[edit]

Lyrica

ほら、私が一番偉いでしょ？

See, aren't I the greatest?

Lyrica defeats Mystia[edit]

Lyrica

あんたは歌に恵まれてないんじゃないの？

You're blessed by song, aren't you?

Lyrica defeats Tewi[edit]

Lyrica

兎が跳ぶときのぴょんって音。
聞いたことある？　もしかして幻想の音？

Have you heard the "pyon" sound when a rabbit jumps?
Or maybe it was an illusion?

Lyrica defeats Aya[edit]

Lyrica

新聞は音が出ないからなぁ……
出るようになればみんな購読するんじゃん？

Because newspapers don't make sound...
If you made it so they could maybe everyone would buy it?

Lyrica defeats Medicine[edit]

Lyrica

そうそう。
音を出す人形は見たことがあるよ。

チンパンジーだったけど。

That's right.
I've seen a doll that makes sound.

But it was a chimpanzee.

Lyrica defeats Yuuka[edit]

Lyrica

音に反応して踊る花の夢を見たわー。

I had a dream about flowers that danced in response to sound.

Lyrica defeats Komachi[edit]

Lyrica

そう言えば、外の世界では硬貨のジャラジャラ
って音も減りつつあるんだってさ。

That reminds me, the sound of jingling coins in the outside world is decreasing as well.

Lyrica defeats Eiki[edit]

Lyrica

ああ、説教の音も幻想の音になっていく。

説教する人間も減りつつあるってね？

Ah, the sound of a sermon is becoming an illusionary sound.

The amount of people preaching is decreasing, right?

Lyrica defeats someone[edit]

Lyrica

このキーボードの霊には、既に亡くなった音が
詰まってるの。

The soul of this keyboard is already packed with sounds that have passed away.

Lyrica

ぎゃふんって音出す人間居ないかなぁ……

I wonder if there is anyone who can make the sound of speechlessness...
