Phantasmagoria of Flower View/Story/Lunasa's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Phantasmagoria of Flower View‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Lunasa defeats Reimu[edit]

Lunasa

巫女なのに少しはしゃぎすぎね。
冷静に異変を見て。

You're a little overexcited for a shrine maiden.
Watch over the incident with composure.

Lunasa defeats Marisa[edit]

Lunasa

貴方みたいな爆弾が浮かれた行動を取ると
怖いわね。

It's scary to take actions against bombs dropped like yours.

Lunasa defeats Sakuya[edit]

Lunasa

さあ、貴方の今の境遇を思い出して。
悪魔に閉じこめられている境遇を。

Hey, now I remember what's going on with you.
You're locked up by a demon.

Lunasa defeats Youmu[edit]

Lunasa

冷静になって……
貴方の今の境遇を考えて。

In a level-headed way....
Think about your current circumstances.

Lunasa defeats Reisen[edit]

Lunasa

花が咲く程度、なんてこと無いわ。
今の貴方の悲しい境遇を見ると。

When I see your sad state, there's no limit to which the flowers could grow.

Lunasa defeats Cirno[edit]

Lunasa

もっと冷静になって。
貴方は馬鹿にされているの。

Calm down.
You're making yourself look foolish.

Lunasa defeats Lyrica[edit]

Lunasa

音が死んでいるわ。
もっと現実に無い音を探すの。

The sound is dying.
I'll look for a more unearthly sound.

Lunasa defeats Merlin[edit]

Lunasa

音が抜けてしまっているわ。
地に足をつける練習もしなさい。

You missed a note.
Practice with your feet firmly on the ground.

Lunasa defeats herself[edit]

Lunasa

あら、ちょっと音が外れてる……。

Huh, the sound's a bit off...

Lunasa defeats Mystia[edit]

Lunasa

歌も選んで歌いましょう。
変な歌ばかりですよ？

Pick a song and let's sing.
Or do you only have weird songs?

Lunasa defeats Tewi[edit]

Lunasa

もっと落ち着いて歩きなさい。

Please walk more calmly.

Lunasa defeats Aya[edit]

Lunasa

自伝でも書いて少し思い直してみたら？
って、何冊になるのかしら。

I wonder how many volumes one's autobiography would be if they looked back a bit.

Lunasa defeats Medicine[edit]

Lunasa

貴方は悲しい境遇の生まれなの。
浮かれてる場合じゃないわ。

You were born in a sad environment.
It's no time to be merry.

Lunasa defeats Yuuka[edit]

Lunasa

向日葵が太陽を向いているのは、羨望と妬み。
決して陽気な花とは言えない。

Sunflowers turn to the sun out of jealousy and want.
Of course I wouldn't call them cheerful flowers.

Lunasa defeats Komachi[edit]

Lunasa

貴方の周りは常に葬式ムードでないと、死んだ
感じがしないと思う。

I think if the mood around you weren't always that of a funeral, it wouldn't feel like you've died.

Lunasa defeats Eiki[edit]

Lunasa

地獄の裁判は騒々しいのかしら。
幽霊の反抗心を奪ってから裁くのかしら？

I wonder if the trials in hell are noisy.
Do you judge because you're fascinated by the opposition of the ghosts?

Lunasa defeats someone[edit]

Lunasa

そう。
少しはしゃぎすぎだから落ち着いて。

That's right.
You're a little too excited, so calm down.

Lunasa

もっと落ち着いて行動しないと、解決する物も
解決しない。

If you don't calm down a little more, even what's been determined won't settle.
