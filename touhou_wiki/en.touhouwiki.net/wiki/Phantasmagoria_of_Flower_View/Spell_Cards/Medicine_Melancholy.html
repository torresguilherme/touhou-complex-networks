Spell Card Charge
「ポイズンビー」 ("Poizun Bii")
"Poison Bee"
Owner
Medicine Melancholy
Editor's Comment
Medicine fires a spread of bullets that curve around her, tracing out a flower shape. These bullets slow down near the end of their flight and disappear after a short time.
Spell Card EX
「スウィートポイズン」 ("Suwiito Poizun")
"Sweet Poison"
Owner
Medicine Melancholy
Editor's Comment
A poison cloud appears near the opponent. The opponent's movement while within this cloud is slowed. Note that the effect of these clouds stacks, and several overlapping clouds can render the player nearly immobile. Also, note that these clouds are not removable in any way. That is, even the bullet removing rings that result from Lv2 and higher attacks will not remove these clouds. They will only disappear by themselves several seconds after they appear.
Spell Card Lv2
毒符「神経の毒」 (Dokufu "Shinkei no Doku")
Poison Sign "Nerve Poison"
Owner
Medicine Melancholy
Editor's Comment
A spread of randomly arranged bullets appear from the center of the opponent's screen and spread outwards. Many of these bullets move in slightly different directions, making this spread look even more irregular. The level of this attack affects the number of bullets that make up this spread. Half of these bullets are removable.
Spell Card Lv3
毒符「神経の毒」 (Dokufu "Shinkei no Doku")
Poison Sign "Nerve Poison"
Owner
Medicine Melancholy
Editor's Comment
Same as her Lv2, except that the removable bullets are now replaced by red triangle bullets.
Spell Card Boss
毒符「憂鬱の毒」 (Dokufu "Yuu'utsu no Doku")
Poison Sign "A Poison for Melancholy"[1]
Owner
Medicine Melancholy
Editor's Comment
Boss summon.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ This spellcard's name is a reference to A Medicine for Melancholy, a short story by Ray Bradbury.
