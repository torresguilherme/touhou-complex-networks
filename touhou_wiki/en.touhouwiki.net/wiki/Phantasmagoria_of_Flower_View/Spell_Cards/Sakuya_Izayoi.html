Spell Card Charge
「ジャック・ザ・リッパー」 ("Jakku za Rippaa")
"Jack the Ripper"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Editor's Comment
Sakuya's charge has an initial start-up time, where the blades can be seen whirling around her. She then fires off a short stream of knives, each auto-targeting (but not homing).
Spell Card EX
「アナザーマーダー」 ("Anazaa Maadaa")
"Another Murder"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Editor's Comment
Sakuya generates a stream of knives (about 5-6 end to end) from the point of EX generation (as opposed to the normal flying sphere that places an EX object) that cross screens into the opponent's. The knife stream targets the enemy when it's created, and travels in that direction, only becoming "real" (a deeper shade of blue) when in the opponent's screen. The knives will follow their original path and exit the screen regardless.
Spell Card Lv2
時符「プライベートヴィジョン」 (Jifu "Puraibeeto Vijon")
Time Sign "Private Vision"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Editor's Comment
Sakuya temporarily freezes time to place a series of knives in a small arc (roughly 120 degrees) around the opponent. Each knife has a dot bullet around its "handle." When the time freeze ends, all knives (and bullets) move forward in the direction they were pointing.

A time freeze stops everything on the screen from moving (both sides), except for any Sakuyas. This means that the user of the Spell Card has free movement, and can pre-emptively fire ahead of time (you can see the ghost of the knives she shoots while time is frozen). However, if your opponent is Sakuya, she also is not affected by the time freeze. If not, no movement or action is possible.

Higher Spell Card levels increase the speed at which the knives appear, and shorten the duration of the time freeze consequently. It also affect knife movement speed.
Spell Card Lv3
時符「プライベートヴィジョン」 (Jifu "Puraibeeto Vijon")
Time Sign "Private Vision"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Editor's Comment
In the level three version, each knife in level 2 now has four knives stacked on top of each other, each at 90 degree rotations from the original. When time is restored, these knives move in the direction they're pointing at. The level 2 note about time freezing still applies here.
Spell Card Boss
時符「ミステリアスジャック」 (Jifu "Misuteriasu Jakku")
Time Sign "Mysterious Jack"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Editor's Comment
Boss attack.
