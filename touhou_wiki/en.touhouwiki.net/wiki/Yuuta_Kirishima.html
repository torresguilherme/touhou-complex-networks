Yuuta Kirishima (桐島 雄太, Kirishima Yuuta) is a subordinate for Lagunas, the Stage 2 boss and Stage 5 midboss/supporting boss of Banshiryuu. He uses a flying tank in battle, and in the C74 version, he uniquely used an "earth destruction bomb" that would result in an immediate game over if destroyed.

General Information[edit]

Yuuta is a young boy who has a very tenacious personality. He's the type that will always have a grudge against other people. According to VIVIT-r, Yuuta is going to live for another 100 years.

Character Basis[edit]

Name[edit]

His full name is Yuuta Kirishima (桐島 雄太) His surname Kirishima (桐島) means "Paulownia Isle", while his first name Yuuta (雄太) means "Noble Masculine". His given and surname are actual names in Japanese, where his given name is genderless.
