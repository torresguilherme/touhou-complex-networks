幽閉少女アクティブNEETs

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A joint circle composed of 3 doujin circles: Yuuhei Satellite, Shoujo Fractal and 交響アクティブNEETs. All of the albums feature orchestral rearrangements of previously arranged songs by Yuuhei Satellite and Shoujo Fractal.

Profile

Yuuhei Shoujo Active NEETs

2016 - present
Genre
Vocal, Orchestral
Members

Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)

第2幕 協奏曲「月に叢雲華に風」 SIDE A
