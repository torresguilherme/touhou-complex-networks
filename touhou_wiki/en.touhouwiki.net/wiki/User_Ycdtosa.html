About me and Touhou[edit]

I first heard of Touhou through Mario Hacks but didn't have any idea of what it was, I just assumed it was a really hard Mario Hack like Kaizo Mario or something. I ended up stumbling across the game again in late 2013 though Smooth McGroove's acapella cover of UN Owen which actually included gameplay. I was completely captivated by the game when I finally saw it, started playing it pretty much that same day and ended up putting allot of time into EoSD. I ended up finding TouhouSubs in late 2014 and it introduced me to to fan side of Touhou which I found really interesting and fun despite only knowing the early windows era characters at the time. Shortly after that I started paying attention to doujin music around Comiket 87 and then 1 year later I decided to start contributing to the wiki which ended up teaching me allot about Touhou arrangement circles. I've played most of the windows era games at this point but I've only put any realy time into EoSD, IN, SA, DDC & LoLK.
