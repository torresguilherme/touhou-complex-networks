Sea of Tranquility (静かの海, Shizuka no Umi) or Sea of Tranquillity (see spelling differences), also known as Mare Tranquillitatis in Latin is a lunar mare, a large and dark plain that's located on the Near Side of the Moon (月の表側). Unlike reality, it's shown to have an actual sea.

Sea of Tranquility Appearances[edit]

Games[edit]

Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom

The Sea of Tranquility appears as the setting for Stages 5 and 6 of Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and it seems to have its own atmosphere due to the life force of the fairies invading it.
