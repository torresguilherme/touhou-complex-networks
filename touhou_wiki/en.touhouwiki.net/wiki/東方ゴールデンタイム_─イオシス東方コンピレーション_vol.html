Touhou Golden Time -IOSYS Touhou Compilation vol.25- (東方ゴールデンタイム ─イオシス東方コンピレーション vol.25─) is a doujin album by IOSYS released on 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14). It features vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Mountain of Faith, Immaterial and Missing Power and Double Dealing Character.
