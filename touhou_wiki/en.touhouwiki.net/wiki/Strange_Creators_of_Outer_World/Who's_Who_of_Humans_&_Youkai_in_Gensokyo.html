Who's Who of Humans & Youkai in Gensokyo (幻想郷人妖名鑑, Gensoukyou Nin You Meikan) is a series of articles in the official Touhou magazine Strange Creators of Outer World, providing background information for major Touhou characters in easily-digestible format. Unlike the profiles in the Gensokyo Chronicle, which were written in-universe by Hieda no Akyuu and used to introduce new details to the setting, Who's Who is written from an out-of-universe perspective and serves mainly to summarize what is already known.

Chart of Listed Characters[edit]

The characters on the red boxes are already introduced, while those on the black columns are not. Note that a number in each box indicates what volume of the magazine she is listed on, and that the certain volumes are fixed in the numbers so as to be arranged in the chronical order: "vol. 2018 Spring!" is vol.5, "vol. 2018 Autumn!" vol.6, "2019 Spring!" vol.7.
