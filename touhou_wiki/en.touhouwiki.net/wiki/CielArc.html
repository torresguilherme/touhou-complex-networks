Discography
Scarlet Sky
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream', Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

Primary Report
Release date: 2014-11-24 (Autumn Reitaisai)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Shoot the Bullet in addition to an original opening track.

Mystical World
Release date: 2015-10-18 (Autumn Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Hopeless Masquerade and Urban Legend in Limbo.

Welcome to my hell
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom. It features 2 tracks that would be released on Ancient Story.

Ancient Story
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Urban Legend in Limbo, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Magical Astronomy.

Mystery Cat
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Touhou Hisoutensoku and Dr. Latency's Freak Report.

Everlasting
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Hopeless Masquerade and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

天空のフロンティア
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

例大祭14限定おまけCD
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil plus an original track.

Prismatic
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Fairy Wars.

Parallel Gate
Release date: 2017-10-15 (Autumn Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

残響のタクティクス
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

Majestic GIG
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Antinomy of Common Flowers.

例大祭15 会場限定特典CD
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Hopeless Masquerade.

CielArc×あらいぐまファクトリー会場限定コラボ特典CD
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
A collaboration album with あらいぐまファクトリー featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power and Hopeless Masquerade.

幻想のプラニスフィア
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy and Dr. Latency's Freak Report.

CielArc Acoustic Collection Vol.1
Release date: 2018-10-14 (Autumn Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Hopeless Masquerade, Urban Legend in Limbo, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, Hidden Star in Four Seasons and Violet Detector.

Unlimited
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Fairy Wars, Urban Legend in Limbo, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

Dramatic Traveler
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Violet Detector.

Synchronicity
Release date: 2019-05-05 (Reitaisai 16)
A collaboration album with Spoo and Nick featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

灼熱のBlazin' Beat
Release date: 2019-05-05 (Reitaisai 16)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Antinomy of Common Flowers.

Other works

  • Happy Magic released 2015-10-25 (M3-36) is an album released by CielArc vocalist ゆずり featuring original vocal compositions.
