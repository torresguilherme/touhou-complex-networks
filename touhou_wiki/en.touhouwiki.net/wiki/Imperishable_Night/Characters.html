Playable Characters[edit]

幻想の結界チーム (Illusionary Barrier Team)[edit]

 楽園の素敵な巫女
Wonderful Shrine Maiden of Paradise

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character; Stage 4-A Boss



境目に潜む妖怪
Youkai that lurks in the boundary

Yukari Yakumo
Playable Character; Reimu Hakurei's Partner (While holding the Shift key)



禁呪の詠唱チーム (Aria of Forbidden Magic Team)[edit]

 普通の黒魔術師
Ordinary Black Magician

Marisa Kirisame
Playable Character; Stage 4-B Boss



七色の人形遣い
Seven-Colored Puppeteer

Alice Margatroid
Playable Character; Marisa Kirisame's Partner (While holding the Shift key)



夢幻の紅魔チーム (Visionary Scarlet Devil Team)[edit]

 紅魔館のメイド
Maid of the Scarlet Devil Mansion

Sakuya Izayoi
Playable Character



紅い悪魔
The Scarlet Devil

Remilia Scarlet
Playable Character; Sakuya Izayoi's Partner (While holding the Shift key)



幽冥の住人チーム (Netherworld Dwellers' Team)[edit]

 半人半霊
Half-Human Half-Phantom

Youmu Konpaku
Playable Character



華胥の亡霊
Dreaming Ghost

Yuyuko Saigyouji
Playable Character; Youmu Konpaku's Partner (While holding the Shift key)
