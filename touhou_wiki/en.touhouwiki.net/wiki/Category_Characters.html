Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame reminisce about characters they've encountered, from the Silent Sinner in Blue Prologue.

The Touhou Project series features a great number and variety of characters. With a cast of over 150 characters, the Touhou world is amazingly large for a shooting game series, and is one of the reasons why the games have become so popular in fanon.
