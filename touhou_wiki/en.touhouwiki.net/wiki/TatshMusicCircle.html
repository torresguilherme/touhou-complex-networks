TatshMusicCircle

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A one-man circle with occasional vocals from singers. Widely known by fans of Dance Dance Revolution and Beatmania as the one behind the song "Xepher".

Profile

Dates
2006 - Present
Website
TatshMusicCircle official website
Genre
Instrumental (primarily), Electronic, Vocal
Members
Tatsh

Description

"Xepher" was originally released as part of Beatmania IIDX, a popular rhythm game, before going on to the sister series, Dance Dance Revolution. What was unique about the song was its original animation (done by MAYA) and the song's genre (Medieval Choir) with the lyrics in English.

The above date includes the original release of Xepher (in 2006), and he would start releasing in Comikets for both original (starting in C74, 2008) and Touhou-related music (starting C76, with FAR EAST OF EAST).


Discography
FAR EAST OF EAST
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, and Undefined Fantastic Object, as well as two original compositions.

FAR EAST OF EAST II
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, and Undefined Fantastic Object, as well as two original compositions.

FAR EAST OF EAST III
Release date: 2010-08-15 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST IV
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Fairy Wars, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST V 東方大清掃
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST VI
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Ten Desires, as well as two original compositions.

FAR EAST OF EAST VII
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Magical Astronomy, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST -VIII-
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night. This album is a compilation of tracks from previous albums.

FAR EAST OF EAST -IX-
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Double Dealing Character, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST -X-
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, as well as one original intro track.

FAR EAST OF EAST -XI-
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Double Spoiler, Ten Desires, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, as well as one original intro track.
