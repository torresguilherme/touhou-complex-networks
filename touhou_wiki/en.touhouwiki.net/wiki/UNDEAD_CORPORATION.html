Discography
幻想郷から超鋼鉄重低爆音
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Eastern and Little Nature Deity and Strange and Bright Nature Deity.

満月の夜に超旋律的重低爆音
Release date: 2010-10-31 (M3-26)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.

極鉄
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

鬼伽草子
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, and Undefined Fantastic Object.

レアトラックス
Release date: 2011-05-01 (M3-27)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism, and Undefined Fantastic Object.

私はもう死んでいる？
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Eastern and Little Nature Deity.

一撃
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism, and Fairy Wars.

紅染の鬼が哭く
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, and Subterranean Animism.

O.D.
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An original album featuring vocal compositions and an instrumental opening.

Parallelism・γ
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, as well as one original composition.
This album is a collaboration between Unlucky Morpheus and UNDEAD CORPORATION.

暴君
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Ten Desires.

TOHO COMPLETE BOX
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
A 4 disc box set featuring every UNDEAD CORPORATION Touhou album up to it's release excluding off vocal tracks in addition to a bonus disc.

神速
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Ten Desires and Double Dealing Character.

兵-つはもの-
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Subterranean Animism.
