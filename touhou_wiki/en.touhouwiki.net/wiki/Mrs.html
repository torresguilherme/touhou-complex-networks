Mrs. Estacion (Mrs. エスタシオン) is a vertical danmaku shooter made by 楓実クルト. It's not specifically a Touhou Project fangame, but many of the bullet patterns and spell cards are references to those found in various Touhou Project games. In addition, the soundtrack is in a style ZUN would use in the official Touhou games.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

Mrs. Estacion features a single playable character to choose from, with three shot types.

The scoring system is loosely based on that of Subterranean Animism, with items automatically being collected if the player grazes for enough time. Also, a bullet will award graze points as long as the player is close enough to it.
