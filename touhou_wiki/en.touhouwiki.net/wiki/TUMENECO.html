TUMENECO Color CD series

TUMENECO Color CD is a series consisting of seven singles. Each CD contains three tracks: an instrumental arrangement, a vocal arrangement and its instrumental version. The series was created to celebrate the 10th anniversary of TUMENECO. At Comiket 93, ナナイロノキセツ‐Under the Rainbow- was released. It is a compilation containing the vocal and instrumental arrangements from every entry in the series, but it does not contain the instrumental versions of the vocal arrangements.
