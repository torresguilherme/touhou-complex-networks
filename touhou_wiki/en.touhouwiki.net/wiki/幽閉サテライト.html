Yuuhei Satellite (幽閉サテライト) is a vocal and instrumental arrangement circle who became famous for providing the music for the popular Touhou anime short "Fantasy Kaleidoscope - The Spring Snow Incident" produced by Manpuku Jinja. They were invited to be Guests of Honor for TouhouCon 2015. In 2014 a sister circle to Yuuhei Satellite called 少女フラクタル was founded.
