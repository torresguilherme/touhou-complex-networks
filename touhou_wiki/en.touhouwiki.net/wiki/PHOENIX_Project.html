Members
ゆう (Yuu) — arrangement. Also member of the side circle (Dark PHOENiX).


Discography
VAIN CRY
Release date: 2005-08-14 (Comiket 68)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Phantasmagoria of Flower View, as well as many other non-Touhou games.

Up to Death
Release date: 2005-12-30 (Comiket 69)
A collaboration with SEPIA-SIS. It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

Reprocessing II
Release date: 2006-08-13 (Comiket 70)
A collaboration with SEPIA-SIS. It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night, as well as several other non-Touhou songs.

Scarlet Inferno
Release date: 2009-06-21 (Touhou Suukeisei)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night and Subterranean Animism.

Pre-Innocence
Release date: 2010-07-18 (Aka no Hiroba 4)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.
It is a compilation of tracks by Yuu released in other albums as a guest arranger, with a few bonus tracks.

Pre-Innocence II
Release date: 2011-01-30 (Aka no Hiroba 5)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Subterranean Animism.
It is a compilation of tracks by Yuu released in other albums as a guest arranger, with a few bonus tracks.

Deviation
Release date: 2011-02-27
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

ReiMei -黎明-
Release date: 2011-8-13 (Comiket 80)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise and Ghostly Field Club.

Unknown Climax
Release date: 2011-09-19 (Aka no Hiroba 6)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power and Magical Astronomy.

Scarlet Isolation
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ghostly Field Club.

Scarlet Innocence
Release date: 2012-05-29 (Reitaisai 9)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Ten Desires.

ReiMei -黎明-2
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Ten Desires.

ReiMei -黎明- 3
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

F:Scarlet(Innocence)
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.
