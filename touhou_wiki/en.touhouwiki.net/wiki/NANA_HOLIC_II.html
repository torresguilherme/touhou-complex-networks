NANA HOLIC II is a doujin album by SOUND HOLIC released on 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15). It features vocal arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Ghostly Field Club, Immaterial and Missing Power, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Strange and Bright Nature Deity CD 1, and Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream. It is a compilation of tracks with vocals by Nana Takahashi, most of which are from previous SOUND HOLIC albums.
