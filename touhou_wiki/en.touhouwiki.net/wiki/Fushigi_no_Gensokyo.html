Mystery Gensokyo (不思議の幻想郷, Fushigi no Gensokyo) is a roguelike game created by AQUA STYLE. Precisely, most of its game features are derived from the Mystery Dungeon (不思議のダンジョン, Fushigi no Danjyon) series, such as the effects of items/equipment, and enemy abilities.
