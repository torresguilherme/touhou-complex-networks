Midboss Spell Card[edit]

Spell Card 119
猫符「キャッツウォーク」 (Nekofu "Kyattsu Wooku")
Cat Sign "Cat's Walk"
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Stage 5 — Easy
Editor's Comment
The mystery cat paces around, leaving behind small, expanding rings.
Follow its movements to shoot it down in time.
Spell Card 120
猫符「キャッツウォーク」 (Nekofu "Kyattsu Wooku")
Cat Sign "Cat's Walk"
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Stage 5 — Normal
Editor's Comment
The mystery cat paces around, leaving behind expanding rings.
Be careful, as the rings overlap as they expand.
Dodge the rings in the order they're created.

Boss Spell Card #3[edit]

Spell Card 131
贖罪「旧地獄の針山」 (Shokuzai "Kyuujigoku no Hariyama")
Atonement "Needle Mountain of a Former Hell" [2]
Owner
Rin Kaenbyou
Stage 5 — Easy
Editor's Comment
A mountain of needles fall in your general direction.
They are slow, though. Perhaps Orin lacked practice with it in a while.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 1.0 1.1 The 乱歩 (Ranpo) in this spell card could be a reference to a Japanese fiction writer Edogawa Ranpo whose name sounds very similar to Edgar Allan Poe the writer of The Black Cat.
  2. ↑ Mountain of needles: one of the agonies of the Buddhist hells.
