Editor's Comment
A demonic rain that burns everything it touches.
Behind her sweet exterior hides a sinister creature.

Boss Spell Card #1[edit]

Spell Card 3
罠符「キャプチャーウェブ」 (Wanafu "Kyapuchaa Webu")
Trap Sign "Capture Web"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Spiders take great care of their webs. If not constantly maintained, they would find it difficult to catch their food.
This web looks rather simple though. Fitting for an equally simple youkai.
Spell Card 4
罠符「キャプチャーウェブ」 (Wanafu "Kyapuchaa Webu")
Trap Sign "Capture Web"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Where does a spider youkai get its silk? Do they buy it from someone back in the village?
This silk may catch small animals, but it doesn't trouble any real opponent.
Spell Card 5
蜘蛛「石窟の蜘蛛の巣」 (Kumo "Sekkutsu no Kumo no Su")
Spider "Cave Spider's Nest"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Hard
Editor's Comment
The spider's nest is a much more dangerous place.
Quick and meaningful thinking will do you good.
Spell Card 6
蜘蛛「石窟の蜘蛛の巣」 (Kumo "Sekkutsu no Kumo no Su")
Spider "Cave Spider's Nest"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
The cave spider's nest. Not to be confused with those that come from the mountains.
Keep sharp in the face of danger, the spider has you right where it wants you.

Boss Spell Card #2[edit]

Spell Card 7
瘴符「フィルドミアズマ」 (Shoufu "Firudo Miazuma")
Miasma Sign "Filled Miasma"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Easy
Editor's Comment
The air has become heavy with disease.
Spiders don't have power over such things usually, but this spider is different.
Spell Card 8
瘴符「フィルドミアズマ」 (Shoufu "Firudo Miazuma")
Miasma Sign "Filled Miasma"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Toxic haze that strikes fast and without warning.
A youkai with power over sickness could also be a powerful healing tool. She seems a little too carefree for a doctor, though.
Spell Card 9
瘴気「原因不明の熱病」 (Shouki "Gen'in Fumei no Netsubyou")
Miasma "Unexplained Fever"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Deep caves often contain lethal gases, much to the dismay of many birds.
Fevers were once a sign of an encroaching death. Now they are only minor inconveniences.
Spell Card 10
瘴気「原因不明の熱病」 (Shouki "Gen'in Fumei no Netsubyou")
Miasma "Unexplained Fever"
Owner
Yamame Kurodani
Stage 1 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
The spider youkai's finest poison. A bite could be fatal if not treated quickly.
Can you spot the guest appearance?
