Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Reference to the Nine Syllable Cuts, the practice of making nine cuts--five horizontal and four vertical, alternating--in the air with the finger. The Nine Syllables are Rin (臨), Pyō (兵), Tō (闘), Sha (者), Kai (皆), Jin (陣), Retsu (列), Zai (在), Zen (前).
  2. ↑ Miracle fruit: a west African plant that produces berries that, when chewed before a meal, cause bitter and sour foods to taste sweet.
  3. ↑ Id: the unconscious, inaccessible, mostly base and instinctual part of the psyche in Freud's structural model.
  4. ↑ Super-ego: the organized, mostly (but not entirely) subconscious part of the psyche that includes ideals and spiritual goals in Freud's structural model.
  5. ↑ Polygraph: a device that monitors several physiological reactions in a subject, such as heart rate, motion, and perspiration. Colloquially known as the "lie detector", though extensive research has suggested that it is not reliable and easily fooled.
  6. ↑ Rorschach ink blot test: Developed by Hermann Rorschach, a controversial psychological evaluation based on subjects' interpretations of ink blots on paper.
