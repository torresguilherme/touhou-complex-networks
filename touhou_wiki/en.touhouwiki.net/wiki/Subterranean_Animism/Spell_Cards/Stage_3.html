Midboss Spell Card[edit]

Spell Card 27
鬼符「怪力乱神」 (Kifu "Kairyokuranshin")
Oni Sign "Anomalies, Strength, Disorder, and Spirits" [1]
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Four spirals of bullets that dissolve into curtain fire.
Do not lose yourself in confusion. Concentrate on your target always.
Spell Card 28
鬼符「怪力乱神」 (Kifu "Kairyokuranshin")
Oni Sign "Anomalies, Strength, Disorder, and Spirits"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Eight spirals of bullets that dissolve into curtain fire.
The bullets will not spawn on you. Yuugi only wishes to test your strength.
Spell Card 29
鬼符「怪力乱神」 (Kifu "Kairyokuranshin")
Oni Sign "Anomalies, Strength, Disorder, and Spirits"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Ten spirals of bullets that dissolve into curtain fire.
Concentrate on yourself. Only a few of the bullets are threatening, watch for them.
Spell Card 30
鬼符「怪力乱神」 (Kifu "Kairyokuranshin")
Oni Sign "Anomalies, Strength, Disorder, and Spirits"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Twelve spirals of bullets that dissolve into curtain fire.
This attack is born of chaos and power. Oni have reign over pandemonium.

Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma

Editor's Comment
Breaking a ring causes a bullet to linger.
It's probably easier to dodge than the original ring though.

怪輪「地獄の苦輪」 (Kairin "Jigoku no Kurin")
Mysterious Ring "Hell's Wheel of Pain"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma

Editor's Comment
Breaking a ring causes a bullet to linger.
The bullet will eventually seek you. Hell is inescapable once inside.

枷符「咎人の外さぬ枷」 (Kafu "Togabito no Hazusenu Kase")

Yuugi Hoshiguma

Editor's Comment
Arcane manacles that explode when destroyed.
It's easy to destroy them by accident. Well, Hell isn't a forgiving place after all.
Spell Card 34
枷符「咎人の外さぬ枷」 (Kafu "Togabito no Hazusenu Kase")
Shackles Sign "Shackles a Criminal Can't Take Off"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Arcane manacles that explode when destroyed.
These aren't safe for humans. Only an oni would expect you to work with these.

Boss Spell Card #2[edit]

Spell Card 35
力業「大江山嵐」 (Chikarawaza "Ooeyama Arashi")
Feat of Strength "Storm on Mt. Ooe" [2]
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Stones fly through the air!
The winds gust most at the top, but that is not where you are dodging. Do not fear.
Spell Card 36
力業「大江山嵐」 (Chikarawaza "Ooeyama Arashi")
Feat of Strength "Storm on Mt. Ooe"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Boulders fly through the air!
Yuugi moves neither left nor right. Perhaps you can weather this storm by holding your place as well?
Spell Card 37
力業「大江山颪」 (Chikarawaza "Ooeyama Oroshi")
Feat of Strength "Wind Blowing Down from Mt. Ooe"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Boulders threaten to crush you from both sides!
Yuugi does not want to harm you, though. Else the hitboxes would be much larger.
Spell Card 38
力業「大江山颪」 (Chikarawaza "Ooeyama Oroshi")
Feat of Strength "Wind Blowing Down from Mt. Ooe"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Avalanches threaten to crush you from both sides!
She makes causing these storms look so effortless, though. She summons the mighty hail while holding her sake in one hand!

Boss Spell Card #3[edit]

Spell Card 39
四天王奥義「三歩必殺」 (Shitennou Ougi "Sanpo Hissatsu")
Big Four Arcanum "Knock Out In Three Steps"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Easy
Editor's Comment
Two bending layers inside an absurdly powerful trap.
Though the trap can be easily avoided from inside. Not a good arcanum if it's so escapable.
Spell Card 40
四天王奥義「三歩必殺」 (Shitennou Ougi "Sanpo Hissatsu")
Big Four Arcanum "Knock Out In Three Steps"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Normal
Editor's Comment
Two bending layers inside an absurdly powerful trap.
The bullets inside spread out to catch escapees. Yuugi is not holding back as much as thought.
Spell Card 41
四天王奥義「三歩必殺」 (Shitennou Ougi "Sanpo Hissatsu")
Big Four Arcanum "Knock Out In Three Steps"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Two scattered layers inside an absurdly powerful trap.
The inner bullets form dense walls now, and must be read quickly. This is a knock out befitting of the onis.
Spell Card 42
四天王奥義「三歩必殺」 (Shitennou Ougi "Sanpo Hissatsu")
Big Four Arcanum "Knock Out In Three Steps"
Owner
Yuugi Hoshiguma
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Two scattered layers inside an absurdly powerful trap.
Yuugi will mercilessly spawn the bullets on unwary victims. This technique is strong as Yuugi, but... perhaps a bit cheap.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A phrase from the Analects of Confucius that has been listed in the dictionary as "supernatural phenomenon".
  2. ↑ Ōe Mountain (大江山): A mountain in Kyoto. It features prominently in folklore about oni, most notably in the well-known story of Shuten Douji (酒呑童子).
