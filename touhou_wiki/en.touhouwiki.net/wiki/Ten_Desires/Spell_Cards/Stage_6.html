Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Twelve Level Cap and Rank System: A ranking system introduced in 603 during the Asuka period. Officials wore caps with a feather indicating his or her rank. The ranks correspond to the greater and lesser of each of the six Confucian virtues.
  2. ↑ The first words of the bold letter that Prince Shoutoku sent to the Chinese emperor
  3. ↑ Ikaruga-dera, a.k.a. Houryuu-ji: a Buddhist temple commissioned by Prince Shoutoku, who Miko is based off of.
  4. ↑ Out-of-place artifact: An artifact found in an unusual or impossible context. There is an OOParts called "Prince Shotoku's terrestrial globe" in Ikaruga-dera. In fact, it was made in the Edo period.
  5. ↑ Guse Kannon: Another name for Guanyin, the merciful saint in Buddhism. According to legend, Guse Kannon in Ikaruga-dera is the representation of Prince Shotoku.
  6. ↑ Refers to the Seventeen-article constitution, a document authored by Prince Shotoku.
  7. ↑ A rewording of a part of the first article of the constitution.
