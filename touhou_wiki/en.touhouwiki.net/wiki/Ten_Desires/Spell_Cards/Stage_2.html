Boss Spell Card #3[edit]

Spell Card 27
大声「チャージドクライ」 (Oogoe "Chaajido Kurai")
Great Voice "Charged Cry"
Owner
Kyouko Kasodani
Stage 2 — Easy
Editor's Comment
A resonating cry inside an enclosing sphere, slowly spilling out.
The cry seems to be more of a small wail, though.
Spell Card 28
大声「チャージドクライ」 (Oogoe "Chaajido Kurai")
Great Voice "Charged Cry"
Owner
Kyouko Kasodani
Stage 2 — Normal
Editor's Comment
A resonating cry inside an enclosing sphere, slowly spilling out.
The sphere is actually aimed at you, so try to manipulate its direction to your own good.
Spell Card 29
大声「チャージドヤッホー」 (Oogoe "Chaajido Yahhoo")
Great Voice "Charged Yahoo!"
Owner
Kyouko Kasodani
Stage 2 — Hard
Editor's Comment
A resonating "yahoo!" inside an enclosing sphere, rapidly spilling out.
"Yahoo!"? Is Kyouko happy of your frustration? Maybe not.
Spell Card 30
大声「チャージドヤッホー」 (Oogoe "Chaajido Yahhoo")
Great Voice "Charged Yahoo!"
Owner
Kyouko Kasodani
Stage 2 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
A resonating "yahoo!" inside an enclosing sphere, rapidly spilling out.
Kyouko's voice must really be great enough to perform such cruel Spell Card.
