Spell Card 120
桜符「桜吹雪地獄」 (Oufu "Sakura-fubuki Jigoku")
Cherry Blossom Sign "Cherry Blossom Blizzard Hell"
Owner
Yuyuko Saigyouji
Stage 1 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Cherry Blossom Sign "Saigyou Cherry Blossom Blizzard" (spellcard 13 and 14) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 121
山彦「ヤマビコの本領発揮エコー」 (Yamabiko "Yamabiko no Honryou Hakki Ekoo")
Mountain Echo "Yamabiko's Specialty Echo Demonstration"
Owner
Kyouko Kasodani
Stage 2 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment
Bullets fly outward and echo back into the battlefield from all directions. Is there even a place in Gensokyo where echoes can be heard from all sides?

To unlock: Capture Mountain Echo "Long-Range Echo" (Spellcard 23 and 24) and Mountain Echo "Amplify Echo" (spellcard 25 and 26) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 122
毒爪「死なない殺人鬼」 (Dokuzume "Shinanai Satsujinki")
Poison Nail "Undead Murderer"
Owner
Yoshika Miyako
Stage 3 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Poison Nail "Poison Raze" (spellcard 37 and 38) and Poison Nail "Poison Murder" (spellcard 39 and 40) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 123
道符「ＴＡＯ胎動　～道～」 (Doufu "Tao Taidou ~Dou~")
Path Sign "TAO Fetal Movement ~Dao~" [1]
Owner
Seiga Kaku
Stage 4 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Path Sign "Dao Fetal Movement" (spellcard 56, 57, 58 and 59) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 124
怨霊「入鹿の雷」 (Onryou "Iruka no Kaminari")
Vengeful Spirit "Iruka's Thunder" [2]
Owner
Soga no Tojiko
Stage 5 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Thunder Arrow "Gagouji's Cyclone" (spellcard 60 and 61) and "Gagouji's Tornado" (spellcard 62) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 125
聖童女「太陽神の贄」 (Seidoujo "Taiyoushin no Nie")
Saint Girl "Sun Goddess's Sacrifice"
Owner
Mononobe no Futo
Stage 5 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Saint Girl "Oomonoimi's Dinner" (spellcard 75, 76, 77 and 78) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 126
「神霊大宇宙」 ("Shinrei Daiuchuu")
"Divine Spirits' Macrocosm"
Owner
Toyosatomimi no Miko
Stage 6 — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Secret Treasure "Armillary Sphere of Ikaruga-dera" (spellcard 91, 92 and 93) and Secret Treasure "Prince Shotoku's Out-of-place Artifact" (spellcard 94) on each difficulty.
Spell Card 127
「ワイルドカーペット」 ("Wairudo Kaapetto")
"Wild Carpet"
Owner
Mamizou Futatsuiwa
Extra — Overdrive
Editor's Comment

To unlock: Capture Seventh Duel "Wild Deserted Island" (spellcard 116) from Extra Stage.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ May refer to TAO ~道~, a video game made for the Famicom.
  2. ↑ Refers to Soga no Iruka, a member of the Soga clan which Tojiko is in. After he was assassinated, thunder struck the household of his assassin, who died soon after.
