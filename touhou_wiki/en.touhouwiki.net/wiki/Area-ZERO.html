領域ZERO official website

Description

Area-ZERO is an game making and music arrangement circle, Touhou arrangements for the circle's albums and game are provided by a number of notable Touhou arrangement circles such as Rokugen Alice, BUTAOTOME, 5150, Crest, GET IN THE RING and A-One.

Game Productions

  • Touhou Sky Fight (2010)
  • Touhou Sky Arena (東方スカイアリーナ) (2011)
  • Touhou Sky Arena -KURENAI- (東方スカイアリーナ -KURENAI-) (2012)
