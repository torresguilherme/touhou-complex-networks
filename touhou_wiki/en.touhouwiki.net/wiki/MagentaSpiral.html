Discography
Magenta Spiral E.P. Vol.01
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Undefined Fantastic Object.

Magenta Spiral E.P. Vol.02
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith and Ten Desires.

滸にて
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires. It features the 4 tracks previously released on singles the same year in addition to 7 new tracks.

MagentaSpiral E.P. vol.03
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.

Magenta Spiral E.P.04
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith.

Re-birth
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires, Hopeless Masquerade and Double Dealing Character. It features 4 of the 5 tracks previously released on singles the same year in addition to 5 new songs.

Magenta Spiral E.P.05
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ten Desires and Double Dealing Character.

Magenta Spiral E.P.06
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story and Scarlet Weather Rhapsody.

born in blood
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Ten Desires and Double Dealing Character.
