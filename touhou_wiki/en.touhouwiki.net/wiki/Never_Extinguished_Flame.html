Touhou Houkaizou ~ Never Extinguished Flames (東方訪界造　～ Never Extinguished Flame., lit. "Eastern Visiting of the Kingdom Structure") is an fan-made Touhou game for the Danmakufu 0.12m engine, produced by Nekettsukan.
