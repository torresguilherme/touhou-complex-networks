Touhou Gishouten (False Laughter Point) ~ Vaudeville of Falling Disciple (東方偽笑点 ～ Vaudeville of Falling Disciple) is a fan-made Touhou Project game made in DxLib mimicking Ten Desires. It consists of Byakuren Hijiri and Mononobe no Futo as the playable characters and one original character (based heavily on the rakugo TV program Shōten) as the sole boss of the game.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

The game consists of five difficulties: Easy, Normal, Hard, Lunatic, and an unlockable difficulty known as "Brutal".
