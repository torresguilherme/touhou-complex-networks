Crescent enemies are stage enemies in Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, only seen on Chiyuri Kitashirakawa's and Yumemi Okazaki's stages. Nothing is really known about this enemy.

Background Information[edit]

The name Cresent enemies is unofficial. It references the appearance of this enemy, which is a red crescent-shaped creature with two beady eyes. More can be seen in other colours like green, blue and purple. They also can be seen in different sizes. They bare a resemblance to the 3-Up Moon that appears in the Mario Series.

Enemy's Appearance[edit]

Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream

They're a general enemy during Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream on Chiyuri's and Yumemi's stage. They do not shoot danmaku whatsoever, but if defeated, they'll put a small bullet on the opponent's screen. They come in four different colours, each requiring a number of different hits: red (1 hit), green (2 hits), blue (3 hits) and purple (4 hits). There are also 4 different sizes: tiny, small, medium and large.
