About[edit]

I'll be keeping a page open for when I get various replays made and uploaded. This will be mainly used as a way of keeping my replays somewhere 'safe' online if anything happens to the original data. I see myself as sort of a perfectionist, I usually re-do the whole game if I die once on normal and try to not use bombs at all, which is silly since I'm not on the level where I can consistently beat hard or lunatic. I think this is what stops me from progressing, or is it? I think I'm just trying to discipline myself in to a way of thinking which states 'Do it right or not at all', which I have felt in the past to be something that would help me get better. Who knows, it might pay off some day. I'm also going to take a rather fundamentalist / back to basics approach to these replay uploads: I'll be starting from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil easy mode and improving my scores on that until I feel I've done enough then I'll move on.
