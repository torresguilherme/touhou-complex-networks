Gensou Skydrift (スカイドリフト, Gensou sukaidorifuto) is a 3D Racing Game, developed and published by illuCalab. The trial version was originally released for PC on December 29, 2014, at Comiket 87, with the full version released at Comiket 88 on August 14, 2015. It is similar in gameplay to the Mario Kart series, specifically Mario Kart: Double Dash, and the way they race is like Sonic Riders.

According to Variety, several former Nintendo employees who worked on Mario Kart: Double Dash!! were involved, and the game is expected to be released on consoles this summer.[1]
