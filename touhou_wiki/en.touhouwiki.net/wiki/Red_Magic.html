Red Magic is a puzzle flash game created by Chibitami featuring Remilia Scarlet.

Gameplay[edit]

Main article: Gameplay

The game is played by clicking a square from the panel of squares given. When you click a square, it turns either pink, or red, depending on what color it was before. Once a certain amount of red squares are lined up vertically, or horizontally, the player can click the squares to make them disappear, or wait for more to gather for more points. After a set amount of time, a new layer of blocks will fall. If they pile up over the playing area, the game is over, and the player loses.
