Other Works

  • The regret of stars, but stars shine bright. released 2003-12-30 (Comiket 65) is an album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Alice Soft and Key visual novels. It is notably the circle's first album and is one of their few releases to feature arrangements of themes outside of Touhou Project.
  • りにあのうた2 released 2006-12-31 (Comiket 71) is an album featuring original vocal compositions.
  • Fly High released 2007-12-31 (Comiket 73) is a single featuring an original vocaloid composition.
  • Our Little Miracles released 2008-08-23 (VOC@LOiD M@STER 3) is an album featuring original vocaloid compositions.
  • 阿蘇山 released 2011-10-30 (M3-28) is an collaboration album with ALiCE'S EMOTiON featuring original compositions, it is the third in a series of collaborations and is precieded by SAISEN TURN and SAISEN RETURN.
  • LOUD SPEAKER released 2017-12-29 (Comiket 91) is a solo album by ayame featuring original vocal compositions.
  • CLOCK LOCK THINK released 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93) is a solo album by ayame featuring original vocal compositions.
  • BLACKLOTUS released 2017-12-29 (Comiket 94) is a solo album by ayame featuring original vocal compositions.
  • FÄRGRIK - OWL. released 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95) is a solo album by ayame featuring original vocal compositions.
