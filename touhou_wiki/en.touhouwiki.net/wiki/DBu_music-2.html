dBu (Aliases: dBu, Dobu Usagi, どぶウサギ) — Sole member of dBu music, he arranges all the music in his albums. Dobu Usagi means Ditch rabbit (not to be confused with debu, which is fat).


Discography
紅魔狂詩曲 Scarlet Rapsodia
Release date: 2005-05-04 (Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.
It is the first of the four albums in the 弾奏結界 Symphony from The Sanctuary (Performance Barrier Symphony from The Sanctuary) series.

幻葬旋律曲 Necromanza
Release date: 2005-05-04 (Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom.
It is the second of the four albums in the 弾奏結界 Symphony from The Sanctuary (Performance Barrier Symphony from The Sanctuary) series.

夢幻夜想曲 Eternal Nocturne
Release date: 2005-05-04 (Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.
It is the third of the four albums in the 弾奏結界 Symphony from The Sanctuary (Performance Barrier Symphony from The Sanctuary) series.

追憶鎮魂曲 Nostalgic Requiem
Release date: 2005-05-04 (Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square and Shuusou Gyoku.
It is the last the four albums in the 弾奏結界 Symphony from The Sanctuary (Performance Barrier Symphony from The Sanctuary) series.

散華嬉遊曲 Flower Divertimento
Release date: 2005-12-30 (Comiket 69)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, as well as one original composition.

文花風師曲 Arietta Incalzando
Release date: 2006-05-21 (Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet, as well as one original composition.

ICE MiLK TEi
Release date: 2006-07-02 (Ugetsusai 2006)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet, as well as an arrangement from MegaMari.
It is a collaboration album in which a lot of different members from other circles took part in arranging music.

阿礼の子供は夜の鳩山を飛ぶ日本の伝説（仮）
Release date: 2006-12-31 (Comiket 71)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Memento in Strict Sense.

2面に進めませんっ!!
Release date: 2006-12-31 (Comiket 71)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Shuusou Gyoku, as well as arranges of songs from a lot of other series.

兎角宴舞曲 Courante Impromptu
Release date: 2007-05-20 (Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Changeability of Strange Dream and Magical Astronomy.

神交風雅曲 Oratario del Vento
Release date: 2007-12-31 (Comiket 73)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

鬼譚奏鳴曲 Demon Tale Sonata
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism.

天空航進曲 March over sky
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object, it focuses on boss music.

白夜小夜曲 Serenade at midnight sun
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object, it focuses on stage music.

RoYAL MiLK TEi
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.
It is a collaboration album in which a lot of different members from other circles took part in arranging music.

妖精戦奏曲 Scherzo of Fairy Wars
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Fairy Wars.

蘇弾奏結界 尸解変奏曲 Undead Variation
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires.

大弾奏結界 総集篇纂組曲 ～The Suite～
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
A 14-disc instrumental compilation album of all dBu Music's previous albums, as well as various guest tracks on other albums and games.

逆弾奏結界 反逆諧謔曲 Reversal Scherzo
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character.

ふたつの子午線 Orthogonal parallel line
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

歳月
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power and Imperishable Night.

三面楚歌 Only one way to go
Release date: 2015-11-01 (Touhou Kouroumu 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

純弾奏結界 凶夢追奏曲 Nightmare Canon
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

叛弾奏結界 正邪受難曲 Passion of Mutiny
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character and Impossible Spell Card.

チョコレートパフェの最終定理
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Dr. Latency's Freak Report and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".

後弾奏結界 秘匿対舞曲 Backdoor Contredanse
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

記弾奏結界 悪夢幻想曲 Albtraum Traumerei
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Violet Detector.
