Touhou Poketto Ikusasou 2nd (東方ポケット戦争2nd, lit. "Touhou Pocket Wars 2nd"), also Dōngfāng kǒudài zhànzhēng 2nd (東方口袋戰爭2nd) in Taiwanese, is a fan-made Touhou Project strategic RPG based around Gacha Figurines. Released as a sequel to Touhou Pocket Wars EVO and EVO+, the player plays themselves as a Touhou-character themed Gacha collector, entering tournaments and leveling up their figurines and accompanying spell cards to take on stronger and stronger opponents.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Characters

The gameplay of Pocket Wars focuses around collecting Gacha Figurines of various Touhou heroines, leveling them and their spell cards up, then sending teams into a strategic turn-based battle in which positioning greatly affects the effectiveness of abilities.

Story[edit]

Main article: Story

Touhou Pocket Wars 2nd's story is a direct sequel to the story of Touhou Pocket Wars. The player keeps collecting gachas, and his ties to Gensokyo have become so strong that he only ever goes back to Earth to sleep in his room. This time, however, a new character with apparently evil intention approaches. Remilia also seems to be organizing a world-wide gacha tournament?...

Music[edit]

Music is composed by Kiyoma from Golden City Factory. Every character has its own theme which could play during a battle.
