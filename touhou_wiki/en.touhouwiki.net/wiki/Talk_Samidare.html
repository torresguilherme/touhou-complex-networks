Seihou only in spirit[edit]

What does this mean? I'm going to take it out in a few months if someone can't expand it to be a bit more useful. --T. Solamarle 13:54, 10 August 2008 (UTC)

I'm guessing it means it's not actually a Seihou game, but the fact that it's by the same creators and has VIVIT as a boss makes it "Seihou in spirit". Suikama 15:06, 10 August 2008 (UTC)
Nevermind - it's in the freaking FAQ for the game, I was just being an ignorant buffoon:
【 五月雨は某西方Projectのゲームなんですか？ 】 "Is Samidare a Seihou Project game?"
・いえ、違います。五月雨は Project Blankのゲームです。 No, it's not. Samidare is a Project Blank game.
・五月雨は、RebRankメンバーが大学時代に所属していたサークルAmusement Makersで製作されました。 Samidare was first created by RebRank members in their college days when they were part of Amusement Makers.
・またAmusement Makersはサークル名称なので、あくまで集まりの総称です。

　個人だったりグループだったりと様々ですが、内部では何個もゲームが製作されています。 　そのため、同じ Amusement Makers であっても、開発プロジェクトが違い、製作メンバーも別人です。

A lot of the members used the circle name Amusement Makers even for their own games, so quite a few games have been attributed to Amusement Makers. So, while it's an Amusement Makers game, the developing project is different, and the production staff are all different, too.
　余談ですがAmusement Makersは大学サークルであるため、年数が進めば作り手も変わり、作品も変化します。 By the way, since Amusement Makers is a college group, its membership changes over time, as do its productions.

--T. Solamarle 00:54, 11 August 2008 (UTC)
