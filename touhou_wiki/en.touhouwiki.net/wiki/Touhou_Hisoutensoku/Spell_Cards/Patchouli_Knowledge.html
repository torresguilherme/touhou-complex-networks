Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
ワイプモイスチャー (Waipu Moisuchaa)
Wipe Moisture
Cost
1 Orb
Comment
火属性魔法の物理発展形。炎を一繋がりの帯状に発生させ、鞭ようになぎ払う打撃技。重さも無く非力な彼女の振りでも熱としなりで大ダメージになる。
Translation
A physical manifestation of fire magic. Lashes fire at the enemy like a whip. Even though she has no strength, and the fire has no mass, it is still incredibly strong.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
スタティックグリーン (Sutatikku Guriin)
Static Green
Cost
1 Orb
Comment
木属性魔法の対空特化系。手元に球電を発生させ飛び上がり、上空で上下に炸裂させ攻撃する。木属性をベースとした電気操作は安定面で改良の余地が多いらしい。
Translation
Anti-air wood magic. Balls of lightning appear from her hand, shoot upward, and explode. Because the basis of this attack is wood magic, it seems that it could be more stable.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
フォールスラッシャー (Fooru Surasshaa)
Fall Slasher
Cost
1 Orb
Comment
金属性魔法の変則攻撃形。金属性元素から剣を練成し、保持。それを敵へ向けて飛ばす射撃。ホールドすると剣を装着したまま本体が行動不可能になる変り種。
Translation
A shapeshifting metal attack. Creates a sword from metal and throws it at the enemy. She can also wield the sword while moving by holding down the button.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
ダイアモンドハードネス (Daiamondo Haadonesu)
Diamond Hardness
Cost
1 Orb
Comment
土属性魔法の耐性強化形。一時的に体を堅固な状態にする。硬化中は一度だけ敵の攻撃を耐える事が可能で、攻めや守りで少しの無茶が可能になる。
Translation
A resistance technique derived from earth magic. The body becomes hard for a while, and can deflect one attack. Defense and attack can get a bit crazy after this move, since she can take a lot of damage.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
スティッキーバブル (Sutikkii Baburu)
Sticky Bubbles
Cost
1 Orb
Comment
水属性魔法の拘束特化形。泡のスプレーを噴射し、敵を宙に浮かせて捕まえる非破壊攻撃泡はガード不可だが敵が怯まず大量に当てないと効果は無い。
Translation
A restraint device created from water magic. Sprays non-damaging bubbles which trap the enemy in mid-air. The bubbles cannot be blocked, but won't have much of an effect unless they all hit.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
火水符「フロギスティックピラー」 (Kasuifu "Furogisutikku Piraa")
Fire Water Sign "Phlogistic Pillar"
Cost
3 Cards
Comment
上下に向けて火種を放ち、床と天井を広がる炎で追い込むカード。火種が着火するまで威力はないが、地上空中で使用でき、範囲が広い。
Translation
Releases fuses up and down to the ceiling and floor. They have no power until lit with a fire attack, but can be used on ground or in mid-air, and the coverage is quite large.


Spell Card 2
土金符「エメラルドメガロポリス」 (Dokinfu "Emerarudo Megaporisu")
Earth Metal Sign "Emerald Megalopolis"
Cost
4 Cards
Comment
地中から宝石の柱群が出現し、敵を次々打ち上げるスペルカード。展開は緩やかな速度だが、遅さを利用して長時間の行動制限が可能。
Translation
Jewelled pillars rise from the ground and impale the opponent in succession. The speed of this card is quite slow, but the player can exploit this to keep the enemy suppressed for a long time.


Spell Card 3
日月符「ロイヤルダイアモンドリング」 (Nichigeppu "Roiyaru Daiamondo Ringu")
Sun Moon Sign "Royal Diamond Ring" [1]
Cost
5 Cards
Comment
光の渦を発生させて辺りを攻撃。個々は徽弱だが、照射時間とその連射数で場を圧倒する。
Translation
Whirlpools of light form and attack from the side. They are individually weak, but since they come in succession and have a long duration, it can overwhelm the opponent.
