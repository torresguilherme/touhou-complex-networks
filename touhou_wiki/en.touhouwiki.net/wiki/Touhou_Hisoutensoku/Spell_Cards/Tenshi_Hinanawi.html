Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
緋想の剣気 (Hisou no Kenki)
Scarlet Sword Temperament
Cost
1 orb
Comment
緋想の剣で気質を操り飛ばす射撃。一太刀目の立ち上りで近くを、二太刀目の打ち降ろしで遠距離を攻撃する二段射撃。
Translation
Using the Sword of Scarlet Thought, control the temperaments and attack twice: once with a ranged attack that brings her closer to the enemy, and another attack that propels her away from the enemy
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
昇天突 (Shoutentotsu)
Ascension Thrust
Cost
1 orb
Comment
気質を纏って上空へ体当たりすふ。天人の強靭な体ならではの攻撃。地面は出始めに無敵時間が存在。空中は技後に行動不可となる。
Translation
Launch upwards surrounded with temperament, fully utilizing the tough, hardened bodies of celestials. She is invincible from the start, and can use it in the air as well.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
因果の剣 (Inga no Tsurugi)
Sword of Karma
Cost
1 orb
Comment
緋想の剣を豪快に投げる射撃技で、剣はブーメランの如く手元に帰り、剣を使う技を出すと瞬時に戻る。普通に戻りをキャッチすると隙になる為、戻りの動きに注意。
Translation
Throw the Sword of Scarlet Thought with a hearty flick, and it returns like a boomerang or when she uses another sword attack. If she catches it normally, there will be a moment of weakness, so watch out.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
地精の起床 (Chisei no Kishou)
Awakening of the Earth Spirits
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地面に小さな要石を打ち込む技。石は時間で成長し、天子の打撃で割る事で、地震が発生すふ。石が育つほど地震も大きくなるが、成長しすぎると崩れてしまう。
Translation
Pelt the enemy with small keystones. The stones grow over time, and release an earthquake if Tenshi attacks it. The bigger the stones, the stronger the earthquake, but will shatter if they get too big.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
剣技「気炎万丈の剣」[1] (Kengi "Kienbanjou no Ken")
Sword Skill "Sword of Ebullience"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
緋想の剣を振り回しつつ攻撃し、相手をバラバラに引き裂く打撃技。滅多切りしているだけなので、技と呼んで良い物かは怪しげ。
Translation
Swing the Sword of Scarlet Thought around blindly, cutting the enemy into pieces. There's a lot of slicing and dicing in this attack, but not much else, so it's a bit weird to call it a "skill."


Spell Card 2
天気「緋想天促」 (Tenki "Hisoutensoku")
Weather "Scarlet Weather Stimulation" [2]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
気質を飛ばして攻撃する射撃技。気質は相手に当ると拡散し、周囲の気候を活性化させる天気玉に変化。
Translation
Shoot temperaments at the enemy in this ranged attack. If it hits the enemy, the temperament expands and changes the current weather.

Notes[edit]

^ "緋想天促" is pronounced Hisoutensoku.
^  This card is highly likely to be parodying the ブロントさん (Buront-san) meme - A Final Fantasy XI derived meme commonly known in the Japanese Internet community. The description in the comment "A physical move that shreds the enemy into pieces" (相手をバラバラに引き裂く打撃技) is coming from a line in the meme "Want me to shred you into pieces by the Gluttony Swift?" (グラットンスウィフトでバラバラに引き裂いてやろうか？). The max combo that can be accomplished by this card is up to nine times, which is parodying one of a joke in the meme that whenever a conversation that relates to numbers happens the joker replies "fine with nine" (9 [counter word] でいい): This comes from an anecdote of when Buront-san received a dozen of an item called Lizard Skins, he returned back three of them saying "fine with nine" (9枚でいい). The card name itself could be a parody of the meme too - there's a job ability for the Scholar class in FFXI called the "Article of High Spirits" (気炎万丈の章, named "Ebullience" in the English version).
