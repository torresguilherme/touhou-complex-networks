Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
知能と脚の境界 (Chinou to Ashi no Kyoukai)
Boundary of Intellect and Feet
Cost
1 orb
Comment
置線状に境界を走らせ、そこに存在する物を分断する。威力重視技。全てに境界を引けると言う事は二つに分けられぬ物は無いと言う事である。
Translation
Runs a straight boundary, splitting anything in place. Power-oriented technique. Being able to draw any boundary means that there is nothing that can't be cut into two.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
変容を見る眼 (Hen'you wo Miru Me)
Eyes that View Change
Cost
1 orb
Comment
隙間からく目が相手を狙う。目は敵を監視し追撃を自動で行う技。援固定砲台の役を担う。特定の行動を行う点から彼女の一種の左だと思われる。
Translation
An eye from the gap targets the enemy. It becomes a stationary platform that offers supporting fire. It follows a designated path, so it can be regarded as one of Yukari's familiars.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
キマイラの翼 (Kimaira no Tsubasa)
Chimera's Wings
Cost
1 orb
Comment
隙間の通用門を仕込んでおき、必要に応じてそこへ瞬間移動する。仕込が必要な分移動が素早く逃げから連携まで自由自在。
Translation
Places a gap door, instantly moving to it when needed. Very fast speed, usable freely for escape and easy to carry around.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
魔眼「ラプラスの魔」 (Magan "Rapurasu no Ma")
Magic Eye "Laplace's Demon"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
辺り一面に相手を凝視する異形の目が大量に現れる。目は敵を監視隙を見せると攻撃を仕掛けてくる。紫はこの様な方法で幻想郷全体の動向を監視する事もあるらしい。
Translation
A large number of eyes appear staring at the enemy. Once the eyes see an exploitable weakness, they attack. It seems this is how Yukari knows what's going on in all of Gensokyo at all times.
