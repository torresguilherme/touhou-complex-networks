Owner
Sanae Kochiya

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
風起こし (Kaze-okoshi)
Wind Calling
Cost
1 orb
Comment
フィールドに神風を起こし、風の渦を作り出す射撃技。無害だが、飛翔の風を受けると攻撃力を有して流される。
Translation
Calls down a divine wind to the field, and creates a tornado. Not really harmful, but becomes dangerous when it comes into contact with other winds.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
波起こし (Nami-okoshi)
Wave Calling
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地上を左右に水流が走る弾幕技。水は離れるほど弱まるが、限界射程まで確実に水は噴出し、敵の弾幕をかき消して行く。
Translation
A ranged skill that creates water waves. The water is weaker the farther away it is from Sanae. The wave extends out as far as it can, canceling enemy bullets along the way.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
星落とし (Hoshi-otoshi)
Star Falling
Cost
1 orb
Comment
早苗の頭上に輝く星が出現、印を組むと同時に下方へ落下する。星は大雑把だが敵へ向かって落下、下方向へは広い補足範囲を誇る。
Translation
Stars appear above Sanae's head, and fall towards the enemy. The area covered by this attack is pretty high.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
乾神招来　突 (Kanshin Shourai Totsu)
Sky-God Summon: Thrust
Cost
1 orb
Comment
神奈子の神力を借りる技。力が神奈子の形に顕現し攻撃する。性質はスタンダードな突進技で、扱いやすい形になっている。
Translation
A skill that uses Kanako's power. Kanako manifests herself and charges towards the enemy in this standard attack. Pretty easy to use.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
乾神招来　風 (Kanshin Shourai Kaze)
Sky-God Summon: Wind
Cost
1 orb
Comment
神奈子の神力を借りる技。力が神奈子の形に顕現し突風をフィールドに発生させて攻撃。風の帯は飛翔の風を当てることで上下に動かすことが出来る。
Translation
A skill that uses Kanako's power. Kanako manifests herself and creates a wind gust on the field. If the wind hits other winds, it can move up and down.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
乾神招来　御柱 (Kanshin Shourai Onbashira)
Sky-God Summon: Onbashira
Cost
1 orb
Comment
神奈子の神力を借りる技。力が神奈子の形に顕現し御柱の雨が周辺へ降り注ぐ技。早苗自身は無敵で護られており、潰され難しいカウンター技。
Translation
A skill that uses Kanako's power. Kanako manifests herself and rains down onbashira on the enemy. Sanae's impervious during this attack. A very difficult counter to deal with.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
坤神招来　盾 (Kanshin Shourai Tate)
Earth-God Summon: Shield
Cost
1 orb
Comment
諏訪子の神力を借りる技。力が諏訪子の形に顕現して早苗を守る泡の盾を作り出す。盾は一定量のダメージを受けるか、時間経過するまでその場に固定。
Translation
A skill that uses Suwako's power. Suwako manifests herself and creates a bubble that protects Sanae. The bubble stays stationary until it has absorbed a set amount of damage or a set amount of time passes.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
坤神招来　鉄輪 (Kanshin Shourai Tetsurin)
Earth-God Summon: Iron Ring
Cost
1 orb
Comment
諏訪子の神力を借りる技。こちらは顕現した力が敵を直接攻撃し、相手を押さえ込む連携技。早苗は比較的早い段階から自由に動けるので同時攻撃が可能となる。
Translation
A skill that uses Suwako's power. Suwako manifests herself and attacks the enemy directly in a crushing combo move. She's faster than Sanae, so it is possible to move around freely and attack simultaneously.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
坤神招来　罠 (Kanshin Shourai Wana)
Earth-God Summon: Trap
Cost
1 orb
Comment
諏訪子の神力を借りる技。顕現した力が地中に潜み、敵が接近すると地中から攻撃する。地雷のような技で、グレイズ不可。相手の行動を大きく制限できる。
Translation
A skill that uses Suwako's power. She manifests herself and tunnels underground. If the enemy approaches her, she attacks them like a mine. This move can't be grazed, so it's good for limiting movement options.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
おみくじ爆弾 (Omikuji Bakudan)
Omikuji Bomb
Cost
1 orb
Comment
御神籤の札を放り投げる必殺技で、結果に応じた内容を攻撃する。早苗本人にも中は判らないので、どの効果が出るかは神のみぞ知る。
Translation
Sanae throws omikuji bombs at the opponent, which then deal damage depending on the fortune inside. Since Sanae herself doesn't know what's inside, only god knows what will happen.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 11
スカイサーペント (Sukai Saapento)
Sky Serpent
Cost
1 orb
Comment
投げた札が白蛇に転じて飛翔する。白蛇は直進軌道だが、敵を上下に感知すると直角に曲がって追う。
Translation
Throws out a charm that becomes a white snake which flies into the sky. However, once the enemy moves, it takes a 90-degree turn and chases the enemy.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 12
コバルトスプレッド (Kobaruto Supureddo)
Cobalt Spread
Cost
1 orb
Comment
投げた札が地面に潜り、敵の前で炸裂弾になって地中から飛び出す。地中を進む為敵弾の影響を受けず、射撃戦の緩急の役を担う。
Translation
Throws charms which then disappear underground and explode in front of the enemy. Since they're underground, they can't be stopped by the enemy, so they're slower as a result.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
祈願「商売繁盛守り」 (Kigan "Shoubai Hanjou Mamori")
Prayer "Charm of Good Commerce"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
大量の札で相手を押しつぶす技。数の割りに威力は控えめだが、隙も小さく、本体の周囲が手薄になり難い為、気軽に使える。
Translation
Throws a large number of charms at the enemy. Because there are a lot of them, they're not too powerful, but the attack is dense and covers Sanae well, so this skill can be used pretty freely.


Spell Card 2
秘術「グレイソーマタージ」 (Hijutsu "Gurei Soomataaji")
Esoterica "Gray Thaumaturgy"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
星の形に印を打ち、そこから弾ける光で敵を貫く弾幕攻撃。弾速が遅めで範囲が広いため対地対空問わず牽制となる。
Translation
Projects the shape of a star and attacks the enemy with light. Speed is slow but coverage is good, so the move is a good way to limit the opponent on ground or in the air.




Spell Card 3
御籤「乱れおみくじ連続引き」 (Omikuji "Midare Omikuji Renzokubiki")
Omikuji “Random Omikuji Barrage”
Cost
2 cards
Comment
着弾まで効果不明の御神籤を乱射。投げる場所も効果も謎の上。自分にも害をなす結果が出るが、良い目が噛み合うとコスト以上の結果が得られることも。
Translation
Shoots out a random bunch of omikuji. Since she has no idea what the results of this attack are until it's over, it could end up hurting her, or it could deal damage disproportionate to its cost.


Spell Card 4
開海「海が割れる日」 (Kaikai "Umi ga Wareru Hi")
Sea Opening "The Day the Sea Split"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
早苗から左右へ巨大な波が走る。進行速度が緩やかで離れた敵には波に追いついての同時攻撃も可能。
Translation
Large undulating waves appear. While they're slow, they can hit the enemy even if she is far away, and Sanae can also continue attacking.


Spell Card 5
奇跡「白昼の客星」 (Kiseki "Hakuchuu no Kakusei")
Miracle "Daytime Guest Stars"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
敵の頭上に輝く星を呼び出し攻撃。星は一定時間で上へと移動するが、この時下方へ星屑を撒き散らす。
Translation
Calls down a bright star above the enemy's head. The star can move up and down, and sprinkle down stardust on the enemy.


Spell Card 6
秘術「忘却の祭儀」 (Hijutsu "Boukyaku no Saigi")
Esoterica "Forgotten Ritual"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
巨大な星印を組み、周囲を不可侵の結界とする防御系の大技。効果の発揮までワンデンポ有るが、長い無敵で守られている点が強み。
Translation
Creates a giant star-shaped seal which forms an unpenetrable barrier. An excellent defense skill. A bit slow on the start-up but it creates a long period of invincibility.


Spell Card 7
奇跡「客星の明るすぎる夜」 (Kiseki "Kakusei no Akaru-sugiru Yoru")
Miracle "Night With Overly-Bright Guest Stars"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
星の印を組み、上空へ飛ばすと天から強烈な光が降り注ぐ奇跡。光は画面上部を覆い、高所に居る敵に対しての攻撃となる、一種の全域攻撃。
Translation
Creates a star-shaped seal which flies towards the sky. A miraculous, hot light beams down from the sky, and covers the upper half of the screen. An all-encompassing anti-air attack.


Spell Card 8
開海「モーゼの奇跡」 (Kaikai "Mooze no Kiseki")
Sea Opening "Moses's Miracle"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
上空に転移し地上の敵へ強烈な一撃を加える打撃系スペルカード。転移開始で攻撃に転じるため距離を問わず奇襲が可能。
Translation
A powerful melee spellcard that allows Sanae to rain down blows from above the enemy. She's invincible during and after the attack, but she's vulnerable before the attack begins.


Spell Card 9
秘法「九字刺し」 (Hihou "Kujisashi")
Esoterica "Nine Syllable Stabs"
Cost
Cost 5
Comment
九字の印を切り、格子状の結界を画面上に生成する大技。結界は敵の行動を大きく制限し、触れない限り長時間存在するため効果中は早苗の独壇場となる。
Translation
A powerful skill that creates nine boundaries. Severely limits the opponent's ability to move, and as long as they are not touched they will continue to be on the screen, so Sanae can dominate.

Notes[edit]

^ Fafrotskies - the phenomenon where unnatural things fall from the skies, like frogs.
