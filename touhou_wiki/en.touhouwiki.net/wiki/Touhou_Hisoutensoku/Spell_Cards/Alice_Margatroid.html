Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
人形伏兵 (Ningyou Fukuhei)
Doll Ambush
Cost
1 orb
Comment
人形の集団を潜伏させる技で、ボタンを押し続けて潜伏状態を纏続し離すことで攻撃が可能。人形は見えくなっていても敵から攻撃を受ける点には注意。
Translation
A technique that allows dolls to hide. The dolls will lie in wait as long as the button is held, and attack on release. The dolls can be attacked by the enemy even if they can't see them, so be careful.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
人形弓兵 (Ningyou Kyuuhei)
Doll Archers
Cost
1 orb
Comment
背後に展開した人形達から弓でも攻撃を指令する弾幕技。弓の軌道は毎回バラバラだがカバー範囲か広く密度も高い。
Translation
A ranged attack that utilizes doll archers who will attack from behind the player. The trajectories of the arrows are scattered, but the arrows themselves are dense and cover a wide area.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
槍符「キューティー大千槍」 (Yari-fu "Kyuutii Dai-chiyari")
Lance Sign "Cutie Phalanx"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
大量の人形での近距離攻撃を行う。速度や隙も優秀で射程も中々。懐に入られた際の奥の手として開発されたスペルカード。
Translation
Many dolls attack the enemy at close range. Excellent speed and coverage, with pretty good range to boot. A spellcard that was developed as a last resort.


Spell Card 2
人形「レミングスパレード」 (Ningyou "Remingusu Pareedo")
Dolls "Lemmings' Parade"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
爆弾を積んだ人形が行進して特攻地表全体を長詩間制圧し、威力や削り能力も非常に高い。相手が悪いとなぎ倒されることも.
Translation
Explosive-laden dolls march on the ground, resulting in a large-scale, long-lasting suppression attack. Destructive power is extremely high, and can potentially mow down the enemy.
