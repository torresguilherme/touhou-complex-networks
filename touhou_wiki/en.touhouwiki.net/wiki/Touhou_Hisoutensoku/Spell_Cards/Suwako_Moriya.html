Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
大地の湖 (Daichi no Mizu'umi)
Lake of Great Earth
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中に向かって頭から飛び込み体当たりと衝撃で周囲を攻撃する。地中でも使え、相手の虚を突ける。
Translation
A diving attack towards the earth. Tackles the enemy and damages the area. Can also be used underground to catch the enemy off guard.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
古代翡翠 (Kodai Hisui)
Ancient Jade
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中から輝く翡翠を集め、それを高速の弾丸にして敵へ放つ。発射にワンテンポ有るが速度と威力面で優れる射撃技。地中と空中で使用可能。
Translation
Collects jades from underground and shoots them as high-speed projectiles. Short delay before firing, but a powerful attack that can be used both above and below ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
蛙石神 (Kaeru-ishigami)
Stone Frog God
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中から蛙の形をした岩に乗って、突進する技、下段で威力が高く、距離が合えば反撃もされ難い。空中版は地中にワープしつつ発動。
Translation
Rides a frog-shaped boulder from underground and thrusts into the enemy. Pretty strong attack, and if done correctly, can't easily be countered.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
大蝦蟇神 (Ooebi Hikigami)
Giant Toad God
Cost
1 orb
Comment
蛙の姿を模したオーラを放つ技。蛙のシルエットそのままの判定で無敵は無いが置いておく様な牽制。連続技にと接近戦で効果を発揮。地中及び地上で使用可能。
Translation
Shoots out a frog-like aura. The silhouette itself can't be hit but can be used to restrain the enemy, so it's useful in melee combat. Can be used below or above ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
古の鉄輪 (Inishie no Kanawa)
Ancient Iron Ring
Cost
1 orb
Comment
手元で光を纏った鉄の輪を回転攻撃すると同時に移動する打撃技。空中、若しくはしゃがみ状態から出すことが可能で、頭上の牽制と空中制御の要。
Translation
Spins a light-infused metal ring around, and attacks the enemy. Can be used in the air, or on the lily, and is an extremely useful anti-air move.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
手長足長さん (Tenaga-ashinaga-san)
Mr. Long-Arm and Mr. Long-Leg
Cost
1 orb
Comment
手足から四本の追尾光を発する技。空中でのみ使用可能で光の持つ。捕捉範囲は画面全体と非常に広い。
Translation
Four aimed beams of light shoot out from each of Suwako's limbs. Can only be used in mid-air, but the attack covers a very large area. The number of beams shot increases and the level goes up.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
古の間欠泉 (Inishie no Kanketsusen)
Ancient Geyser
Cost
1 orb
Comment
足元に水流を呼び寄せ攻撃する。地中で出すと水流に乗って上移動。空中で出すと下へ降らせて攻撃。
Translation
An attack that spews water from Suwako's feet. She rides the water and attacks from the air.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
水蛙神 (Mizukaeru-gami)
Water Frog God
Cost
1 orb
Comment
帽子から水で出来たカエルを放つ。カエルは地面を跳ねて進み、敵の近くへ着地すると大爆発して攻撃。カエルの進行は遅いので連携にも。
Translation
A water frog springs from Suwako's hat and bounces on the ground towards the enemy. Explodes when near. Because the frog is slow, it is possible to attack while it is moving.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
雨を呼ぶ雨蛙 (Ame wo Yobu Amagaeru)
Rain-calling Frog
Cost
1 orb
Comment
頭上からスプリンクラーの様に大量の水を散布して攻撃する技。弾が高密度でカバー、範囲も広く。比較的スタンダードな射撃となる。
Translation
Brings down rain sprinkler-style from overhead. Projectiles are dense and cover a great range. A pretty normal attack, comparatively. More shots can be fired as long as the button is held down. Water drop bullet size enlarges as the level goes up.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
土着神の祟り (Dochakushin no Tatari)
Native God's Scourge
Cost
1 orb
Comment
祟り神であるミシャグジさまの力を雲状にして放つ追尾弾。威力は低いが当ると呪いが掛かり、相手に状態異常を付与する。高レベルは防いでも呪いが掛かる。
Translation
Using the power of Mishaguji, the scourge god, shoots out homing clouds. They're weak but they can curse the enemy with adverse effects if they hit. At high levels, the opponent will still get cursed even when the move is guarded. Only usable on the ground. The cloud size enlarges as the level increases.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 11
祟られた大地 (Tatarareta Daichi)
Scourged Earth
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中もしくは立ち状態から自分の足元にミシャグジさまの力が渦巻くように展開する設置技。渦の内部に入ると呪いが掛かり、何らかの状態異常を与える。
Translation
Mishaguji's power springs forth from Suwako's feet, creating a vortex of cursed energy, which will affect the enemy. Can be used underground or standing up. Area of effect enlarges if the attack is charged.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 12
ミシャグジさまの祟り (Mishaguji-sama no Tatari)
Mishaguji Scourge
Cost
1 orb
Comment
使用するとこの系列の技が一切使えなくなる代わり、以後相手の特定行動に対して防御低下のベナルティを与える。
Translation
In return for not being able to use Mishaguji's powers for the duration of the match, this move imparts a permanent penalty on the enemy which lowers their defense at certain times.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
源符「諏訪清水」 (Genfu "Suwa Shimizu")
Spring Sign "Suwa Clear Water"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
吹き付けた息が水流に転じ敵を押し流す射撃系スペルカードで、射撃としての性質は高水準しゃがみか空中で使用可能。
Translation
A spellcard that drowns the enemy in water. A good projectile attack that can be used above the ground.


Spell Card 2
神具「洩矢の鉄の輪」 (Shingu "Moriya no Tetsu no Wa")
Divine Tool "Moriya's Iron Ring"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
画面内を跳ね回る鉄の輪を投擲。三つの輪が広範囲に放たれる為、逃げ場の少ない。優秀射撃だが、本体が叩かれると消える点に注意。
Translation
Throws metal rings that jump around inside the screen. Releases three of them, so there's not really a lot of places to run. It's a wonderful skill, but note that the rings will disappear if Suwako is hit.


Spell Card 3
土着神「洩矢神」 (Dochakushin "Moriya-kami")
Native God "Moriya God"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
巨大なカエル型のオーラを纏い、周囲の敵を弾き飛ばす近距離攻撃。無敵時間がある上に速度が速く、判定が強力なので防御にも使える。
Translation
A giant frog aura surrounds Suwako and attacks the enemy. Immune to attacks and fast. Good hitbox, also usable for defensive purposes.


Spell Card 4
開宴「二拝二拍一拝」 (Kaien "Nihai Nihaku Ippai")
Party Start "Two Bows, Two Claps, and One Bow"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
巨大な岩の手が現れ合掌のように相手を挟んで押しつぶす打撃技。地中からも奇襲的に使用可能だが、隙は大きいので注意。
Translation
A giant stone hand appears and squishes the enemy in a pincer attack. Can be used as a surprise attack underground but beware--there are large gaps.


Spell Card 5
土着神「ケロちゃん風雨に負けず」 (Dochakushin "Kero-chan Fuu'u ni Makezu")
Native God "Froggy Braves the Wind and Rain"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
手元から嵐の様な水弾の乱射。撒かれた水は長時間に渡って、辺りに降り注いで相手を妨害する。
Translation
Shoots out a flurry of water bullets from her hands. After a period of time, they rain down upon the enemy.


Spell Card 6
源符「厭い川の翡翠」 (Genfu "Itoigawa no Hisui")
Spring Sign "Jade of the Horrid River"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
画面後方から巨大な鉄砲水が突如襲い掛かってくるスペルカード。大地の創造を司る彼女にとって川一つ曲げることは造作も無い。全盛期なら。
Translation
Unleashes a torrent of water from the back of the screen. For her, who created the earth, bending rivers is a piece of cake. If she's performing at her best, that is.


Spell Card 7
蛙狩「蛙は口ゆえ蛇に呑まるる」 (Kaerugari "Kaeru wa Kuchi yue Hebi ni Nomaruru")
Frog Hunt "The Snake Eats the Croaking Frog"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
地面から蛇の様な岩が飛び出し、蛇に食べらい付く射撃の一種で、発生が遅めだが空中ガード不能。蛙の神様で有る彼女が蛇の造形を使うのは神奈子への対抗意故か？
Translation
A snake-shaped boulder springs from the ground and devours the enemy, Has a slow startup but can't be air blocked. For a frog god to be using a snake-like attack... is this her competing with Kanako?


Spell Card 8
土着神「手長足長さま」 (Dochakushin "Tenaga-ashinaga-sama")
Native God "Lord Long-Arm and Lord Long-Leg"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
両手足から神力を放つ射撃攻撃。見た目通り大の字に展開される。真横以外隙間の無い無敵技だが、射程が短く空中でしか使えない。
Translation
Beams godly power from her limbs. There are no gaps other than the sides, so it's an unbeatable attack, but has a short range and can only be used in mid-air.


Spell Card 9
土着神「宝永四年の赤蛙」 (Dochakushin "Houei Yonen no Akagaeru")
Native God "Red Frogs of Houei 4"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
敵を追尾攻撃する自分を二体生成。本体は長時間動けないが、自分が敵を細かく追い回す。
Translation
Splits and attacks the enemy. She can't move for a while, but her split bodies chase the enemy around.


Spell Card 10
蛙休「オールウェイズ冬眠できます」 (Kawazu-yasumi "Ooruweizu Toumin dekimasu")
Frog Nap "Can Always Hibernate"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
地中でのみ出せる技で、その場でおもむろに寝始めて回復を図る。隙が特大で出る所を反撃されるが、地中技で出現を誤魔化せる為、そこはデッキや読み次第となる。
Translation
Only usable underground. Suwako sleeps and recovers health. Highly vulnerable, so use an underground move to confuse. This all depends on your deck and how well your enemy can read your moves, though.


Spell Card 11
祟り神「赤口（ミシャグチ）さま」 (Tatarigami "Mishaguchi-sama")
Scourge God "Mishaguchi-sama"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
崇り神であり諏訪子が従えるミシャグジさま達に相手を捧げる。諏訪子の周りから広がる呪いの土に捕まれば逃げる術は無い。
Translation
The scourge god obeys Suwako, and spreads curses around to the enemy. Can't escape once caught.
