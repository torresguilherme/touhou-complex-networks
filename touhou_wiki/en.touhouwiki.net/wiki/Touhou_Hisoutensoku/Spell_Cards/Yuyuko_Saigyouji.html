Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
死還の大地 (Shikae no Daichi)
Land of Death
Cost
1 orb
Comment
幽霊が飛び出しては地中へ移動を繰り返して前進して行く射撃技。幽霊ならではの気紛れが際立つ変則射撃である。
Translation
Ghosts fly out and advances towards the enemy repeatedly in this ranged attack. An attack that fully showcases the whimsical nature of ghosts.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
センスオブエレガンス (Sensu Obu Eregansu)
Sense of Elegance
Cost
1 orb
Comment
その場で回転して熱いをつけて鋭い扇の打ち上げ打撃を見舞う舞う[sic]様な動きは魂から魅了される危い美しさがある。
Translation
Yuyuko spins in place, using her sharp fans to hit the enemy. A graceful, beautiful and deadly dance.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
故人のお届け物 (Kojin no Otodoke-mono)
Gifts to the Deceased
Cost
1 orb
Comment
上空へ舞い上がり敵を？む投げ。この時敵へ幽霊を憑依させておき。この技を当てる度に攻撃させる。食らった側の感想は「極端に腹が空いたようになる」だとか
Translation
Yuyuko flies skywards, grabbing the enemy and throwing her. Since she depends on ghosts for this move, whenever she performs it she becomes extremely hungry.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
「反魂蝶」　 ("Hangonchou")
"Resurrection Butterfly"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
周囲に伸びる光の帯と舞い上がる蝶の群れで攻撃するスペル。始めは貧弱な性能だが、繰り返し使う事で徐々に強力な攻撃へと変化して行く。
Translation
Beams of light eminate[sic] from Yuyuko, and swarms of butterfly attack. It starts out weak, but becomes stronger and stronger as it is used more often.


Spell Card 2
桜符「センスオブチェリーブロッサム」 (Sakura-fu "Sensu Obu Cherii Burossamu")
Cherry Blossom Sign "Sense of Cherry Blossom"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
螺旋状に練り上げた。妖力を空へ解き放つ打撃系スペルカード。準備に時間が掛かるのが難点だが威力、範囲は蝶…超一級。
Translation
Unleashes a spiral of energy skywards. Takes a long time to prepare, but has unparalleled range and power.
