Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
芳波 (Houha)
Fragrant Wave
Cost
1 orb
Comment
円弧の動きで気を循環させ、前方へ収束させて放つ気攻波。有限射程だが、届く範囲ならば瞬時に攻撃が可能で貫通性も高い。
Translation
A semicircle of chi circles and is released as a forward beam. Limited range, but instantaneous. High penetration.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
水形太極拳 (Suigyou Taikyokuken)
Water Taijiquan
Cost
1 orb
Comment
渦状に気の流れを制御して射撃を飲み込む気の防壁を作り出し、巻き込んだ敵弾諸共撃ち返す。彼女なりのエコを気遣っている。
Translation
Creates a barrier of chi out of a whirlpool-shaped chi flow, which absorbs all projectile attacks. Her way of caring for the environment.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
彩雨 (Saiu)
Colorful Rain
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周囲に気を循環させ、熱いのまま辺りへ飛び散らせる射撃技。本来は無心で規則的に放つ技だが、彼女に夕食予想等の雑念がある為、ばら撒かれる軌道はランダム。
Translation
Gathers chi from the surroundings and disperses it around. It should have been dispersed uniformly if one’s mind is calm, but because she’s always thinking about dinner and such, the paths are random.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
紅砲 (Kouhou)
Red Cannon
Cost
1 orb
Comment
斜め上へ向け練った気の塊を放つ。発射までは拳に直接攻撃力もあり、上空の敵へ対して遠近共に効果的。
Translation
A chi bomb that directly attacks the upper diagonal. Connecting with the fist also causes damage, so it’s a great anti-air and melee move.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
紅寸剄 (Kousunkei)
Red Energy Release
Cost
1 orb
Comment
腕に気を集中し、さらに全身の力を乗せ絶大な威力の一撃を打つ。初の試し撃ちでうっかり紅魔館の塀を破壊してしまい、キッツイお灸を据えられた曰く付きの技。
Translation
Gathers chi into the arms and unleashes a huge attack from her entire body. The first time she tried it, she destroyed the wall of the Scarlet Devil Mansion, so this is an extremely intimidating attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
虎剄 (Kokei)
Tiger Chi Release
Cost
1 orb
Comment
全身の気を流れを一時的に活性化打撃の際に撃ち込む事で強化する。この状態で触れた最初の打撃は強制的にカウンターヒットし、高レベルでは威力も増加する。
Translation
Activates all chi within Meiling and makes her attacks stronger. The first hit she lands in this condition is extremely strong and is a counterhit. Becomes more powerful at high levels.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
螺光歩 (Rakouho)
Spiral-Light Steps
Cost
1 orb
Comment
気を腕へ集めて練り上げ、それを敵へと叩き込む踏み込み突き。高レベルになると踏み込みにも攻撃力が発生する。
Translation
Gathers chi into her arms and punches and stomps the enemy. At high levels, the stomps also do damage.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
烈虹拳 (Rekkouken)
Intense Rainbow Fist
Cost
1 orb
Comment
高速で突きを連打し、そこから気弾を連続して放って敵を止め、最後に強力な裏拳を見舞う技。接近状態での牽制と、そこからの致命打を狙って作られた。
Translation
First unleashes a high-speed punch and then fires a few balls of chi at the enemy to stop her. Finally, she unleashes one last powerful punch. A move created to suppress and then pummel the enemy.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
降華蹴 (Koukashuu)
Kick of Showering Brilliance
Cost
1 orb
Comment
円弧を描く蹴りで頭上を攻める。見ての通り中段攻撃で、蹴りの軌道上空対空でも打ち負けづらい。
Translation
Attacks overhead with this sweeping arc kick. A mid-level attack, hard to be bested even in mid-air.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
黄震脚 (Oushinkyaku)
Yellow Quake Kick
Cost
1 orb
Comment
練り上げた気を大地へ打ち込む。気は地中を拡散し、周囲を攻撃する。気を循環と大気、大地への放出は基本として重要な位置である。
Translation
Directs chi towards the earth and it spreads throughout the ground and attacks. The release and cycling of chi through the earth is of fundamental importance.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 11
地龍波 (Chiryuuha)
Earth Dragon Wave
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中に打ち込む気の質を収束させ、距離による減衰を小さくした技。しかし基本空で戦うことが多い幻想郷では日の目を見ることが少ない悲運の技でもある。
Translation
Stomps the ground and deals damage to the enemy with a wave. In Gensokyo, where most battles are aerial, this move sees very little use.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 12
天龍脚 (Tenryuukyaku)
Sky Dragon Kick
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中に打ち込む気の反動を使い、上空へ向け強烈な飛び蹴りを放つ空を翔る龍の如きシルエットを持ち、彼女のお気に入り技の一つである。
Translation
Using the counter-reaction from stomping the ground, she kicks strongly into the air. With a dragon like silhouette, this move is one of her favorites.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
彩符「彩光風鈴」 (Saifu "Saikou Fuurin")
Colorful Sign "Colorful Light Wind Chime"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
全身で気の渦を作り出し攻撃する。攻撃範囲はそれなりだが手軽な無敵技であり、防御の基本である。
Translation
Using all of her chi, she creates a whirlpool and attacks. A very handy and easy invincible attack and a staple of defense.


Spell Card 2
虹符「烈虹真拳」 (Niji-fu "Rekkou Shinken")
Rainbow Sign "True Intense Rainbow Fist"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
前方に向け気を込めた突きを乱打。一見射撃だが完全な打撃技。判定は扇状に前方をカバーし、距離が合えば地上も空中も射程内レバーで前後移動も可能である。
Translation
A wild, chi-infused attack forwards. The hitbox is fan-shaped and covers the front. If the distance is correct, she can move on the ground and still remain in range.




Spell Card 3
気符「星脈弾」 (Kifu "Seimyakudan")
Chi Sign "Star Pulse Bullets"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
前方に高度に練り上げた気の塊をぶつける射撃系スペルカード。スタンダードな射撃で弾も大きく扱いが容易である。
Translation
Releases chi bullets forwards. A fairly standard ranged attack, and the bullets are easy to use because they’re large.


Spell Card 4
撃符「大鵬拳」 (Gekifu "Taihouken")
Strike Sign "Dapeng Fist"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
上方へ向けて渾身の打撃を打つ。無敵は上半身のみだが判定が強い敵の落下が早いためタイミングが難しいが、落ちてくる相手へ対し、追撃が可能になっている。
Translation
An all-out attack upwards. Only invincible from the waist up, but has a large hitbox. The enemy falls down quickly, so it’s difficult to time, but it is possible to hit the enemy on their way down.


Spell Card 5
気符「地龍天龍脚」 (Kifu "Chiryuu Tenryuukyaku")
Chi Sign "Earth Dragon Sky Dragon Kick"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
七色の気を練り込んだ跡び蹴り。踏み込みで相手を浮かせ、そこに蹴りで追撃できる為距離が近いと発生の遅さがカバーされる。
Translation
A flying, seven-colored, chi-filled kick. The kick suspends the enemy in mid-air, and Meiling can continue to hit her. Because of this, the move is slow on startup if you use it close to the enemy.


Spell Card 6
極彩「彩光乱舞」 (Gokusai "Saikou Ranbu")
Extreme Color "Vivid Chaotic Dance"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
全身で強力な気の嵐を巻き起こす。発生する気が強力になったためその熱いで宙に舞うように変化。攻撃範囲も広く、信頼性は高い。
Translation
Whips up a storm using powerful chi from all over her body. Because the chi is strong, she can fly into the sky. Great range, very reliable.


Spell Card 7
彩華「虹色太極拳」 (Saika "Niji-iro Taikyokuken")
Colorful Chinese Flower "Rainbow Taijiquan"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
波紋の様に地表を流れる気の波を発生させる特殊な射撃カード。波は非常にゆっくりと広がり長時間相手の行動を制限する。
Translation
A special ranged attack that creates waves of chi that undulate like waves. They are really slow, but spread and limit the enemy’s movement.


Spell Card 8
気符「猛虎内剄」 (Kifu "Moukonaikei")
Chi Sign "Fierce Tiger Energy Release"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
全身の気の流れを爆発的に高める。以後一定時間連続技の初撃が必ずカウンターヒット扱いになり、ダメージも激増する。
Translation
Explosively raises the level of chi in the body. During this time, all first hits on all combos are treated as counterhits and gain an extremely large attack bonus.


Spell Card 9
星気「星脈地転弾」 (Seiki "Seimyaku Chitendan")
Star Chi "Earth-Moving Star Pulse Bullets"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
長時間かけて気を練り上げる事で、巨大な七色の気弾を作り出す。練り上げている最中の気弾も攻撃能力が有り、この部分の発生速度は非常に早い。
Translation
Takes a while to create a huge, seven-colored bullet of chi. Even while being created, the bullet has a hitbox. On top of that, it also forms very quickly.


Spell Card 10
熾撃「大鵬墜撃拳」 (Shigeki "Taihou Tsuigekiken")
Blazing Strike "Dapeng-Felling Fist"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
三連続で強烈な打撃を見舞う。出始めにアーマーが有り、攻撃を耐えて無理やり通すことも可能。威力は超一級で追撃も可能のため一発逆転のロマン溢れる一枚。
Translation
A three-tiered powerful attack. She can power through enemy attacks and still use this move; extremely powerful and able to be linked into, this is one move that can turn the tables completely in a match.


Spell Card 11
華符「彩光蓮華掌」 (Kafu "Saikou Rengeshou")
Flower Sign "Colorful Light Lotus Flower Palm"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
大量の気を直接相手に流し込み、スパークさせることで大打撃を与える超大技打撃。悪魔やアンデッドに大ダメージとの事だが、ガセネタのようだ。
Translation
Moves a large amount of chi directly into the enemy, then sparks the chi within them with a huge attack. Super effective against demons and undead… or maybe not.
