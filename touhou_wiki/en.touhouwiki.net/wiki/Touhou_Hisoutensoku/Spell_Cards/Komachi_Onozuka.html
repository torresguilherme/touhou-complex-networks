Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
三途の舟 (Sanzu no Fune)
Boat of the Sanzu
Cost
1 orb
Comment
船に飛び乗り体当たりする突進技。地面の上を船？と思う所だが、本人は細かい事は気にしていない。船は支給品でガタも来ている為上司にこの件で良く怒られている。
Translation
Charges the enemy with her boat. A boat on land? She doesn't care too much, but since the boat was issued to her and she's wearing it down, her superior gets quite angry with her over this.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
離魂の鎌 (Rikon no Kama)
Spirit-Detaching Scythe
Cost
1 orb
Comment
怨霊を集めた刃を作り相手を斬る。飛び散らせた体力を回収したり活性爆破の種として使用可能。この技自体でも活性爆破ができる。
Translation
Cuts the opponent with her spirit filled scythe. If she touches the spirits that emerge from her scythe after the attack, she can regain some health. She can also cause the spirits to explode.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
魂符「生魂流離の鎌」 (Konfu "Seikonryuuri no Kama")
Soul Sign "Scythe of Roaming Spirits"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
怨霊を集めて作った刃を放つ刃は敵を切ると生命力を奪った。怨霊が飛び出し、小町が回収して間接的に体力を吸収できる。本人曰く余り使いたくない技。
Translation
Unleashes a blade filled with spirits which steal life from the enemy. The spirits fly back to Komachi and allow her to regain health. According to her, she doesn't use this skill too often.
