Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
燐気斬 (Rinkizan)
Phosphoric Slash
Cost
1 orb
Comment
短い距離を広がり戻る剣気を放つ。有効射程は狭いが空振り時の戻りでも当るため、近距離での攻めに特化しているスキル
Translation
A short-range attack that unleashes a burst of energy from Youmu's sword. Even when it returns it hits the enemy, so it's a good skill for short-range attackers.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
頭上花剪斬 (Zujouka Senzan)
Slashing a Flower Upon One's Head
Cost
1 orb
Comment
頭上に跳躍し渾身の兜割りを放つ。移動中は無防備だが速度が速くガードさせればその後の展開も悪くないので、相手の頭上をガンガン強襲する事が可能。
Translation
Jumps above the enemy and slashes down with all her might. Even if the enemy guards, it's a good move, and Youmu can exert massive pressure on the opponent from above.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
転生剣「円心流転斬」 (Tenseiken "Enshin Rutenzan")
Reincarnation Sword "Slash of Circular Vicissitude" [1]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
楼観剣での高速連続斬りから勢いを利用し横薙ぎに払い抜ける。無敵時間は早い内に切れるが判定が強く発生も早い。
Translation
Deals repeated high-speed hits using Roukanken, and overwhelms the enemy while remaining invincible. A strong attack with very fast activation.


Spell Card 2
空観剣「六根清浄斬」 (Kuukanken "Rokkon Shoujouzan")
Voidness Sword "Slash Clearing the Six Senses" [2]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
白楼剣で敵の攻撃をいなした後、フットワークを活かした踏み込み斬撃を無数に叩き込む大技。
Translation
Block the enemy's attack with Hakurouken, and then use quick footwork to rush the enemy in this massive attack.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 円心: "center of a circle". Also a phrase in Buddhism, meaning "a mind that seeks complete attainment of Nirvana."
  2. ↑ To see nothing, hear nothing, smell nothing, taste nothing, feel nothing, think nothing of impurities — to cut away from the impure world.
