Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Refer to Brocken Spectre
  2. ↑ This title is a parody of Korejanai Robo (This-Isn’t-That Robot, コレジャナイロボ). The toy is named to represent sad feeling that parents are likely to give their children another one different from what they really want and it causes children screaming “This isn't what I want!!”, because almost all toys look too similar for parents to choose by theirselves. Somehow that toy won prize of Good Design Award in 2008.
  3. ↑ The kanji for Hisoutensoku is written as 非想天則: Hi (非) is a prefix for "negative" akin to "non-/un-", sou (想) is "thought/imagine", and tensoku (天則) means the "rule of nature/the universe". Therefore, "Cannot think of the law of nature" (天則を想うに非ず).
