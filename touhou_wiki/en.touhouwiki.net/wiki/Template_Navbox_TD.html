Description[edit]

To display a navigational box under every Ten Desires related article.

Usage[edit]

Simply place {{Navbox TD}} at the bottom of each related article.

Optionally, you can disable template's auto categorization function by simply include {{Navbox TD|nocat}} in articles that should not be categorize.

The above documentation is transcluded from Template:Navbox TD/doc. (edit | history)
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (create | mirror) and testcases (create) pages.
