Super Marisa World[edit]

0-1-2[edit]

SMW_Dyne-0-1-2.rpy

Perfect run on this level. If anyone can think of a better way to get the stars above the last B block, it'll likely be a lot smarter than my method.

0-2-1[edit]

SMW_Dyne-0-2-1.rpy

Perfect run on this level. Time is your worst enemy for this one, but this is the easiest level in World 0 to perfect.

0-2-2[edit]

SMW_Dyne-0-2-2.rpy

Perfect run on this level. I'm still kicking myself over how easy it really is when you know about the black yin-yang orbs.

0-2-3[edit]

SMW_Dyne-0-2-3.rpy

Perfect run on this level. All of the stars are a little tricky to find.

1-1-1[edit]

SMW_Dyne-1-1-1.rpy

Perfect run on this level. The last group of stars is a real pain to get.

1-1-2[edit]

SMW_Dyne-1-1-2.rpy

Perfect run on this level. Do NOT lose your broom.

5-1-3a[edit]

SMW_Dyne-5-1-3a.rpy

After a little practice getting through the fourth room, managed to beat this level and here's the replay for the alternate exit. 1 death, and there's a trick to starting the hopping up along the blue fuzzes at the end.

5-1-4[edit]

SMW_Dyne-5-1-4rpy

Here's a little fun replay against Youmu that shows a couple of stupid bugs that are possible to exploit(?) after defeating her.

6-2-1[edit]

SMW_Dyne-6-2-1.rpy

Took exactly 79 tries to beat this over the course of the past month. Yes, I kept a count. Feel free to laugh at the really stupid death in the room before Koakuma.

6-2-2[edit]

SMW_Dyne-6-2-2.rpy

Perfect score for this level. The only real way to die on this one is by running out of time or running into Eirin. Otherwise, it's almost impossible if you drill kick.

6-2-3[edit]

SMW_Dyne-6-2-3.rpy

For the maze that covers the last 1/3 of the stage: Top-Bottom-Middle-Top-Bottom-Bottom-Middle-Bottom-Top-Middle

7-2-4[edit]

SMW_Dyne-7-2-4.rpy

It took me several tries and about an hour to beat Suika. When I did the first time, the game crashed. After two more tries, got this 1LCer and no crash.
