Touhou Illusionary Play of Sounds ～fAIRYtAILoVERdRIVE～ (東方幻奏響UROBOROS弐 ～fAIRYtAILoVERdRIVE～, Touhou Gensoukyou UROBOROS) is a doujin album by とらのあな released on 2014-12-28 (Comiket 87). It consists of 6 albums with diverse arrangements by 49 circles. The first three discs have all new tracks, and the last three consist of re-recordings.
