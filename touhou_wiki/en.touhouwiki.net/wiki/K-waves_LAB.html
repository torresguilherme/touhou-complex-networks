Discography
幻想郷ポポルカント
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

風萃めのシャルモ
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith.

無常幻想詩
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Undefined Fantastic Object, Touhou Hisoutensoku and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

ゴツまさ倶楽部
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
A collaboration album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes and Magical Astronomy. It is a collaboration with O-LIFE.JP.

幻想郷に恋したミューズ
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Double Spoiler and Ten Desires.

妖かしのまほろば
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

幽かのたまゆら
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Dealing Character.

旅情逃飛行
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires, Double Dealing Character and Impossible Spell Card.
