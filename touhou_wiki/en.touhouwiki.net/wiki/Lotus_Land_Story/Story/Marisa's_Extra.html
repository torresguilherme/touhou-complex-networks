Extra[edit]

A Quiet Closing for Wonderful You ~ Puckish Angel

すばらしい君に静かな瞑りを

A Quiet Closing for Wonderful You

Puckish Angel

Puckish Angel

BGM: 禁じざるをえない遊戯

BGM: The Inevitably Forbidden Game

Marisa

・・・すごい敵だったわ
しかも、誰かいる！？

...Those were some tough enemies.
And there's someone here!?

Mugetsu ENTERS

BGM: メイド幻想　～ Icemilk Magic

BGM: Illusion of a Maid ~ Icemilk Magic

？？ (Mugetsu)

誰かしら？

Who might you be?

Marisa

あら、誰かしら？

My, who might you be?

？？ (Mugetsu)

・・・（怒）

... (angry)

Mugetsu

私は、夢月。
で、ここは、私の世界。
あなたが、勝手にこっちに
入り込んできたみたいだけ
ど・・・

I'm Mugetsu.
And this is my world.
It seems like you just
barged your way in,
but...

Marisa

ひまつぶしよ。

I'm just killing time.

Mugetsu

・・・（怒）

... (angry)

Marisa

よって、あなたもあいて
になって貰おうかしら。

So, I'll need you to
go along with me.

Mugetsu

変わった人間ね。
よりによって私を？
人間の命なんか、なんとも
思っていないのよ、私。

You're a strange human.
Why me, of all people?
I think of nothing
of human lives, you know.

Marisa

じゃ、やめようかな。

Then maybe I'll stop.

Mugetsu

どのみち、帰すつもりはな
いけど。
せいぜい頑張ることね。

In any case, I have no intention
of letting you go back.
So just try your hardest now.

Mugetsu DEFEATED

Marisa

それだけ？

That's it?

Mugetsu

うぐ～
っていうことで、あとは
姉さんに任したわ（汗）

Ugh...
Since it's come to this,
I'll leave the rest to my sister. (sweat)

Marisa

ねえさん？

Sister?

Gengetsu ENTERS

BGM: かわいい悪魔　～ Innocence

BGM: Cute Devil ~ Innocence

？？ (Gengetsu)

って、何負けてんのよ（怒）

Hey, what did you go and lose for!? (angry)

Marisa

なんだい、次から次と。

Seems it's one after the other today.

？？ (Gengetsu)

初対面の相手になによ（怒）

Isn't that a bit rude for our first meeting? (angry)

Gengetsu

まぁ、私は幻月。夢月の姉
ですわ。
よくわかんないけど、夢月
に任されたから、あなたを
倒せばいいんだよね。

Anyway, I'm Gengetsu, Mugetsu's
older sister.
I don't really know what's going on, but,
since she left it to me,
all I have to do is beat you, right?

Marisa

ひまつぶしなんだけど（汗）

I'm just here to kill time... (sweat)

Gengetsu

う～ん（汗）
まぁ、いきますよ

Uh-huh... (sweat)
Well, here I go!

Gengetsu DEFEATED

Gengetsu

え～ん（泣）強すぎるわ。

*whine* (cry) You're too strong!

Marisa

おもしろかった～☆
また遊んでね。

That was fun~☆
Let's play again sometime.

Gengetsu

遊ばれてたの（泣）

I was played with! (cry)

Marisa

そのと～し☆

Exactly! ☆
