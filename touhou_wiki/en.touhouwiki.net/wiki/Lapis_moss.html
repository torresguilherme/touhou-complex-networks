Description

Lapis moss is the personal circle of arranger and vocalist こばきょん, she was also a member of T.Piacere before it disbanded in 2014. Early albums by the circle saw lyrics and vocals contributed by こばきょん while arrangements where contributed exclusively by 天音 of Rolling Contact. It was the release of 月下美心 in 2010 that marked a shift towards including a greater number of guest arrangers (though 天音 remained a constant), it was in this time from 2010 to 2014 that こばきょん began arranging Touhou themes herself. By 2014 she was contributing multiple arrangements per album and in 2015 she started the なないろ album series with なないろミラージュ, the album featured her arranging all of the music and inviting prominent female vocalists from other Touhou arrangement circles as guests, a trend which future なないろ albums would uphold.
