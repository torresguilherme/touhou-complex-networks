Description

The circle previously active under a name 363. The band's name can be translated as "I Saw a Blue Moon"


Discography
Snowdrop
Release date: 2016-04-02
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

華が咲いた。退紅の昔日と共に
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, as well as 3 original compositions.

Tranquilizer e.p.
Release date: 2016-10-15 (Autumn Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Subterranean Animism.

Iris e.p.
Release date: 2016-10-20 (M3-38)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of a theme from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, as well as 2 original compositions.

其の少女は、藿香薊の花が枯れるのを待ち侘びた。e.p.
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Perfect Cherry Blossom.

バンド・デシネ
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism and Double Dealing Character.

Other works

  • "EP" released 2016-01-23 is a fully original album.
  • "ruins" released 2017-10-29 (M3-40) is a fully original album. It was later released digitally on booth.pm event called APOLLO-07.
