Genji Asai (唖采弦二, Asai Genji) is an artist who draws illustration of Touhou. He was responsible for illustrating the whole of Curiosities of Lotus Asia while ZUN created the story. He also had involvement in Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red, Perfect Memento in Strict Sense and The Grimoire of Marisa.

  • 東方文花帖　～ Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red (Ichijinsha August 2005)
