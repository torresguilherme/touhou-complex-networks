Profile

Shinigiwa Satellite
Dates
2009 - 2017
Website
死際サテライト official website
Genre
Punk, Post-hardcore, Vocal
Members
衛☆星太郎 (Ei Seitarou) – Arrangement, Lyrics, Guitar, Bass, Vocals
千影 (Chikage) – Vocals (guest)
めらみぽっぷ (Meramipop) – Vocals, Lyrics (guest)

Description

A punk/post-hardcore circle. First EPs had male vocals, then they switched to female vocals since 2011.
