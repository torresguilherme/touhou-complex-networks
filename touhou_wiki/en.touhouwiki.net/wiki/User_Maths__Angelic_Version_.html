Hi. I decided to join this wiki after noticing that the pages on several of my favourite doujin circles were outdated, so now I try to complete their discographies. Here's a list of artists whose pages I work on (or at least check once in a while) and the progress. If you decide to complete some of them yourself, feel free to let me know. You may think of my worklist as "let's try to complete these discographies".

  • A-One: Up to date as of Comiket 95, although a few articles are missing.
  • AdamKadmon: Complete of Comiket 95.
  • Applice: Up to date as of Comiket 95, although most articles are missing. (Inactive since RTS15)
  • 暁Records (Akatsuki Records): Complete as of Comiket 95.
  • Amateras Records: Up to date as of Comiket 95. Some missing articles.
  • CrazyBeats: Comiket 95 albums have not been added. Some articles are missing.
  • GET IN THE RING: Complete as of Comiket 95. Their original single is not mentioned.
  • 紺碧Studio: Complete as of Comiket 95.
  • monochrome-coat: Last updated for Comiket 90 (not that they've been very active since then...). Some articles are missing.
  • R-note: Complete as of Comiket 95.
  • サリー (Sally): Up to date as of Comiket 95 (it's a dead circle, apparently), although a few articles are missing.
  • Seventh Heaven Ammy's: Complete as of Comiket 95.
  • Seventh Heaven MAXION: Complete as of Comiket 95 (nothing released since Reitaisai 14, and that was a compilation album... it's probably safe to say the circle is dead).
  • 少女フラクタル (Shoujo Fractal): The discography is only complete up to C93. Some articles are missing.
  • Silver Forest: Last update at Comiket 93. The most recent compilation albums are missing articles.
  • SYNC.ART'S: Mostly complete (missing some Alternative preview albums) as of Comiket 95.
  • TUMENECO: The discography is only complete up to C93.
  • Unlucky Morpheus: Outdated - missing the C94 album. Some missing articles.
  • 幽閉サテライト (Yuuhei Satellite): Mostly up to date up to Comiket 94. Some instrumental albums and and limited CDs are missing from their discography (insert rant about frustrating limited CDs are here).
