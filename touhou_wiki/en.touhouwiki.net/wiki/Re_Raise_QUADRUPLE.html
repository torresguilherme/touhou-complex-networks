Re:Raise QUADRUPLE is a doujin album by TAMAONSEN released on 2017-10-15 (Autumn Reitaisai 4). An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.
