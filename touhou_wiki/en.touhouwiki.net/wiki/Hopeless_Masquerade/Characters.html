Background characters[edit]

Hakurei Shrine stage Chen, Luna Child, Patchouli Knowledge, Ran Yakumo, Remilia Scarlet, Sakuya Izayoi, Star Sapphire, Sunny Milk, Yukari Yakumo, a pair of Fairy Maids
Human Village stage Alice Margatroid, Eirin Yagokoro, Fujiwara no Mokou, Kaguya Houraisan, Keine Kamishirasawa, Kosuzu Motoori, Reisen Udongein Inaba, Rinnosuke Morichika, Suika Ibuki, Tewi Inaba, Yuugi Hoshiguma, several humans
Treasure Ship Above the Clouds stage Iku Nagae, Lily White, Lunasa Prismriver, Lyrica Prismriver, Merlin Prismriver, Minamitsu Murasa, Nazrin, Nue Houjuu, Tenshi Hinanawi
Myouren Temple stage Aya Shameimaru, Hatate Himekaidou, Kogasa Tatara, Kyouko Kasodani, Shou Toramaru, several humans
Hall of Dreams' Great Mausoleum stage Cirno, Letty Whiterock, Medicine Melancholy, Mystia Lorelei, Wriggle Nightbug, Luna Child, Star Sapphire, Sunny Milk, Yuuka Kazami, Lily White, Rumia
Divine Spirit Mausoleum stage Eiki Shiki, Yamaxanadu, Komachi Onozuka, Lunasa Prismriver, Lyrica Prismriver, Merlin Prismriver, Seiga Kaku, Soga no Tojiko, Yoshika Miyako, Youmu Konpaku, Yuyuko Saigyouji
Genbu Ravine stage Aya Shameimaru, Hatate Himekaidou, Kanako Yasaka, Minoriko Aki, Hina Kagiyama, Sanae Kochiya, Shizuha Aki, Suwako Moriya, several kappa
Palace of Earth Spirits stage Parsee Mizuhashi, Rin Kaenbyou, Satori Komeiji, Utsuho Reiuji, Yamame Kurodani, several animals, several vengeful spirits
Youkai Tanuki Forest stage Cirno, Hina Kagiyama, Iku Nagae, Kogasa Tatara, Lily White, Luna Child, Minoriko Aki, Parsee Mizuhashi, Rin Kaenbyou, Sanae Kochiya, Satori Komeiji, Shizuha Aki, Shou Toramaru, Star Sapphire, Sunny Milk, Suwako Moriya, Tenshi Hinanawi, Utsuho Reiuji, Wriggle Nightbug (All of them are disguised tanuki)
Youkai Tanuki Forest (Night) stage Several tanuki
Human Village (Night) stage Several humans (with masks)
