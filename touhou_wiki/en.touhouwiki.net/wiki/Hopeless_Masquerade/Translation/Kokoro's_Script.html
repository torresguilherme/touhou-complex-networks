Hopeless Masquerade/Translation/Kokoro's Script

Kokoro defeats Reimu[edit]

Kokoro [Womanly Mask]

貴方程度の希望では
満たされないの

Your level of hope can't fulfill me.

Kokoro defeats Marisa[edit]

Kokoro [Hyottoko Mask]

ねえねえ　またやろ♪
これが楽しみの表情

Hey hey let's do it again♪
This is the face for joy.

Kokoro defeats Ichirin[edit]

Kokoro [Womanly Mask]

ねえねえ
これが仏像のまねする表情ー

Hey hey
This is the face for pretending to be a Buddha statue~

Kokoro defeats Byakuren[edit]

Kokoro [Hannya Mask]

そうかー
感情を自由に制御しないとねー

I see~
I need to control my emotions freely~

Kokoro defeats Futo[edit]

Kokoro [Surprised Mask]

ねえねえ
これが皿回しのまねする表情ー

Hey hey
This is the face for pretending to be a plate spinner~


Kokoro defeats Miko[edit]

Kokoro [Old Woman mask]

新しい希望の面……
あんまり被りたくないな

The new mask of hope...
I don't really want to put it on.

Kokoro defeats Nitori[edit]

Kokoro

あの屋台に並んでいるお面って
どうやって作ってるの？

How do you make those masks that you put up in your stall?

Kokoro defeats Koishi[edit]

Kokoro [Fox Mask]

見つけたぞ！
私の宿敵よ！

I found you! My nemesis!

Kokoro

返せったらぁ
……しかしまあ

もう新しいお面も貰ったから
本当は要らないんだけどね

Even if you return it...

...well...

I got a new mask already, so
I don't really need it anymore.

Kokoro defeats Mamizou[edit]

Kokoro [Fox Mask]

ねえねえ
これが狸のまねする表情ー

Hey hey
This is the face for pretending to be a tanuki~

Kokoro defeats herself[edit]

Kokoro [Womanly Mask]

何だ何だ？
こういう時は
どういう表情が……

What, what?
What face should I have for this situation...?

Kokoro defeats someone[edit]

Kokoro [Hannya Mask]

話にもならぬわ！
これが怒りの表情

Not enough to even talk about!
This is the face for anger.

Kokoro [Laughing Mask]

わーい勝っちゃった
これが喜びの表情

Yay~ I won.
This is the face for happiness.

Kokoro [Surprised mask]

弱い！　弱すぎる！
これが驚きの表情

Weak! Too weak!
This is the face for surprise.

Kokoro [Hyottoko Mask]

ねえねえ
これが肘鉄する時の表情

Hey hey
This is the face for rebuff.

Kokoro [Hyottoko Mask]

弱すぎてつまんない……
これが哀しみの表情

Too weak, so boring...
This is the face for sadness.
