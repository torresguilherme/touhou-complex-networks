Ichirin defeats Reimu[edit]

Ichirin

巫女を制すれば
この宗教戦争は勝ったも同然

If I best the shrine maiden,
this whole religious war has already been won.

Ichirin

さあ　倒した妖怪の数を数えよ！
それがお前が落ちる
地獄の深さだ！

Now, count the number of youkai you defeated!
That's how deep you'll be going in hell!

Ichirin defeats Marisa[edit]

Ichirin

在家でも仏教始められるよん

You can also enter Buddhism as a layperson.

Ichirin

はーい
出家にごあんなーい

Okay,
now I'll tell you how to join the priesthood.

Ichirin defeats herself[edit]

Ichirin

……狸か狐に化かされた？

...... Is this a transformed tanuki or fox?

Ichirin defeats Byakuren[edit]

Ichirin

おつとめ
有難うございます

Thank you for reciting the scriptures, Mistress.

Ichirin

聖様はどうお考えですか？

呑んではいけないという
仏教の教えについて

Mistress, may I ask for your opinion?

What do you think about the Buddhist
scriptures that forbid drinking alcohol?

Ichirin defeats Futo[edit]

Ichirin

仏教の敵か……
そんな悪意のある教えでは
無いのに　何故だろう

Enemies of Buddhism?
We don't have bad intentions,
so why fight us?

Ichirin

皿の後片付けはやっておいてね！

Clean up these plates right now!

Ichirin defeats Miko[edit]

Ichirin

宇宙の真理を自分の為のみに
使うなんて邪教だね

To only use the secrets of the cosmos
for yourself is heresy, you know.

Ichirin defeats Nitori[edit]

Ichirin

お寺に入れば胡瓜だけで
生きられるようになる

If you enter our temple,
you can live only on cucumbers.

Ichirin defeats Koishi[edit]

Ichirin

まさか……
無我の境地とは……

Could this be...
what complete mental selflessness is?

Ichirin defeats Mamizou[edit]

Ichirin

貴方って鵺さんのご友人
なんですね

You're a friend of Miss Nue,
aren't you?

Ichirin

雲山の真似はしないんですか？

Won't you pretend to be Unzan?

Ichirin defeats Kokoro[edit]

Ichirin

モンキーポゼッション……
何それ

So...
What's "Monkey Possession"?

Ichirin

お面って臭くならないんですか？

Don't the masks become smelly?

Ichirin defeats someone[edit]

Ichirin

悔い改めよ！

Repent!

Ichirin

えっ？なになに？
弱いものイジメは嫌だ？
雲山は手ぬるいね

Huh? What's that, Unzan?
You hate bullying the weak?
You sure are lenient...
