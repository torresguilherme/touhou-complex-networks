Description

sound sepher was formed in the winter of 2003 as a new project by Jun.A who had moved on from previous circle P:M who's releases stretched as far back as the year 2000. Despite eventually becoming Jun.A's mainstay the circle's initial run would only last until winter of the following year, after releasing 2 albums under the circle a new project called Liverne was formed which would be active through 2006. In 2008 Jun.A returned to activity with the circle and would even continue the Touhou arrangement series started with Liverne (東方幻奏祀典"Banquet" and 東方幻奏祀典2"Canon") through sound sepher.

Releases by the circle can be broadly divided into two groups; albums with guest arrangers which include releases in the Cradle and 東方幻奏祀典 series, and albums solely featuring arrangements by Jun.A which include doujin game OSTs and the sepher.jp compilation albums.


Discography
Cradle - 東方幻樂祀典
Release date: 2004-08-15 (Comiket 66)
A 2 disc album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night. It is notable for featuring guest arrangements by a number of different circles even by the circle's own standard and for the 2 tracks which ZUN himself arranged for the album.

Rebirth
Release date: 2004-12-30 (Comiket 76)
A mini album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland ,Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream and Lotus Land Story. Unlike much of the circle's early discography the mini album doesn't features guest arrangements.

Cradle2
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object. The first album released by sound sepher after a 4 year hiatus, it is a follow up to the album Cradle - 東方幻樂祀典.

東方幻奏祀典3"Deary"
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night, Immaterial and Missing Power, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Touhou Hisoutensoku, Undefined Fantastic Object, Changeability of Strange Dream and Ghostly Field Club. It is a continuation of the series Jun.A started with a circle called Liverne, the previous 2 albums in the series are 東方幻奏祀典"Banquet" and 東方幻奏祀典2"Canon".

東方幻奏祀典4"Excess"
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler.

東方幻奏祀典5"Fable"
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Strange and Bright Nature Deity and Silent Sinner in Blue.

東方幻奏祀典6"Grimoire"
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler.

sepher.jp 東方アレンジコレクションI
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Magical Astronomy. It features arrangements by Jun.A previously released on albums by sound sepher, Liverne and more (guest arrangements) as well as one new arrangement.

妖々剣戟夢想 オリジナルサウンドトラック
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An OST featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Touhou Hisoutensoku and Strange and Bright Nature Deity. It is the original sound track for Youyou Kengeki Musou by あんかけスパ.

東方幻奏祀典7"Harmonia"
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Ten Desires. The album notably features fewer guest arrangements than other albums in the 東方幻奏祀典 series.

東方幻奏祀典8"Idea"
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Trojan Green Asteroid and Strange and Bright Nature Deity. The album notably features fewer guest arrangements than other albums in the 東方幻奏祀典 series.

東方幻奏祀典9"Joker"
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Magical Astronomy.

東方幻奏祀典10"Kalpa"
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Fairy Wars and Magical Astronomy.

さとりのダンジョン王国 オリジナルサウンドトラック
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An original sound track featuring instrumental compositions, it is the sound track to the Touhou doujin game さとりのダンジョン王国 by コココソフト.

sepher.jp 東方アレンジコレクションII
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Double Spoiler, Fairy Wars, Double Dealing Character, Ghostly Field Club and Changeability of Strange Dream. It features arrangements by Jun.A released in 2010 on the albums 東方幻奏祀典3"Deary", 東方幻奏祀典4"Excess" and 東方幻奏祀典5"Fable" as well as 2 new arrangements.

さとりのダンジョン王国2 OST
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An original sound track featuring instrumental compositions, it is the sound track to the Touhou doujin game さとりのダンジョン王国2 by コココソフト.

Omnia
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith as well as original compositions. The album is notably doesn't feature any guest arrangements.

Cradle Re:BOOT - 東方幻樂祀典
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
A 4 disc compilation album meant to commemorate sound sepher's 10 anniversary, it features most of the arrangements from Cradle - 東方幻樂祀典 and Cradle2 on the first 3 discs as well as several tracks from 東方幻奏祀典2"Canon" by Liverne and 10 new arrangements on the 4th disc.

sepher.jp 東方アレンジコレクションIII
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Ten Desires, Strange and Bright Nature Deity and Trojan Green Asteroid. It features arrangements by Jun.A released from 2011 to 2012 on the albums 東方幻奏祀典6"Grimoire", 東方幻奏祀典7"Harmonia" and 東方幻奏祀典8"Idea" as well as 1 new arrangement.

東方幻奏祀典11"Libretto"
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Fairy Wars, Ten Desires and Magical Astronomy. The album is notable for being the only in the 東方幻奏祀典 series to not feature guest arrangements.

sepher.jp 東方アレンジコレクションIV
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Magical Astronomy. It features arrangements by Jun.A released from 2012 to 2014 on the albums 東方幻奏祀典9"Joker", 東方幻奏祀典10"Kalpa", Omnia and others.

sepher.jp 東方アレンジコレクションV
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Strange and Bright Nature Deity. It features re-recordings of arrangements by Jun.A released in 2011 on the album 妖々剣戟夢想 オリジナルサウンドトラック as well as 3 re-arrangements.
