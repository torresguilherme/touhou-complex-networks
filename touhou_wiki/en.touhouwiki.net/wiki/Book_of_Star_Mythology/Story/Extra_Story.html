　戒められし禰津＆解き放たれしフーマを無事撃破したいつもの三人。
雨も無事に止み、いつもの夏が戻ったかのように思われた。		 Nezu the Admonished and Fuma the Unsealed were safely defeated by our usual trio.
The rain had stopped, and one would almost think that the regular summer had returned.
　フーマ「ところで、フーマは最後のスペルで72の悪魔を召喚した。

あれの何柱かがまだレメゲトンに戻っていない」

三人「「「な、なんだってー！？」」」		 Fuma: "By the way, Fuma summoned the 72 demons with that last spell.

But some of them haven't yet returned to the Lemegeton."

Trio: " " "WH-- WHAAAAT?!" " "
　封印されしソロモンの悪魔、その真の力は、たとえ一柱でも幻想郷の上位の

神々に匹敵する。（かも知れない）

彼らが解き放たれれば、本物の戦国時代が齎される。（かも知れない）		 The sealed demons of Solomon-- the true power of even one of them could far surpass even Gensokyo's highest gods. (Well, maybe.)
If they were left unsealed, a true Era of Warring States could be brought about! (Mmmmaybe.)
　さあ、彼らが完全に力を取り戻す前に、残らず探し出して捕獲せよ！
三人はフーマと共に、不穏なる感情の流れが渦巻く三途の川を遡る。		 Now, go out and capture every one of them, before they regain their full power!
The three heroes and Fuma race upstream at the Sanzu River, where disquieting emotions whirl and churn.
