魔宝城に居るフーマを倒すがよい。

Nezu admits defeat.
If you wish to resolve the incident,
defeat Fuma in the castle known as Mahoujou up ahead.

Marisa

ああそうそう、フーマは
お前を封印する腹積もりなんだっけ？

Right, that whole thing. Fuma was plannin'
to seal you away or somethin', right?

Nezu

お主にはどの道フーマを退けて貰う。
そうしなければこの結界から帰さぬ。

Whatever the case, you will eliminate Fuma for me.
Otherwise, you cannot leave through the barrier.

Marisa

フーマにお前を操る術を教わって来てやるぜ。

Easy. I'll just get Fuma to teach me the spell to control you.

Stage 6[edit]

A Demonic Wind Blown Across the Era of War

乱世に吹いた魔の風

A Demonic Wind Blown Across the Era of War [4]

魔宝城

Mahoujou (Magic Relic Castle)

風魔の尊厳たる魔宝は、光と影を自在に操る。
光も影もない虚無の中、
彼女はただ自覚の時を待っていた。

The Fuuma clan's sacred relic controls light and shadow at will.
In this empty void of neither light nor shadow,
she simply waited for the time of her awakening.

BGM:光と影の魔宝城

BGM: The Mahoujou of Light and Shadow

Nezu Akitsu ENTERS

Nezu

かかったな！
魔宝城はこの禰津が城！
よくぞここまで踏み入った物ぞ…
褒美に死をやろう！

I have you now!
This castle, the Mahoujou, belongs to Nezu!
You did well to progress this far...
As thy reward, I grant thee death!

Nezu Akitsu DEFEATED

Marisa

かつて、
人が空を飛べなかった時代があった。
人は空の星を見上げて、
叶う事の無い願いを託した。
さて、お前はそれを独り占めするのか？

Long ago,
there was an era when humans couldn't fly.
People looked up at the stars in the sky,
an' entrusted them with wishes that they couldn't imagine comin' true.
Now, do you think you can just take all of that for yourself?

???

まるで夢物語、だな。

A tale straight out of a dream, indeed.

Fuma Mishandra ENTERS

Fuma

強い者が上に立ち、弱い者は利用される。
世界はそのように出来ていた。

The strong rise above, and the weak are taken advantage of.
That's the way in which the world was made.

Marisa

夢物語で出来ているのが幻想郷だぜ。

Those dream-like tales are what Gensokyo's made of, I'l have ya know.

Fuma

私の名はフーマ。
またの名を魔法書レメゲトン。
魔法使い、君は世界に一冊しか無い
レメゲトンを分け合えるのか？

My name is Fuma.
Another name of mine is the grimoire Lemegeton.
Can you, magician, manage to share the one and only
volume of Lemegeton that exists in the world?

Marisa

いい事を聞いた。本題 (ゴエティア) 以外の
部分は誰にでも好きにくれてやる。

Now that sounds interestin'. As long as I get the main
Goetia book, I'll lend the rest t' anyone who wants it.

Fuma

力は使われなければ意味が無い。
その為にフーマは生まれて来た。
決して叶う事の無い願いも、
形を変えれば別の願いを叶えられる。

If such power cannot be used, its existence is pointless.
That is why Fuma was born.
Even a wish that can't ever come true
can grant a different wish if it just changes form.

Marisa

そうか、目立つ大三角星は囮。
周りの小さい星座を三つ繋いで
逆向きの三角形を用意する。
それを組み合わせれば…ソロモンの星。
お前は何が狙いだ？
本当に狐を封印したいだけか？

I see, now. The big Summer Triangle is a decoy.
If you connect the three smaller constellations nearby,
they'll form a similar, inverted triangle.
An' if you combine the two... you get the Star of Solomon.
What are you tryin' to do?
Do you really just want to seal that fox?

Fuma

フーマの願いは乱世の覇者となる事だった。
少なくともフーマを生んだ者の
願いはそうだった。
しかし人々の願いを集めて気付いた。
この願い、
この世界にはどうやらそぐわぬ物だ。

Fuma's wish was to become the supreme ruler of the wartime era.
At the very least, that was the wish
of the man who created Fuma.
However, in gathering the desires of the people, I realized something.
It seems that this wish is ill-fitting
for the world as it now stands.

Marisa

乱世も下克上も全部霊夢と私が潰すからな。
さあて、だからレメゲトンを寄越せ、
早く早く。

Yeah, 'cause Reimu an' I will crush any war or uprising in town.
No~ow, how 'bout you hand over the Lemegeton?
C'mon, hop to it!

BGM: 言ノ葉ノ大魔法　～　Starry Wisdom

BGM: Great Magic of the Power of Words ~ Starry Wisdom

Fuma

鍵の力は王の力。
人を超えた者にしか扱えぬ力。
霧雨の魔法使い、
君がフーマの主と、王となると言うのか？

The power of the Key is the power of the king.
A power that none but those surpassing humans can wield.
Magician of the name "Kirisame",
can you truly become Fuma's master? Become that king?

Marisa

本は水に弱い。
霧雨が本に負ける筈が無いぜ。

Books are weak to water.
There's no way someone with the name "drizzling rain" could lose to a book!

Fuma

フーマは神話を紡ぐ本。
星の神話を語り継ぎ、英雄の神話を遺す魔本。
君が新たな一頁に相応しいか、
決めるのはフーマ自身だ！

Fuma is a book that crafts legends.
A magic book that passes down legends of the stars, and bequeaths legends of heroes.
Are you worthy of a new page in that book?
Fuma herself will be the one to decide!

Fuma Mishandra DEFEATED

BGM: 風な恨みそ花の春　～　Eternal Wind

BGM: Hate not the Wind, Spring Blossoms ~ Eternal Wind

Fuma Mishandra REVIVES

Fuma

本は知識の泉、
本は先人が生きた魂の証。
万巻の書を読み、
我が魂は千里の道を知る。
戦の世が終わり、
風魔の忍びはもう居ない。
フーマとは何なのか？
72の悪魔になれぬフーマは何者なのか？
ならばフーマは名乗ろう、
72の悪魔になれなかった者の名を。
ならば風魔の秘術を以て名付けよう。
風魔の術を継ぎ、
72の悪魔を統べる者――その名を！

Books are a wellspring of knowledge.
Books are proof that our predecessors' souls live on.
Having read tens of thousands of texts,
my soul has known a journey of a thousand miles.
The era of war has ended,
and the Fuuma clan's shinobi no longer exist.
Just who is Fuma?
Just what am I, being unable to embody the 72 demons?
In that event, Fuma shall take that name--
The name of the one who couldn't join those 72.
In that event, allow me to name myself with the Fuuma clan's arcanum.
The inheritor of the Fuuma's spells,
and the one who commands the 72 demons-- thus do I dub myself...!

Fuma Mishandra DEFEATED

If player has continued or has not faced Fuma's Last Spell
