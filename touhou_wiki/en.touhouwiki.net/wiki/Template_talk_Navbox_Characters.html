I can't explain what I mean, but I will show it. Instead of [[Merlin Prismriver]], it is now [[|Prismriver Sisters|Merlin Prismriver]]. Wich doesn't make sense to me, since the links themselves are already linking to that specific page, plus their specific part in the article. ~ Quwanti 19:54, 5 May 2011 (UTC)

I see what you mean. Merlin Prismriver is a redirect page to the Prismriver page. If it was to use this instead of [[Prismriver Sisters|Merlin Prismriver]], the navbox wouldn't show "Merlin Prismriver" in bold and stay as a link to it's redirect page, and that would just be useless. For instance, all characters who appear on the Nameless Characters page have "[[Nameless Characters|" before the name. You click on the Five Magic Stones, but then you might want to then go to the Mysterious Ball of Light (don't know why) and you didn't know it was on the same page. You'll see in the navbox that these two names are bold, meaning that both these characters are on the same page (I don't know, but this may have been a bad example, as I can't explain more clearly...). ～ Tony64 (Talk/Con.) 21:18, 5 May 2011 (UTC)
