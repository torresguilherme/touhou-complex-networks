Touhou Doumeiju (Life-Guiding Tree) ~ Mystical Power Plant (東方導命樹 ～ Mystical Power Plant) is a fan-made Touhou Project script for the Touhou Danmakufu ph3 shooting game engine, programmed by ido (known for his previous fangame, The Last Comer). The demo version was released on March 15, 2013, and the full version was released on June 28, 2013. Version 1.00a was later released on August 24, 2013 featuring the Extra stage. As of 2019, the game is also available from the website Freem!.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

The main gameplay mechanic of Mystical Power Plant is based around collecting leaf items to fill a gauge, which causes the player to 'level up' and gain various benefits as the game proceeds.

Story[edit]

Main articles: Story and Characters

Written by Oyasu Milka, the story centers around a giant tree that Kanako Yasaka has brought to the Youkai Mountain from the outside world. The main theme of the story is Sugawara no Michizane, a famous scholar and poet who came to be revered as a god after his death.

Music[edit]

Main article: Music

Mystical Power Plant features original music in the style of ZUN, composed by Wanwan.
