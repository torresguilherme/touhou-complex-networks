Old Testament Tavern ~ Dateless Bar "Old Adam" (旧約酒場　～ Dateless Bar "Old Adam"., Kyuuyaku Sakaba ~ Dateless Bar "Old Adam") is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90). It is the ninth volume of "ZUN's Music Collection". The CD contains five original compositions by ZUN, as well as five rearranged tracks from earlier games. It is meant to be a continuation of ZUN's previous album, Dr. Latency's Freak Report.

The CD was announced on July 22, 2016, on ZUN's blog, Invisible Games and Japanese. It was sold at Comiket 90 for 500 yen.
