Discography
東方霊譚曲
Release date: 2007-12-21 (Comiket 73)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

地平線のむこう側へ
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

東方友宴歌
Release date: 2009-06-07 (Comic Communication 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.

Through the Butterfly
Release date: 2011-05-08 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from featuring arranges from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.
