Touhou Magical Girl: Ultimate☆Reimu (東方魔法少女 アルティメット☆れいむ, Touhou Mahou Shoujo Arutimatto☆Reimu) is a doujin album by とらのあな released on 2012-12-29 (Comiket 83). It's a package containing 3 CDs of Touhou arrangements, an artbook showcasing various artists drawing Touhou characters and a small comic book.
