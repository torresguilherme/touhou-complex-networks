Discography
東方夢世代
Release date: 2007-05-20 (Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes form Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

東方新世代 ～河童のにとりと夏休み～
Release date: 2007-08-17 (Comiket 72)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

東方旧世代 ～東方クラシックス～
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

Progressive Overdrive
Release date: 2008-08-16 (Comiket 74)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

Countdown to Distortion
Release date: 2009
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, an alternate version titled "Countdown to Distotion ～One Years old～" would be released the following year.

The Symphony of the Imperishable Night ～やごころを、君に～
Release date: 2009-07-19 (Touhou Suukeisai 2)
An album featuring an instrumental arrangement of themes from Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

Baroque Stroke ～東方クラシックス２～
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

Touhou Legendary Pack
Release date: 2010-04-??
An compilation album featuring the circle's first first 3 albums 東方夢世代, 東方新世代 ～河童のにとりと夏休み～ and 東方旧世代 ～東方クラシックス～.

Countdown to Distotion ～One Years old～
Release date: 2010-05-05 (M3-25)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism.

龍神革命
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring image songs for 東方紅龍奇伝 released by ドラゴンズ・ドリーム.

東方クラシックス３ 未完の少女達
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Subterranean Animism. The album also mixed Touhou themes with with pieces of classical music.

ベリーを踏んだらシェスタにしよう
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

雪かきは、お昼寝のあとで
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom.

お月様はリビング
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.

まだお昼休みではないはずですよね？
Release date: 2015-08-16 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

連休明けのシャッターチャンス
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet.

信仰と眠気の狭間
Release date: 2016-08-14 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

東方ボーカルアレンジアルバム サード・インパクト
Release date: 2016-12-30 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.
