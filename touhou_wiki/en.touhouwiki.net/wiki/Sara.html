Sara (サラ) is a being residing in the mountain cave that houses the gateway into Makai. During the events of Mystic Square, Sara attempted to prevent the heroine from entering Makai, referring to Reimu Hakurei as "prey". Because she is officially the gatekeeper of the door to Makai, it was her job to keep the heroines out. However, she was easily defeated.

Character Basis[edit]

Name[edit]

Her name is Sara (サラ), which is named after Sara Brownie from Izumi Takemoto's story The Crystal Egg (水晶の卵, Suishoo no tamago) from the compilation Niji-iro♪Bakuhatsu Musume♥ (虹色♪爆発娘♥).

Design[edit]

Sara's design shows that she has purple eyes, purple hair, and wears a red dress with a white scarf and white sleeves.

Story[edit]

Mystic Square
Main article: Mystic Square Story

Sara tries to prevent the protagonists from entering Makai, but is defeated shortly after.

Relationships[edit]

Shinki

Shinki is the creator of Makai and everything within it. In light of this, Shinki may also have created Sara and she would be Shinki's gatekeeper.

Additional Information[edit]

  • Despite being Makai's official gatekeeper, she is a lower stage boss than Louise, who is only an average resident of Makai.
  • One of her attacks resembles Rumia's Moon Sign "Moonlight Ray".
