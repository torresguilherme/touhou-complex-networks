Cradle - Touhou Gengaku Shiten (Cradle -東方幻樂祀典) is a doujin album by sound sepher released on 2004-08-15 (Comiket 66). It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.
