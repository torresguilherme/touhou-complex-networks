MASTER BURNER is pretty much is SEGA's old arcade game After Burner but starring Marisa Kirisame instead.

Gameplay[edit]

A 3rd person 2.5D game in 3D, this game mimics SEGA's After Burner quite well with Marisa as the main star. Flying into the screen, you blast fairies and other foes with your "vulcan" and lockable Christmas trees, and every few stages you meet a boss.
