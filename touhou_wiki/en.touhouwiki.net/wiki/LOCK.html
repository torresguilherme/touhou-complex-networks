LOCK is a doujin album by monochrome-coat released on 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14). It exclusively features vocal arrangements sung by めらみぽっぷ of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Magical Astronomy, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, and Dr. Latency's Freak Report.
