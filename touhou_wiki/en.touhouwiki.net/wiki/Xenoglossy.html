Discography
ParaBox
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Ten Desires and Magical Astronomy.

distorted incident
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Fairy Wars and Seihou Project's Shuusou Gyoku.

chromatograph
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

ELECTRET
Release date: 2013-10-13 (Touhou Kouroumu 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Magical Astronomy.

Bracing Sky Blue
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.
