Japanese store with no English version[edit]

I've recently found a store (www.bk1.jp) which sells quite a few of the print works (though I wasn't able to tell exactly which ones, since I can't read kanji and not all of them have images) at good prices (the better I've seen so far, but that may vary WILDLY depending on country and exchange rates) and many shipping options/fees.

Problem is, the site is entirely in Japanese. With some patience (and a little help from GoogleTranslator) I was able to find pretty much everything I wanted, including info on exports etc, without any real trouble (it's worth pointing that my knowledge of Japanese goes little beyond "arigatou" and "baka"). But this may cause problems for some, for example, when writing destination adresses on the order form or responding to eventual e-mails (since they'll likely be in Japanese).

So, should this store be added to the wiki? Perhaps with some warnings, or maybe a little tutorial on the ordering process? -- Peroim
