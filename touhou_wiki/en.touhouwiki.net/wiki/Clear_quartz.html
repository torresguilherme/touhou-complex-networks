Discography
東方響乱樂
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Shuusou Gyoku.

東方響乱樂 弐式
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Undefined Fantastic Object and Magical Astronomy.

東方響乱樂 参式
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

東方響乱樂 四式
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

東方響乱樂 四式半
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Perfect Cherry Blossom.

東方響乱樂 五式
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Dealing Character.

星条旗のピエロ / 私はあなたの使い魔だから
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

東方響乱樂 六式
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

東方響乱樂 七式
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.

東方響乱樂 八式
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Hidden Star in Four Seasons and Forbidden Scrollery (CD).

東方響乱樂7.5式
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.
