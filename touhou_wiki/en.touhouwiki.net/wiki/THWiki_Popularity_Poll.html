The Touhou Popularity Poll (東方シリーズ人気投票, Touhou Shiriizu Ninki Touhyou) is an annual survey conducted by the Japanese Touhou Wiki since 2003, with the exception of 2007 and 2013. An English guide on how to vote can be found here.

Usually held each February, it consists of Character, Music, Voter Questionnaire, and occasionally Spell Card categories. As the most inclusive in voter choice and with a high turnout each year, even allowing overseas fans, it may be the leading Touhou ranking of any sort. Previously, the Seihou Project was part of the survey, but currently the focus has been narrowed down to creations of Team Shanghai Alice. Thus, the games and characters of Seihou are not included, but the music created for the series by ZUN is. Included here are the results of every poll as they appear on the Japanese site.
