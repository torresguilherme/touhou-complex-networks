Discography
Fantasía de Conciertos
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An instrumental album featuring arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Subterranean Animism, and Magical Astronomy. It is a collaboration work with Music Advance.

Colors*
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, and Subterranean Animism. It is a collaboration work with NIZI-RINGO.

Artificial Flowers
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power, Shoot the Bullet, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, and Subterranean Animism, as well as an original intro.

the Logic of Alice
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism, and Undefined Fantastic Object, as well as an original intro.

ひまわりはじめ
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story and Phantasmagoria of Flower View, as well as an original intro.

Dear Dead Doll
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, as well as an original intro.

Sanctuary
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, and Perfect Cherry Blossom.

斥状回輝線
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Magical Astronomy, as well as one original composition.

斥量零度点
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid, and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

March of Dolls
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom, and Touhou Hisoutensoku. It is a doll-themed album, with all of the tracks being arrangements of Alice Margatroid's themes or 人形のある風景 (The Scenery of Living Dolls).

RE:code
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Magical Astronomy and Trojan Green Asteroid.

二行の詩 -Parallel Line-
Release date: 2015-11-01 (Touhou Kouroumu 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Mountain of Faith. It is a collaboration work with Crest.

霍香の雛が見せた夢
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

BLIND BLUE
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Imperishable Night, and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

スイセイトリップ
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Neo-traditionalism of Japan, and Dr. Latency's Freak Report .

ハピネスパレエド
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Neo-traditionalism of Japan and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".

Time LaPlace
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from, Neo-traditionalism of Japan, Dr. Latency's Freak Report , and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".

Once Upon A Time -the Best of Applice-
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.
