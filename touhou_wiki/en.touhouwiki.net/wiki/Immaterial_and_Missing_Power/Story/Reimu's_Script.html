Reimu beats Marisa[edit]

Reimu

という訳で、今日も食事の用意はお願いね。

In that case, the food's on you today.[1]

Reimu beats Sakuya[edit]

Reimu

という訳で、お茶の用意でもしてってよ。

Well, go get some tea.

Reimu beats Alice[edit]

Reimu

人形って言えば、この間、神社の裏の木に打ち付け
てあったけど、あれもあんたのかしら？

Speaking of dolls, there were some nailed to the trees
behind the shrine the other day. Was that your doing?[2]

Reimu beats Patchouli[edit]

Reimu

私の前では火遊びは止めることね。
火では私には勝てないもの。

Don't play with fire in front of me.
You can't beat me with fire.

Reimu beats Youmu[edit]

Reimu

あんまり、斬って歩くんじゃないわよ。
物騒だし。

Don't run and cut at the same time.
It's dangerous.

Reimu beats Remilia[edit]

Reimu

そう言えば、最近見かけなかったわね。
梅雨だったから？

Speaking of which, I haven't seen you in a while.
Is it monsoon season?

Reimu beats Yuyuko[edit]

Reimu

巫女が亡霊に負けるようになったらお終いだから。

If a shrine maiden lost to a ghost, then it'd be all over.

Reimu beats Yukari[edit]

Reimu

あんたは、いっつも邪魔な時に出てきて……
邪魔な時に居ないんだから。

You always show up at the worst times...
since you're not around at the worst times.

Reimu beats Suika[edit]

Reimu

その鎖は……
ただの飾りかしら？

Are those chains...
just decorations?

Reimu

その瓢箪……

いつも腰につけていて重たくない？
中身入ってるんでしょう？

That gourd...

Isn't it heavy, hanging around your waist all the time?
There's stuff inside, right?

Reimu beats someone[edit]

Reimu

また、私の勝ちね。

I win, again.

Reimu

まぁ、再戦はお茶でも飲んだ後にしましょ。

Well, we'll fight after we have tea.

Reimu

あれ？居たの？

Wait... you were here?

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ You can find that Reimu and Marisa is fighting regularly to push the labor of food preparation to one another in chapter 3 of Curiosities of Lotus Asia.
  2. ↑ In her article of Bohemian Archive in Japanese Red, Alice nailed a straw dool at Hakurei Shrine. To accept the date printed on the newspaper, look at the time line; this aggressive action of her had been made before this dialogue of Reimu with her.
