Yukari beats Reimu[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
鉄下駄かしら？　体が宙に浮かない為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing iron sandals?
So you don't float away.

Yukari beats Marisa[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
コルク栓かしら？　魔法がだだ漏れしない為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing a cork?
So magic doesn't leak out.

Yukari beats Sakuya[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
槌かしらね？　石橋を叩く為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing a hammer?
To strike stone bridges.[1]

Yukari beats Alice[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
付属品かしら？　妖怪らしくなる為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing accessories?
To look more youkai-like.

Yukari beats Patchouli[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
ビタミンかしら？　健康になる為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing vitamins?
So you can become healthy.

Yukari beats Youmu[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
遊びかしら？　切れ味が良くなる為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing fun?
In order to become sharper.

Yukari beats Remilia[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
日光浴かしら？　色白にならない為の。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing a sunbath?
So you won't be as pale.

Yukari beats Yuyuko[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
って、幽々子には言わなくても判るわよね。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing--
Wait, you should know that even if I don't tell you.

Yukari beats Suika[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
カルシウムかしら？　もっと背を伸ばすための。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing calcium?
So you can grow taller.

Yukari beats Meiling[edit]

Yukari

そうねぇ……貴方に足りないものは、
透明な水かしら？　赤色を薄めるための。

Ah... Maybe you're missing missing clear water?
To get the red out.

Yukari beats someone[edit]

Yukari

撃つ、斬る、衝く、放つ、殺す……
何を取っても私には効きません。

Shooting, cutting, stabbing, hitting, killing...
It's all useless against me.

Yukari

貴方だけにこっそり教えてあげましょう。
私に弱点は……何にも無いのですよ。

I'll tell you a secret.
...I don't have any weaknesses.

Yukari

幻想郷は全てを受け入れるのよ。
それはそれは残酷な話ですわ。

Gensokyo accepts everything.
And that's a cruel, cruel thing.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Japanese idiom "looking before you leap", lit. "knocking on a strong stone bridge with the hammer before crossing it" (石橋を叩いて渡る, ishibashi wo wataru).
