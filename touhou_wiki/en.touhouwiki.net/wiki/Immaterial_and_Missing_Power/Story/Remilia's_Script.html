Immaterial and Missing Power/Story/Remilia's Script

Remilia beats Reimu[edit]

Remilia

お経？祝詞？真言？よく分からないけど、
ただ喋ってるだけでは何も起きないよ。

Sutras? Prayers? Mantras? I don't really understand,
but it's just idle chatter.

Remilia beats Marisa[edit]

Remilia

非力だねぇ……人間って奴は。
可哀相になってくるよ。

Humans are so... weak.
I'm beginning to pity you.

Remilia beats Sakuya[edit]

Remilia

咲夜には色々仕事を頼んであった気がしたけど……

I feel like I've asked you to do many things, but...

Remilia beats Alice[edit]

Remilia

その人形……

全部紐で操ってるの？
ほんとうに？今度教えてよぉ。

Those dolls...

They're all controlled by strings?
Really? Teach me how next time.

Remilia beats Patchouli[edit]

Remilia

やっぱり運動不足じゃない？
そんなんじゃあ太るよ。

You really don't exercise much, do you?
You're going to get fat.

Remilia beats Meiling[edit]

Remilia

で、門番がこんなところにいる理由は
どう説明してくれるのかしら？

Can you please explain why a doorguard
is in a place like this?

Remilia beats someone[edit]

Remilia

弱すぎて退屈だよ。

You're too weak, how boring.

Remilia

本気の１０分の１ね。
今のは。

I only used 1/10th of my strength.
Just now.

Remilia

無駄無駄無駄ぁ。
お前に勝ち目を用意する暇も無かったわ。

Useless, useless, useless.[1]
I don't have time to let you win.


References[edit]

  1. ↑ Since Remilia is a vampire, this is likely to be a reference to Dio's battle cry (from JoJo's Bizarre Adventure) who is also a vampire.
