その剣に教え込ませるといいわ！

The power of oni, power to gather, and power to disperse,
to think that you could cut something like that...

It would be good to train you in that sword!

Final Battle

Suika DEFEATED

''Ending No. 06 (Youmu Good Ending)
Doesn't matter if you continued as long as you win.''

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Reference to Reimu's ability of flying the sky. And the kanji "空" can be read as both "sky" (sora) and "nothingness" (kuu).
  2. ↑ The expression くわばらくわばら (kuwabara kuwabara) is apparently something you say to ward off thunder.
  3. ↑ ○すべて (○ for all) is an attribute, which's seen in the video game "Saga" and its spinoff. They were presented by Square (today's Square Enix) for Gameboy console. It means resist for all actions
  4. ↑ Youmu did cause a lot of trouble in Perfect Cherry Blossom.
  5. ↑ All hear and live the wise teachings in the Book of Slash. Amen.
  6. ↑ 鬼が島 (Onigashima) is an island where many oni live. It's the destination of Momotaro].
