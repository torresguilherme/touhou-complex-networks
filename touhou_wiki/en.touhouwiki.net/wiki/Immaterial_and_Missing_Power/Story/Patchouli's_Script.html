Patchouli beats Reimu[edit]

Patchouli

ちゃんと準備してきてあるんだから。
貴方に勝つには……ワサビが良いわね。

I was prepared.
To beat you... wasabi's good.[1]

Patchouli beats Marisa[edit]

Patchouli

ちゃんと準備してきてあるんだから。
貴方に勝つには……ウナギ。

I was prepared.

To beat you... use eel.[2]

Patchouli beats Sakuya[edit]

Patchouli

ちゃんと準備してきてあるんだから。
貴方に勝つには……高麗人参。

I was prepared.
To beat you... use Asian ginseng.[3]

Patchouli beats Alice[edit]

Patchouli

ちゃんと準備してきてあるんだから。
貴方に勝つには……唐辛子かしら？

I was prepared.
To beat you... use chili peppers, I guess?[4]

Patchouli beats Youmu[edit]

Patchouli

ちゃんと準備してきてあるんだから。
貴方に勝つには……ゴーヤ。咲夜と同じね。

I was prepared.
To beat you... use bitter melons. Just like Sakuya.[5]

Patchouli beats Remilia[edit]

Patchouli

流石に、レミィの方が弱点が多いんだから……
不意打ちじゃないと私には勝てないでしょ？

As expected, you have lots of weaknesses...
If you don't catch me by surprise, you know you can't win, right?

Patchouli beats Yuyuko[edit]

Patchouli

アンデッドは火に弱い。
弱点剥き出しね。

The undead are weak against fire.
I've exposed your weak point.

Patchouli beats Yukari[edit]

Patchouli

貴方は……貴方を意味する季節から考えて、
梅干し辺りなんかに弱そうね。

You... Judging from the seasons you're active in,
you're probably weak against dried plums.[6]

Patchouli beats Suika[edit]

Patchouli

今日は、炒った大豆を持ってきたわ。
勝負にならないよ。

I brought some fried beans.
There's no way you can win.

Patchouli beats Meiling[edit]

Patchouli

図書館を荒らすネズミが最近多いけど
本当うちの猫はザルばっかりねぇ…

There have been lots of mice in the library recently,
but it seems our cats are just sieves...

Patchouli beats someone[edit]

Patchouli

弱点さえ見つければ、どんな者にも勝てるわ。

If you can find their weakness, you can beat anyone.

Patchouli

物には必ず属性がある。
属性は弱点と同じなの。

All things have their attributes.
Attributes and weaknesses are the same.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Wasabi or "spiciness" is metal in Wu Xing. Reimu's head is "spring" and she is "east," which are wood. The Hakurei Shrine is on the eastern extremity of Gensokyo. Metal overcomes wood (metal is strong against wood).
  2. ↑ Eel is of day of the ox in midsummer (土用の丑の日) and is therefore earth. Marisa is "black" and lives in the place where sun (yang) does not shine, has the surname "Kirisame," which are water. Earth overcomes water (earth is strong against water).
  3. ↑ "Bitter" taste is fire. Sakuya's knives are metal. Fire overcomes metal (fire is strong against metal).
  4. ↑ "Spicy" taste is metal. Color (or sense of sight) is wood. Alice is the seven-colored magician. Metal overcomes wood (metal is strong against wood.
  5. ↑ "Bitter" taste is fire. Swords are metal. Sakuya also uses metal knives. Fire overcomes metal (fire is strong against metal).
  6. ↑ "Sour" taste is wood. The border of seasons is earth. Wood overcomes earth (wood is strong against earth).
