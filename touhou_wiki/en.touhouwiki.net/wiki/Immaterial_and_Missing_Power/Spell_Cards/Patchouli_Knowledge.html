Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「セントエルモエクスプロージョン」 (Fu no Ichi "Sento Erumo Ekusupuroojon")
First Card "St. Elmo's Explosion"[1]
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Marisa Stage 3, Alice Stage 4, Youmu Stage 3, Suika Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Patchouli throws a fireball toward the player, which becomes a pillar of flame on impact.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「デリュージュフォーティデイ」 (Fu no Ni "Deryuuju Footi Dei")
Second Card "Deluge Forty Days"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Marisa Stage 3, Alice Stage 4, Youmu Stage 3, Suika Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Patchouli sprays water droplets forward. Bubbles also float in from each side of the screen, bursting into a splash of water when the player moves below them.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
金土符「ジンジャガスト」 (Kin-do-fu "Jinja Gasuto")
Metal Earth Sign "Ginger Gust"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Marisa Stage 3, Alice Stage 4, Youmu Stage 3, Suika Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Patchouli leaps into the air, pushing the player backward with wind and summoning tornadoes that drift forward.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
火金符「セントエルモピラー」 (Ka-kin-fu "Sento Erumo Piraa")
Fire Metal Sign "Saint Elmo's Pillar"[1]
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Throws a fireball towards the opponent, which becomes a pillar of flame on impact.
Spell Card A-2
日符「ロイヤルフレア」 (Nichifu "Roiyaru Furea")
Sun Sign "Royal Flare"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Covers the entire screen in a huge explosion.
Spell Card B-1
土水符「ノエキアンデリュージュ」 (Do-sui-fu "Noekian Deryuuju")
Earth Water Sign "Noachian Deluge"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Fires a stream of water droplets forward.
Spell Card B-2
月符「サイレントセレナ」 (Getsufu "Sairento Serena")
Moon Sign "Silent Selene"[2]
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Streams of vertical lasers shoot up from the ground around Patchouli.
Spell Card C-1
金木符「エレメンタルハーベスタ」 (Kin-moku-fu "Erementaru Haabesuta")
Metal Wood Sign "Elemental Harvester"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Patchouli jumps forward, surrounded by rotating sawblades.
Spell Card C-2
火水木金土符「賢者の石」 (Ka-sui-moku-kin-do-fu "Kenja no Ishi")
Fire Water Wood Metal Earth Sign "Philosopher's Stone"
Owner
Patchouli Knowledge
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Summons five colored crystals. Upon using one of Patchouli's special moves, the crystal corresponding to the move's element will break and shoot additional projectiles at the opponent.
