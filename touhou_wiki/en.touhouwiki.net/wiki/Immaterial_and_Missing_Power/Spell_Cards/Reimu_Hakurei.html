Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「夢想妙珠連」 (Fu no Ichi "Musou Myoujuren")
First Card "Fantasy Orb String"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Marisa Stage 5, Sakuya Stage 1, Alice Stage 2, Youmu Stage 1, Remilia Stage 3, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 5, Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Fires a series of multicolored orbs towards the player.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「陰陽散華」 (Fu no Ni "Onmyou Sange")
Second Card "Yin-Yang Scattering"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Marisa Stage 5, Sakuya Stage 1, Alice Stage 2, Youmu Stage 1, Remilia Stage 3, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 5, Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Releases a line of yin-yang orbs that bounce off the ground.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
符の参「魔浄閃結」 (Fu no San "Majousenketsu")
Third Card "Exorcising Border"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Marisa Stage 5, Remilia Stage 3, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 5, Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Creates pairs of light pillars extending up from the ground, which spread outward from Reimu.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
力符「陰陽玉将」 (Ryokufu "Onmyou Gyokushou")
Power Sign "Yin-Yang King Piece" [1]
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Marisa Stage 5, Yukari Stage 5, Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Creates a large orb that drifts forward. The orb's size increases with the difficulty level.
Spell Card Story Mode 5
夢戦「幻想之月」 (Musen "Gensou no Tsuki")
Dream Battle "Illusionary Moon"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
A ring of orbs surrounding Reimu fires a large number of amulets that stop, then drift forward again.
Spell Card Story Mode 6
無題「空を飛ぶ不思議な巫女」 (Mudai "Sora wo Tobu Fushigi na Miko")
Untitled "Mysterious Flying Shrine Maiden"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Suika Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Reimu floats in the center of the arena while firing rings of amulets. Several orbs orbiting around her also fire streams of amulets.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
霊符「夢想妙珠」 (Reifu "Musou Myouju")
Spirit Sign "Fantasy Orb"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Fires a series of multicolored orbs towards the opponent.
Spell Card A-2
神霊「夢想封印」 (Shinrei "Musou Fuuin")
Divine Spirit "Fantasy Seal"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Fires a series of multicolored orbs towards the opponent.
Spell Card B-1
夢符「封魔陣」 (Mufu "Fuumajin")
Dream Sign "Evil-Sealing Circle"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Reimu places her hand on the ground and summons a pillar of light around herself.
Spell Card B-2
神技「八方鬼縛陣」 (Kamiwaza "Happou Kibakujin")
Divine Arts "Omnidirectional Oni-Binding Circle"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Reimu places her hand on the ground and summons a wide pillar of light around herself.
Spell Card C-1
宝符「陰陽宝玉」 (Houfu "Onmyou Hougyoku")
Treasure Sign "Yin-Yang Treasured Orb"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Creates a medium-sized orb directly in front of Reimu.
Spell Card C-2
宝具「陰陽鬼神玉」 (Hougu "Onmyou Kishingyoku")
Holy Relic "Yin-Yang Kishin Orb"
Owner
Reimu Hakurei
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Creates a large orb in front of Reimu, then launches it across the screen.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ A portmanteau of onmyou-gyoku ('yin-yang orb') and gyoku-shou (the king piece in shogi).
