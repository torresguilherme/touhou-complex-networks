Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「四重結界」 (Fu no Ichi "Shijuu Kekkai")
First Card "Quadruple Barrier"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Reimu Stage 6, Marisa Stage 6, Sakuya Stage 6, Alice Stage 6, Patchouli Stage 6, Youmu Stage 6, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Yukari floats in midair while surrounded by two square barriers. The sides of each square intermittently release bullets in straight lines.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「八雲卍傘」 (Fu no Ni "Yakumo Bangasa")
Second Card "Manji-Parasol of Yakumo" [1]
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Reimu Stage 6, Marisa Stage 6, Sakuya Stage 6, Alice Stage 6, Patchouli Stage 6, Youmu Stage 6, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Yukari spins her parasol and either releases streams of bullets, or dashes forward while surrounded by laser blades. On Normal and up, she fires the blades towards the player after leaping into the air.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
符の参「八雲藍」 (Fu no San "Yakumo Ran")
Third Card "Ran Yakumo"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Reimu Stage 6, Marisa Stage 6, Sakuya Stage 6, Alice Stage 6, Patchouli Stage 6, Youmu Stage 6, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Yukari summons Ran, who cycles between dashing towards the player, stopping in place, then dashing off the screen. On Normal and up, Yukari spins her parasol in midair and releases streams of bullets. On Hard and up, Yukari also summons Chen; on Lunatic, Chen dashes straight across the screen without pausing.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
外力「無限の超高速飛行体」 (Gairyoku "Mugen no Choukousoku Hikoutai")
Outer Force "Infinite Superspeed Flying Object"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Reimu Stage 6, Marisa Stage 6, Sakuya Stage 6, Alice Stage 6, Patchouli Stage 6, Youmu Stage 6, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Sets of horizontal lasers appear with a sound effect, then silently expand. The expanded lasers can't be blocked or grazed.
Spell Card Story Mode 5
幻想「第一種永久機関」 (Gensou "Dai'isshu Towa Kikan")
Fantasy "Perpetual Motion Machine of the First Kind"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Reimu Stage 6, Marisa Stage 6, Sakuya Stage 6, Alice Stage 6, Patchouli Stage 6, Youmu Stage 6, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 6
Editor's Comment
The player is surrounded by a ring of orbs, which fire bullets in all directions outside of the ring. The orbs intermittently release bullets towards the player.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
境符「四重結界」 (Kyoufuu "Shijuu Kekkai")
Border Sign "Quadruple Barrier"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Creates a damaging field directly in front of Yukari.
Spell Card A-2
結界「魅力的な四重結界」 (Kekkai "Miryokuteki na Shijuu Kekkai")
Barrier "Charming Quadruple Barrier"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Creates a damaging field directly in front of Yukari, which draws the opponent toward it.
Spell Card B-1
式神「八雲藍」 (Shikigami "Yakumo Ran")
Shikigami "Ran Yakumo"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Fires Ran towards the opponent. If the hit connects, Ran will spin in place to attack.

Spell Card B-2
式神「八雲藍＋」 (Shikigami "Yakumo Ran Purasu")
Shikigami "Ran Yakumo +" (Forward)
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
The 236 version fires Ran towards the opponent. The 214 version summons Chen, who bounces along the ground.

Spell Card C-1
境符「二次元と三次元の境界」 (Kyoufu "Nijigen to Sanjigen no Kyoukai")
Border Sign "Boundary of 2D and 3D" (Forward)
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Creates a flame that crawls along the edges of the screen. 236 fires it forwards, 214 fires it backwards.
Spell Card C-2
結界「客観結界」 (Kekkai "Kyakkan Kekkai")
Barrier "Objective Barrier"
Owner
Yukari Yakumo
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Creates two flames that crawl along the edges of the screen, both forwards and backwards.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 卍傘: a pun on 番傘 (traditional Japanese umbrella). Both are pronounced 'bangasa'.
