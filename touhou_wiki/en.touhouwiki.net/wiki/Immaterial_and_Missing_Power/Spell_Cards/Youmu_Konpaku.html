Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「二重の苦輪」 (Fu no Ichi "Futae no Kurin")
First Card "Double Wheel of Pain" [1]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Reimu Stage 3, Sakuya Stage 4, Alice Stage 5, Patchouli Stage 4, Remilia Stage 4, Yuyuko Stage 1, Yukari Stage 3, Suika Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Youmu's ghost half turns into a clone of Youmu (two on Hard and Lunatic) which copies her movements after a slight delay. She also frequently uses her 623 special, swinging her sword in a circle around herself.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「心眼迷想斬」 (Fu no Ni "Shingan Meisouzan")
Second Card "Slash of Delusional Inner Eye"
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Reimu Stage 3, Sakuya Stage 4, Alice Stage 5, Patchouli Stage 4, Remilia Stage 4, Yuyuko Stage 1, Yukari Stage 3, Suika Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Youmu swings her sword down, creating a large shockwave in its wake. The size increases with the difficulty level.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
符の参「業風神閃斬」 (Fu no San "Goufuu Shinsenzan")
Third Card "Sudden Divine Severing of Karmic Wind" [2]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Reimu Stage 3, Sakuya Stage 4, Alice Stage 5, Patchouli Stage 4, Remilia Stage 4, Yukari Stage 3, Suika Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Youmu's ghost half floats overhead and releases bubble bullets. After standing in place for a moment, Youmu performs a fast slash along the ground; the bubbles break into smaller bullets and drift away in the direction of Youmu's slash.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
奥義「西行春風斬」 (Ougi "Saigyou Shunpuuzan")
Arcanum "Slash of Saigyou Spring Wind"
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Alice Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Youmu rapidly dashes back and forth, creating a pink shockwave that crawls along the ground with each dash. The number of dashes increases with the difficulty level.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
人符「現世斬」 (Jinfu "Genseizan")
Human Sign "Slash of Present World"
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Youmu dashes forward very quickly, slashing low to the ground.
Spell Card A-2
人鬼「未来永劫斬」 (Jinki "Mirai Eigouzan")
Human Oni "Slash of the Eternal Future"
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Youmu dashes forward very quickly, slashing low to the ground. If the hit connects, the opponent is juggled into the air while Youmu slashes wildly around them.
Spell Card B-1
断命剣「冥想斬」 (Danmyouken "Meisouzan")
Life-Cutting Sword "Slash of Nether Meditation" [3]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Youmu swings her sword down, creating a medium-sized shockwave in its wake.
Spell Card B-2
断迷剣「迷津慈航斬」 (Danmeiken "Meishin Jikouzan")
Hesitation-Cutting Sword "Slash of Departure from Hesitation" [4]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Youmu swings her sword down, creating a large shockwave in its wake.
Spell Card C-1
魂符「幽明の苦輪」 (Konfu "Yuumei no Kurin")
Soul Sign "Wheel of Pain of the Living and Dead" [5]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Youmu's ghost half turns into a clone of her for a brief period of time, copying her movements after a slight delay.
Spell Card C-2
魂魄「幽明求聞持聡明の法」 (Konpaku "Yuumei Gumonji Soumei no Hou")
Konpaku "Dharma of Gumonji Wisdom of the Living and Dead" [6]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Youmu's ghost half turns into a clone of her for a brief period of time, copying her movements after a slight delay.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 苦輪: A phrase referring to the cycle of pain that reincarnating beings are trapped in.
  2. ↑ One of Youmu's spell cards from PCB.
  3. ↑ 冥想 (meisou): a pun on 瞑想 (meisou, 'meditation') and 冥界 (meikai, 'Netherworld').
  4. ↑ 迷津慈航 ("departure from hesitation"): the name of the boat that ferries souls to Higan from the world of the unenlightened.
  5. ↑ 幽明: A phrase referring to the world of the living and the world of the dead.
  6. ↑ "Gumonji" (求聞持) is to be able to definitely not forget something seen or heard once, said to be able to acquire infinite wisdom. The founder of Shingon Buddhism, Kobo-Daishi, comprehended this at the age of 19 and gained an enlightenment. The name of Hieda no Akyuu's ability of not forgetting is also called "Gumonji."
