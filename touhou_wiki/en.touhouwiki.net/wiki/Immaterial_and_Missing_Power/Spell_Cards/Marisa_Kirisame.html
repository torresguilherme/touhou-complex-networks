Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「スターダストレヴァリエ」 (Fu no Ichi "Sutaadasuto Revarie")
First Card "Stardust Reverie"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Reimu Stage 1, Sakuya Stage 3, Alice Stage 1, Patchouli Stage 1, Youmu Stage 2, Remilia Stage 2, Yuyuko Stage 2, Yukari Stage 4, Suika Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Marisa dashes forward on her broom, leaving stars in her wake.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「アステロイドベルト」 (Fu no Ni "Asuteroido Beruto")
Second Card "Asteroid Belt"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Reimu Stage 1, Sakuya Stage 3, Alice Stage 1, Patchouli Stage 1, Youmu Stage 2, Remilia Stage 2, Yuyuko Stage 2, Yukari Stage 4, Suika Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Marisa releases a large number of small stars from her hand.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
符の参「マスタースパーク」 (Fu no San "Masutaa Supaaku")
Third Card "Master Spark"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Sakuya Stage 3, Yukari Stage 4, Suika Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Fires a huge laser cannon straight forward.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
星符「ドラゴンメテオ」 (Seifu "Doragon Meteo")
Star Sign "Dragon Meteor"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Suika Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Marisa jumps up and off the screen, then fires a laser cannon vertically towards the ground. The laser slowly moves towards the player.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
恋符「マスタースパーク」 (Koifu[1] "Masutaa Supaaku")
Love Sign "Master Spark"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Fires a huge laser cannon straight forward.
Spell Card A-2
魔砲「ファイナルスパーク」 (Mahou "Fainaru Supaaku")
Magicannon "Final Spark"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Fires an even bigger laser cannon straight forward.
Spell Card B-1
魔符「スターダストレヴァリエ」 (Mafu "Sutaadasuto Revarie")
Magic Sign "Stardust Reverie"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Marisa dashes forward on her broom, leaving stars in her wake.
Spell Card B-2
彗星「ブレイジングスター」 (Suisei "Bureijingu Sutaa")
Comet "Blazing Star"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Marisa leaps back to the far end of the screen, then dashes forward on her broom while surrounded by a large aura.

Spell Card C-1
儀符「オーレリーズサン」 (Gifu "Ooreriizu San")
Astrologic Sign "Orreries Sun" (Forward)
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Summons four orbs that orbit Marisa. Upon activation, the 236 version spins the orbs around Marisa's immediate vicinity, while the 214 version fires the orbs forward as a ring.

Spell Card C-2
天儀「オーレリーズソーラーシステム」 (Tengi "Ooreriizu Sooraa Shisutemu")
Celestial Apparatus "Orreries Solar System" (Forward)
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Summons six orbs that orbit Marisa. Upon activation, the 236 version spins the orbs around Marisa's immediate vicinity, while the 214 version fires the orbs forward as a ring.
