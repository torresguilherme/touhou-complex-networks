Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
符の壱「投擲の天岩戸」 (Fu no Ichi "Touteki no Ama-no-Iwato")
First Card "Throwing Ama-no-Iwato" [1]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Suika hops into the air and throws a large rock at the player. On Normal and up, she'll also jump towards the player and grab them; if successful, she embeds the player in a rock that she then throws.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
符の弐「坤軸の大鬼」 (Fu no Ni "Konjiku no Daiki")
Second Card "Giant Oni of Earth's Axis"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Suika leaps up and off the edge of the screen. Following a short delay, she stomps down on the player's position while gigantic.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
符の参「追儺返しブラックホール」 (Fu no San "Tsuina-gaeshi Burakku Hooru")
Third Card "Counter-Tsuina Black Hole" [2]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Throws a small bullet, which expands into a black hole after travelling some distance. The black hole sucks in bullets from outside the screen, which gather in its center.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
鬼火「超高密度燐禍術」 (Onibi "Choukoumitsudo Rinkajutsu")
Will-o'-Wisp "Superdense Phosphorus Disaster Art" [3]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Suika punches the ground, causing several fireballs to erupt from the ground underneath the player. The fireballs bounce off the ground several times while shrinking in size.
Spell Card Story Mode 5
疎符「六里霧中」 (Sofu "Rokuri-muchuu")
Sparse Sign "Beyond All in the Fog" [4]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Suika splits into several clouds of mist, which drift around the screen and fire streams of bullets at the player before eventually recombining.
Spell Card Story Mode 6
「百万鬼夜行」 ("Hyakumankiyakou")
"Night Parade of a Million Demons" [5]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Reimu Stage 7, Marisa Stage 7, Sakuya Stage 7, Alice Stage 7, Patchouli Stage 7, Youmu Stage 7, Remilia Stage 7, Yuyuko Stage 7, Yukari Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Suika floats in the center of the screen and releases bullets. As she takes damage, orbiting streams of bullets appear, followed by Suika throwing large orbs at the player and creating a black hole around herself. Sections of the UI also disappear as she takes damage.

Player Spell Cards[edit]

Spell Card A-1
萃符「戸隠山投げ」 (Suifu "Tokugashiyama-nage")
Gather Sign "Mt. Togakushi Toss" [6]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Suika dives at the opponent and grabs them. If grab is successful, she stuffs them into a large rock, which she then throws.
Spell Card A-2
萃鬼「天手力男投げ」 (Suiki "Ame-no-Tajikarao-nage")
Gathering Oni "Ame-no-Tajikarao Toss" [7]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Suika dives at the opponent and grabs them. If grab is successful, she stuffs them into a large rock, which she then throws.
Spell Card B-1
酔符「鬼縛りの術」 (Suifu "Onishibari no Jutsu")
Drunk Sign "Art of Oni Binding"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Suika grabs the opponent with a chain, causing their spirit bar to drain over a period of time.
Spell Card B-2
酔夢「施餓鬼縛りの術」 (Suimu "Segakishibari no Jutsu")
Drunken Dream "Art of Segaki Binding" [8]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Suika grabs the opponent with a chain, causing their spirit bar to drain over a period of time and refill Suika's in the process.
Spell Card C-1
鬼符「ミッシングパワー」 (Kifu "Misshingu Pawaa")
Oni Sign "Missing Power"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Initial
Editor's Comment
Suika becomes giant for a period of time. While giant, Suika can use simple melee moves and cannot be knocked back by attacks.
Spell Card C-2
鬼神「ミッシングパープルパワー」 (Kishin "Misshingu Paapuru Pawaa")
Kishin "Missing Purple Power"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Player - Final
Editor's Comment
Suika becomes super-giant for a period of time. While giant, Suika can use simple melee moves and cannot be knocked back by attacks.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Ama-no-Iwato: the cavern where the sun goddess Amaterasu hid herself away in legend. The boulder blocking the entrance was hurled away by the god Ame-no-Tajikarao when Amaterasu left.
  2. ↑ 追儺 (tsuina): A ceremony of driving out evil spirits, originally from China. Japan's Setsubun ceremonies are derived from tsuina.
  3. ↑ 燐禍 (rinka, lit. "phosphorus disaster"): a play on 燐火 (rinka, one of several JP terms for will-o'-wisps), as well as possibly 輪禍 (rinka, "car accident").
  4. ↑ Literally "in a six-league fog". A play on words that's one step above the idiom 五里霧中 (lit. "in a five-league fog"), which means 'to be in utter confusion'.
  5. ↑ Hyakki Yako (百鬼夜行): A night parade of one hundred demons. This spell's name is a coined term from this parade, times ten thousand (百万: a million, 百: a hundred).
  6. ↑ Mt. Togakushi: a (real life) mountain said to have been created from the boulder flung from Ama-no-Iwato.
  7. ↑ Ame-no-Tajikarao: the god who hurled away the boulder blocking the entrance to Ama-no-Iwato.
  8. ↑ 酔夢 (suimu, "drunken dream"): pronounced identically to part of the game's Japanese title (萃夢, "gathering dream")
