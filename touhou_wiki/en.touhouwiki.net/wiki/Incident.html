An incident (異変, ihen) is an unusual phenomenon that occurs within Gensokyo, with a scale that extends over Gensokyo, and are of unknown cause at the time that they occur.[1] The main plot of most Touhou games generally surrounds the occurrence of an incident. There may also be incidents that occur outside the games, although these tend to be of less urgency and concern. There are also games like Mountain of Faith that focus on non-incidents.

List of Game Incidents[edit]

  • Scarlet Mist Incident (Embodiment of Scarlet Devil)
  • Spring Snow Incident (Perfect Cherry Blossom )
  • Night Parade of One Hundred Oni Every Four Days (Immaterial and Missing Power)
  • Eternal Night Incident (Imperishable Night)
  • Sixty Year Cycle Great Barrier Incident (Phantasmagoria of Flower View)
  • Urban Legend Incident (Urban Legend in Limbo)
  • Four Seasons Incident (Hidden Star in Four Seasons)

  • Vampire Incident
