Description

Currently they are friends with DiGiTAL WiNG, LiLA'c Records, A-One, NJK Record, macaroom, and Plum.
They are also friends with the following circles (occasional guest appearances etc): EastNewSound, こなぐすり
On the 3rd of September 2017, circle representative sumijun announced on Twitter that the circle would suspend its activities after October, 2017.[1] They cited three main reasons, including the following:
-"My abilities can't catch up with the knowledge I have at my disposal, and I can't make music I'm satisfied with anymore (this is probably the most important reason)"
-"Since 2014, the circle has become too commercial."
-"A lot of personal things have happened (both good and bad... lots of things happen in life!)"[2]
