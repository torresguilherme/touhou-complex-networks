I Love You
Release date: 2010-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 6)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Silent Sinner in Blue (CD).

4
Release date: 2011-05-08 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Fairy Wars and Ghostly Field Club.

ACROSS THE FANTASY
Release date: 2011-06-26 (Leave the Love Magic to Marisa! 3)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet.

Agitation
Release date: 2011-09-11 (Reitaisai SP2)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense (CD).

A MAN OF THE BLUFF
Release date: 2011-10-16 (Touhou Kouroumu 7)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Perfect Memento in Strict Sense (CD) and Kioh Gyoku plus an original track.

Siren Song Of The Fantasy Culture
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires plus an original track.

3YEARS OLD PROMISS
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Leave the Love Magic to Marisa! 4)
A collaboration album with EASTARS and SANSYOYU featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler.

MORSE EP
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
A collaboration album with Shinigiwa Satellite featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.

9 ICE CRACKERS
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Fairy Wars plus an original track.

PSYCHO EP
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
A collaboration album with Shinigiwa Satellite featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism and Ghostly Field Club.

狐六弦1.2
Release date: 2015-02-01 (Dai-Kyuu-Shuu Touhousai 11)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires.

Here The Light Shines
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Hisoutensoku and Fairy Wars.

WOW!! EP
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
A collaboration album with denshūto, Find Your Way, Ochimusuya Grind and Shinigiwa Satellite featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character.

Dirty Paradise
Release date: 2017-10-22 (Touhou Kouroumu 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Double Dealing Character, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Kioh Gyoku.

Forgotten songs vol.1
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Fairy Wars and Ten Desires.

Borderline between reality and illusion
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

BURN IT UP!
Release date: 2019-05-05 (Reitaisai 16)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism, Double Dealing Character, Urban Legend in Limbo, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Forbidden Scrollery (CD).
