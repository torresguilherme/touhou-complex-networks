Touhou Jaseishou (Wicked Star Chapter[1]) ~ The Last Comer (東方邪星章 ～ The Last Comer) is a fan-made Touhou Project script by ido, Yuuyu and Hatsuke for the Touhou Danmakufu shooting game engine, released in full on August 26, 2012.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

The main gimmick of The Last Comer is based around collecting stars to fill a gauge, which is similar to Ten Desires' Trance gauge.

Story[edit]

Main articles: Story and Characters

Scripted by Oyasu Milka, the religion of Christianity features as its main theme.

A star has fallen into Gensokyo, setting aflame the forest and the cherry trees. A mysterious light has also appeared in the sky. Reimu Hakurei, Marisa Kirisame, and Sakuya Izayoi go out to investigate the cause of these strange occurrences.

Music[edit]

Main article: Music

The Last Comer features original music in the style of ZUN, composed by Wanwan.
