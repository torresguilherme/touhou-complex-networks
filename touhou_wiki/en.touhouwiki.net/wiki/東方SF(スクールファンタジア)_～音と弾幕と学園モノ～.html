Touhou School Fantasia ~the Sound, Danmaku and the Campus~ (東方SF(スクールファンタジア) ～音と弾幕と学園モノ～, Touhou School Fantasia ~Oto to Danmaku to Gakuen mono~) is a doujin album by とらのあな released on 2011-08-12 (Comiket 80). It's a package containing a music album with Touhou arrangements, illustrations by various artists drawing Touhou characters and a small comic book.
