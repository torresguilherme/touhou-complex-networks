Touhou Chouseisho (Tide Bible) ~ Sapphire Panlogism (東方潮聖書 ～ Sapphire Panlogism) is a fan-made Touhou Project script for the Touhou Danmakufu ph3 shooting game engine, created by Team Urban Mimzie. The game is planned to have 6 stages and an Extra stage.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

Story[edit]

Main articles: Story and Characters

Gensokyo has been dealing with an unusual drizzle as of late. Somehow, saltwater is raining down from the sky in large quantities. Just what kind of person could be causing this, and for what reason...?

Music[edit]

Main article: Music

Sapphire Panlogism features original music in the style of ZUN, composed predominantly by Frogmask, with some tracks by other composers such as Spectral Nexus.
