Description

Girls Logic Observatory are a Touhou arrangement circle who's music is primarily arranged and performed by sole member テラ who initially arranged primarily vocal Rock but has since branched out to include arrangement styles such as Jazz, Salsa and Pop. Vocals for the circle are notably contributed by both male and female vocalists, テラ himself sang on every album released by the circle up until the release of their third full album Reversible Negophilia in 2014. Releases by the circle regularly feature female vocalist 咲子 who is the circle's longest running recurring vocalists having debuted on the circles first mini album over knowledge in 2012, female vocalist うきね who debuted on the circle first full album Like a Retrospective in 2013 is another regular vocalist along with male vocalist 保科 who debuted on the circles fourth full album Hollow Masquerade in 2015. In addition to テラ and the circle's group of recurring support vocalists there are also a number of guest vocalists who contribute on full length albums, noteworthy past guests included ななせ of じゃねっと亭, イチ of 少年ヴィヴィッド and 556t of Kairo while as of 2016 vocalists あやぽんず＊ of ShinRa-Bansho and ななひら of Confetto have made regular appearances.

Nearly every album released by the circle notably features unlisted bonus tracks, these tracks most commonly include off vocal versions of the album's vocal tracks in addition to a commentary track narrated by テラ himself discussing the specific album's tracks or concept. All of the circle's discography has been neatly divided into either singles, mini albums, full albums or collaboration albums. singles and mini albums primarily feature vocals by the circle's group of regular vocalists and feature tracks that often end up being re-recorded for later full albums. Full albums released by the circle notably feature a number of guest vocalists both male and female in addition to the circle's regular cast of support vocalists and will often feature new material in addition to re-recordings/re-masters of tracks from earlier mini albums and singles. Collaboration albums released by the circle often feature new material by the involved circles, re-arrangements or covers by the circles of each others past works and new collaborative material.


Discography
Feroce e.p.
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
1st single
A single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. This short 2 track single would be fully re-recorded for the mini album Radio Disturbance (2012).

over knowledge
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
1st mini album
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith in addition to off vocal tracks. Most of the mini album would be re-recorded for the full album Like a Retrospective (2013) and single Dancing NightBug e.p. (2013). It is the first album to feature female vocalist 咲子.

Radio Disturbance
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
2nd mini album
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object in addition to off vocal tracks. Most of the mini album would be re-recorded on later releases by the circle.

Like a Retrospective
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
1st full album
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and more...?? It is the first album to feature female vocalist うきね.
