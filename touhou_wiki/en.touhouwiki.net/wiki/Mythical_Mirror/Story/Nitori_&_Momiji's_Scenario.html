Stage 1[edit]

何処かで見た何処かの森

Somewhere in Some Forest

漂う魔と茸の香り

Wandering Demons and Aroma of Mushrooms

BGM:伊勢

BGM:Ise

Nitori

うーん、暫く進んでみたけれど、何もないね

Hmm, we've been progressing for a while, but nothing's happened.

Momiji

ですね、四方八方全て山です。
町か何かあればここがどこなのか尋ねられるのですが……

No matter where we go, it's nothing but mountain.
I'd ask if a town or anything else was here....

Nitori

まあ、風景を見る限り幻想郷みたいだし。
飛んでればそのうち何処か着くよー

Well, if you look at the landscape, it looks like Gensokyo.
We'll arrive somewhere if we fly!

Momiji

ポジティブですね

I'm positive.

Nitori

幻想郷は丸いからね

It's because Gensokyo is spherical.

Momiji

そう来ましたか……

Someone came...

???

まったく、呆れるくらいに前向きですこと

Aren't you amazed by this place at all?

Marisa Kirisame ENTERS

Marisa

奇妙な反応を感じて来て見れば、
妙な河童と白狼天狗がいらっしゃる

I came here and felt a strange reaction
coming from a kappa and white wolf tengu.

Marisa

これは一体何なのでしょう？

What the heck is this?

Momiji

あれ？

Huh?

Nitori

魔理沙だ

So it's you, Marisa.

Marisa

しかも名乗る前からこちらのことを知っているご様子。
ますます疑念が深まっていくわ

It seems you knew who I was before you met me.
My doubts are becoming increasingly deep.

Momiji

魔理沙だけど……

Is it really Marisa...?

Nitori

……何か、凄く違うね。ニセモノ？

...I can tell there's something different about her. Maybe she is an imposter?

Momiji

うーん、じゃあ確かめてみましょう

Well, let's confirm it.

Marisa

あの、貴方達。
人前でこそこそと話すのは失礼じゃなくて？

Hey guys
It's bad to do sneak talk in public, you know?

Momiji

すみません、一つ質問させてください。
貴方はパン派ですか？ご飯派ですか？

Sorry about that. By the way, we have a question for you
Have you eaten any bread for breakfast?

Marisa

パン派よ、ご飯今まで１７杯しか食べたことがないわ

I didn't, I only had 17 cups of rice.

BGM:小宇宙

BGM:Microcosmos

Nitori

コイツやっぱりニセモノだ！

I knew it, she is an imposter!

Marisa Kirisame DEFEATED

Momiji

ふう、中々大変だった

Phew, that was quite difficult.

Nitori

あんなのに構ってないで、早く行くよ！

It bothered me too, go early!

Momiji

元気ですね、にとりさんは

I'm fine, Miss Nitori.

Stage 2[edit]

おまわりさんの巡回領城

The Police Officer's Patrol Region

国家権力にご注意を

BGM:カラス

BGM:Karasu

Momiji

何とか町に辿りつけましたが、
これといって情報は掴めませんでしたね

We reached the town,
but we couldn't grasp any information.

Nitori

妖怪の山がどっちの方向にあるかもわからないだなんて。
相当遠くだったのかなぁ、あの穴の出口

I don't know which direction the Youkai Mountain's in.
We went an equivalent distance, that hole is the exit.

Momiji

困りましたね……

This is troubling....

Nitori

困っちゃったね……

Troubling, indeed...

???

お困りと聞いて

Do you need help?

Meimei Kamichikama ENTERS

Meimei

よろしければ私が相談に乗りましょうか？

Would you like to take some consultation from me?

Momiji

えっと、貴方は？

Huh, who're you?

Meimei

「上知我麻命名」と申します。
この幻想郷の管理者のお手伝いをしている者です

My name is Meimei Kamichikama
I am a servant of the administrator of this Gensokyo.

Nitori

……管理者、霊夢のってこと？

...By administrator do you mean Reimu?

Momiji

あの不真面目な方の巫女が……？

That lazy shrine maiden?

Meimei

れいむ？巫女？
私はそのような人に仕えていないのですが……

Reimu? Shrine maiden?
I don't serve such a person.

Momiji

何だか食い違っていますね

I've somehow staggered.

Nitori

ならちょっと知ってることを教えてもらおうかな？

Would you mind telling us what you know?

BGM:Kamitachi

BGM:Wind Shear

Meimei

へ？なにする気ですか？

What? What do you mean?

Meimei Kamichikama DEFEATED

Nitori

ふむふむ、あっちの方角に行けばいいと

Hmmmm, I'd be good to go in the other direction.

Momiji

……相変わらず山しか見えませんが、
信用できる情報なんでしょうか

...I still can't see the mountain,
is this information reliable?

Nitori

山を越えてみればわかることだよー

Maybe there's something beyond the mountain.

Momiji

まあ、そうですね。
他に帰宅の手がかりもわかりませんし

Well, yes.
I don't have a clue about how to return home.

Stage 3[edit]

這い動く山脈

Creeping Mountain Range

龍の眠る場所

Where the Dragons Sleep

BGM:轍

BGM:Wadachi

Nitori

頑張って昇った結果、ようやく頂上だけれど

Our good luck has ascended and as a result, we've reached the summit.

Momiji

むう

Acceptable.

Nitori

結果は芳しくないみたいね。
ガセ言いそうな雰囲気の子じゃなかったのにな～

The results are poor though.
Not even children would want to be in this sort of atmosphere~

Momiji

いえ……なんというか、その、
視界が悪くて遠くが良く見えないんです

Though....I wouldn't say that it's poor,
even though it doesn't look good far away.

Momiji

雨と雷、それに大気の揺らぎ……？
なんですかこれ、超常現象過ぎますよ

Rain and thunder, and the fluctuations of the atmosphere....?
The weather is paranormal, too.

???

実は人為的な現象なのよね……

It's actually a human phenomenon....

Orochi Ichiyasan ENTERS

Momiji

誰ですか、そこの見るからに妖しい人

Who is this mysterious person?

Orochi

誰でもいいじゃない、そんなこと……。
どうせわたし、どうでもいい子だしぃ……

No matter what you say, I'm not the cause of such an event...
Anyway, I'm a good child...

Nitori

やばい、すごく、ダウナーな人だ！

She's really dangerous, it's a downer person!

Momiji

どうでもいいわけないじゃないですか。
何だか思わせぶりなこと呟いてたし

Whatever, that's not a good excuse.
Besides, she was muttering something somewhat suggestive.

Orochi

別に……あんた達とは何の関係もないって

That's different...it had nothing to do with you guys.

Orochi

ただ少し遠くの天候をいじって、
視界を悪くしてる程度の子だから……

A couple of children were messing with the weather far away,
which is also causing the bad visibility....

Momiji

いえ、十分関係してるじゃないですか！

No, that's not a good relationship!

Nitori

どうして私達に意地悪するの？
何かきみに悪いことした覚えはないんだけど

Why are you being mean to us?
I don't remember us doing anything bad to you.

Orochi

いやあ、本分なんですよ。
誰かの邪魔したり、罠にはめたりするの

Gee, it's my duty.
To bother someone, or lure them into a trap.

BGM:松風

BGM:Wind in the Pines

Nitori

自覚がある分、尚更タチが悪いですね……

The minute that someone loses consciousness, it just makes things worse...

Orochi Ichiyasan DEFEATED

Nitori

これでどう？
もう視界は晴れたでしょ？

Now what?
Another clear sight?

Momiji

ええ、ばっちり千里先まで見えます

Yeah, it looks like we're reaching our destination.

Nitori

何があるかわかる？

What do you know?

Momiji

んー……あれは……神社、でしょうか？
何だか祭りをしてるみたいですよ

Look, is that....a shrine over there?
It looks like they're having a party.

Stage 4[edit]

年に一度の祭囃子

The Annual Matsuribayashi

紛れ遊ぶ者

The Playful One, The Deceitful One

BGM:神楽

BGM:Shinto Music

Nitori

森から町へ、町から山へ、
山を下って今度は神社……

From the forest to the town, to the mountain from the town
Now down the mountain to the shrine......

Nitori

さすがに疲れた

As expected, we're tired.

Momiji

上ではお祭りがやっているみたいですし、
ついたら休憩にしましょうか？

Seems like there is a festival above
Do you wish to have a break when we got down?

Nitori

異議なし。
冷えたキュウリと日本酒が欲しい気分だよぉ

I don't see why not
I want some cold cucumbers and sake.

Momiji

……でも階段、終わりませんね。
登っても登っても頂上に近づいてる気がしません

...... But these stairs don't have an end.
No matter how much we climb, we haven't reached our destination.

Nitori

それにこの風景、どこかで見たことが……

And this scenery seems familiar somehow...

???

こんにちは、お二人さん方！

Hi there, you two!

Coral ENTERS

Oral ENTERS

Oral

こんにちは……知らない方々……

Hello....People I don't know...

Coral

この先に何か御用かな？？

Do you want me to??

Nitori

同じ顔が二つ……

Those girls have the same face...

Momiji

これまた随分と可愛らしい方が出てきましたね

They look like lovely people.

Coral

可愛いだなんて照れるなぁ～

Oh, you're making me bashful~

Oral

照れてる場合、違う

You must be shy.

Coral

とと、そうだったね。お姉さん達が
何でここにいるのかを聞きに来たんだった

And so it was. Me and my
older sister were listening to something.

Momiji

随分抽象的な物言いですね、
主語がないから察しかねるのですが

It was fairly abstract,
and I couldn't observe it so it had no subject.

Coral

言葉通りだよ、
この『幻想郷』になんでいるのかなってさ

Literally,
why are you in this fantastic version of "Gensokyo".

Nitori

そう言われてもよくわからないんだけど……

I don't even know myself....

Oral

……何にしても、貴方達のような妖怪を
この先には行かせない

....Anyways, since you guys are youkai,
we cannot let you go ahead.

BGM:摩天楼

BGM:Skyscraper

Coral

だね、一方的で悪いけど回れ右してもらおうかな！

Look, we're rather one-sided so, we're sorry!

Coral AND Oral DEFEATED

Nitori

倒しちゃったけど、正当防衛だからセーフだよね？

And they went down, we were safe because of self defense?

Momiji

襲ってきた方が悪いんです。……っと、
ようやく鳥居が見えてきましたね

Attacking that way is wrong. Innovative, we finally see the shrine's tori.

Nitori

今の子達を倒した途端に……？
術か何かで辿りつくのを邪魔されてたのかな

Why did those children attack us just now...? Maybe there were disturbed by dark arts or something.

Momiji

そうかもしれませんね。
とにかく、行ってみましょう

Perhaps so.
Anyway, let's go

Stage 5[edit]

BGM: Utsushiyo

Momiji

……入り口から何となく
見覚えがあるなと思ってましたが

...We've finally reached the entrance
This place looks familiar somehow.

Nitori

細部が違うけど博麗神社だよね、ここ

This is certainly the Hakurei Shrine, but the details are different.

Momiji

その割にはあの巫女がいませんし、
博麗神社でお祭りなんて聞いたことがありません

This place doesn't seem to have a maiden,
I have never heard of a festival at the Hakurei Shrine.

Nitori

宴会しかしないもんね。
一体どういうことなのかな……

It seems to be a banquet.
What the heck does this mean...?

???

それはこっちのセリフよ

It's right out here.

Genya Amakusa ENTERS

Genya

この八経津神社に入ってくる怪しい人間は、
一体どこのどちら様かしら

A suspicious person coming to the Hakurei Shrine,
I wonder who's there.

Momiji

妖怪の山の犬走椛です

I'm a mountain youkai, Momiji Inubashiri.

Nitori

同じく妖怪の山の川城にとりです

And I'm Nitori Kawashiro, another mountain youkai.

Genya

い、いや自己紹介をしろって
言ったわけじゃないんだけどなぁ……

Yes, and it looks like
I need to introduce myself as well...

Nitori

そういうあんたは誰なのかな。どうして博麗神社に？
あと八経津神社ってどういうことなの？

I wonder who you are. Why the Hakurei Shrine?
What is this place anyway?

Genya

待って、待って。聖徳太子じゃないんだから、
そんな一回にたくさん質問しないでよ！

Wait, wait. I'm not Prince Shoutoku, you know?
One question at a time, please!

Momiji

じゃあ、貴方は誰ですからで

Well then, who're you?

Genya

あたしは天草幻夜。
この幻想郷の保安係よ

My name is Genya Amakusa
I'm the security clerk of this Gensokyo.

Nitori

……幻想郷に、保安係なんていたの？

...Was there a security engagement in Gensokyo?

Momiji

いえ、初耳です

Nope, it's a new concept.

Genya

とぼけないでよ、知らないはずないでしょ。毎日平和を
守るため、あっちへこっちへ大活躍のこのあたしを

Don't play dumb, you probably know about this anyway. In order to
protect the peace everyday, people come to me for big success.

Momiji

でも、実際に見かけるのは初めてなんですが

But, I've only seen this one time.

BGM:ムラマサ

BGM:Muramasa

Genya

そう、ならしっかりと目に焼き付けることね。この
華麗なる剣技と美しき弾幕のコラボレーションを！

So, your eyes are burning firmly. With
the collaboration of this sword technique and this danmaku!

Genya Amakusa DEFEATED

Nitori

二個目と三個目の質問に答えてほしかったのに、
伸びちゃった

I wanted you to answer the second and third questions,
it had extended.

Momiji

華麗というほど、凄い剣技ではなかったような……。
ただ刀をぶんぶん早く振ってるだけのような……

So brilliant, but your sword skills aren't that good...
But you were just waving that buzzing sword earlier...

Nitori

細かい事はいいとして、どうする？

It was a fine thing, what will you do?

Momiji

……そうですね

...Well.

Momiji

私はあの本来の博麗神社にはない、
奥へ続く小道が気になります

It's not that of the original Hakurei Shrine,
but there's a footpath in the back.

Stage 6[edit]

BGM: Misagi

BGM: Not Fraud

Momiji

大分深いところまできましたね

We're going much deeper.

Nitori

本当の博麗神社の後ろにも木はあったけど、
こんな広い森ってわけじゃなかったし……

There were a few trees behind the Hakurei Shrine,
but I didn't think there'd be an entire forest...

Momiji

……本当におかしいところです。
知ってる場所のはずなのに、少しずつのズレがある

...This is really funny.
You should know where we are, the gap is getting closer little by little.

Nitori

まるでパラレルワールドみたいだね

It looks like a parallel world.

Momiji

ぱ、ぱら……れる？

Pa-para...llel?

Nitori

別の次元の幻想郷に迷い込んだんじゃないかって。
そうなら、あの穴に吸い込まれたのも納得できる

I think we wandered into another dimension from Gensokyo.
If so, I'm also convinced we were sucked into that hole.

???

半分正解、半分はずれです

Your answer is half correct, half wrong.

???

ここはもう一つの幻想郷。
幻想郷を模倣して作り出された幻想郷

This is a fantasy town.
This Gensokyo was created to mimic the original Gensokyo.

Hyuga Yafutu ENTERS

Hyuga

初めまして、私はここの主代行をしている者です

Nice to meet you, I have a major delegation here.

Momiji

模倣された幻想郷……？

An imitation of Gensokyo....?

Nitori

ご丁寧な挨拶ありがとう……
それで、どういうことかなそれは

Thanks for your polite greetings...
So, what do you mean?

Hyuga

言葉の通り、ここは
貴方達の幻想郷を参考にして作った幻想郷

As you see, this place
was created by using your Gensokyo as a reference.

Hyuga

その世界に何らかの影響で、貴方達は
迷い込んでしまったようですね

We took a lot of influence from your world,
but it seems you've wandered in.

Momiji

ようですねって、
貴方でも理由がわからないんですか？

It sounds like,
I'm not sure why you even did this?

Hyuga

大凡の予想はつきますが、
確かではないことなので言えません

Although luck is expected to approximate,
I cannot say because I'm not sure.

Nitori

まあ、興味はあるけど今私達がほしいのは
元の幻想郷への戻り方なんだよね

Well, I'm interested, but now we want a way back
to the original Gensokyo.

Hyuga

こう、ぱぱーっと帰してはくれないかな？

This, i wonder if that's attributed to the innovation paper.

Hyuga

もちろんそのつもりですが、今すぐとはいきません。
踏む手順がありますので

Of course we're working on that, but not right now.
Since there is a procedure to follow.

Momiji

そうですか、何にせよ帰れるなら一安心です

Is that so, it's a relief to finally be able to go home.

Hyuga

ですのでその戻るまでの時間潰しに……

So, let's kill time until you're able to return...

Hyuga

一戦、弾幕ごっこなどいかがでしょうか？

How about a danmaku fight?

Nitori

そうきたかい、いけるクチなの？

You're out of you mind, you know that?

Hyuga

幻夜やオーラル、コーラルよりは。
是非本家の弾幕を見せては頂けませんか？

You already defeated Genya, Oral and Coral.
So, will you show me your danmaku or not?

Momiji

まだ若輩者ですが、それでもよければ

I'm still the youngest person here, but still good.

Nitori

異議なし！

No objection!

Hyuga

では、ご案内しましょう

Then, let me guide you.

BGM:翡翠

BGM:Jade

Hyuga

……合わせ鏡のその世界に

...Through this mirrored world.

Hyuga Yafutu DEFEATED
