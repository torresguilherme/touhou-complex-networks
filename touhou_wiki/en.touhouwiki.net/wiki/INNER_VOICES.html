INNER VOICES is a doujin album by Hatsunetsumiko's 「発熱巫女～ず」 released on 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92). It features vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Undefined Fantastic Object, Trojan Green Asteroid, and the Story of Eastern Wonderland.
