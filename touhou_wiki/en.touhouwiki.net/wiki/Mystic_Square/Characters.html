Playable Characters[edit]

 巫女さん
Shrine Maiden

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character

The Hakurei Shrine maiden; has natural spiritual powers, but never trains. She can attack with spiritual wards and release magical energy through her spiritual attack. With that and the power of the Yin-Yang Orb she hunts youkai. A very easy-going person. Strange things always happen around Reimu, but she always enjoys it. She's very straightforward with her feelings, so Marisa always teases her.


魔法使いさん
Magician

Marisa Kirisame
Playable Character

Magician, but a normal girl. Seems overconfident, but she has the power to back it up. She's extremely sharp and persistent, but still a child. Nobody knows what she does all day *g* She's very hard-working. She seems to have different behavioural standards than Reimu.


悪霊さん
Evil Spirit

Mima
Playable Character

A spirit who's lived(?) a very long time. However, she certainly doesn't remember it all, so she's rather laid-back. She's become like the (evil) spirit of the Hakurei Shrine, but her personality is more human than some humans'.


妖怪さん
Youkai

Yuuka
Playable Character

The strongest of the youkai around Hakurei Shrine, but one can never tell what she's thinking, so she's a very eccentric and ill-thought-of youkai. She can be pretty level-headed, but...
