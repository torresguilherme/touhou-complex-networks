Kurumi (くるみ) is a vampire and the gatekeeper to the entrance of the Lake of Blood at the center of a mountain. During Lotus Land Story, she is defeated by both Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame on their way to Mugenkan. Why she guards the entrance to the lake, and the "gate" she is referring to, are unknown.

Character Basis[edit]

Design[edit]

In Lotus Land Story's official art, Kurumi has yellow eyes, long yellow hair, and has a white bow in her hair. She wears a white shirt and red suspenders and has a red bow over her chest. She also has large purple wings.

Story[edit]

Lotus Land Story
Main article: Lotus Land Story Story

The main character meets Kurumi as the guardian of the lake. She said that she has defeated all the intruders who tried to enter the lake (at least up until the events of Lotus Land Story). She was easily defeated by either Reimu Hakurei or Marisa Kirisame. On the third stage of Lotus Land Story, Reimu or Marisa, depending on which character the player is using, entered Mugenkan before the other character. Presumebly, they must have passed Kurumi somehow.

Relationships[edit]

Kurumi is not seen interacting with anyone outside of the player character.

Additional Information[edit]

  • Sylphid (from Project Blank) uses some attacks that are very similar to Kurumi's "wall explosions".
  • Kurumi's the only PC-98 boss to serve as her own midboss.
  • With the exception of Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame, Elly (who is the guard of Mugenkan) hadn't fought for a long time preceding the events of Lotus Land Story. This may be because Kurumi possibly defeated all intruders prior to both Reimu and Marisa's arrival.
