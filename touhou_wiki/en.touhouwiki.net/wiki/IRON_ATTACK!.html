Profile

IRON ATTACK! in 2016 delivering a message about their 2016 European tour. From left to right: 元帥 (drums), IRON-CHINO (guitar), YAMA-B (vocals), Kira☆ (guitar), Saka-than (bass), まいなすいょん (vocals)

Discography
UNLEASHED IN THE EAST
Release date: 2007-08-17 (Comiket 72)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Magical Astronomy and Shuusou Gyoku.

EVIL MOUNTAIN
Release date: 2007-12-31 (Comiket 73)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

BLADE OF ANCIENT TEMPLE
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
A collaboration album with Kissing the Mirror featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power and Imperishable Night.

Scythe for the Guilty Riversides
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
A collaboration album with Kissing the Mirror featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Lotus Land Story, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Strange and Bright Nature Deity.

DEAD HEAT REFRAIN
Release date: 2008-08-16 (Comiket 74)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism and Changeability of Strange Dream.

THUNDER CONCERTO
Release date: 2008-12-30 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Subterranean Animism.

SAVAGE FLAMES
Release date: 2009-03-08 (Reitaisai 6)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Subterranean Animism.

SPARKING
Release date: 2009-05-05 (M3-23)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object.

FAR EAST JUDGMENT
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.

CRYING DESTINY
Release date: 2009-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 5)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Silent Sinner in Blue. The album is notable for being the first to features vocalist まいなすいょん of Sister's Spread-i as well as being one of the only albums to feature majority vocal arrangement in the circle's early discography.

Miss Understanding
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Silent Sinner in Blue.

Vampire Concerto
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, The Grimoire of Marisa and Shuusou Gyoku.

BEST COLLECTIONS
Release date: 2010-05-05 (M3-25)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Highly Responsive to Prayers, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise and Magical Astronomy.
It is a collection of tracks released in previous albums.

BLACK HISTORY
Release date: 2010-05-05 (M3-25)
A compilation album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith.
It is a collection of instrumental arrange tracks plus one vocal track released in previous collaboration albums, with the addition of one new track.

嵐のハリケーン
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Double Spoiler.

Pre-historic Eruption
Release date: 2010-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 6)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Undefined Fantastic Object.

Poltergeist
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

Concerto of the Scarlet Elements
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. The album is notable for being the first to feature vocals by IRON-CHINO.

ANGEL MOUNTAIN
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith.

BULLET WIND
Release date: 2011-10-16 (Touhou Kouroumu 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Double Spoiler and Ten Desires.

Deamon's seal ～魔界の封印～
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires.

STAR DUST
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Shuusou Gyoku, Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires. The album marked a notable shift away from albums featuring majority instrumental arrangements towards majority vocal arrangements.

GUITAR ODYSSEY
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Subterranean Animism.
It is a collection of tracks released in previous albums.

SAILING DESTINY
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
A compilation album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith.
It is a collection of tracks released in previous albums.

PAPER MOON
Release date: 2012-10-07 (Touhou Kouroumu 8)
It features instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.

BURN IN HELL
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism and Shuusou Gyoku. The album is notable for being the first to feature vocals by IRON-CHINO who sings on every vocal arrangement.

HEAVEN'S SWORD
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Scarlet Weather Rhapsody.

Plastic Mind
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

FAIRY TALE
Release date: 2013-10-13 (Touhou Kouroumu 9)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism and Double Dealing Character.

THUNDER CRACK
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character.

Scarlet Furor
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

ICICLE FALL
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Impossible Spell Card.

センチメンタル シンフォニア
Release date: 2014-10-12 (Touhou Kouroumu 10)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Impossible Spell Card.

Dim.STARLIGHT
Release date: 2014-12-30 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, and, Subterranean Animism.

Death and Rebirth
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Impossible Spell Card.

STAR DUST MEMORY～IRON ATTACK!ボーカルベスト①～
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
A compilation album featuring arrangements from previous IRON ATTACK! albums.

SISTER OF PUPPETS～IRON ATTACK!ボーカルベスト②～
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
A compilation album featuring IRON ATTACK! arrangements from previous Sister's Spread-i albums.

WORLD TOUR
Release date: 2015-10-18 (Autumn Reitaisai 2)
A live album recorded at several different venues featuring arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Double Dealing Character and Kantai Collection.

ぷらす兄さんまいなす姉さん
Release date: 2015-11-01 (Touhou Kouroumu 11)
A short single featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.

上海牡丹
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Ten Desires, Urban Legend in Limbo and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

Bullet Blitz ～天孫降臨～
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

Twilight Under World
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

宇宙とファンタジー
Release date: 2016-10-09 (Touhou Kouroumu 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club and Magical Astronomy.

Perpetual Nightmare
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

凱 ～Power and Glory～
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Kantai Collection.

Keep Your Faith
Release date: 2017-5-7 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.
This album is notable for being very aggressive and mostly sung in English.

本当は怖い東方世界
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Mountain of Faith, Double Dealing Character, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

士魂 ~占守島の戦い~
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism, Mountain of Faith, Double Dealing Character, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.
