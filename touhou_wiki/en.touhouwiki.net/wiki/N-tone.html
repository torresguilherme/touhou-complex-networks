Description

The circle's discography has been made available for download through Dust_Box_49 official page.


Discography
East side fragment
Release date: 2005-08-14 (Comiket 68)
A preview disc for East side material, featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power and Imperishable Night.

East side material
Release date: 2005-12-30 (Comiket 69)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power and Imperishable Night.

Photograph of Phantasmagoria
Release date: 2006-05-21 (Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

Faith of Format
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

Miscellaneous Fragment
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Changeability of Strange Dream.

SNOW PRINT-in the fairy tale-
Release date: 2008-11-02 (Touhou Kouroumu 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Scarlet Weather Rhapsody.

Dungeon&Danger
Release date: 2009-03-08 (Reitaisai 6)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

LUNAR TRIPS
Release date: 2009-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Immaterial and Missing Power.

Side material "FLOW"
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Akyu's Untouched Score vol.5.

Trance for Treasureship
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

水蜜桃
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

Tone Box
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from collaboration with Dust_Box_49. It is an featuring arranges from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.
It is a "Best of" compilation album featuring instrumentals from the collaboration albums Photograph of Phantasmagoria, Trance for Treasureship, Faith of Format and Dungeon&Danger.

やさしくひかるかえでのき
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ghostly Field Club and Changeability of Strange Dream.

Ghost ride acceleration
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

Beyond the Last Archive
Release date: 2012-05-12 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. It is a collaboration with Dust_Box_49.

FAIRY BREATH
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.
