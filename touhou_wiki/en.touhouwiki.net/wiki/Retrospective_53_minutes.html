East-West Tokaido ~ Retrospective 53 minutes (卯酉東海道　～ Retrospective 53 minutes, Bouyu Toukaidou ~ Retrospective 53 minutes) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2006-05-21 (Reitaisai 3). It is part of "ZUN's Music Collection" and it is the fourth volume (vol.4).
