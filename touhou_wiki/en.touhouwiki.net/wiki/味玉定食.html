Description

AZITAMATEISHOKU is a Touhou Project band arrangement circle consisting of sole member 味付け玉子. The circle's arrangement style strikes an uncommon balance between loyalty to ZUN's original composition and the addition of original elements, this results in arrangements that do much more than supplant melodies into an already fleshed out piece of music. Instead, arrangements by the circle are often integrally structured around these original melodies, translating their feeling and movement into a Rock arrangement, song structuring tools such as bridges and transitions often act as the only additional elements. The circle's musical style itself has remained largely unchanged with their discography being composed primarily of their characteristic vocal focused thrashy Rock arrangements, with the occasional Rock ballad or instrumental opening track sprinkled throughout. 味付け玉子 himself performs the guitar and bass parts In addition to handling the mix and mastering himself, this has resulted in a gradual improvement to the recording and sound quality in his works since he first began releasing music on niconico in 08'. Albums by the circle will often feature arrangements of themes from a single game or arrangements of themes associated with specific characters, later releases would even feature uncommonly arranged themes such as the title screen, ending & staffroll themes (for example CRIMSONEAR, which features arrangements of Embodiment of Scarlet Devil's 紅より儚い永遠 and 赤より紅い夢).

All of AZITAMATEISHOKU's early discography going as far back as their first release (AZITAMASPARK!!) up until the 2011 album little good-bye has been re-recorded for albums later in their discography, these early releases were shorter than what would become the standard for them and often featured 6 or fewer tracks. It wasn't until the released of 月の追憶 in 2012 that the circle graduated from shorter 6 track albums to more fleshed out full length albums, because of this transition, releases from 2012 and onward had room to accommodate not only uncommonly arranged themes but also unlisted bonus tracks which began appearing infrequently from then on. Illustrations for the circle's albums have been provided primarily by artist ヒヂノタキ of ヒヂノイズ (twitter, pixiv) who's art appeared on almost every album released by the circle up until mid 2016. In addition to the circle's own releases they have also contributed a great number of guest arrangements to various collaboration/compilation albums (see guest arrangement section), a sizable percentage of which are arrangements of themes associated with Alice Margatroid.

味付け玉子 is admittedly a huge fan of Touhou Project character Alice Margatroid who's themes his circle has arranged numerous times, he has even released albums themed entirely around her such as SHE IS ALICE! and Hers. as well as contributing to a 4 part seasonal themed Alice Margatroid project called 季刊Alice (see other works section). 味付け玉子 has also performed his music for a live audience on several occasions with the support of AZITAMATEISHOKU's live band 音戦玉子 (ON-SEN TAMA), in 2016 he began work on a new Touhou arrangement circle called green sky, it notably features female vocals and arrangements styles both in the same vane as his original circle and in styles unexplored by his original circle.


Discography
AZITAMASPARK!!
Release date: 2009-09-27 (Sunshine Creation 45)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Immaterial and Missing Power and Imperishable Night. Much of AZITAMASPARK!! would later be re-recorded for the full length album 月の追憶 (2012).

おまえらまとめてけつをだせ！
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
A short 2 track album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. A small pressing of 20 discs was distributed at Reitaisai 7 free of charge.

Non-REM
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism. Every track from Non-REM would be later re-recorded for the full length album Silent Box (2013).

Vampire of Red
Release date: 2010-09-19 (Reitaisai SP)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. Every track from Vampire of Red would later be re-recorded for the full length album CRIMSONEAR (2013).

scarlet for the maiden
Release date: 2010-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 6)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. Every track from scarlet for the maiden would later be re-recorded for the full length album CRIMSONEAR (2013).

little good-bye
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object, it features a noticeable difference in sound quality when compared with the albums released prior and shortly after. Every track from little good-bye would later be re-recorded for the full length album REUNION (2015).

Hers.
Release date: 2011-05-22 (Nanairo Majo no Ningyou Budoukai)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mystic Square and Perfect Cherry Blossom. The album is themed around Alice Margatroid and features arrangements of themes associated with her, it's also notable for being the only AZITAMATEISHOKU album to feature female vocals. Vocals for the album are performed by リーベ, ハナビ雨季 (空色作戦), ランコ (BUTAOTOME), 蒼-aoi- (Water Color Melody.) and mineko (MN-logic24).

月の追憶
Release date: 2012-03-18 (Tsuki no Utage 5)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night. The album marks a shift away from shorter 6 track albums towards longer full length releases, several of it's tracks are re-recordings of tracks from the circle's first album AZITAMASPARK!! (2009).

飛花落葉、妖しは夢。
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Perfect Cherry Blossom.

二つの扉
Release date: 2012-10-07 (Touhou Kouroumu 8)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

Silent Box
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism. It features re-recordings of the album Non-REM (2010) in it's entirety along with a number of new arrangements.

CRIMSONEAR
Release date: 2013-10-13 (Touhou Kouroumu 9)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. It features re-recordings of the albums Vampire of Red (2010) and scarlet for the maiden (2010) in their entirety along with a handful of new arrangements.

無限輪廻
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires.

境界ヨゾラ
Release date: 2014-10-12 (Touhou Kouroumu 10)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes and Magical Astronomy.

REUNION
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object. It features re-recordings of the album little good-bye (2011) in it's entirety along with a number of new arrangements.

弾き語り集　Vol.1
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring acoustic guitar covers of previously released songs.

ロジカ ロマンティカ
Release date: 2015-11-01 (Touhou Kouroumu 11)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

SHE IS ALICE!
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith. It features redone versions of previously released Alice Margatroid themed arrangements, a number of it's tracks were originally released as guest arrangements for other circles (see guest arrangement section) before being redone for the album.

アジタマスト!
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
A "best of" album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires. It features alternate versions of previously released tracks.

弾き語り集～Vol.2
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy and Dr. Latency's Freak Report. It features "unplugged" or acoustic guitar versions of previously released tracks.

弾き語り集　～秘封
Release date: 2017-09-18 (Science Era Café Terrace 2017)
An album featuring arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.. It features "unplugged" or acoustic guitar versions of Secret Sealing Club arrangements from 境界ヨゾラ and ロジカ ロマンティカ.

Are You Still There?
Release date: 2017-10-15 (Autumn Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

罪の螺旋
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shoot the Bullet.

Pilgrim of Soul
Release date: 2019-05-05 (Reitaisai 16)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Phantasmagoria of Flower View.

Other Works

Collaboration Albums
咲アリ×サクリファイス！
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
A collaboration album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. It's themed around the pairing of Sakuya Izayoi with Alice Margatroid and features arrangements by a number of different Touhou Rock circles each of whom contributed one track.

ツインドライブ!!
Release date: 2014-11-24 (Autumn Reitaisai)
A collaboration album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires and Impossible Spell Card. It is a collaboration between AZITAMATEISHOKU and pikapika notably featuring vocal duets by 味付け玉子 and 飛花 on every track.

EASY SHOOT!!!
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An omnibus album featuring arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. It is a collaboration between Tetsuwan Tokage Tanchiki, AZITAMATEISHOKU and Monokuro Satsujin Genba Shashin (abbreviated as 鉄玉殺) each of whom contributed 1 new arrangement and one re-arrangement of a track previously released by another of the contributing circles.
Collaborative Print Works

In 2012 AZITAMATEISHOKU contributed to a series of 4 collaborative Alice Margatroid themed illustration books known as Quarterly Alice (季刊Alice).

  • 季刊Alice ～Fairy spring～ released 2012-04-01 (Nanairo Majo no Ningyou Budoukai 2) is the spring themed release of Quarterly Alice.
  • 季刊Alice ～Shining summer～ released 2012-8-11 (Comiket 82) is the summer themed release of Quarterly Alice.
  • 季刊Alice Nostalgia～autumn released 2012-10-07 (Touhou Kouroumu 8 is the autumn themed release of Quarterly Alice.
  • 季刊アリス～Winter Garden released 2012-12-03 (Comiket 83) is the winter themed release of Quarterly Alice.
