Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Zenki and Goki: the names of a pair of oni defeated and turned into vessels for shikigami by onmyouji Abe no Seimei or (in other stories) by En no Ozunu. Yukari also has two crow shikigami named after these oni.
  2. ↑ 荼吉尼天 (Dakiniten): a Japanese Buddhist goddess based on the Indian dakini who rides on a white fox. She is commonly identified with Izuna-Gongen (飯綱権現) and Inari (稲荷).
  3. ↑ 呪い can be pronounced "noroi" (curse) or "majinai" (spell).
  4. ↑ 罔両 (魍魎; Mouryo): evil spirits of the mountains, rivers, trees, and stones.
  5. ↑ Spiriting Away: Refers to the mysterious disappearance of a person that happens when an angered god takes a person away.
