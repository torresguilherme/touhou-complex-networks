Enemy Characters[edit]

 氷の妖怪
Youkai of the Ice

Cirno
Stage 1 Midboss

The midboss of Stage 1, Cirno says hello with a small flurry of icicles. Her power over cold seems rather weak today. Maybe it's because everyone is used to the winter chill by now and is dressed for the occasion.


冬の忘れ物
What Winter Left Behind

Letty Whiterock
Stage 1 Boss

The boss of Stage 1, Letty is a youkai of the winter season, and is happy but slightly troubled to see winter lasting as long as it has. As a youkai that can only exist during winter, she appears to you as a possible mastermind behind the delay of spring. Her attacks consist of snowball barrages and icy winds.


凶兆の黒猫 (Stage 2)
すきま妖怪の式の式 (Extra Stage)
Black Cat of Bad Omens (Stage 2)
Shikigami of the Shikigami of the Sukima Youkai (Extra Stage)

Chen
Stage 2 Midboss and Stage 2 Boss; Extra Stage Midboss

The boss of Stage 2, Chen is the acting guardian of Mayohiga, and attempts to chase you away, even though you only ended up there because you got lost in the snow. As a nekomata, she has cat-like speed and agility, which she uses to misdirect you in battle. She also seems to like spinning a lot. And she's the mid-boss of the extra stage too.


七色の人形使い
Seven-Colored Puppeteer

Alice Margatroid
Stage 3 Midboss (twice) and Stage 3 Boss

The boss of Stage 3, Alice is actually part of the magician race, whereas Marisa is a magician by occupation. She is a doll collector and although her dolls seem to be living they are in fact being managed by her with strings of magic.


春を運ぶ妖精
Spring Bringing Fairy

Lily White
Stage 4 Midboss

The midboss of Stage 4, Lily announces the coming of spring and attacks with a barrage of directed and unconcentrated bullets.


騒霊ヴァイオリスト
Poltergeist Violinist

Lunasa Prismriver
Stage 4 Boss

One of the bosses of Stage 4. She is the eldest daughter of the ghost orchestra (Prismriver Sisters) made by Layla Prismriver and her instrument of choice is the violin.


騒霊トランペッター
Poltergeist Trumpeter

Merlin Prismriver
Stage 4 Boss

One of the bosses of Stage 4. She is the middle daughter of the ghost orchestra (Prismriver Sisters) made by Layla Prismriver and her instrument of choice is the trumpet.


騒霊キーボーディスト
Poltergeist Keyboardist

Lyrica Prismriver
Stage 4 Boss

One of the bosses of Stage 4. She is the youngest daughter of the ghost orchestra (Prismriver Sisters) made by Layla Prismriver and her instrument of choice is the keyboard.


幽人の庭師
Phantom-Human Gardener

Youmu Konpaku
Stage 5 Midboss and Stage 5 Boss; Stage 6 Midboss

The boss of Stage 5, Youmu is the half-human, half-ghost gardener of Hakugyokurou. She is the one tasked with gathering enough spring so that her master, Yuyuko Saigyouji, can unseal the body underneath the Saigyou Ayakashi. She is also the fencing teacher of Yuyuko. Her attack style is focused on using her swords to assault from all directions.


幽冥楼閣の亡霊少女
Ghost Girl in the Netherworld Tower

Yuyuko Saigyouji
Final Stage Boss

The boss of the Final Stage, Yuyuko is the Master of Hakugyokurou. She is the true mastermind behind the ongoing winter and seeks to take the spring that the player has gathered. With the ability to invoke death in living beings, she's a formidable opponent.


すきま妖怪の式
Shikigami of the Sukima Youkai

Ran Yakumo
Extra Stage Boss; Phantasm Stage Midboss

The boss of the Extra Stage.


神隠しの主犯
Mastermind behind the Spiriting Away

Yukari Yakumo
Phantasm Stage Boss

The boss of the Phantasm Stage.
