Description

CYTOKINE is a doujin label run by Unagi Kabayaki that primarily produces illustrative works based on the Touhou universe.

From 2006 to 2011, Linjin also released arrangement albums under the CYTOKINE label, but as of the end of 2011, CYTOKINE became a circle solely for illustrated works, and all of Linjin's new musical works are now released under the new circle name ZYTOKINE.
