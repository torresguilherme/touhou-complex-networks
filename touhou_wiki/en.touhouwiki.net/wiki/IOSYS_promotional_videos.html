The are some animations that are similar to the ones listed here, except they don't feature an IOSYS song. This is because flash making circles also produce promotional animations for other musical groups, such as Conagusuri.

Stops at the affected area and immediately dissolves ~ Lunatic Udongein[edit]

患部で止まってすぐ溶ける　～ 狂気の優曇華院 (Stops at the affected area and immediately dissolves: Lunatic Udongein, Kanbu de tomatte sugu tokeru ～ Kyouki no Udongein) was made by locker room production. The title indeed contains jokes about suppositories, which is what Reisen's danmaku look like. The song is also known as just Overdrive, as this word is repeated successively in the beginning, and the song's actual title is quite obscure.

The song is an arrangement of Reisen Udongein Inaba's theme, and the flash video features her and other characters from Imperishable Night. It's very tangled, making a reference to her ability to induce lunacy.

The video has made the meme "Easy modo" well-known to the Western community.

Usatei[edit]

Song
Circle

Album

Touhou Suisuisuusuu (2007)

Read lyrics

Flash video
Creator

locker room production

Watch

The title Usatei (ウサテイ) is composed from the word usagi (ウサギ, rabbit) and Tewi Inaba's first name (Tei is the alternative form).

This flash features song that is an arrangement of Tewi's theme from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, with some parts from stage 5 theme from Imperishable Night, although only the full song includes the latter. It has been arranged by myu314 and sung by beatmario (ビートまりお), who are the members of COOL&CREATE circle. However, this song has been released only under IOSYS label.

Touhou Makashinsai[edit]

Border of Extacy[edit]

Song
Circle

IOSYS

Album

Touhou Makashinsai (2008)

Read lyrics

Flash video

Watch
Watch (more explicit)

This infamous flash video for infamous song wasn't released by IOSYS. It's listed here for convenience. IOSYS released the other[1] non-flash PV for this song. The song is an arrangement of Yuyuko Saigyouji's theme from Perfect Cherry Blossom.

It's considered "not safe for work" because of sexually suggestive elements, which the song itself also includes, though. However, the song is more figurative, while the video contains explicit content.

There are two versions of this flash, they're very similar, but the second one contains a bit more explicit action.
