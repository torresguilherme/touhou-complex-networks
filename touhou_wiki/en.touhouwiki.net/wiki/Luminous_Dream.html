Touhou Luminous Dream is a fan-made Touhou Project game made for in the Danmakufu 0.12m engine by Professor_scissors. The game is full-fledged of 6 stages and an extra stage, and has 2 playable characters, Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame.

Gameplay[edit]

Main articles: Gameplay, Strategy, and Spell Cards

There are two playable characters for the game, Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame, each having their unique shot type.

Story[edit]

Main articles: Story and Characters

The sun in Gensokyo was stolen from the sky, so the heroines set out to bring it back. Luminous Dream features nine original boss characters and four endings.

Music[edit]

Main article: Music

The music for Luminous Dream was composed by Joel "Raven-kun" B.
