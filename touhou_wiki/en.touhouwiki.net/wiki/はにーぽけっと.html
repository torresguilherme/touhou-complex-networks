Discography
恋綴里
Release date: 2011-09-11 (Reitaisai SP2)
An album featuring arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object. It features 6 redone versions of arrangements contributed to A-One album and 2 new arrangements.

恋綴里-第二話-
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires.

恋綴里-第三話-
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Trojan Green Asteroid.

恋綴里-第四話-
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Fairy Wars, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

恋綴里-第五話-
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.

恋綴里-第六話-
Release date: 2015-05-01 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Hopeless Masquerade, Double Dealing Character, Impossible Spell Card, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise, Ghostly Field Club and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense.

恋綴里-改訂版-
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Magical Astronomy. It is a remake of the circle's first Touhou arrangement album 恋綴里 and features redone versions of the album minus one track as well as 3 tracks not from the original album.

恋綴里-第七話-
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Double Dealing Character, Urban Legend in Limbo and Changeability of Strange Dream.

恋綴里-第八話•上-
Release date: 2016-10-30 (Kagaku Seiki no Café Terrace 6)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Dr. Latency's Freak Report. The album is notable for featuring a number of guest guitarists.

恋綴里-第八話•下-
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Dateless Bar "Old Adam". The album is notable for featuring a number of guest guitarists.

マスターカルテットスパーク！！
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
A collaboration album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Dealing Character. It features one arrangement by A-One, Karento & Eurobeat Union each.
