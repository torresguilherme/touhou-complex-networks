Discography
セツナライト
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

サクラツナギ
Release date: 2009-03-08 (Reitaisai 6)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

Ghost Ship e.p.
Release date: 2009-09-02
A short original album featuring vocal and instrumental compositions.

REFLECT
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An original album featuring vocal compositions.

コガネイロファンタスティック
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

カナデレイン
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night.

PARALLEL
Release date: 2011-12-31 (Comiket 81)
An original album featuring vocal compositions.

Star Ocean e.p.
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Ten Desires.

カズラナデシコ
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

Acoustic Time
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Scarlet Weather Rhapsody. It features acoustic arrangements of songs previously released by the circle.

TALK TO THE MUSIC
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
An original album featuring vocal compositions.

白い宇宙
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires.

スリーセブン
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Mountain of Faith. It is a 7 year anniversary album featuring full band re-recordings of songs released by the circle between 2008 and 2010.

スリーセブン -The Second Half-
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes form Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism and Ten Desires. It is a 7 year anniversary album featuring full band re-recordings of songs released by the circle between 2011 and 2013.

アヤメクリスタル
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Double Spoiler and Ghostly Field Club.

MYSTIC
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An original album featuring vocal compositions.

KILLER TUNES
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
an album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Dealing Character.
