Description

About SuganoMusic
 SuganoMusic is a music circle with a focus on making Eurobeat. Our activity is making original music CD, and offer the music for the planning CD, the game, music band, idol and so on… Originally “SuganoMusic” is the name for using only Sugano as working myself, but from July 2012, we started as circle and invite the member. We welcome for asking offer the music for the planning CD, making the music! Please give me the message in BBS and mail! And we always inviting for helping as a vocal, a songwriter, a instrumentalist and so on!
