Touhou Mayohiga TV ~Hakurei Shrine ☆ Produce~ (東方マヨヒガＴＶ～博麗神社☆プロデュース～, Touhou Mayoiga TV ~Hakurei Jinja ☆ Produce~) is a doujin album by とらのあな released on 2011-12-29 (Comiket 81). It's a package containing a music album with Touhou arrangements, illustrations by various artists drawing Touhou characters and a small comic book.
