Sci-Fi ROMANCE TRAVELER is a doujin album by 暁Records released on 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92). An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid, Neo-traditionalism of Japan, Dr. Latency's Freak Report and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".
