BGM: 晚星之梦 BGM: The Evening Star's Dream
——守矢神社
坐落于幻想乡北部妖怪之山上，即使是天气很好的日子也很少有人会爬到这么高的的方来
今天也会是一如既往地没有一个人来参拜的吧？		 ——The Moriya Shrine.
Located at the northernmost part of Gensokyo atop Youkai Mountain, few people would climb to such a high place even on days with perfect weather.
Perhaps today will be yet another day without a single visitor?

早苗：『啊！你们是前一阵子引起流星异变的桃雨姐妹吧』

Sanae: "Ah! You're the Momosame sisters who caused the shooting star incident a while back."

洛薰：『你好，前些日子真是给你们添麻烦了』

Rakukun: "Hello, sorry for causing you inconvenience during those past few days."

洛熙：『十分抱歉』

Rakuki: "Really sorry about that."

早苗：『没关系的啦，都已经是过去的事情了』
早苗：『初来乍到幻想乡，谁都难免做出一些比较出格的事情来嘛』

Sanae: "Oh, don't worry about it, it's in the past already."
Sanae: "When people first arrive in Gensokyo, they almost always do something rather out of line."

神奈子：『所以说，你们是来请教一些初入幻想乡的生活经验？』

Kanako: "So, you are here to ask us about our experience as newcomers to Gensokyo?"

洛薰：『是的，之前靠灵梦小姐的帮助，总算是在人里住了下来』
洛薰：『但是发现幻想乡这个神奇的地方与外界差别太大了』
洛薰：『有很多没有的东西，也有许多只存在于幻想中的新鲜事物』

Rakukun: "Yes, thanks to Reimu's help previously, we considered staying in the Human Village,"
Rakukun: "but we realised that this mysterious Gensokyo is too different from the Outside World."
Rakukun: "There are many things that don't exist in Gensokyo, yet there are also many new things that only exist here!"

神奈子：『嘛，要说经验的话倒是也没什么，只要注意不要做出一些出格的事情就好』
神奈子：『早苗，你有什么建议吗？』

Kanako: "Well, there's not much regarding our experience in Gensokyo, just take care not to overstep your bounds."
Kanako: "Sanae, do you have any suggestions?"

早苗：『唉？我吗？』
早苗：『其实，我们是做好准备之后搬来幻想乡的』
早苗：『所以比起物质上的变化，心态的转变才是更重要的吧』

Sanae: "Eh? Me?"
Sanae: "Well, actually, we had already made some preparations before moving into Gensokyo."
Sanae: "So compared to material changes, an attitude change is more important."

洛熙：『心态上的转变吗？』

Rakuki: "A change in attitude?"

诹访子：『进入了幻想乡，就说明你们相对外界已经变成了幻想的存在』
诹访子：『你们已经穿过了现实与幻想的境界』

Suwako: "If you've entered Gensokyo, it shows that your existence is but a fantasy to the Outside World."
Suwako: "You have already crossed the boundary between reality and fantasy."

洛薰：『所以之前的祈祷术才会失败？』

Rakukun: "So is that why the prayer ritual earlier failed?"

诹访子：『是这样的。存在于幻想之中的人是无法直接去干涉现实的』

Suwako: "That's the case. It is not possible for those existing in a fantasy to directly intervene with reality."

洛熙：『也就是说幻想乡与外界是类似“庄周梦蝶”的关系吗？』

Rakuki: "So the Gensokyo and the Outside World have a relationship similar to Zhuangzi's butterfly dream?"

神奈子：『并不完全是，幻想与现实二者都是“真实”』

Kanako: "Not entirely, because both Gensokyo and the Outside World are 'real'."

洛熙：『那么，我们就真的回不去了吗？』

Rakuki: "So, there really is no way for us to return?"

神奈子：『为什么要回去呢？』
神奈子：『会来到幻想乡，就说明你们身上一定发生了什么』
神奈子：『即使返回外界，也只是被所有人遗忘的存在』
神奈子：『对现在的你们而言，幻想乡无疑是最好的容身之所』

Kanako: "Why do you want to go back?"
Kanako: "If you were able to enter Gensokyo, it means something must have happened to you."
Kanako: "Even if you returned to the Outside World, your existence would be forgotten by everyone."
Kanako: "For you right now, Gensokyo is undoubtedly the best place to take refuge in."
桃雨姐妹相顾无言
即使返回外界，的确留给她们的只有一片悲痛
虽然曾经的家园难以被忘却
但是那已经变为既不存在于幻想也不存在于现实的虚无了
不过她们应该很快就能适应幻想乡的生活，开启新的人生的吧		 The Momosame sisters were speechless.
Even if they returned to the Outside World, all that awaited them there was heartbreak.
Although they would never forget their home,
it no longer existed in reality or fantasy any longer.
However, they should be able to adapt to living in Gensokyo quickly, and start new lives.
Ending No. 03 来自外界的新邻居
恭喜全部通关！		 Ending No. 03: The new neighbor from the Outside World
