Discography
anoare
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Double Spoiler, Ten Desires and Double Dealing Character'.

anoare TD
Release date: 2015-10-18 (Autumn Reitaisai 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires.

anoare DDC
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character.

dot
Release date: 2016-10-16 (Autumn Reitaisai 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power, Shoot the Bullet, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Double Spoiler, Fairy Wars, Hopeless Masquerade and Impossible Spell Card.

ordinary strange
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object. It is the first to incorporate arrangements by people other than なかじまみや and features a greater variety of arrangement styles.

anoare PCB
Release date: 2017-10-15 (Autumn Reitaisai 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom.

wired
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

weather break
Release date: 2018-10-14 (Autumn Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Double Dealing Character, Hidden Star in Four Seasons and Changeability of Strange Dream.

present day
Release date: 2019-05-05 (Reitaisai 16)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Hidden Star in Four Seasons and Strange and Bright Nature Deity 1 (CD).
