Description

iTi are a Kyoto based Touhou rock arrangement circle made up of 4 guys, the circle boasts live instrumentation in their arrangements as apposed to programming.[1]


Discography
FADE TO SCARLET
Release date: 2016-10-09 (Touhou Kouroumu 12)
An album featuring arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Phantasmagoria of Flower View. The album's original release featured sound issues with track 3, but an updated version featuring the original 4 tracks + a bonus was released at Reitaisai 14.[2][3]

roomside
Release date: 2017-10-22 (Touhou Kouroumu 13)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom and Mountain of Faith.

DAWNBREAK e.p.
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.
