Murasame Umbrella (群雨アンブレイラ, murasame anbureira) is a circle established by シイナフユキ (Shiina Fuyuki)that mainly focuses on Story attached Rock & Electronic arrangements.

Description

群雲サンシェイド(Murakumo Sunshade) - A related vocal arrange circle founded by シイナフユキ, for more information on the circle see Murasame Umbrella Archive.
