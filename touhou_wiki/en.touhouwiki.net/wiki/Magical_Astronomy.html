Celestial Wizardry ~ Magical Astronomy (大空魔術　～ Magical Astronomy, Oozora Majutsu ~ Magical Astronomy) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2006-08-13 (Comiket 70). It is part of "ZUN's Music Collection" and it is the fifth volume (vol.5).

Tracks

Cosmic and hard music collection performed by Shanghai Alice Illusionary Band, Part 5

    • Welcome to the Moon Tour
  • 02. 天空のグリニッジ (03:43)
    • Greenwich in the Sky
  • 03. 東の国の眠らない夜 (04:07)
    • Sleepless Night of the Eastern Country
    • original title: 東の国の眠らない夜
    • source: 東方文花帖　～ Shoot the Bullet
  • 04. 車椅子の未来宇宙 (05:06)
    • The Wheelchair's Future in Space
  • 05. Demystify Feast (04:31)
    • original title: Demystify Feast
    • source: 東方萃夢想　～ Immaterial and Missing Power
  • 06. 衛星カフェテラス (04:12)
    • Satellite Café Terrace
  • 07. G Free (04:09)
  • 08. 大空魔術　～ Magical Astronomy (04:28)

  • 09. ネクロファンタジア (06:45)
