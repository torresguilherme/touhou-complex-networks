Description

pikapika is the personal circle of lyricist and vocalist 飛花, albums by the circle are characterized by her high pitched vocals that often emulate a cutesy tone. Releases by the circle will most commonly feature lyrics and vocals by 飛花, with arrangements being contributed by a number of Touhou arrangement circles. Arrangers who have been regularly featured on albums by pikapika include: つきいち of Retractable, 松浦 of MatsuYani, もちとん of mochi_season, 味付け玉子 of AZITAMATEISHOKU, あるす of Satellite Himawari and ユキ of SNOWBLUE.


Discography
東方でざいあ
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
A short 2 track single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Subterranean Animism.

Chocolate Pain
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object. Its theme is "Sickly cute", it is the first album to feature this theme with ワールド・エンド・ファナティック being the second.

紅ベビィドォル
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil. It's a yuri album themed around Flandre and Remilia Scarlet.

promise of eternity
Release date: 2012-09-09 (Kyoukai kara Mieta Keshiki -Meguru-)
A collaboration album with mochi_season featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

幻影モノクローム
Release date: 2012-10-07 (Touhou Kouroumu 8)
A short 2 track collaboration single with 深層記憶システム featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Magical Astronomy and Ghostly Field Club.

境界パラドクス
Release date: 2012-10-07 (Touhou Kouroumu 8)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Magical Astronomy, Retrospective 53 minutes and Ghostly Field Club.

アコースティックレイン
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires.

BABy,LoLLiPoP
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires. Its theme is "Pop and cute".

恋色アリス
Release date: 2013-06-30 (Nanairo Majo no Ningyou Budoukai 3)
A short 2 track single featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom.

アップルテイスト
Release date: 2014-02-02 (Dai-Kyuu-Shuu Touhousai 9)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Undefined Fantastic Object. It notably features arrangements provided by AZITAMATEISHOKU exclusively.

ワールド・エンド・ファナティック
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Double Spoiler, Ten Desires, Hopeless Masquerade and Double Dealing Character. Its theme is "Sickly cute", it is the second album to feature this theme with Chocolate Pain being the first.

ブルーアワーにとけたゆめ
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

ツインドライブ!!
Release date: 2014-11-24 (Autumn Reitaisai)
A collaboration album with AZITAMATEISHOKU featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires and Impossible Spell Card. It notably features vocal duets by 味付け玉子 and 飛花 on every track.

デウス・エクス・マキナ
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Fairy Wars and Ten Desires. Its theme is "Puberty".

恋・罠・虹フランドール
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
A 2 disc Flandre Scarlet themed album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Subterranean Animism. It features a handful of re-recordings and a wealth of arrangements contributed by a number of circles.

少女のための空想理論
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Neo-traditionalism of Japan, Dr. Latency's Freak Report and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".
