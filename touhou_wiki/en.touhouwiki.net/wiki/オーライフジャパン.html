Discography
東方奔放戯
Release date: 2008-05-25 (Reitaisai 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith.

東方満月響
Release date: 2008-08-16 (Comiket 74)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night and Silent Sinner in Blue.

東方温泉祭
Release date: 2008-11-02 (Touhou Kouroumu 4)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Subterranean Animism.

望郷舞踏宴
Release date: 2009-03-08 (Reitaisai 6)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night.

異臭幻想狂
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
A Metal album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Magical Astronomy.

円盤満作祭
Release date: 2009-10-11 (Touhou Kouroumu 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Undefined Fantastic Object.

鋭鋒乃風標
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith.

気象無原則
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Touhou Hisoutensoku, and Touhou Hisoutensoku.

歌唱能音盤
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, and Subterranean Animism.

ゴツまさ倶楽部
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes and Magical Astronomy. It is a collaboration with k-waves LAB.

特臭幻想狂
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
A Metal album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, and Ten Desires.

壮快廟探訪
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ten Desires.

激写人妖録
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet and Double Spoiler.

謀叛和楽陣
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
A Traditional album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Double Dealing Character.

積極懐古団
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Mystic Square and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

序臭幻想狂
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
A Metal album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires, Double Dealing Character and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

こちら秘封探偵事務所 Music Collection 紅魔郷編
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense. The album is meant to compliment a novel series by Rhythm Five centering around Renko Usami and Maribel Hearn.

月面弾幕会
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

オーライフジャパンベスト
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
A best-of compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Touhou Hisoutensoku. It features 2 or 3 arrangements from each of the 9 albums released by O-LIFE.JP from 2008 to 2010.

オーライフジャパンゲスト
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
A compilation album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires. It features remastered and improved versions of guest arrangements released on albums by 梶迫小道具店, ShibayanRecords and Toranoana.

四季音方陣
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Hidden Star in Four Seasons.
