Stage Titles[edit]

There are a total of 6 stages in the full version of the game, plus one extra stage:

Stage 1 八百万の秋の神 Myriads of Autumn Gods
Stage 2 神々の疵痕 Scars of the Gods
Stage 3 瑕疵無き要塞 The Flawless Fortress
Stage 4 要塞の山 The Mountain Fortress
Stage 5 霊山に風が吹く A Wind Blows on the Sacred Mountain
Stage 6 あゝ風の神よ神湖の地に Ah, the God of Wind on the Grounds of the Divine Lake
Extra Stage 愉快な日本の神様 A Merry Japanese God

Difficulty Levels[edit]

Like most of the other Windows games, each of the difficulty levels have a short summary.

Easy - A difficulty level to get you used to the controls. Advancement is basic for humans.
Normal - A difficulty level to enjoy the game. Having faith in yourself will bring happiness.
Hard - A difficulty level for those with a good deal of self-confidence. No need to stop and linger to enjoy the performance.
Lunatic - The usual. If you don't know what that means, don't pick it.
Extra - Oh, you want to play? You're pretty curious, aren't you?
