Boss Spell Card #3[edit]

Spell Card 23
創符「ペインフロー」 (Soufu "Pein Furō")
Wound Sign "Pain Flow"
Owner
Hina Kagiyama
Stage 2 — Easy
Editor's Comment
An infinite source of torment.
Pain is the last thing to go.
Spell Card 24
創符「ペインフロー」 (Soufu "Pein Furō")
Wound Sign "Pain Flow"
Owner
Hina Kagiyama
Stage 2 — Normal
Editor's Comment
An infinite source of torment.
You never get used to pain; it changes just when you think you're okay.
Spell Card 25
創符「流刑人形」 [2] (Soufu "Ryuukei Ningyou")
Wound Sign "Exiled Doll"
Owner
Hina Kagiyama
Stage 2 — Hard
Editor's Comment
A flurry of small shots deters wanderers from the source of woe.
Exile can be self-imposed too. Should we pity those who choose to do so?
Spell Card 26
創符「流刑人形」 (Soufu "Ryuukei Ningyou")
Wound Sign "Exiled Doll"
Owner
Hina Kagiyama
Stage 2 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
A blizzard of small shots deters wanderers from the source of woe.
It's for others' good, though. Hina is doing a good thing.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Reference to the last member of the Ohgane house in Tōtōmi, who died without designating an heir. She would continue to haunt her former territories as a will-o'-the-wisp after her death.
  2. ↑ 流刑 (ryuukei): means "banishment/exile", but also contains the kanji 流 ("drift/flow"), tying in with Hina's nature as a nagashibina (流し雛, doll set adrift in a river).
