Spell Card 41
河童「のびーるアーム」 (Kappa "Nobīru Āmu")
Kappa "Exteeeending Aaaaarm" [2]
Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Stage 3 — Hard
Editor's Comment
Nitori uses a laser device designed to grip intruders and halt them.
It isn't quite an airtight trap, but it works well enough to snare most. Keep watching the web as it forms.
Spell Card 42
河童「スピン・ザ・セファリックプレート」 (Kappa "Supin za Sefarikku Purēto")
Kappa "Spin the Cephalic Plate" [3]
Owner
Nitori Kawashiro
Stage 3 — Lunatic
Editor's Comment
Nitori attempts to mystify you with a spell as esoteric as its name.
Does it make it easier to understand if the spinning circles align?

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 1.0 1.1 Pororoca is a tidal bore, with waves up to 4 meters high that travels 13 kilometers inland upstream the Amazon River (Brazil). The Pororoca occurs when the river water meets the Atlantic Ocean, best seen in February and March. In the indigenous Tupi Language, Pororoca means: great destructive noise.
  2. ↑ Possibly in reference to a detail in folklore where a kappa's arms were connected in the middle of their body, and pulling on one arm would stretch it out while shrinking the other.
  3. ↑ The cephalic plate is the frontmost plate of the chiton, a form of mollusk.
