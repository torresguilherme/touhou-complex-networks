Back Border of Performance Set of Concealed Music and Dance Backdoor Contredanse (後弾奏結界 秘匿対舞曲 Backdoor Contredanse, Godansou Kekkai Hitoku Taibukyoku Backdoor Contredanse) is a doujin album by dBu music released on 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93). It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Hidden Star in Four Seasons.
