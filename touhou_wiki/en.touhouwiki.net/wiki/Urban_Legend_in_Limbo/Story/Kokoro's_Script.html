Kokoro defeats Reimu[edit]

Kokoro

ぎゃあ
顔とお面の隙間に女がいる！

おっとお面が違うわ
驚きはこっちこっち

Gya!
There's a woman in the gap between my face and my mask!

Oops, wrong mask.
Here’s the one for surprise.

Kokoro defeats Marisa[edit]

Kokoro

その校長先生のお面（？）を
ちょーだいな

Hey, gimme that principal’s mask(?).

Kokoro defeats Ichirin[edit]

Kokoro

ポポポポーン
それ何処から発してるの？

Popopo~n.
Where did that originate?

Kokoro defeats Byakuren[edit]

Kokoro

うわあビックリした
変な婆さんが二輪車でー！

えーとお面間違えた
これが驚きのお面

Uwaa, you startled me!
A weird old lady on a motorbike!?

Uuum, I got the wrong mask...
This is the mask for surprise.

Kokoro defeats Futo[edit]

Kokoro

お皿、貸してあげるわ
余りにも惨めで……

I'll let you borrow a plate from me.
This is just too miserable...

Kokoro defeats Miko[edit]

Kokoro

私はピンクのマントがいいわー

I want the pink cape!

Kokoro defeats Nitori[edit]

Kokoro

うわああでっかい怪獣だー
心臓が止まるかと思った

えーとえーと
驚きのお面を付けなきゃ

Uwaaaa, what a huge monster!
I thought my heart was gonna stop...

Um, ummm...
I gotta put on my surprised mask!

Kokoro defeats Koishi[edit]

Kokoro

私は全方位に顔を持っている
背後に廻った所で驚かないぞ

I have faces pointed in all directions.
Even if you sneak up behind me, I'm not gonna be surprised!

Kokoro defeats Mamizou[edit]

Kokoro

宇宙人可愛い！
こういう時の表情って
これで合ってるかなぁ

Aliens are so cute!
Would this be the right expression for times like this, I wonder?

Kokoro defeats herself[edit]

Kokoro

私だけは知っている
実はこの中にドッペルゲンガーの
オカルト持ちがいる事を

I’m the only one who knows that in reality,
there’s someone among us using the Doppelganger occult.

Kokoro defeats Mokou[edit]

Kokoro

私には死ぬ死なないの概念が
よく判っていないのです

I don't think I really understand
the concept of dying or not dying...

Kokoro defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Kokoro

あの逆さのお城の主ですか
欠陥住宅は大変ですね

Are you the master of that reversed castle?
Construction defects sure are awful.

Kokoro defeats Kasen[edit]

Kokoro

三つの願いを叶えたら
何が起こるのです？

What happens
if all three wishes are granted?

Kokoro

その包帯……
古傷が痛む事もあるのでしょうか

Those bandages...
Do even scars occasionally hurt, I wonder?

Kokoro defeats Sumireko[edit]

Kokoro

貴方、若い人間ね
希望ある若者は実に羨ましい

You’re a young human, right.
I'm truly jealous of hopeful youths...

Kokoro

外の世界か……
希望ある世界は実に羨ましい

The Outside World, huh.
I'm truly jealous of hopeful worlds...

Kokoro defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Kokoro

その赤い眼こわーい

What a scary red eye.

Kokoro

その赤い眼こわーい
なんだか……

何か複雑な感情で支配されそう
これが狂気の感情？

What a scary red eye.
What's this... I feel overwhelmed by some complicated emotions.
Is this the feeling of madness?

Generic victory[edit]

Kokoro

ねえねえ
アタシキレイ？

Hey, hey.
Am I pretty?

Kokoro

ねえねえ
アタシキレイなの？

Hey, hey.
Do you think I'm pretty?

Kokoro

ねえねえ
アタシキレイでしょ？

Hey, hey.
I'm pretty, right?
