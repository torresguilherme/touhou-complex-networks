Urban Legend in Limbo/Story/Shinmyoumaru's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Urban Legend in Limbo‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Shinmyoumaru defeats Reimu[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

そんな隙間
縫い付けてやる！

I'm gonna take that gap
and sew it up!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Marisa[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

そんな大きな厠に
いかないもん

I can't use
such a big bathroom.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Ichirin[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

八尺……
一尺が十寸だから八十倍……

8 Shaku....
1 Shaku is 10 sun, so that's times 80....[1]

Shinmyoumaru

一寸法師と八尺様って
何か名前からして
仲間だと思うの

"Issun Boushi & Hasshaku-sama".
If you go by their names, it somehow sounds like they’d be friends.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Byakuren[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

私だって
乗り物に乗ってるもん

I’ve also got my own ride, hmph!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Futo[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

お皿貸してみ？
この針で皿回し出来るもん

Lemme borrow a plate?
I can spin it on my needle and everything!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Miko[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

血が抜かれる都市伝説なんて
怖くないもん
赤も青もいらないもん

I’m not scared of no Urban Legend about having your blood drawn!
And I don’t want either red nor blue, hmph.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Nitori[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

頭のこれはお皿じゃないよ
河童じゃないよ
ほんとだよ

This thing on my head isn’t a plate!
I’m not a kappa!
I’m really not!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Koishi[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

ぎゃあ後ろにいたー！

Gyaaaa, she was right behind meee!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Mamizou[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

物語で狸はえてして最後
タヌキ汁になるのよ

In the stories, tanuki often become tanuki-soup at the end.

Shinmyoumaru

このお椀にタヌキ汁入れたら
匂いが取れなさそうだなー

If I fill this bowl with tanuki-soup,
I bet the smell would never come out...

Shinmyoumaru defeats Kokoro[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

私サイズのお面が
無いなんてつまらない

It’s so boring they don’t even make masks my size.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Mokou[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

熱い熱い
耐熱お椀じゃ無ければ
煮えていたわ！

Hot, hot, hot!
If I didn't have a heat resistant bowl,
I would'a been cooked up!

Shinmyoumaru defeats herself[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

やったー
私に勝ったー！

Hooray!
I beat me!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Kasen[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

仙人？　貴方が？
ふーん、何か変な匂いがするねぇ

You? A hermit?
Huh. Smells fishy to me.

Shinmyoumaru

貴方を見ていると

目を突いてやりたくなるのよね
理由は判らないけど

When I’m looking at you,
just feel like I wanna poke your eyes out, see.
No idea why though.

Shinmyoumaru defeats Sumireko[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

ぎゃあ、あの変態だー！
逃げろー

Gyaa, it's that pervert!
Run awaaay!

Shinmyoumaru

今は夢の中だから
捕まえないって？
でも逃げろー

So, you’re saying you can’t catch me because you’re dreaming right now?
Still, I will run awaaay!

Shinmyoumaru defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

あんた月の兎なんだって？
色々話を聞きたいわー

So you are a rabbit from the moon?
Tell me about that place.

Shinmyoumaru

可愛いのを臼に入れるの辞めて！
キャラが被るぅ！

Please, stop putting that cute thing in your mortar!
That's my role!

Generic victory[edit]

Shinmyoumaru

小が大を兼ねたって良いじゃない

The small should just band together with the big.

Shinmyoumaru

全部集めて大きくなるぞー！

I'm gonna collect them all and get bigger!

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Hasshaku means "Eight Shaku". Shaku (尺) and Sun (寸) are units of length, corresponding approximately 30.3 centimeter or 11.9 inches and 30.3 millimeters or 1.2 inches respectively.
