Ah, yeah,

Someone interesting?

違う違う

お前の後ろには超巨大な

No, that's not it.

It's behind you.

What?

冗談なもんか

そ、そんな脅したって

A-threat like that
won't work on me.
There's nothing there.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Despite being up against Mokou, this stage takes place in front of Miko's dojo, and not in the Bamboo Forest. This provides the context for their conversation.
  2. ↑ Probably a parody (or idea-borrowing) of a TV program USO!? Japan (ＵＳＯ！？ジャパン). It introduced some strange tales like spirit photos, spirit spots, urban legends, etc.. Some teenangers took these topics as truth, but others deride them to point out that the title "USO" was "lie" (嘘) in Japanese, while the program explained that it was the abbreviation of "Unconfirmed Story Object", i.e. "rumor".
