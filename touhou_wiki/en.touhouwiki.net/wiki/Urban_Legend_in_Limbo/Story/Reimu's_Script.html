Urban Legend in Limbo/Story/Reimu's Script

Reimu defeats herself[edit]

Reimu

ドッペルゲンガー？
だれのオカルト？

A doppelganger?
Whose urban legend is that?

Reimu defeats Marisa[edit]

Reimu

怪奇は数が多ければ強い
ってもんじゃないでしょ

It's not like your mysteries are stronger
just because you've got more of them.

Reimu defeats Ichirin[edit]

Reimu

敗因はオカルトが
マイナーすぎるからよ

You lost because your
urban legend is too minor.

Reimu defeats Byakuren[edit]

Reimu

何か知らないけど
あんた見た目相当ヤバイわ

I’m not sure why,
but that look is awfully fitting for you.

Reimu defeats Futo[edit]

Reimu

そう、お皿割っちゃったの……
それは呪われて仕方が無いわね

Yeah, you broke the plate...
Guess it figures you'd be cursed for that.

Reimu defeats Miko[edit]

Reimu

うーん意表を突いて
青が良い！

Hmm. I'm gonna defy expectations
and say "blue"!

Reimu

やっぱり赤にしようかな

Maybe I should go with red after all?

Reimu defeats Nitori[edit]

Reimu

あんたは都市伝説を
はき違えている

You're going about this 'urban
legend' thing the wrong way.

Reimu defeats Koishi[edit]

Reimu

電話って拒否できないの？
煩わしいわねぇ

Can you not just turn down a phone call?
Sounds like a pain.

Reimu defeats Mamizou[edit]

Reimu

宇宙人は案外可愛いわね

Aliens are cuter than I expected.

Reimu defeats Kokoro[edit]

Reimu

裂け女って
般若の面の事なの？

By "Kuchisake-onna", do you mean
that Hannya mask of yours?[1]

Reimu defeats Mokou[edit]

Reimu

あんた一応人間でしょ？
何で燃えてるのよ

You are basically human, right?
Why are you on fire?

Reimu defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Reimu

お椀にお湯を注いでやろう
いい出汁が取れるかも知れないわ

I'm gonna pour some hot water into that bowl.
I might get a nice broth out of it.

Reimu defeats Kasen[edit]

Reimu

その手で願いが叶うなら
オカルトボールは要らないわ

If you can grant wishes with that paw,
you don't need the Occult Balls.

Reimu defeats Sumireko[edit]

Reimu

夢の中でも肉体ごと
こっちに来てるのねぇ

Huh, you come over here in the flesh
even when you’re dreaming.

Reimu

結局さぁ……

月の都のオカルトボールは
あんたの物じゃ無いのね？
どういうことだろう

So, basically...

The Lunar Capital's Occult Ball
isn't yours, is it?
What's up with that?

Reimu defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Reimu

都市伝説異変を解決するためには
また月の都に行くしかないのかな

Reimu

この都市伝説異変って
あの無口な月の民が
起こしたんだって？

いつになったら収まるのよ

that reticent person of the moon

Generic victory[edit]

Reimu

あー
隙間があったら入りたい

Ahh...
I just wanna crawl into a gap and disappear.[2]

Reimu

普通にやれば
もはや負ける要素はない

If I just go about things normally,
there's no way I'll lose.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 般若, Hannya is one of the most iconic Noh masks. Resembling an oni, it is used to portray the common archetype of the vengeful woman who lost her humanity. It is one of Kokoro's 66 masks and the one she uses to symbolize the Kuchisake-onna's revealed face.
  2. ↑ The Japanese line is a plays on the expression 穴があったら入りたい, If there was a hole I'd want to crawl into it, which is a manner of expression embarrassment or awkwardness.
