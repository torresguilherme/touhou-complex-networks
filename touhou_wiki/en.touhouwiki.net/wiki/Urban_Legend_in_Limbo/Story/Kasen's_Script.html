Urban Legend in Limbo/Story/Kasen's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Urban Legend in Limbo‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Kasen defeats Reimu[edit]

Kasen

またうちで修業する？
今度は爬虫類コースよ

Want to train at my place again?
It'll be the Reptile Course this time.

Kasen defeats Marisa[edit]

Kasen

魔力の使い方が
まるで判っていない
いつか死ぬよ？

It’s as if you don’t know how to use magic properly.
You will die eventually, you know?

Kasen defeats Ichirin[edit]

Kasen

八尺様ってもしかして
尺八を逆さに持っているって事？

"Hasshaku-sama"? Does that mean you're
holding a shakuhachi backwards?

Kasen

尺八を逆さに持っているのが
八尺様だとすると

ポポポってのは
上手く吹けないから出る音で……
なる程

If "Hasshaku"-sama refers to
a "shakuhachi" held backwards...

Then is the 'popopo' sound the noise
that comes from the shakuhachi when you
play it poorly...? I see.

Kasen defeats Byakuren[edit]

Kasen

バ、バイク？
ババアがバイクとはこれぞ怪奇

A m-motorcycle?
An old granny on a motorcycle is definitely bizarre.

Kasen defeats Futo[edit]

Kasen

私はそんな低レベルな仙人と
一緒にされたくないわね

I'd really prefer not to be lumped together
with such a low-level hermit.

Kasen defeats Miko[edit]

Kasen

丹余ってない？
ちょっと切らしちゃって

Got any cinnabar?
I'm running a little low.

Kasen defeats Nitori[edit]

Kasen

河童は相変わらずか……

Well, the kappa never change...

Kasen

霊夢に商売の仕方を
教えてやって

見るに堪えないわ

Go teach Reimu some business.

She's just dreadful to watch.

Kasen defeats Koishi[edit]

Kasen

地霊殿のお嬢ちゃんね
お姉ちゃんが心配してたわよ

Oh, you're the young missy from the Palace.
Your big sister's been worried about you.

Kasen defeats Mamizou[edit]

Kasen

狸はやっぱり食えない
扱いにくい動物ね

As expected, tanuki are both inedible and hard to tame.

Kasen defeats Kokoro[edit]

Kasen

今年もまた能をやるんでしょ？
今年の演目はやっぱり口裂け女？

You're performing noh this year too, right?
This year’s performance is going to be Kuchisake-onna, as expected?

Kasen defeats Mokou[edit]

Kasen

もう死神からも狙われない
孤独で哀れな死人よ
私は同情するよ

Oh, poor, lonely departed one
who even the shinigami no longer target.
I can sympathize.

Kasen

自らを燃やすのは
擬似火葬という訳だ

私はそれを人体自然発火現象と
名付けてやろう

You setting yourself on fire would equal a mock-cremation.

I will name this “Spontaneous Human Combustion”.

Kasen defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Kasen

緑の小人って
七人いる奴だっけ？

Was this the one with the seven green dwarfs?

Kasen defeats herself[edit]

Kasen

これは怪奇現象
じゃないのかしら？

Could this be some kind of
supernatural phenomenon?

Kasen defeats Sumireko[edit]

Kasen

夢の中だけなんて……
どういう力が働いているのか

To think this is only your dream...
Just what force is at work here?

Kasen

私には判るわ

貴方は自分が持つ
本当の都市伝説を
隠しているでしょう？

You can’t fool me!

You’re hiding your own true Urban Legend, aren’t you?

Kasen defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Kasen

最近、吹っ切れたみたいね
月の都との因縁は
綺麗になったのかしら？

Kasen

霊夢から聞いたわ

都市伝説異変は月の民が
引き起こした物なんだって？

I heard from Reimu.

Apparently this urban legend incident was caused by the People of the Moon.
Was this your doing?

Generic victory[edit]

Kasen

こんな所で
本気を出すわけが無い

There's no way I'd reveal my full strength
at a time like this.

Kasen

猿の手で願いが叶うなら
私は……

猿の手のままでいて貰おう

If the monkey's paw can grant wishes,
then I...

I think I'll just stick with the monkey's paw.
