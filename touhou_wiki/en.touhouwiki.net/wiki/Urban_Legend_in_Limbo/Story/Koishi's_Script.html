Urban Legend in Limbo/Story/Koishi's Script

From Touhou Wiki

Jump to navigation Jump to search

Koishi defeats Reimu[edit]

Koishi

神社に行っても
誰も私に気付かないのよね

Even if I go to the shrine, no one will notice me.

Koishi

私の方が隙間にいるみたい

It's as if I'm the one in a gap.

Koishi defeats Marisa[edit]

Koishi

貴方の帽子重いわね
何が入ってるの？

Your hat looks really heavy.
What'cha got in there?

Koishi defeats Ichirin[edit]

Koishi

健康そうで良いなー
私も地上に住もうかなー

It must be nice to be so healthy...
Maybe I should live on the surface too!

Koishi defeats Byakuren[edit]

Koishi

その乗り物良いなー！
乗らせてよー

I love that vehicle!
Lemme ride it!

Koishi

そうそう貴方のお寺に
水蜜って奴いるでしょ？ あの子、未だに血の池地獄の味が
忘れられずたまに戻ってくるのよ
知ってた？

Oh yeah, there’s a girl at your temple called Minamitsu, right?
She still can’t forget the charm of the Hell of Blood Pools, so she visits sometimes.
Didja know?

Koishi defeats Futo[edit]

Koishi

はい、念願の十枚目は
今貴方の後ろに……

Yes, the tenth plate you desire is
right behind you...

Koishi defeats Miko[edit]

Koishi

私は青が良いわー！

I want the blue one!

Koishi

不老不死って言ったって
やっぱり死ぬんでしょ？
みんなそうだもん

Even if you say you have everlasting youth and life,
you really will die, right?
Everyone does.[1]

Koishi defeats Nitori[edit]

Koishi

ねー雑貨屋さーん
その怪獣が欲しいなー

Hey, Miss shopkeeper!
I really want that monster!

Koishi defeats herself[edit]

Koishi

もしもーし
私も後ろに居るの

Hello, hello!
I'm right behind me, too.

Koishi defeats Mamizou[edit]

Koishi

そのボール
オカルトボールならぬ
エイリアンボール……！

‘Tis ball....
‘Tis ain’t no Occult Ball... but an Alien Ball!

Koishi defeats Kokoro[edit]

Koishi

私の方がキレイだもん

I’m prettier than you anyway!

Koishi defeats Mokou[edit]

Koishi

不老不死って言ったって
いずれ死ぬんでしょ？
みんなそうだもん

Even if you say you’re immortal you’ll die eventually, right?
Everyone does.[2]

Koishi defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Koishi

私もそのお椀に入りたーい！

I wanna get inside that bowl, too!

Koishi

あー、お椀の中整理してないなー
判る判るー　私も片付け苦手でねー

Aha, so you haven’t cleaned up your stuff in there!
Oh, I totally get it! I’m terrible with cleaning, too!

Koishi defeats Kasen[edit]

Koishi

願いは三つでしょー？
欲しいのは天と地と人かなー

You can wish for 3 things right?
Hmm, I think I'd want everything, nothing, and anything...[3]

Koishi

願いは三つでしょー？
やっぱり過去と現在と未来かなー

You can wish for 3 things right?
No question, I want Past, Present and Future...

Koishi defeats Sumireko[edit]

Koishi

貴方の携帯電話？

それっぽいのは私が持ってるわ
最近、うんともすんとも
言わなくなっちゃったけど

Your mobile phone? I’ve got something like that.
I’ve been at a loss for what to do lately though.

Koishi

え？　今は夢の中なの？
ふっしぎー、夢の中に入ったぞー

Eh? You’re dreaming right now?
How fantastic, I’m appearing in someone’s dream!

Koishi defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Koishi

あ、兎さん
こんにちわ
その眼には私の姿が映るのね

Oh, miss Rabbit.
Hello there.
So you can see me from those eyes of yours.

Generic victory[edit]

Koishi

もしもーし
今貴方を倒したの

Hello, hello!
Right now, I've just beaten you.

Koishi

もしもーし
今貴方の背中の上にいるの

Hello, hello!
Right now, I'm on top of your back.

Koishi

もしもーし
今貴方の屍を越えて
未来に向かおうとしているの

Hello, hello!
Right now, I’m stepping over your corpse as I try to advance into the future.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ There is very similar dialogue in the previous Tasofro fighting game, Hopeless Masquerade.
  2. ↑ Same as above.
  3. ↑ 天と地と人, Heaven, Earth and People is the phrasing used in the original Japanese. This term refers to all of the world: the known universe, everyone and everything existing within it.
