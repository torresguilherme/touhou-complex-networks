Urban Legend in Limbo/Story/Nitori's Script

From Touhou Wiki
< Urban Legend in Limbo‎ | Story
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Nitori defeats Reimu[edit]

Nitori

ニッチな怪奇現象を
選ぶなんてやるな

You sure know how to pick
a niche phenomenon, huh.

Nitori defeats Marisa[edit]

Nitori

学校自体がよく判らん

I don’t really get what schools are all about.

Nitori

新しい箒いります？
三十二年前から変わらぬ値段です

Would you care for a new broom?
The price remains the same as 32 years ago![1]

Nitori defeats Ichirin[edit]

Nitori

大きさなら負けないぞ！
遥かにな

I'm not gonna lose in a contest of size!
Not by a long shot!

Nitori defeats Byakuren[edit]

Nitori

そ、その乗り物は……？
うーん興味深い
未知のテクノロジーだ

W-what is that vehicle....?
Oh, I am highly interested in this
yet-to-be discovered technology.

Nitori defeats Futo[edit]

Nitori

あ、お皿買いますか？
今なら九枚セットに
もう一枚おつけします

Ah, are you in the market for plates?
If you order now, we'll throw in
one free plate with every nine purchased!

Nitori defeats Miko[edit]

Nitori

ただで貰えるなら
赤と青、両方貰うよ

If you’re giving ‘em away for free,
then I’ll take both red and blue.

Nitori defeats herself[edit]

Nitori

今のはモブの河童Ａだ！

That was Generic Kappa A!

Nitori

今のは疲れ目からくる幻像だ！

That was an illusion caused by my tired eyes!

Nitori defeats Koishi[edit]

Nitori

け、携帯電話だと？
そうか都市伝説には
そんな使い方が……

A-a mobile phone?
I see, so that’s a way
of using Urban Legends...

Nitori defeats Mamizou[edit]

Nitori

宇宙人がそんなローテクでは
許されないぞ！

These low-tech aliens are no good!

Nitori defeats Kokoro[edit]

Nitori

新しいお面をご紹介します
どれもお似合いですよ

Allow me to show you our new selection of masks.
They would all look great on you!

Nitori defeats Mokou[edit]

Nitori

うちの機械は火気厳禁なんでねぇ

Our machines are inflammable after all.

Nitori defeats Shinmyoumaru[edit]

Nitori

まさか、うちのシマで
魚釣ってたのはお前だなー

Unbelievable. So you're the one
who's been going fishing on our turf.

Nitori defeats Kasen[edit]

Nitori

うちのネッシーの話が判るって？
本当に？

You can understand what my Nessie’s saying?
Really?

Nitori

霊夢さんに
言ってやってくださいよ

うちの商売の邪魔になるから
変な事しないでってー

Could you please
let Miss Reimu have it?

She’ll just be interfering with our business,
so tell her to not do anything weird.

Nitori defeats Sumireko[edit]

Nitori

水に強い道具が
無いなんて遅れてるな

You’re so behind on the times
you don’t even have waterproof tools.[2]

Nitori

スマホ？
何だ、水に弱いのか、使えん

A smartphone?
What, it’s not waterproof? Not interested.[2]

Nitori

面白いもんいっぱい持ってるな
ちょっと見ーせーろーよー

You’ve sure got a lot of interesting stuff.
Hey, come on and show me!

Nitori defeats Reisen (PS4 Only)[edit]

Nitori

なんだい
今更、祭りに参加してきたって
分け前やんないよ

Nitori

お、その拡声器見せてみな……
何だか未知の技術で出来ているな
これがルナテクノロジーって奴か
うーん何一つ判らん

Generic victory[edit]

Nitori

この高いオカルトエネルギーを
全てネッシー号に！

This high occult energy
is all going to Nessie!

Nitori

オカルトボールの
エネルギー変換率が５％以下か
何やってるんだ、低すぎる！

The Occult Ball Energy
conversion rate is less than 5%.
What the hell’s going on, that's way too low!

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ It refers to Nitori's vs-mode dialog against Marisa of Hopeless Masquerade ― Urban Legend in Limbo was released two years after Hopeless Masquerade.
  2. ↑ 2.0 2.1 A reference to Nitori's vs-mode dialog against Mamizou in Hopeless Masquerade.
