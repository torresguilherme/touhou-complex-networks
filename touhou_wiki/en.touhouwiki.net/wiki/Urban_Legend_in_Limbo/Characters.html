Urban Legend in Limbo/Characters

From Touhou Wiki
< Urban Legend in Limbo
Jump to navigation Jump to search

In Urban Legend in Limbo, up to 14 (15 including Unzan) characters are selectable in the game, each with their own scenario. The complete cast of Hopeless Masquerade re-appear in this game, plus four new characters; two from previous games, one originally from a manga, and one début.

Playable Character

Revealed on the official website.


Unzan

Banchou! The Plate-Breaking Shikaisen

Mononobe no Futo

怪奇！二色マント魔人
Bizarre! The Fiend of the Two-Colored Mantle

Toyosatomimi no Miko
Playable Character; Ichirin Scenario, Byakuren Scenario, Kokoro Scenario, and Sumireko Scenario Final Boss

Revealed on the official website.

Ancient! The Triassic Kappa

Playable Character; Occult Boss

Revealed on the official website.

侵略せよ！宇宙妖怪狸
Invade! The Youkai Tanuki from Space

Playable Character; Kasen Scenario and Shinmyoumaru Scenario Penultimate Boss; Nitori Scenario Final Boss

激熱！人間インフェルノ
