Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ The super-ego is one of the three parts of the human psyche according to Freudian psychology. It contains the moral conscience and is always repressing the id (see below) from influencing the ego unconsciously. Repressing these morals would mean she has no concept of morals at all and runs purely on natural instinct.
  2. ↑ The id is another of the three parts of the human psyche according to Freudian psychology. It is also unconscious like the super-ego, but it's aggressive and demanding to the both the ego and the super-ego. Freud believes that it mostly thinks about sex and instinct, an infamous and widely controversial assumption, but he also stated that somewhere in this unconscious area are the memories of a person's first three years after being born, which are the most important and shaping years of a person's life in his psychological field. Generally speaking, the majority of Freudian psychology is spent on understanding the id and finding and unlocking these repressed memories.
  3. ↑ A variant of one of the spell cards of EX mode of Subterranean Animism, Symbol "All Ancestors Standing Beside Your Bed" (表象「夢枕にご先祖総立ち」).
