Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ ('soushi souai') is a phrase originating in Yankee delinquent subculture, referring to risking one's life in motorcycle riding. The phrase is a play on the identically-pronounced phrase "相思相愛", meaning 'deeply in love with one another'.
  2. ↑ All of Buddhist cosmology is encompassed by the Great Trichiliocosm (三千大千世界, trisāhasra-mahāsāhasra-lokadhātu). A chiliocosm (from Greek chilioi "thousand" + kosmos "world") is 1000 world systems of the Indian cosmological sense. A thousand chiliocosms become a medium dichiliocosm, and a thousand dichiliocosms become a great trichiliocosm. It is said that a single supreme nirmanakaya buddha has mastery over the trichiliocosm.
  3. ↑ Five Element Mountain (五行山 is a mountain which Sun Wukong was sealed by Buddha after Buddha tricked him on the palm.)
