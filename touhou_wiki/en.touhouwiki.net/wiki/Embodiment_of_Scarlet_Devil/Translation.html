An English patch for the game is also available, though this old patch is based on much older translations.

Shot Types[edit]

Reimu[edit]

Basic Performance

移動速度：★★★ Movement Speed: ★★★
攻撃範囲：★★★★ Attack Range: ★★★★
攻撃力：★★★ Attack Power: ★★★
Reimu A[edit]
霊の御札 Spirit Spell Card
: 「ホーミングアミュレット」 : Homing Amulet
: 「夢想封印」 : Fantasy Seal
Excellent against swarms of weak enemies, but less effective against bosses.
Reimu B[edit]
夢の御札 Dream Spell Card
: 「パスウェイジョンニードル」 : Persuasion Needle
: 「封魔陣」 : Evil-Sealing Circle
A good balance of damage and coverage, but the bomb is fairly weak.

Marisa[edit]

Basic Performance

移動速度：★★★★ Movement Speed: ★★★★
攻撃範囲：★ Attack Range: ★
攻撃力：★★★★ Attack Power: ★★★★
Marisa A[edit]
魔の御札 Magic Spell Card
: 「マジックミサイル」 : Magic Missile
: 「スターダストレヴァリエ」 : Stardust Reverie
Excellent against bosses, but only covers a small area of the screen.
Marisa B[edit]
恋の御札 Love Spell Card
: 「イリュージョンレーザー」 : Illusion Laser
: 「マスタースパーク」 : Master Spark
A little lacking in both damage and coverage, but the bomb is very strong.
