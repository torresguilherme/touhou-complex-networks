Super Forest Beat VOL.2 is a doujin album by Silver Forest released on 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93). It features vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Immaterial and Missing Power and Hidden Star in Four Seasons, as well as seven original compositions.
