Description

The circle was formed by 3 flute players after their previous circle Machikado-Mapoze disbanded. The circle produces miscilanious goods as well as doujin albums which are often acompanied by illustrations.


Discography
針妙丸のちいさな冒険
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires, Double Dealing Character, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense. The album is accompanied by a set of illustrations themed around Shinmyoumaru Sukuna.

お嬢様の華麗なファルス
Release date: 2016-01-24 (紅のひろば13)
An illustration package featuring an instrumental arrangement of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

喫茶化猫でおしゃべりを
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Urban Legend in Limbo, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, Ghostly Field Club], Magical Astronomy and Neo-traditionalism of Japan as well as compositions by Stephen Foster, Paolo Tosti, Gustav Holst and more.

古明地姉妹のしあわせレシピ
Release date: 2016-06-05 (こめいじコンプレックス)
An Satori Komeiji and Koishi Komeiji themed goods package featuring an instrumental arrangement of themes from Subterranean Animism

こもれびスケッチブック
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Fairy Wars, Double Dealing Character, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Shuusou Gyoku.

紅魔館アルバムプレビュー版
Release date: 2016-10-09 (Touhou Kouroumu 12)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, it is a previous of the album 私の紅魔館.

私の紅魔館
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

今年の思い出 -2016-
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
A best of compilation album featuring arrangements released by the circle from late 2015 through to 2016.

狼と幻想郷
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Touhou Hisoutensoku, Double Dealing Character and Forbidden Scrollery.
