Discography
葉椿
Release date: 2007-08-17 (Comiket 72)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom and Imperishable Night. The album's tracks would be featured on 葉椿／花椿.

葉椿／花椿
Release date: 2008-08-16 (Comiket 74)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Retrospective 53 minutes. It features the 6 tracks previously released on 葉椿 in addition to new arrangements.

sasanqua e.p.
Release date: 2008-12-29 (Comiket 75)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism and Ghostly Field Club.

confeit e.p.
Release date: 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Mountain of Faith.

dominoes e.p.
Release date: 2009-12-30 (Comiket 77)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Retrospective 53 minutes.

stereophonic wonder
Release date: 2010-08-13 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism.

stereophonic voyager
Release date: 2010-12-29 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ghostly Field Club.

stereophonic discover
Release date: 2011-12-29 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Double Spoiler.

honey
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Undefined Fantastic Object, Fairy Wars and Ten Desires.

sweet
Release date: 2011-12-30 (Comiket 81)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, and Ten Desires.

bitter
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense.

魔法少女と活字1
Release date: 2012-08-11 (Comiket 82)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Ten Desires, Trojan Green Asteroid and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense.

FLOWERs:1
Release date: 2012-12-30 (Comiket 83)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Ten Desires, Retrospective 53 minutes, Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

FLOWERs:2
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Undefined Fantastic Object and Ten Desires.

FLOWERs:3
Release date: 2013-08-12 (Comiket 84)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Undefined Fantastic Object, Double Dealing Character and Ghostly Field Club.

stereophonic remaster + remixes
Release date: 2013-12-30 (Comiket 85)
A 3 disc compilation album featuring every track from the albums stereophonic wonder, stereophonic voyager (save for 1 track) and stereophonic discover split across 2 discs as well as a 3rd remix disc.

PATCHOULI KNOWLEDGE RACER (Direct Audio)
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Mystic Square, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Subterranean Animism as well as an original composition.

honey,sweet &bitter
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 12)
A 2 disc compilation album featuring featuring every track from the albums honey, sweet and bitter split across both discs.

rosa canina
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
A Kasen Ibaraki themed album featuring instrumental arrangements her theme from Urban Legend in Limbo exclusive. Remixes of every track from the album were released on rosa canina remixes the following year.

rosa canina remixes
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
A Kasen Ibaraki themed album featuring instrumental arrangements her theme from Urban Legend in Limbo exclusive. It features remixes of every track from the previous album rosa canina.

cirno out #1
Release date: 2016-08-13 (Comiket 90)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Double Dealing Character, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Retrospective 53 minutes.

cirno out #2
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom, Dateless Bar "Old Adam" and Silent Sinner in Blue.

魔法少女と活字2
Release date: 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Double Dealing Character, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

magical star collection 01
Release date: 2017-12-29 (Comiket 93)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet, Undefined Fantastic Object and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.

cirno out #3
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Urban Legend in Limbo, Antinomy of Common Flowers, Hidden Star in Four Seasons and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

magical star collection 02
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object and Antinomy of Common Flowers.

PATCHOULI KNOWLEDGE RACERS (Direct Audio)
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
Two albums featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Undefined Fantastic Object, Urban Legend in Limbo and Hidden Star in Four Seasons.
