Description

"封滅の蓮花蝶" from the album 星影メモリア featuring ℃iel on vocals.

MISTY RAIN is a Touhou doujin music circle who's arrangements are primarily done in a high tempo Hard-Rock style characterized by vintage guitars & equipment (Fender Telecaster USA '68, Fender Jazzmaster Custom shop NOS '63, etc.) often resulting in anachronistic guitar tones in addition to also being characterized by punchy sounding technical drums. Music by the circle is arranged and primarily preformed by sole member ながもん often with bass support from FND, おつぽん or タカ of Sun Flower Field & Iris Drop. Vocals are primarily contributed by female vocalist 青砥 雫 (aka ℃iel) who has been featured on nearly every album by the circle and has even been the only vocalist featured on some albums (Star Guide Literacy and 星天の夢見草), female vocalists レジ (niconico mylist (Requires account)) and tompi are also featured on many of the circle's releases.

Much of the circle's early discography (specifically their first 3 albums) has been re-recorded and featured on later releases, most notably the circle's 2nd and 3rd albums Scarlet Scenery & 風詠神楽 originally released in 2010 & 2011 respectively were completely redone in 2014 featuring re-recordings & remasters of tracks from the original albums in addition to new arrangements. A 3D Touhou animation titled "SONIC SPARK" by MUD SPIKE!/impulse (creator of touhou-dogfight) was produced featuring the opening track from the album 風詠神楽 (2011) and is now featured on MISTY RAIN's official website, it can be viewed here on Youtube.


Discography
STAR DUST FAN CLUB
Release date: 2010-02-28 (Touhou Ezomatsuri)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Mountain of Faith, Shoot the Bullet, Subterranean Animism and Shuusou Gyoku. A mostly instrumental album, many of the tracks from it would be re-recorded with vocals for later releases by the circle.

Scarlet Scenery
Release date: 2010-08-14 (Comiket 78)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Shuusou Gyoku. Roughly half of it's tracks would be re-recorded for the 2014 album of the same name Scarlet Scenery.

風詠神楽
Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object. The entire album with the exception of the closing acoustic track would be re-recorded for the 2014 album of the same name 風詠神楽.

白南風　靡いて　雲の峰
Release date: 2011-08-13 (Comiket 80)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Imperishable Night and Mountain of Faith. A short 4 track album featuring 2 songs that would appear on the circle's next release in addition to 2 songs from the previous album sung by different vocalists.

Star Guide Literacy
Release date: 2011-09-11 (Reitaisai SP2)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Shoot the Bullet and Dolls in Pseudo Paradise.

星天の夢見草
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Ten Desires, Undefined Fantastic Object and Magical Astronomy.

星影メモリア
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Subterranean Animism, Fairy Wars and Shuusou Gyoku.

Drizzle Incident
Release date: 2013-10-13 (Touhou Kouroumu 9)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night and Subterranean Animism. A short 4 track album featuring vocals by mineko of MN-logic24, it contains 2 re-recordings of previously released tracks in addition to 2 new songs unique to the album.

Scarlet Scenery
Release date: 2014-05-11 (Reitaisai 11)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night and Shuusou Gyoku. It is a redo of the circle's 2rd album Scarlet Scenery released in 2010 and features 4 re-recorded tracks from the original album album, 2 new tracks and 2 remasters from the album 星天の夢見草.

風詠神楽
Release date: 2014-10-12 (Touhou Kouroumu 10)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Mountain of Faith and Undefined Fantastic Object. It is a redo of the circle's 3rd album 風詠神楽 released in 2011 and features remasters/re-recordings of almost the entire original album in addition to 2 new songs.

春宵パラレルライン
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Undefined Fantastic Object.

暁月クラウン
Release date: 2016-10-09 (Touhou Kouroumu 12)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom and Initial D.

idealism
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy, Neo-traditionalism of Japan and Dr. Latency's Freak Report

余りに日常的な
Release date: 2018-05-06 (Reitaisai 15)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night and Shoot the Bullet.

Other Works

Perfect Blue
Released Date: 2012-10-28 (M3-30)
A split album featuring original vocal compositions by ながもん of MISTY RAIN and タカ of Iris Drop & Sun Flower Field, ながもん's arrangements for the album features more pop overtones compared to his Touhou works.
