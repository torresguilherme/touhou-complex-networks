Sweetest Paranoia is a doujin album by Amateras Records released on 2017-08-11 (Comiket 92). An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim. Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.
