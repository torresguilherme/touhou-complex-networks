少女フラクタル

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Shoujo Fractal (少女フラクタル) is Yuuhei Satellite's sister circle formed in 2014. Songs of this circle are arranged by Yuuhei Satellite's arrangers along with two new members as vocalists.

Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)

インヴィジブル

君の気付かない君の優しさ

An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View and Ten Desires in addition to two original compositions.

秘神は背中で燃えている
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith, Double Dealing Character and Hidden Star in Four Seasons in addition to two original compositions.

Realidea
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Mountain of Faith and Subterranean Animism in addition to four original compositions. Riza Yuzuki's solo album.

孤独月
Release date: 2018-08-10 (Comiket 94)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Mountain of Faithin addition to five original compositions. Partially consists of vocal covers of Yuuhei Satellite's songs; Miya Amamiya's solo album.

絵空事でいいから。
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires, Hidden Star in Four Seasons, Dolls in Pseudo Paradise and Kioh Gyoku in addition to two original compositions.

シングルズベストVol.1 果てなき風の軌跡さえ
Release date: 2018-12-30 (Comiket 95)
An album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Mountain of Faith and Hopeless Masquerade. It is a collection of singles released previously.
