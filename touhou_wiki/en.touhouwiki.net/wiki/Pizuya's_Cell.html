Description

Pizuya's Cell are a doujin music circle known for their arrangements of themes from the Touhou Project series of games. The circle's musical style can be broadly described as a combination of 3 elements; Metal/Rock, Classical/Orchestral and Electronic, combinations of these 3 styles (Electro-Metal with Strings for example) are what makes up the majority of the circle's music. Early albums by the circle were primarily instrumental Metal but releases since have branched out to feature vocal arrangements and Electronic elements, instrumental Piano centric albums have become a staple for the circle are are released in tandem with their regular albums.
