Discography
秘封倶楽部の午後
Release date: 2012-05-27 (Reitaisai 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes and Magical Astronomy·

Trio ～Girls meet A Girl～
Release date: 2012-09-09 (Kyoukai kara Mieta Keshiki -Meguru-)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense. The album notably features Jazz style arrangements.

弦樂三郭天
Release date: 2012-11-04 (Kagaku Seiki no Café Terrace 2)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith and Double Spoiler. The album notably features Classical style arrangements.

round trip
Release date: 2013-05-26 (Reitaisai 10)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey, Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

Starry Fairy Party
Release date: 2013-10-13 (Touhou Kouroumu 9)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil and Fairy Wars. The album notably features Jazz style arrangements.

こはくの幻想盤
Release date: 2013-11-03 (Kagaku Seiki no Café Terrace 3)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Ten Desires and Perfect Memento in Strict Sense as well as an original composition inspired by a scene from Forbidden Scrollery.

den-gen
Release date: 2015-05-10 (Reitaisai 13)
A collaboration album with Mikan Box featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Undefined Fantastic Object, Changeability of Strange Dream and Retrospective 53 minutes.

From... To...
Release date: 2015-10-12 (Kagaku Seiki no Café Terrace 5)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Changeability of Strange Dream, Retrospective 53 minutes, Magical Astronomy, Trojan Green Asteroid and Neo-traditionalism of Japan .

夜虹の入江
Release date: 2015-11-01 (Touhou Kouroumu 11)
A collaboration album with にぬきボ村 and Forest306 featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and The Grimoire of Marisa.

vous ensemble
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes form Ghostly Field Club, Magical Astronomy, Unknown Flower, Mesmerizing Journey and Neo-traditionalism of Japan.

Nothing is Over
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
An album featuring instrumental arrangements of themes from Ghostly Field Club, Dr. Latency's Freak Report and Dateless Bar "Old Adam".
