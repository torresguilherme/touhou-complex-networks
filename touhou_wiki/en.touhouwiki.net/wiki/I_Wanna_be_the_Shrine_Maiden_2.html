I Wanna be the Shrine Maiden 2 is an English-made Touhou Project spin-off of the popular masochistic platformer "I wanna be the guy" by Kayin. The platformer is a sequel to I Wanna be the Shrine Maiden with many moving platforms, invisible blocks, spikes that fly out at you, and bosses that attack you with danmaku. Like the previous game, the game is free and can be downloaded here. It has been described by the developer as "INCREDIBLY difficult, and INCREDIBLY frustrating."
