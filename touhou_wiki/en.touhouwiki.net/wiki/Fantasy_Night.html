Touhou Kireiyoru ~ Fantasy Night (東方綺麗夜 ～ Fantasy Night, lit. "Eastern Beautiful Night"), known in English as Fantasy Night of TouHou Project, is a fan-made vertical shooting game for the iPhone. It's notable for being one of the first Touhou Project derivative works to go against ZUN's content distribution guidelines, as well as for using fanart and assets from the official games without permission, mainly Imperishable Night. ZUN is aware of its existence,[1] and at one time had it removed from the AppStore. As of December 19th, 2011, it has returned to the iTunes store as a free app.
