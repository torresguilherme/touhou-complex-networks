Touhou Gensou Mahjong Original Soundtrack (東方幻想麻雀オリジナルサウンドトラック, Tōhō gensō mājan orijinarusaundotorakku) is a doujin album by Disaster + D.N.A.Softwares released on 2009-08-15 (Comiket 76). It features instrumental arrangements of themes from Highly Responsive to Prayers, Story of Eastern Wonderland, Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Immaterial and Missing Power, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Changeability of Strange Dream and Uwabami Breakers as well as several original compositions. It is the original soundtrack to the game Touhou Unreal Mahjong and notably features several compositions by ZUN.
