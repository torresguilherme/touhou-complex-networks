How to watch replays[edit]

  • For EoSD ~ PCB, SA ~ UFO: save the replay on the Replay subfolder. Name structure for user replays is "thxxudyyyy.rpy".
  • For MoF: save the replay on the Replay subfolder. MoF does not support user replays, meaning you'll be limited for 25 player replays. Name structure for player replays is "thxxyy.rpy".
  • For FW ownards: unlike the previous games, the Replays subfolder is not located in the game's subfolder, but on the user's appdata instead, at "%appdata\ShanghaiAlice\" inside the according game's folder. Name structure is the same as EoSD~etc games.
