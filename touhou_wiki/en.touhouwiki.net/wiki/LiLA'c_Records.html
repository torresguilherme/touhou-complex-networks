LiLA'c Records

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

LiLA'c Records (リラレコーズ, Rira rekōzu) is a doujin music circle, which creates Touhou albums, among others.

Profile

Dates
2010 - Current
Website
LiLA'c Records official website
Genre
Electronic, House, Trance, Techno, Hardcore, DnB, Jpop, Vocal, Instrumental
Members
Irus (also known as irus8) — Executive Producer, Composer, Arranger, Guitarist, Bassist & Lyricist (twitter)
Ex-members
lapix — Producer, Composer & Arranger (twitter)
DiEL — Arranger & Designer
ddl — Enginner & Arranger

Description

Sometimes you will see "ILL" as an arranger of LiLA'c Records album. That means the track was arranged by Irus and lapix.

FAR EAST BEAT

LiLA'c Records SAMPLER 01
Release date: 2010-03-14 (Reitaisai 7)
An album featuring instrumental and vocal arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

HAKUREI RAVE 01
Release date: 2010-06-20 (Tora Matsuri 2010)
It is An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object. It's a non-stop (re)mix of tracks from different albums and includes track unique to the album

LiLA'c Records SAMPLER 02

STAR GAZE
Release date: 2010-12-30 (Comiket 79)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Undefined Fantastic Object, and Fairy Wars. It's a non-stop (re)mix of tracks from different albums.

Release date: 2011-03-13 (Reitaisai 8)

An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism and Undefined Fantastic Object.

An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object, Touhou Hisoutensoku and Ten Desires. It is a compilation of tracks released on albums by the circle from 2009 to 2012.

REVIVE 2 ARCADIA

TOHO SPEED 02
Release date: 2014-08-16 (Comiket 86)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Hopeless Masquerade and Double Dealing Character.

TOHO SPEED 03
Release date: 2014-12-29 (Comiket 87)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Undefined Fantastic Object, Fairy Wars and Hopeless Masquerade.

TOHO SPEED 04
Release date: 2015-08-14 (Comiket 88)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith and Scarlet Weather Rhapsody.

Nature2
Release date: 2015-12-30 (Comiket 89)
A compilation album featuring vocal arrangements of themes from Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Hopeless Masquerade.

TOHO SPEED -Vertex Tuned-
Release date: 2016-05-08 (Reitaisai 13)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom. It features a number of remixes and re-recordings.

TOHO SPEED 05
Release date: 2016-10-09 (Touhou Kouroumu 12)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Undefined Fantastic Object and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom.

TOHO SPEED -VERTEX TUNED2-
Release date: 2016-12-29 (Comiket 91)
An album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Imperishable Night, Undefined Fantastic Object, Ten Desires and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom as well as an original composition.

TOHO SPEED BEST TRAX [2013-2017]
Release date: 2017-05-07 (Reitaisai 14)
A 2 disc best-of album featuring vocal and instrumental arrangements of themes from Phantasmagoria of Dim.Dream, Lotus Land Story, Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Perfect Cherry Blossom, Imperishable Night, Phantasmagoria of Flower View, Shoot the Bullet, Mountain of Faith, Scarlet Weather Rhapsody, Subterranean Animism, Undefined Fantastic Object and Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom. It features 5 new arrangements in addition to a selection of tracks from the TOHO SPEED and Vertex Tuned album series.
