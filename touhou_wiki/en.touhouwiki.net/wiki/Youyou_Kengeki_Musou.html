Youyou Kengeki Musou (妖々剣戟夢想, meaning Youyou Swordplay Dream) is a 3D platformer game starring Youmu.

Gameplay[edit]

This game plays much like Ys.

Story[edit]

Youmu Konpaku, the gardener of Hakugyokurou, is worried that her master, Yuyuko Saigyouji, didn't come to the sword training (among other things, Youmu is Yuyuko's sword instructor). Yuyuko never showed much interest to the training, but had never abandoned it before. When Youmu pointed out it to her, Yuyuko dodged the question and then set a condition that Youmu should defeat Reimu Hakurei, and then she would consider her demand.

Youmu knew that she would hardly be able to defeat Reimu at her current level, and that Yuyuko probably just wanted to shirk the training, but she had no other ideas. Will she be able to defeat Reimu? With these thoughts she leaves Hakugyokurou to find the shrine maiden.
