About me[edit]

I've been a Touhou fan since 2009, when I stumbled upon some kickass arrangements by Demetori and WAVE.
I sucked big time at the games at first, but my skills gradually improved over the years.

Favourite games:

  • Perfect Cherry Blossom - 1cc Lunatic with ReimuB and default lives; it only took 5 years of practice ^.^
  • Subterranean Animism - 1cc Hard with all characters
  • Shoot the Bullet - cleared all spellcards; combined photo score: 30,815,470

Favourite fangame: Labyrinth of Touhou 2

Favourite music circles: Demetori, EastNewSound, Liz Triangle, Magnum opus, SJV-SC, SYNC.ART'S, TAMUSIC, WAVE, Yellow Zebra
