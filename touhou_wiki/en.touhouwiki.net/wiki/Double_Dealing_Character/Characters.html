Playable Characters[edit]

In Double Dealing Character, there are three playable characters to choose from.

楽園の巫女
Shrine Maiden of Paradise

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character

Her purification rod suddenly started acting on its own. When it spotted a youkai, it leapt out of her hands to drive them away. However, that was convenient for the shrine maiden, so she left it be. On one such day, she heard that the youkai at the Misty Lake were running wild. They were normally supposed to be quiet and peaceful youkai, and yet... This was, without a doubt, the beginning of an incident.


普通の魔法使い
Ordinary Magician

Marisa Kirisame
Playable Character

It seemed that the youkai at the Misty Lake were running wild. She scrambled to exterminate them before the shrine maiden took action. But at that time, her "Mini-Hakkero" was acting strangely. It started reacting to youkai, shooting fire at them on its own. But she didn't bother to repair it and let it be. It had more firepower than before, which was appealing in and of itself.


悪魔のメイド
Maid of the Devil

Sakuya Izayoi
Playable Character

The usually quiet Misty Lake was in an uproar. She could see it from the Scarlet Devil Mansion, which stood on the bank of the lake. She figured that the problem would pass, but that plan didn't work out. It seemed the lake wasn't the only place where the youkai had started running wild. With a newly acquired bewitched dagger in hand, she set off to investigate. How it came into her possession, however, is a mystery even to her...
