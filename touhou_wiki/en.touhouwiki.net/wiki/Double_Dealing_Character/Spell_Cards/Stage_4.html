Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Heikyoku (平曲) is a recitation of the Heike Monogatari with biwa accompaniment. The opening poem of the Heike Monogatari references the same bell that the spell card is named after.
  2. ↑ A reference to the opening poem of the Heike Monogatari.
  3. ↑ 送筝曲 (sousoukyoku): a pun on 筝曲 (soukyoku, 'koto music') and 葬送曲 (sousoukyoku, 'funeral dirge').
