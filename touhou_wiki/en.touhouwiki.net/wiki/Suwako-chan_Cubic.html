すわこちゃんcubic is a Touhou fan game made in 2012 by UTG Software. It is the follow-up to Suwako no Danmaku Pyonpyon Daisansaku, and is often referred to as Suwapyon 2.

In 2013 an HD version was released. In November 2013 a version was released for the Oculus Rift, which supported a partial English translation in addition to Japanese. On 3 August 2014, the HD version became available as a free download.

Gameplay[edit]

Main article: Gameplay

The game is set up in an isometric perspective, where bullets are shot by enemies in a three dimensional space. Suwako is able to move along the ground in a 2d plane, but also has the ability to jump. The game consists of 6 stages plus an Extra stage. There are also 7 difficulty levels, two of which need to be unlocked by beating the game in the hardest available difficulties.
