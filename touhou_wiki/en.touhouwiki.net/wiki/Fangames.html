The popularity of the Touhou Project has led to the creation of a high number of fan games, often featuring crossovers with many other video game franchises. The following list will try to feature as many of them as possible.

Note that if a letter in the languages column is enclosed in parentheses, it means that an unofficial (fanmade) translation patch exists in that language.

Some fangames were chosen to be ported to PlayStation 4, Vita, and/or Nintendo Switch as part of Play, Doujin!.

The release of Hidden Star in Four Seasons on Steam, as well as ZUN's blessing for other potential doujin developers to release their own Touhou games on the platform, brought forth many indie/doujin Touhou fangame releases on Steam as a result.
