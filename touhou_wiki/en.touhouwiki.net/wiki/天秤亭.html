Description

TenbinTei are a Touhou arrangement and game production circle who's work is rooted in tabletop RPGs. They has released Touhou themed supplements to games like Sword World RPG as well as arranged a number of Touhou themes with specific RPGs such as Sword World RPG, Paranoia, THE DAMNED STALKERS and Satasupe in mind.

Vocalist 冬乃桜 (her blog) is the circle's longest running recurring guest having first appeared on the circle's fourth album 東方音遊戯session4 as well as every album since.
