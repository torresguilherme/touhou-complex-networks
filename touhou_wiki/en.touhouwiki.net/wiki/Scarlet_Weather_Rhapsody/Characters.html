Characters[edit]

All these characters are playable in Scarlet Weather Rhapsody and have appearances in various story modes.

快晴の巫女
Shrine Maiden of Fair Weather

Reimu Hakurei
Playable Character; Remilia Scenario and Komachi Scenario Penultimate Boss; Aya Scenario and Tenshi Scenario Final Boss



霧雨の魔法使い
Magician of the Drizzle

Marisa Kirisame
Playable Character



小夜嵐のメイド
Maid of the Evening Storm

Sakuya Izayoi
Playable Character



雹の人形遣い
Puppeteer of the Hail

Alice Margatroid
Playable Character



花曇の魔女
Witch of the Spring Haze

Patchouli Knowledge
Playable Character



蒼天の庭師
Gardener of the Blue Sky

Youmu Konpaku
Playable Character; Tenshi Scenario Penultimate Boss



濃霧の吸血鬼
Vampire of the Heavy Fog

Remilia Scarlet
Playable Character; Aya Scenario Penultimate Boss



雪の亡霊
Ghost of the Snow

Yuyuko Saigyouji
Playable Character



幻想の狐の嫁入り
Phantasmal Wedding of the Kitsune / Sunshower of Fantasy

Yukari Yakumo
Playable Character; Remilia Scenario Final Boss; Reisen Scenario and Iku Scenario Penultimate Boss



疎雨の百鬼夜行
The Night Parade of Hundred Demons of Sprinkle

Suika Ibuki

Trivia[edit]

  • All the character's titles are a variation of their weather. For instance, Marisa's weather is Drizzle, so her Character Title is "Magician of the Drizzle".
