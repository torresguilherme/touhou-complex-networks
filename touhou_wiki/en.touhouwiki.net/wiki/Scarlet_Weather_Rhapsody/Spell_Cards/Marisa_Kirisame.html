Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
星符「ポラリスユニーク」 (Seifu "Porarisu Yuniiku")
Star Sign "Polaris Unique"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Reimu Stage 1, Sakuya Stage 1, Patchouli Stage 3, Yuyuko Stage 2, Yukari Stage 3, Aya Stage 2, Komachi Stage 1, Tenshi Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Creates an exploding star that also creates star bullets on Normal or harder.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
天儀「オーレリーズユニバース」 (Tengi "Ooreriizu Yunibaasu")
Celestial Apparatus "Orreries Universe"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Reimu Stage 1, Sakuya Stage 1, Patchouli Stage 3, Yuyuko Stage 2, Yukari Stage 3, Aya Stage 2, Komachi Stage 1, Tenshi Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Summons orbs that can fire stars, lasers or laser bullets. Harder difficulty = more orbs.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
邪恋「実りやすいマスタースパーク」 (Jaren "Minoriyasui Masutaa Supaaku")
Perverse Love "Easily-Fruitful Master Spark"
Owner
Marisa Kirisame
Patchouli Stage 3, Yukari Stage 3, Aya Stage 2, Tenshi Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Fires a thin laser, and then expands into a Master Spark.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
ウィッチレイライン (Wicchi Reirain)
Witch Leyline
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔力で箒を補強し、それを利用し
突撃する魔理沙の基本必殺技
Translation
Reinforces her broom with magic and uses it to charge forward. One of Marisa's basic special moves.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
ミアズマスウィープ (Miazuma Suwiipu)
Miasma Sweep
Cost
1 orb
Comment
箒に捕まり上昇攻撃を行う対空技
無敵時間が有り接近戦に強い
Translation
An anti-air attack where she hangs on to the broom and launches into the air. Invincible frames make it good for close combat.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
グラウンドスターダスト (Guraundo Sutaadasuto)
Ground Stardust
Cost
1 orb
Comment
接地起爆する魔法瓶を投げつける
着弾まで間が有るが投げる位置は
敵を正確にサーチ
Translation
Throws a magic potion that explodes on contact with the ground. Has some delay before impact, but is thrown precisely towards the enemy's position.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
メテオニックデブリ (Meteonikku Deburi)
Meteonic Debris
Cost
1 orb
Comment
小型マジックミサイルを乱射する
広範囲だがムラが有るのが欠点
Translation
Fires small magic missiles. Fairly wide-range, but prorates significantly.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
ラジアルストライク (Rajiaru Sutoraiku)
Radial Strike
Cost
1 orb
Comment
箒を銃身として使うことで
レイラインの推力を弾に変換した
発射は遅いが弾速、威力共に優秀
Translation
Uses the broom as a gun barrel to convert Witch Leyline's thrust into a bullet. Although it takes a while to fire, the bullet's speed and power are spectacular.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
バスキースウィーパー (Bosukii Suwiipaa)
Bosky Sweeper
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔力を移動に集中したスウィープ
箒の直接攻撃力は無くなったが
代わりにバーニアが攻撃力を持つ
Translation
A version of Miasma Sweep where the magic is used to enhance mobility. The broom itself no longer does any damage, but the thruster does damage in exchange.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
デビルダムトーチ (Debirudamu Toochi)
Devildom Torch
Cost
1 orb
Comment
改良薬剤を使用して着弾地点から
広範囲に炎を撒き散らす魔法瓶
重さで飛距離が下がるのはご愛嬌
Translation
A magic potion that uses improved compounds to scatter flames from the point of impact. It's quite charming how the increased weight decreases its flight distance.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
ナロースパーク (Naroo Supaaku)
Narrow Spark
Cost
1 orb
Comment
前方にレーザーを照射して攻撃
発射が遅いが射撃の貫通力が高く
打ち合いに捻じ込めば強力
Translation
Fires a laser straight ahead. It's slow to fire, but good at piercing through projectiles, so it's quite powerful if you can get one in edgewise during a firefight.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
アップスウィープ (Appu Suwiipu)
Up Sweep
Cost
1 orb
Comment
箒の推力を利用して回転上昇
バーニアがそのまま攻撃も兼ねる
アクロバティックな技
無敵時間は無いが判定が強いため
カウンター性能はそれなりに高い
Translation
Uses the broom's thrust to make a spinning ascent. An acrobatic technique where the thruster also does damage. No invincible frames, but its strong hitbox makes it fairly good for counters.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
ステラミサイル (Sutera Misairu)
Stellar Missile
Cost
1 orb
Comment
真上に向けマジックミサイル発射
弾丸は一定距離を飛行後炸裂する
炸裂まで無判定だが爆風は広く
持続するため上空の制圧や
空中での盾に使うことが出来る
Translation
Aims straight up and fires a magic missle. The bullet travels a fixed distance and explodes; until then, it has no hit box, but the explosion is wide and lasts long, so it's useful for air domination or as an anti-air shield.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 11
グリーンスプレッド (Guriin Supureddo)
Green Spread
Cost
1 orb
Comment
広範囲に細い拡散レーザーを発射
収束させそこに魔力球を生成する
拡散レーザーに判定はないが
反面、相殺されずに爆風で攻撃が
可能と見ることもできる
Translation
Fires wide-range spread lasers which converge to create a ball of magic energy. The spread lasers have no hitbox, but on the other hand, that means you don't have to worry about the lasers being cancelled before the blast happens.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
恋符「マスタースパーク」 (Koifu "Masutaa Supaaku")
Love Sign "Master Spark"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
彼女の代名詞である極太レーザー
照射時に前方以外に回り込まれる
リスクには要注意
Translation
Her signature freakin' huge magical laser. Be aware that you're open to attack from directions other than straight ahead while shooting.


Spell Card 2
魔砲「ファイナルスパーク」 (Mahou "Fainaru Supaaku")
Magicannon "Final Spark"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
大出力レーザーを惜しみなく照射
前方であれば逃げ場はほぼ無い上
直撃の威力も消費に対し高い
Translation
Lavishly fires a high-output laser. On top of giving enemies in front hardly anywhere to dodge, a direct hit does very high damage relative to its card cost.


Spell Card 3
星符「ドラゴンメテオ」 (Seifu "Doragon Meteo")
Star Sign "Dragon Meteor"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
上空から大出力レーザーを発射
威力が高く角度の制御も可能
射出時に上空へ移動するので
上手く使えば回避も可能
Translation
Fires a high-output laser from the sky. Power is high, and you can control the firing angle. Since Marisa leaps into the air when firing, a skilled player can use it for evasion as well.


Spell Card 4
魔符「スターダストレヴァリエ」 (Mafu "Sutaadasuto Revarie")
Magic Sign "Stardust Reverie"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
ウィッチレイラインを強化した技
突進速度と威力が大きく伸びた
Translation
A powered-up version of Witch Leyline. Dash speed and damage are significantly increased.


Spell Card 5
星符「エスケープベロシティ」 (Seifu "Esukeepu Beroshiti")
Star Sign "Escape Velocity"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
ミアズマスウィープを大幅改良
接近状態でのカウンターや上空の
敵を討ち取るのに効果を発揮
Translation
A hugely improved version of Miasma Sweep. Effective as a close-range counter or for hitting enemies in midair.


Spell Card 6
彗星「ブレイジングスター」　 (Suisei "Bureijingu Sutaa")
Comet "Blazing Star"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
一度画面外に退避し高速で再突入
攻撃はほぼ画面全体に及び
止める事も不可能である強力な技
欠点は退避を挟む為発生が遅い事
Translation
Leaves the screen, then comes charging back in at high speed. A powerful, unstoppable attack that covers almost the entire screen. Downside is that it's slow to start.


Spell Card 7
星符「メテオニックシャワー」 (Seifu "Meteonikku Shawaa")
Star Sign "Meteonic Shower"
Cost
1 card
Comment
前方に大量の弾をばら撒く射撃
燃費が安く気楽に使用が出来る
Translation
Fire a large number of scattering shots forward. Card consumption is low, so you can use it easily.


Spell Card 8
光符「ルミネスストライク」 (Koufu "Ruminesu Sutoraiku")
Light Sign "Luminous Strike"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
箒を砲身として使い魔法弾を発射
弾丸が大きく速度も速い為
レーザーとは違う味わいを持つ
Translation
Fires a magic bullet using her broom as a gun barrel. It's a fairly large and fast bullet, so it has a different sort of flavor compared to lasers.


Spell Card 9
儀符「オールレリーズサン」 (Gifu "Ooreriizu San")
Astrologic Sign "Orreries Sun"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
魔力で制御するビットを装着する
装着中は射撃がビットからに変化
大幅に強化される
Translation
Equips orbiting bits controlled via magic. While equipped, the bits fire projectiles in Marisa's stead, and the projectiles are greatly strengthened.


Spell Card 10
邪恋「実りやすいマスタースパーク」 (Jaren "Minoriyasui Masutaa Supaaku")
Perverse Love "Easily-Fruitful Master Spark"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
細いレーザーを導線に使うことで
マスタースパークの出力を増幅
超大火力の前方攻撃を可能にした
Translation
Amplifies Master Spark's power output by using a narrow laser as a conductor. Makes it possible to unleash a super-high-firepower forward attack.
