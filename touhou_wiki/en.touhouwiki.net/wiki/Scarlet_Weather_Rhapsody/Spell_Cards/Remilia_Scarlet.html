Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
運命「ミゼラブルフェイト」 (Unmei "Mizeraburu Feito")
Destiny "Miserable Fate"
Owner
Remilia Scarlet
Reisen Stage 1, Aya Stage 4, Iku Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Also a player spellcard. Shoots chains that loop around the player before detonating.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
夜符「ボンバードナイト」 (Yafu "Bonbaado Naito")
Night Sign "Bombard Night"
Owner
Remilia Scarlet
Reisen Stage 1, Aya Stage 4, Iku Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Kicks either towards the opposite wall or straight down to the floor, making shockwaves upon impact.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
蝙蝠「ヴァンパイアスウィープ」 (Koumori "Vanpaia Suwiipu")
Bat "Vampire Sweep"
Owner
Remilia Scarlet
Reisen Stage 1, Aya Stage 4, Iku Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Flies diagonally off-screen while releasing bats, then reappears where a cluster of bats converge.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
神鬼「レミリアストーカー」 (Shinki "Remiria Sutookaa")
God Devil "Remilia Stoker" [1]
Owner
Remilia Scarlet
Aya Stage 4
Editor's Comment
A spellcard from Shoot the Bullet. Charges laser bursts and then fires the bursts and bat bullets.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
デーモンロードウォーク (Deemon Roodo Wooku)
Demon Lord Walk
Cost
1 orb
Comment
吸血鬼の身体能力を活かし
目にも留まらぬ速度で駆け抜けて
敵に打撃を加える
Translation
Uses her vampiric physical prowess to rush forward at blinding speed and hit the enemy with a melee strike.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
サーヴァントフライヤー (Saavanto Furaiyaa)
Servant Flier
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔法陣を展開し蝙蝠弾を発射
魔法陣の位置はランダムであるが
広く展開し時間差で攻撃するため
牽制と接近の布石に役立つ
Translation
Uses magic circles to fire bat bullets. The circles' positions are random, but their wide spread and delayed firing makes them useful for zoning and gaining ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
デーモンロードクレイドル (Deemon Roodo Kureidoru)
Demon Lord Cradle
Cost
1 orb
Comment
上空へオーラを纏った突進攻撃
隙は大きいが無敵時間が有り
カウンター、連続技に役立つ
Translation
An upward dash attack surrounded by a red aura. It has blind spots, but also has invincible frames, so it's useful for counters and combos.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
デーモンロードアロー (Deemon Roodo Aroo)
Demon Lord Arrow
Cost
1 orb
Comment
後方の壁に飛び退き反動で突撃
相手の位置目掛けて角度は固定
突撃に弾幕回避能力が有る
Translation
Leaps to the back wall, then uses the recoil to dash forward. The angle is fixed on the opponent's position, and the dash grazes bullets.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
ヴァンパイアクロウ (Vanpaia Kurou)
Vampire Claw
Cost
1 orb
Comment
入力を繰り返せば三連続で攻撃を
繰り返せる打撃コンビネーション
全て当てた時の威力は高い
Translation
A melee combo that can strike three times in a row by repeating the input. Very powerful if it hits all three times.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
チェーンギャング (Cheen Gyangu)
Chain Gang
Cost
1 orb
Comment
鎖の形状をした赤いオーラを発射
敵へと向かって伸びる追尾弾
ホールドで鎖の範囲を伸ばせるが
その間動けないので注意
Translation
Creates a red, chain-shaped aura and fires it to chase after the enemy. You can increase its length by holding the button down, but you can't move while doing so, so watch out.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
ロケットキックアップ (Roketto Kikku Appu)
Rocket Kick Upper
Cost
1 orb
Comment
高速の楔弾をキックから発射する
地上はほぼ無防備だが、頭上の
敵には強力な対抗手段になる
Translation
Releases a high-speed wedge-shaped bullet from a kick. Almost totally defenseless against ground attacks, but very effective against airborne enemies.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
シーリングフィア (Shiiringu Fia)
Ceiling Fear
Cost
1 orb
Comment
天井へ移動し逆さ釣りになる
その後真下へ向けて高速で落下
Translation
Flies up and hangs from the ceiling, then rockets straight down to the ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
デモンズディナーフォーク (Demonzu Dinaa Fooku)
Demon's Dinner Fork
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔力を槍状に収束した弾丸を投擲
ボタン押し続けでチャージ可能で
同時に投げる槍の本数が増加する
Translation
Throws spear bullets made of concentrated magic. Charging the attack will increase the number of spears thrown.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
紅符「不夜城レッド」 (Koufu "Fuyajou Reddo")
Scarlet Sign "Red the Nightless Castle"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
周囲を赤いオーラで焼き払う技で
迎撃能力が高く切り返しの要
縦に射程を持つので空中の敵にも
強力なスペルとなる
Translation
A technique that burns away her surroundings with a red aura. Good at intercepting attacks and thus turning the tables. Since it has a long vertical range, it's a powerful spell against midair enemies too.


Spell Card 2
必殺「ハートブレイク」 (Hissatsu "Haato Bureiku")
Certain Kill "Heart Break"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
槍状の貫通力が高いオーラを放つ
大抵の弾幕を一方的にかき消し
敵を一瞬で貫く
オーラを武器状に収束する系列は
バリエーションに富む
Translation
Fires a spear-shaped aura bullet that easily cuts through most danmaku and pierces the enemy instantly. Attacks that use weapons made of aura boast a wide range of variety.


Spell Card 3
夜符「デーモンキングクレイドル」 (Yafu "Deemon Kingu Kureidoru")
Night Sign "Demon King Cradle"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
吸血鬼の身体能力を活かした
直接攻撃系のスペルカード
隙は大きいがカード消費に対して
威力などのスペックは高め
Translation
A direct-attack Spell Card that makes use of her vampiric physical prowess. Leaves a wide opening, but its power and other stats are high for the card cost.


Spell Card 4
紅魔「スカーレットデビル」 (Kouma "Sukaaretto Debiru")
Scarlet Devil "Scarlet Devil"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
不夜城レッドの上位版スペル
効果の及ぶ範囲と威力が激増した
存在だけで敵を威圧できる
正に赤い悪魔のカードである
Translation
Upgraded version of Red the Nightless Castle. The damage and attack range are drastically increased. Overwhelms enemies with her mere presence; truly a card for a Scarlet Devil.


Spell Card 5
神槍「スピア・ザ・グングニル」 (Shinsou "Supia Za Gunguniru")
Divine Spear "Spear the Gungnir"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
ハートブレイク上位版
槍のサイズと威力が向上した
速度はもはやレーザーに等しく
距離があっても一瞬で敵を穿つ
Translation
Upgraded version of Heart Break. The spear's size and power are increased. Its speed is like a laser at this point; even at a distance it can pierce the enemy instantly.


Spell Card 6
夜王「ドラキュラクレイドル」 (Yaou "Dorakyura Kureidoru")
Night Lord "Dracula Cradle"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
赤いオーラを纏い弾丸と化す技
打撃に対し圧倒的だが、突進中は
飛び道具に無防備なのが唯一の欠点
Translation
A technique where the user is surrounded with a red aura and becomes a bullet. Superb against melee moves, but its one drawback is that you're defenseless against projectiles while in midair.


Spell Card 7
夜符「バッドレディスクランブル」 (Yafu "Baddo Redi Sukuranburu")
Night Sign "Bad Lady Scramble"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
素早く壁に飛びつき、相手へ向け
錐揉み体当たりを仕掛ける
対弾幕貫通力が高く、後方移動で
敵を惑わすことも可能である
Translation
Quickly clings to the wall, then nosedives towards the enemy and body-slams them. Good at piercing through danmaku, and has the potential to confuse the enemy with the backwards dash.


Spell Card 8
運命「ミゼラブルフェイト」 (Unmei "Mizeraburu Feito")
Destiny "Miserable Fate"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
鎖の形状をした赤いオーラを発射
多数を敵へ向けて伸ばし追跡する
Translation
Fire out many chains made from red aura that chase the enemy.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Stoker - Bram Stoker: Irish writer famous for writing Dracula.
