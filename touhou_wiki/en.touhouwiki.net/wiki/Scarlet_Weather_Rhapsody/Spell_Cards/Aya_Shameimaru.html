Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
突風「猿田彦の先導」 (Toppuu "Sarutahiko no Sendou")
Squall "Sarutahiko's Guidance" [1]
Owner
Aya Shameimaru
Reimu Stage 4, Marisa Stage 4, Sakuya Stage 4, Yuyuko Stage 4, Suika Stage 1, Iku Stage 2
Editor's Comment
Aya dashes across the screen in a tornado and releases wind bullets upon hitting the wall.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
旋符「飄妖扇」 (Senpu "Hyouyousen")
Whirl Sign "Fluttering Fey Fan" [2]
Owner
Aya Shameimaru
Reimu Stage 4, Marisa Stage 4, Sakuya Stage 4, Yuyuko Stage 4, Suika Stage 1, Iku Stage 2
Editor's Comment
Aya releases several wind gusts that fly in a circle before spreading outward.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
旋風「鳥居つむじ風」 (Senpuu "Tori'i Tsumujikaze")
Whirlwind "Torii Whorl-Wind" [3]
Owner
Aya Shameimaru
Reimu Stage 4, Marisa Stage 4, Sakuya Stage 4, Yuyuko Stage 4, Suika Stage 1
Editor's Comment
Aya makes a tornado that crawls forward, spanning the height of the screen. The tornadoes bounce off the screen edges and linger for some time.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
「幻想風靡」 ("Gensou Fuubi")
"Illusionary Dominance"
Owner
Aya Shameimaru
Suika Stage 1
Editor's Comment
Aya flies back and forth fast enough to turn into a laser, which rotates around the screen while centered on a magic circle that slowly follows you.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
疾風扇 (Hayate-sen)
Gust Fan
Cost
1 orb
Comment
団扇から突風を巻き起こして攻撃
空中から撃つと地面を這う特殊な
軌道に変化する
Translation
Attacks by blowing a gust of wind from her fan. When used in midair, the wind follows a special trajectory and crawls along the ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
疾走風靡 (Shissou Fuubi)
Dashing Domination
Cost
1 orb
Comment
風を纏って前方へ高速突撃する
風がシェルターになり移動中は
グレイズ属性が付加されている
Translation
A high-speed forward dash shrouded by wind. The wind shelters Aya, allowing you to graze during the dash.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
天狗の立風露 (Tengu no Tachifuuro)
Tengu's Mist Standing [4]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
空中でつむじ風の足場を作り出す
任意方向へ再ジャンプ可能になり
足場を反動で下方向へ飛ばす
空中専用で地上での入力は不可能
Translation
Creates a whirlwind in mid-air to stand on, allowing you to jump again in your direction of choice. The jump's recoil shoots the whirlwind in the opposite direction. Usable in air only, not on the ground.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
暗夜の礫 (An'ya no Tsubute)
Pelting in the Dark Night
Cost
1 orb
Comment
大量の鴉を呼び寄せて攻撃させる
攻撃の時間差が大きく連携し易い
呼び出されるのは普通の鴉なので
攻撃を受けると逃げてしまう点に注意
Translation
Calls upon a murder of crows to attack. The attack delay is lengthy, making it easy to chain off of it. Since they're just normal crows, they flee when attacked.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
烈風扇 (Reppuusen)
Gale Fan
Cost
1 orb
Comment
カッター状の突風を広範囲に拡散
弾速は下がったが攻撃範囲は増加
ボタンで拡散角度を調整可能
Translation
Scatter cutter-shaped gusts over a wide area. The projectile speed is slower than the default version, but the spread is wider. The angle is adjustable via the attack button.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
疾走優美 (Shissou Yuubi)
Dashing Grace
Cost
1 orb
Comment
疾走風靡の変形版
速度とグレイズ能力を失ったが
一回の方向転換が可能になった
Translation
Variant of Dashing Domination. Loses the speed and grazing, but gains the ability to change directions once.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
天狗のダウンバースト (Tengu no Daunbaasuto)
Tengu's Downburst
Cost
1 orb
Comment
風を纏った急降下キックで攻撃
着地時に突風で周囲を吹き飛ばす
ボタンで突風の性質が変化する
こちらも空中専用なので注意
Translation
A rapidly-descending kick attack shrouded in wind. Gusts are created upon touchdown, blowing away anyone nearby. The type of wind is adjustable via the attack button. Like the default version, can only be used in mid-air.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
鎌風ベーリング (Kamakaze Beeringu)
Wind Sickle Veiling
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周囲に風の鎧を纏う特殊な技
鎧は文の移動に追従し接触だけで
敵を攻撃することが出来る
地上、空中間の移動で消滅する
Translation
A unique move that surrounds Aya with wind armor, which follows her around and attacks enemies just by touching them. Disappears upon moving between the ground and the sky.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
天狗ナメシ (Tengu-nameshi)
Tengu Tree-Felling [5]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
前方上空へ飛び出し後ろへ向けて
風の塊を叩き付ける変わった技
距離が適正なら敵の背後を突き
奇襲として効果を発揮する
Translation
A rather odd move where Aya leaps diagonally up and forwards, while flinging a cluster of wind behind her. At an appropriate distance, it's effective as a surprise attack to the enemy's rear.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
天狗の太鼓 (Tengu no Taiko)
Tengu's Drumbeats [6]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
その場から跳躍し敵を踏みつける
ヒットすると後方にジャンプ
この時軌道は有る程度操作可能で
ボタンを押すと追加攻撃へ派生
Translation
Hops up into the air and stomps on the enemy. If it connects, Aya jumps behind them. The second jump's trajectory can be adjusted somewhat, and releasing the attack button triggers a follow-up attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
旋符「紅葉扇風」 (Senpu "Momiji-ba Senpuu")
Whirl Sign "Autumn Leaf Fan's Wind"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
遥か上空まで届く竜巻を発射
飛距離は短いが巻き込んだ敵を
無防備に上空へ打ち上げる
Translation
Creates a tornado reaching all the way to the skies. Doesn't travel very far, but any enemy caught in it will be helplessly launched into the sky.


Spell Card 2
竜巻「天孫降臨の道しるべ」 (Tatsumaki "Tenson Kourin no Michishirube")
Tornado "Guidepost for the Advent of the Divine Grandson"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
自分自身を巨大な竜巻で包み込む
威力が高く無敵時間も持つため
反撃や上空の敵への攻撃に強力
Translation
Wraps Aya herself up in a huge tornado. Does huge damage and renders her invincible, so it's strong as a counter or anti-air attack.


Spell Card 3
逆風「人間禁制の道」 (Gyakufuu "Ningen-kinsei no Michi")
Headwind "Route Forbidden to Man"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
しばらくフィールドに風を起こし
飛んでくる飛散物で敵を攻撃する
風には敵を押す効果もあるため
画面端へ追い込む際にも有効
Translation
Fills the screen with wind for a while, attacking the enemy with flying debris. Because the wind pushes the enemy, it's also good for herding them into one corner of the screen.


Spell Card 4
突符「天狗のマクロバースト」 (Toppu "Tengu no Makurobaasuto")
Thrust Sign "Tengu's Macroburst"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
空中専用のカードで、上空から
下方向に風圧弾を一斉発射
発射地点に近いほど弾が当たり
威力や削りが増大する
Translation
A midair-only card that fires a scattering of air pressure shots downward. The closer the enemy is to the point of firing, the more bullets will hit, increasing the damage (or spirit damage) dealt.


Spell Card 5
風符「天狗道の開風」 (Fuufu "Tengu-dou no Kaifuu")
Wind Sign "Opening Wind of the Tengu Realm" [7]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
団扇を横に一閃し手元から
旋風を射出するシンプルな射撃
Translation
A simple whirlwind projectile, shot from a sweep of her fan.


Spell Card 6
「幻想風靡」 ("Gensou Fuubi")
"Illusionary Dominance"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
敵を体当たりで跳ね上げ
最大加速を付けた状態で何度も
追撃を行う超大技
左右に振って追撃高度を上げられ
敵に高度を合わせると威力上昇
Translation
A dramatic move that bodyslams the enemy to knock them upwards, then juggles them in midair at maximum speed. Tapping left and right adjusts Aya's altitude, allowing you to keep a lock on the enemy and increase the damage dealt.


Spell Card 7
魔獣「鎌鼬ベーリング」 (Majuu "Kamaitachi Beeringu")
Demon Beast "Kamaitachi Veiling"
Cost
1 cards
Comment
周囲に風の鎧を纏う特殊なスペル
鎧は文の移動に追従し接触だけで
敵を攻撃することが出来る
地上、空中間の移動で消滅する
Translation
A unique spell that surrounds Aya with wind armor, which follows her around and attacks enemies just by touching them. Disappears upon moving between the ground and the sky.


Spell Card 8
突風「猿田彦の先導」 (Toppuu "Sarutahiko no Sendou")
Squall "Sarutahiko's Guidance"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
疾走風靡の純粋強化版スペル
風を利用した最大加速で敵へ突撃
グレイズ属性はそのままに速度と
威力が強化された
Translation
A purely stronger version of Dashing Dominance. Uses wind to accelerate to top speed and charge into the enemy. Grazes just the same, and has increased speed and power.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Sarutahiko is the god who guided Ninigi-no-Mikoto when he descended from heaven, and is thought to be a progenitor of tengu folklore. During mikoshi-togyo processions, where a Shinto shrine's god is paraded through town in a portable vessel, the leader of the procession is often dressed as Sarutahiko.
  2. ↑ 飄妖 (hyouyou): a pun on 飄揺 (hyouyou, "fluttering"), with the second kanji replaced with 妖 (you, "strange, bewitching")
  3. ↑ 鳥居 (torii): the gate in front of a Shinto shrine. 鳥居つむじ (torii tsumuji): the condition of having a double hair whorl, which is thought to indicate that someone is both clever and cunning. つむじ風 (tsumuji kaze): a whirlwind.
  4. ↑ 立風露 (tachifuuro, literally 'standing wind mist') is also a name for Geranium krameri.
  5. ↑ "Tengu nameshi": one of several similar names for a phenomenon, allegedly caused by tengu, where a sound like a tree falling over can be heard, but no tree is in sight when one goes to look.
  6. ↑ Reference to a phenomenon, allegedly caused by tengu, where drumbeat-like noises come from the mountains.
  7. ↑ Tengu Realm (天狗道): in the 12th century, tengu were thought to be heretical priests who had fallen from the Six Realms of Buddhism into the "tengu realm" upon reincarnation.
