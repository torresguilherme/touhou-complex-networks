Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
迫符「脅迫幻覚(オブセッショナー)」 (Hakufu "Obusesshonaa")
Force Sign "Obsessioner (Menacing Hallucination) "
Owner
Reisen Udongein Inaba
Marisa Stage 3, Remilia Stage 4, Komachi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Shoots several bullets that curve towards the player and explode.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
幻弾「幻想視差(ブラフバラージ)」 (Gendan "Burafu Baraaji")
Illusion Bullet "Bluff Barrage (Illusion Parallax) "
Owner
Reisen Udongein Inaba
Marisa Stage 3, Remilia Stage 4, Komachi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Blankets the screen with invisible bullets, which become real when you step into fields Reisen creates. The fields are either circles that linger on-screen, or a cone covering Reisen's line of sight.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
幻兎「平行交差(パラレルクロス)」 (Gento "Parareru Kurosu")
Illusion Rabbit "Parallel Cross (Concurrent Crossing) "
Owner
Reisen Udongein Inaba
Marisa Stage 3, Remilia Stage 4, Komachi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Reisen splits into several clone holograms that float downward while shooting.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
マインドエクスプロージョン (Maindo Ekusupuroojon)
Mind Explosion
Cost
1 orb
Comment
ロケット弾に似た弾丸を発射
何かに触ると精神波を撒き散らす
弾丸自体は低速だが爆発効果で
弾幕の押し合いに強い
Translation
Fires a rocket-shaped bullet that releases mental energy on impact. The bullet itself is slow, but the exploding effect makes it useful for cancelling enemy danmaku.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
イリュージョナリィブラスト (Iryuujonarii Burasuto)
Illusionary Blast
Cost
1 orb
Comment
前方に狂気の波長を収束して攻撃
所謂レーザー攻撃に近いため
貫通性能が非常に高い
Translation
Attacks with lunatic wavelengths that converge directly ahead. It's basically a laser attack, so it pierces extremely well.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
フィールドウルトラレッド (Fiirudo Urutorareddo)
Ultrared Field
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周囲に波長変換フィールドを設置
内部に入った敵の視覚を混乱させ
相手は射撃を当てられなくなる
Translation
Places a wavelength conversion field nearby. It disturbs the enemy's vision as long as they're inside it, making them unable to land a hit with projectiles.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
ディスビリーフアスペクト (Disubiriifu Asupekuto)
Disbelief Aspect
Cost
1 orb
Comment
波長変化を利用した幻影を投射
幻影は射撃による攻撃を行う
幻影は実体のように動くため
一種の幻惑効果も併せ持つ
Translation
Emits a hologram via wavelength conversion, which then launches a projectile attack. Since the hologram moves like the real Reisen, it can serve as a diversion too.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
マインドペンディング (Maindo Pendingu)
Mind Bending
Cost
1 orb
Comment
広範囲に散弾タイプの弾丸を発射
初速も速くなり敵に当てやすいが
代わりに威力に大きなムラが出る
欠点を抱える事となってしまった
Translation
Fires a bullet that explodes into a wide circle of shrapnel. It's faster than the default version and easier to hit with, but in exchange, one downside is that its damage varies wildly.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
アイサイトクリーニング (Aisaito Kuriiningu)
Eyesight Cleaning
Cost
1 orb
Comment
視線の範囲を示すサイトを展開
キーを押し続けて範囲を拡大し
離した時に範囲内に相手が居れば
狂気の視線波が敵を瞬時に捉える
Translation
Marks Reisen's field of view with a laser sight that expands as you hold the button. If the enemy is within the area when you let go, they'll be briefly seized by a lunatic eye wave.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
フィールドウルトラバイオレット (Fiirudo Urutorabaioretto)
Ultraviolet Field
Cost
1 orb
Comment
敵の認識を狂わせる波長を展開
相手は無い筈の物が見えてしまい
虚像の射撃が実弾の効果を持つ
Translation
Places a wavelength field that disturbs the enemy's cognition. They'll see things that aren't there, which makes Reisen's hologram bullets have real effects.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
ディスオーダーアイ (Disuoodaa Ai)
Disorder Eye
Cost
1 orb
Comment
映像を周囲に複数飛ばす
散開したダミーは射出地点へ収束
敵へダメージを与える
映像展開後収束まで本体は無敵
Translation
Fires several dummy holograms around Reisen. The dummies spread out, then converge back at the same point and damage the enemy. The real Reisen is invincible until the images regroup.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
マインドドロッピング (Maindo Doroppingu)
Mind Dropping
Cost
1 orb
Comment
上空へ向けて弾丸を発射
空中で弾け飛び地上へ降り注ぐ
分裂前も対空射撃として機能し
散弾で本体との同時攻撃も可能
Translation
Fires a bullet into the air, where it bursts into falling shrapnel. It's also useful as an anti-air attack before bursting, and the delay allows Reisen to attack simultaneously with the shrapnel.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
リップルヴィジョン (Rippuru Vijon)
Ripple Vision
Cost
1 orb
Comment
リング状の視線ビームを発射
リングは時間と共に巨大化し
遠距離ほど回避が困難になる
Translation
Fires a ring-shaped eye beam. The beam expands over time, so it's more difficult to avoid the further you are.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 11
アンダーセンスブレイク (Andaasensu Bureiku)
Undersense Break
Cost
1 orb
Comment
直接攻撃能力を持つ狂気の視線波
シンプルに攻撃や防御に役立ち
実戦で扱いやすいタイプ
Translation
A lunatic eye wave that deals damage at close range. It's simple, effective for both attack and defense, and easy to use in direct combat.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
惑視「離円花冠(カローラヴィジョン)」 (Wakushi "Koroora Vijon")
Charming Look "Corolla Vision (Disjointed Circular Corolla) "
Cost
1 card
Comment
飛距離比例で巨大化するリング弾
消費が少ない割りに範囲が広く
牽制等気軽に使いやすい
Translation
Ring shots that expand as they travel further. Covers a wide range despite the low cost, making it easy to use for zoning.


Spell Card 2
幻爆「近眼花火(マインドスターマイン)」 (Genbaku "Maindo Sutaamain")
Illusion Bomb "Mind Starmine (Myopic Firework) "
Cost
3 cards
Comment
全方向へ精神爆弾の弾丸を一斉に
発射する射撃スペルカード
弾丸は時間差で炸裂し周囲の敵を
一斉に攻撃する
Translation
A projectile Spell Card that fires mental explosion bullets in every direction. The bullets explode after a brief delay, attacking the whole surrounding area at once.


Spell Card 3
幻惑「花冠視線(クラウンヴィジョン)」 (Genwaku "Kuraun Vijon")
Fascination "Crown Vision (Corolla Glance) "
Cost
3 cards
Comment
飛距離で巨大化するリング弾を
高速で連射するスペル
Translation
A spell that fires a rapid beam of rings that expand as they travel farther.


Spell Card 4
「幻朧月睨(ルナティックレッドアイズ)」 ("Runatikku Reddo Aizu")
"Lunatic Red Eyes (Stare of the Hazy Phantom Moon) "
Cost
5 cards
Comment
相手を狂気に追いやる波長の渦を
広範囲に発生させる。威力、範囲
に優れるが発動はそれ相応の重さ
Translation
Covers a wide radius with a vortex of wavelengths that drive the opponent to madness. It has extensive range and power, but a proportionately long wind-up.


Spell Card 5
弱心「喪心喪意(ディモチヴェイション)」 (Jakushin "Dimochiveishon")
Weak Heart "Demotivation (Empty Heart, Empty Mind) "
Cost
1 card
Comment
至近距離から狂気の視線を浴びせ
相手の精神にショックを与える
食らうと一枚カードが破壊され
使用することなく失ってしまう
Translation
Shocks the opponent's mind with lunatic eye waves at point-blank range. If it hits, it'll break and waste one enemy card.


Spell Card 6
喪心「喪心創痍(ディスカーダー)」 (Soushin "Disukaadaa")
Empty Heart "Discarder (Scars of an Empty Heart) "
Cost
3 cards
Comment
精神破壊効果のある弾丸を発射
命中するとカードを二枚破壊する
消費に対して破壊量は少ないが
技の命中率や威力を上げるため
コストの増大を余儀なくされた
Translation
Fires a bullet that causes mental damage. Destroys two cards if it hits. Doesn't destroy many cards compared to its cost, but had to cost more to improve its accuracy and damage.


Spell Card 7
毒煙幕「瓦斯織物の玉」 (Doku Enmaku "Gasu-orimono no Tama")
Poison Smokescreen "Orb of Gas-Treated Weave"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
空気と反応してガス化する薬品
ガスは比重が空気より重いため
地表に溜まり敵の体力を奪う
ちなみに本人は解毒薬服用済み
Translation
A drug that reacts with air and becomes gas. Since the gas is heavier than air, it sticks to the ground and drains the opponent's health. Reisen already took the antidote, in case you're wondering.


Spell Card 8
生薬「国士無双の薬」 (Shouyaku "Kokushi Musou no Kusuri")
Life Elixir "Peerless Patriot's Elixir"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
永琳印の強壮薬を使用
身体能力が向上し攻撃力、防御力が
試合中に渡り服用毎に上昇する
なお使い過ぎるとアレな物だが
本人には知らされていない
Translation
Takes a swig of Eirin-brand tonic. Each dose boosts Reisen's attack and defense further for the entire duration of the match. Taking too much of it has... consequences, but nobody told Reisen about that.
