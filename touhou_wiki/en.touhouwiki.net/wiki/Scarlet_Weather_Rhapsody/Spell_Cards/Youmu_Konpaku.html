Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
人智剣「天女返し」 (Jinchiken "Tennyogaeshi")
Human Knowledge Sword "Turning Angel Cut" [1]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Sakuya Stage 2, Alice Stage 2, Remilia Stage 3, Komachi Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 7
Editor's Comment
A spellcard from Shoot the Bullet. Creates a laser and then fires another laser toward you that also fires bullets on Normal or harder.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
桜花剣「閃々散華」 (Oukaken "Sensen Sange")
Cherry Blossom Sword "Flashing Scattered Flowers" [2]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Sakuya Stage 2, Alice Stage 2, Remilia Stage 3, Komachi Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Dashes off-screen, then slashes either in random areas or following the player's position before returning.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
断想剣「草木成仏斬」 (Dansouken "Soumoku Joubutsuzan")
Thought-Cutting Sword "Slash of the Buddhahood of Flora" [3]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Remilia Stage 3, Komachi Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Either slashes in a pillar in front of Youmu, or in a forward-moving wave. Cherry petals rain down afterward.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
瞑斬「楼観から弾をも断つ心の眼」 (Meizan "Roukan kara Tama wo mo Tatsu Kokoro no Me")
Closed-Eye Slash "The Bullet-Cutting Spirit Eye from Roukan" [4] [5] [6]
Owner
Youmu Konpaku
Tenshi Stage 7
Editor's Comment
Youmu disappears and slashes from off-screen while her ghost half shoots bullets. The bullets split apart when slashed.

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
人符「現世斬」 (Jinfu "Genseizan")
Human Sign "Slash of Present World"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
前方へ踏み込み相手を斬り抜ける
空中の相手に命中し難いが
地上戦では非常に強力
Translation
Dashes forward and slices through the enemy. It's hard to hit a midair target, but is very powerful in a ground battle.


Spell Card 2
断命剣「冥想斬」 (Danmyouken "Meisouzan")
Life-Cutting Sword "Slash of Nether Meditation" [7]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
楼観剣を妖力媒体として使い
巨大な光の刀身を生成して斬る
楼観剣強化スペルの基本形
Translation
Uses Roukanken as a medium for mystical energy, and slashes forward with a huge blade made of light. The basic version of her Roukanken-strengthening spell.


Spell Card 3
魂符「幽明の苦輪」 (Konfu "Yuumei no Kurin")
Soul Sign "Wheel of Pain of the Living and Dead" [8]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
半身が本体をトレースし追加攻撃
時間差で追加攻撃するため
隙が減るに等しい
Translation
Her ghost half traces behind her living half, copying its attacks. Since the copy's attacks are slightly delayed, the enemy is given fewer openings.


Spell Card 4
人鬼「未来永劫斬」 (Hito Oni "Miraieigozan")
Human Oni "Slash of the Eternal Future"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
踏み込み斬りで敵を宙へ舞い上げ
連続攻撃で空中へ縫い付ける
威力は非常に高く無敵時間も持つ
攻防に置ける万能完成形の一つ
Translation
Flips the enemy into the air with a dashing slash, then repeatedly juggles them with more slashes. Does extremely high damage and has invincibility time, making it a perfected skill that's useful for both offense and defense.


Spell Card 5
断迷剣「迷津慈航斬」 (Danmeiken "Meishinjikouzan")
Hesitation-Cutting Sword "Slash of Departure from Hesitation" [9]
Cost
4 cards
Comment
楼観剣に大量の妖力をつぎ込み
巨大な刀身を作り出して薙ぎ払う
前方に対して圧倒的攻撃力を誇り
相手は回避することすら難しい
Translation
Pours a huge amount of energy into Roukanken, mowing down her opponent with a massive blade. Boasts immense forward power, and is very hard to dodge.


Spell Card 6
魂魄「幽明求聞持総明の法」 (Konpaku "Yuumei Gumonji Soumei no Hou")
Konpaku "Dharma of Gumonji Wisdom of the Living and Dead" [10]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
半身が妖夢の攻撃をトレースする
消費は多いが効果時間が長くなり
半身との同期も一瞬で完了する
Translation
Youmu's ghost half traces her attacks. Card cost is high, but the effect lasts longer and the ghost half's synchronization is near-instantaneous.


Spell Card 7
剣伎「桜花閃々」 (Kenki "Ouka Sensen")
Sword Skill "Cherry Blossom Flashing"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
高速で前方に移動、間髪入れず
軌道上に桜色の剣閃が走る
可憐な技であるが庭の剪定から
生まれた生活感溢れる技術だとか
Translation
Youmu quickly dashes forward, followed moments later by cherry-pink sword slashes in her wake. It's a beautiful yet homey-feeling technique, apparently inspired by time spent pruning the gardens.


Spell Card 8
断霊剣「成仏得脱斬」 (Danreiken "Joubutsu Tokudatsuzan")
Spirit-Cutting Sword "Slash of Ascension to Buddhahood"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
白楼剣、楼観剣の二本を交差させ
上空への剣気の柱を作り出す
射出まで無敵で対空、割り込み
上空への連続技に活用できる
Translation
Crosses both swords and creates a pillar of energy reaching into the sky. You're invincible until the pillar appears, and it can be applied as an anti-air, counter, or upward combo attack.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ One of Youmu's spell cards from StB. A reference to the "Turning Swallow Cut" (燕返し), a sword technique famously used by samurai Sasaki Kojirō. 'Turning around' the kanji "天女" also results in "妖", a kanji used in Youmu's name.
  2. ↑ 散華 (sange): a phrase that refers to the practice of scattering flowers during Buddhist ceremonies. It also refers to dying honorably in battle.
  3. ↑ 草木成仏: a condensing of "草木国土悉皆成仏", a phrase from certain Buddhist schools meaning 'all grasses, trees, and lands can become Buddhas'.
  4. ↑ "Closed-Eyed (瞑)" is a reference to the state of meditation where one's eyes are closed in prayer.
  5. ↑ "Roukan (楼観)" refers the larger blade Youmu carries (Roukanken(楼観剣)- Tower-Watching Blade.
  6. ↑ The "spirit eye (心の眼)" is a possible reference to the Brow Chakra/Third-Eye which in Hinduism/Buddhism is believed to govern the ability to separate reality from fantasy or delusions.
  7. ↑ 冥想 (meisou): a pun on 瞑想 (meisou, 'meditation') and 冥界 (meikai, 'Netherworld').
  8. ↑ 幽明: A phrase referring to the world of the living and the world of the dead.
  9. ↑ 迷津慈航 ("departure from hesitation"): the name of the boat that ferries souls to Higan from the world of the unenlightened.
  10. ↑ "Gumonji" (求聞持) is to be able to definitely not forget something seen or heard once, said to be able to acquire infinite wisdom. The founder of Shingon Buddhism, Kobo-Daishi, comprehended this at the age of 19 and gained an enlightenment. The name of Hieda no Akyuu's ability of not forgetting is also called "Gumonji."
