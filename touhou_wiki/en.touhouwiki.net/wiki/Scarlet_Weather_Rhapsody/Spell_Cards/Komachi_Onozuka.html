Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
霊符「古き地縛霊の目覚め」 (Reifu "Furuki Jibakurei no Mezame")
Spirit Sign "Awakening of the Ancient Earthbound Spirits"
Owner
Komachi Onozuka
Reimu Stage 3, Youmu Stage 3, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Summons ghosts on the player's position, then detonates them.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
死符「死者選別の鎌」 (Shifu "Shisha Senbetsu no Kama")
Death Sign "Scythe of Final Judgement"
Owner
Komachi Onozuka
Reimu Stage 3, Youmu Stage 3, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Creates a vertical field that hampers the player's movement, then brings down a huge laser blast on the player's position.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
魂符「魂の遊戯」 (Konfu "Tamashii no Yuugi")
Soul Sign "Game of the Soul"
Owner
Komachi Onozuka
Reimu Stage 3, Youmu Stage 3, Remilia Stage 6, Yuyuko Stage 3, Yukari Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Summons rings of bullets that converge on the player's position. Also swaps Komachi and the player's positions from time to time.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
迷わず生きた人霊 (Mayowazu Ikita Ninrei)
Human Spirits who Lived Without Hesitance
Cost
1 orb
Comment
鎌を打ちつけ怨霊を地中に射出
地面を潜って進み敵を検知すると
垂直に向きを変え浮上する
Translation
Fires a vengeful spirit into the ground with a scythe strike. The spirit crawls along the ground, then floats upward when it detects an enemy perpendicular to it.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
浮かばれない地縛霊 (Ukabarenai Jibakurei)
Earthbound Spirits who Find No Rest
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地中の霊を活性化し噴出させる
見た目通り周囲広範囲へ攻撃
Translation
Invigorates underground spirits and makes them erupt upward. As its appearance indicates, it's a wide area attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
脱魂の儀 (Dakkon no Gi)
Ritual of Ecstasy
Cost
1 orb
Comment
自分と相手の位置を入れ替える
移動手段や幻惑だけでなく
相手に隙を作る事も可能
特定の動作やシナリオでは無効
Translation
Switches positions with the opponent. In addition to being useful for movement or distraction, it can force the enemy to leave themselves open to attack. (No effect during certain moves or in story mode.)
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
怠惰に生きた浮遊霊 (Taida ni Ikita Fuyuurei)
Wandering Spirits who Lived Indolently [1]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
画面を漂う怨霊弾を連射する
速度は遅いが独特の軌道で飛び
相手をゆっくりと追い詰める
Translation
Fires vengeful spirits that drift across the screen. Though slow, they fly in a unique trajectory and can gradually corner the opponent.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
死神の大鎌 (Shinigami no Oogama)
Reaper's Scythe
Cost
1 orb
Comment
円弧状に鎌を一閃しなぎ払う
射程が独特で軌跡の外も内側も
当てるには慣れが要る
Translation
Sweeps Komachi's scythe forward in a crescent arc. Has a unique attack range; both its close-up and long-distance trajectories take some practice to score a hit.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
死出の風 (Shide no Kaze)
Wind of Passing Away
Cost
1 orb
Comment
鎌を回転させ周囲を切り刻む
攻撃範囲が広く置いておくように
振り回して使え、先読み気味の
カウンターから連続技に威力発揮
Translation
Spins her scythe around to slice a wide area nearby. You can whip it out casually just on its own, but it really shines in combos or when used as a counter after reading the opponent's next move.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
無間の道 (Mugen no Michi)
Path of Avici
Cost
1 orb
Comment
距離を操る能力を駆使した技で
内側で左右への移動を妨害する
場を作り出す技
敵は場を越える移動が困難になり
場の中に入ると脱出も難しくなる
Translation
Uses her distance-manipulating ability to create a field that limits horizontal movement while inside. Crossing over the field or escaping from it once inside will be very difficult for the opponent.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
寂しがり屋の緊縛霊 (Samishigari-ya no Kinbakurei)
Lonely Bound Spirit
Cost
1 orb
Comment
一見無害の幽霊を纏わり付かせる
そのままでは飾りに過ぎないが
下強射で活性爆発を起こせる
Translation
Releases a harmless-looking spirit that tracks the opponent. Does nothing on its own, but can be detonated with Down+C.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
通りすがりの人霊 (Toorisugari no Ninrei)
Human Spirit Passing By
Cost
1 orb
Comment
地面に鎌を打ち、画面端から
地を這う飛び道具を発射する技
ボタンに応じて前後を選択でき
不意打ち、同時攻撃を得意とする
Translation
Strikes the earth with her scythe, firing a projectile that races across the ground from the screen edge. Its direction changes based on the button used. Good for surprise attacks and simultaneous follow-ups from Komachi herself.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
お迎え体験版 (Omukae Taikenhan)
Last Farewell: Trial Edition
Cost
1 orb
Comment
一見普通に鎌を振るだけだが
構え中に敵を寄せる効果を持つ
構え状態はホールドで延長でき
鎌の射程外に逃げられ難くなる
Translation
It looks like a normal scythe swing, but draws the opponent closer while she has the scythe raised. Holding the button will prolong the wind-up, making it harder to escape the scythe's range.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
舟符「河の流れのように」 (Shuufu "Kawa no Nagare no You ni")
Boat Sign "Flow of the River" [2]
Cost
1 cards
Comment
三途の川の水を少し呼び出し
船に乗って体当たりする豪快な技
性質自体はシンプルな突進技
Translation
A dramatic move that calls forth a little bit of the Sanzu River's water, and rides her boat atop it to slam into the opponent. Basically a regular charge attack.


Spell Card 2
薄命「余命幾許も無し」 (Hakumei "Yomyou Ikubaku mo Nashi")
Poor Fate "Your Days Are Numbered"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
敵の体力を半分に削る恐ろしい技
性質上試合の早い内に当てれば
大きな成果を得られる反面
機を逸すると消費に見合わない為
的確な運用が要求される
Translation
A fearsome skill that cuts the opponent's life bar in half. This naturally makes it very advantageous to use early in the match, but conversely, waiting too long will mean it's not worth the cost. As such, it demands precise use.


Spell Card 3
霊符「何処にでもいる浮遊霊」 (Reifu "Doko ni Demo Iru Fuyuurei")
Spirit Sign "Abundant Wandering Spirits" [1]
Cost
1 cards
Comment
広範囲に幽霊をばら撒く射撃
攻撃範囲が広く幽霊は画面内を
ふらふらと長時間滞在する
Translation
A projectile move that scatters ghosts over a wide area. The attack range is wide, and the ghosts stay floating on the screen for a long time.


Spell Card 4
死歌「八重霧の渡し」 (Shika "Yaegiri no Watashi")
Death Song "Ferry of the Deep Fog" [3]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
幽霊を先導して群れを作りだす技
幽霊は先導に従って気ままに飛び
ダメージを与える弾幕になる
実は職権乱用技らしい
Translation
A move that leads many ghosts to form a pack. The ghosts, following Komachi's lead, will fly forth without restraint and become damaging danmaku. (From what we've heard, this is actually an abuse of her authority.)


Spell Card 5
換命「不惜身命、可惜身命」 (Kanmei "Fushaku Shinmei, Kashaku Shinmei")
Exchange "Unvalued Life, Valued Life" [4]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
体力を入れ替える前代未聞の技
ピンチが一瞬でチャンスに変わり
態と攻撃される事すら可能になる
そんな技が簡単に当たる事は無く
消費も発生速度も重いカード
Translation
An unprecedented move that switches Komachi and the opponent's life bars. Can turn a crisis into a perfect chance for victory, and even allows you to take damage strategically. Of course, it's far from easy to land the hit, and it has both a significant cost and casting delay.


Spell Card 6
恨符「未練がましい緊縛霊」 (Konfu "Mirengamashii Kinbakurei")
Malice Sign "Regretful Bound Spirits"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
強力な誘導性を持つ悪霊を呼ぶ
悪霊は敵に纏わり付き追尾するが
特にこれといった攻撃はしない
しゃがみ強射での活性爆発専用技
Translation
Summons evil spirits with strong homing capabilities. The spirits cling to the enemy and follow them, but don't really do anything to attack. A card meant for use with Komachi's Down+C explosion trigger.


Spell Card 7
死符「死者選別の鎌」 (Shifu "Shisha Senbetsu no Kama")
Death Sign "Scythe of Final Judgement"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
地面に鎌を打ち込み、上空から
相手の頭上に射撃を落下させる
相手をサーチして落ちてくる反面
動く相手には空振りしやすい
Translation
Slams her scythe into the ground, and drops a projectile from the sky right onto the opponent's head. The projectile tracks the enemy before falling, but conversely, it can easily whiff if the opponent is moving around.


Spell Card 8
地獄「無間の狭間」 (Jigoku "Mugen no Hazama")
Hell "Narrow Confines of Avici"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
敵を斬り付けると同時に横移動を
阻害する場を作り出す
効果中は間合いの主導権は小町が
完全に掌握することが出来る
Translation
Simultaneously slashes the enemy and creates a field that hampers horizontal movement. Komachi can completely control the initiative of the match as long as the effect lasts.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 1.0 1.1 浮遊霊: ghosts that haven't yet realized that they're dead, and continue to wander on Earth.
  2. ↑ A reference to the Hibari Misora song "Kawa no nagare no you ni", pronounced the same way.
  3. ↑ A reference to the 400-year-old Edo River ferryboat, "矢切の渡し" (yagiri no watashi), as well as a Takashi Hosokawa song of the same name. The spell card's name ("yaegiri no watashi") is pronounced similarly to that of the boat.
  4. ↑ 不惜身命: giving up one's well-being for a noble cause. 可惜身命: valuing one's well-being over other things.
