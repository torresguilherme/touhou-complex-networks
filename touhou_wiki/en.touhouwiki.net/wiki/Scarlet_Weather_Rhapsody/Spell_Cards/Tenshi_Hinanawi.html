Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
坤儀の剣 (Kongi no Tsurugi)
Sword of Kun
Cost
1 orb
Comment
気質を地中へ打ち込み隆起させる高威力で左右に広い判定があるが上空の敵には効果が薄い
Translation
Drives spirit into the earth, making it rise. Powerful and wide-range, but useless against airborne enemies.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
天罰の石柱 (Tenbatsu no Sekichuu)
Pillars of Divine Punishment
Cost
1 orb
Comment
要石を落下させ結界とする 石は敵の攻撃を受ける効果が有り強力な盾としても機能する
Translation
Drops a keystone to wall her off. The stone can absorb enemy attacks, so you can also use it as a shield.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
非想の威光 (Hisou no Ikou)
Beams of Non-Perception
Cost
1 orb
Comment
要石から気質を針の様に尖らせたレーザー状の飛び道具を連続発射対射撃における貫通力は強力
Translation
Fires successive laser-like streams of spirit from a keystone. Very useful as an anti-projectile piercing attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
非想の剣 (Hisou no Tsurugi)
Sword of Non-Perception
Cost
1 orb
Comment
緋想の剣を踏み込みから一閃 踏み込み中はグレイズ判定が有り弾幕を無理矢理突き進める
Translation
Rushes forward, attacking with the Sword of Non-Perception. The rush grazes, so you can force your way through danmaku.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
六震-相- (Rokushin -Sou-)
Six Earthquakes -Signs- [1]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
緋想の剣を地面に打ち込み局地的地震を発生させる全体攻撃 相手が地上に居れば攻撃が成立しグレイズでの回避も出来ない反面空中の敵には無力
Translation
Plunges the Sword of Non-Perception into the earth to cause a localized earthquake. Always affects opponents on the ground, but does nothing against airborne enemies.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
守りの要 (Mamori no Kaname)
Guarding Keystones
Cost
1 orb
Comment
石で周囲を守り弾幕避けにする技装着状態で再入力することで石を敵へ向けて飛ばすことも出来る
Translation
Uses keystones circling her to protect her. If you input the command again while they're deploying, the keystones fly towards the enemy.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
天地プレス (Tenchi Puresu)
Heaven and Earth Press
Cost
1 orb
Comment
石で敵を挟み込み押しつぶす ホールドで石の間隔を調節し ぶつける位置を調整できる
Translation
Crushes the enemy between two stones. You can adjust the distance they fly by holding the button.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
緋想の剣 (Hisou no Tsurugi)
Sword of Scarlet Perception
Cost
1 orb
Comment
緋想の剣を闇雲に振り回して攻撃狭い範囲だが複数回攻撃し判定も強いため接近戦で使いやすい
Translation
Attacks by swinging the Sword of Scarlet Perception randomly. Very close range, but it can hit multiple times and has a good hit box, so it's great for close combat.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
地符「不譲土壌の剣」 (Chifu "Fujou Dojou no Tsurugi")
Earth Sign "Sword of Unletting Soil" [2]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
気質を地中へ打ち込み地形を 大きく隆起させて左右を攻撃する隆起の進行が遅いため相対的に　隙が小さい技になった
Translation
Penetrate temperaments into the terrain and create big lumps that attack left and right. As the lumps travel slowly, the skill itself offers few gaps to be attacked.


Spell Card 2
非想「非想非非想の剣」 (Hisou "Hisou Hibisou no Tsurugi")
Non-Perception "Sword of Neither Perception nor Non-Perception"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
緋想の剣が持つ力の一つ 気質の封印を施すスペルカード　斬られた敵はしばらくの間天候の影響を一切受けなくなってしまい天候次第で有利な時間を作れる
Translation
One of the powers of the Sword of Scarlet Thought. Using a temperament seal spell card, the afflicted opponent becomes unaffected by the weather for some time, creating moments of meteorological advantage.


Spell Card 3
天符「天道是非の剣」 (Tenpu "Tendouzehi no Tsurugi")
Heaven Sign "Sword of Divine Justice" [3]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
緋想の剣を構え上空に突撃 外した時の隙は絶望的だが 無敵技として要る物は揃っている
Translation
Charge upwards with the Sword of Scarlet Thought. Missing the target would leave you a despairing gap, but that's needed to balance the otherwise invincible technique.


Spell Card 4
地震「先憂後楽の剣」 (Jishin "Sen'yuugoraku no Tsurugi")
Earthquake "Sword of Pleasure After Hardship" [4]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
地面に剣を刺し地殻変動を発生　しばらく間を置いて地震が起こり地上に居る敵にダメージを与える地上に逃げ場は無いが空には無力時間差も有るので癖の強いスペル
Translation
Stab the sword into the ground to induce tectonic movements. Earthquakes happen after a while to damage enemies on the ground. A strong spell that leaves no space to run on the ground, though there is sufficient time to escape to the skies.


Spell Card 5
気符「天啓気象の剣」 (Kifu "Tenkei Kishou no Tsurugi")
Spirit Sign "Sword of Meteorological Revelation"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
緋想の剣が持つ力の一つ 気質の操作を使ったスペルカード周囲の気質を剣に集め、放つ 天候が乱れてると高威力になるが現在の天候を終了させてしまう
Translation
One of the powers of the Sword of Scarlet Thought. A spellcard that manipulates the temperaments, gathers them at the sword, and releases them. A high-power spell that can even disrupt the climate, causing the current weather to end.


Spell Card 6
要石「天地開闢プレス」 (Kaname-ishi "Tenchi Kaibyaku Puresu")
Keystone "World Creation Press"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
画面上空へ大きく高度を取り 巨大化させた要石を抱えて一気に落下して相手を押しつぶすかなり滅茶苦茶な技
Translation
Taking the great heights above the screen, Tenshi descends onto the opponent with an enlarged keystone in her arms. A wanton technique.


Spell Card 7
気符「無念無想の境地」 (Kifu "Munen Musou no Kyouchi")
Spirit Sign "State of Freedom from Worldly Thoughts"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
一定時間自身の身体強度を上げる効果の続く間ガードは出来ないが被弾してものけぞらなくなる 実は気合で我慢しているだけとか
Translation
For a limited period of time, strengthen the body to withstand hits without entering hitstun, but cannot guard while the effect lasts. She's actually just breathing in to withstand the pain.


Spell Card 8
「全人類の緋想天」 ("Zenjinrui no Hisouten")
"Sky of Scarlet Perception of All Humankind"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
周囲の気質を緋想の剣に凝縮 下方へ一気に解き放つ超大技 発動に周囲の気質を全て使う点に注意しておこう
Translation
A grand technique that concentrates the surrounding temperaments at the Sword of Scarlet Thought and unleashes them below. Beware that this uses all of the surrounding temperaments.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ The six earthquakes that serve as signs of the major events in the Buddha's life. [1]
  2. ↑ Quoting the Records of the Grand Historian: "Mount Tai is majestic because it does not let its soil run."
  3. ↑ Quoting the Records of the Grand Historian: "Is the Way of Heaven just or unjust?"
  4. ↑ Abridged from Fan Zhongyan's poem "Memorial to the Yueyang Tower".
