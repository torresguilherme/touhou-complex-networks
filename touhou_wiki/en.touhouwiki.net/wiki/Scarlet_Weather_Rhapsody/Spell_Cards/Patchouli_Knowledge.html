Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
サマーレッド (Samaa Reddo)
Summer Red
Cost
1 orb
Comment
火属性魔法の基本発展形
前方に大型の火炎弾を射出する
貫通力と威力に優れ使いやすいが
直線ゆえ回避され易くも有る
Translation
A basic form of fire-element magic. Shoots a large fireball straight ahead. Superb in penetration, damage, and ease of use, but as it travels straight, it's also easy to dodge.

Offensively specialized form of metal-element magic. Creates one or more circular saw blades out of metallic elements and throws them at the enemy. When fired up and down, the saws' wide range makes them easy to hit with, and they can bounce off the ground.

Emerald City

1 orb

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
火金符「セントエルモピラー」 (Kakinfu "Sento Erumo Piraa")
Fire Metal Sign "St. Elmo's Pillar" [4]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
火、金の合成符
高密度の熱の塊を作り出し発射
地面に叩き付ける事で火柱とする
射出時に敵の足元を狙うため
踏み込まれると外れやすい
Translation
Synthesis of fire and metal. Creates and throws a high-density heat orb that becomes a pillar of flame upon striking the ground. Since it's aimed at the enemy's feet when thrown, it can easily miss if they rush forward.


Spell Card 2
土水符「ノエキアンデリュージュ」 (Dosuifu "Noekian Deryuuju")
Earth Water Sign "Noachian Deluge" [5]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
土、水の合成符
圧縮した水を前方へ吹き付ける技
消費が少なく弾速、密度も良好で
相手との射ち合いで効果を発揮
Translation
Synthesis of earth and water. Sprays pressurized water forward. Low-cost, but also fast and dense, making it effective in a firefight.


Spell Card 3
金木符「エレメンタルハーベスター」 (Kinmokufu "Erementaru Haabesuto")
Metal Wood Sign "Elemental Harvester"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
金、木の合成符
歯車の形をした刃で周囲を守り
近くの敵を迎撃する防御スペル
Translation
Synthesis of metal and wood. Protects the user's immediate vicinity with gear-shaped blades. A defensive spell that can intercept nearby enemies.


Spell Card 4
日符「ロイヤルフレア」 (Nichifu "Roiyaru Furea")
Sun Sign "Royal Flare"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
小型の太陽を作り出し全てを光で
焼き払う彼女の最高峰魔法の一つ
吸血鬼の館に住む者が使う魔法と
しては何とも因縁めいた物である
Translation
Creates a small sun which burns everything with its light. One of her greatest spells. Quite a portentous spell for a resident of a vampire's mansion...


Spell Card 5
月符「サイレントセレナ」 (Getsufu "Sairento Serena")
Moon Sign "Silent Selene" [6]
Cost
4 cards
Comment
展開した魔法陣から月の光を収束
した光の矢を発射して敵を射抜く
魔法陣の内側は不可侵領域となり
その中心に居るパチュリーには
鉄壁の守りとなる
Translation
Fires arrows of condensed moonlight up from a magic circle that unfurls at her feet, piercing through the enemy. The magic circle's radius serves as an impregnable defense for Patchouli.


Spell Card 6
火水木金土符「賢者の石」 (Kasuimokukinfu "Kenja no Ishi")
Fire Water Wood Metal Earth Sign "Philosopher's Stone" [7]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
元素練成したマジックアンプ
必殺技に石が反応して追加攻撃
弾幕を強固にする
砕けた石は効果時間が続く限り
時間経過で再生する
Translation
Magic amplifiers made from refined elements. Upon using a special move, the corresponding element's gem will react and fire additional bullets. Used-up gems will regenerate after a short delay as long as the card is in effect.


Spell Card 7
水符「ジェリフィッシュプリンセス」 (Suifu "Jerifisshu Purinsesu")
Water Sign "Jellyfish Princess"
Cost
3 card
Comment
泡による防護壁を生成して装着
泡が攻撃を受け止めてくれる
泡が有る限り本体は無敵だが
泡が大きくグレイズ出来ないので
長時間の効果は期待しにくい
Translation
Creates a protective bubble shield. The bubble stops enemy attacks. The user is invincible while the bubble exists, but because you can't graze, it's hard to maintain it for long.


Spell Card 8
月木符「サテライトヒマワリ」 (Getsumokufu "Sateraito Himawari")
Moon Wood Sign "Satellite Himawari" [8]
Cost
3 cards
Comment
上空に自立攻撃を行う光弾を放つ
光弾は螺旋状に上昇しながら下へ
弾をばら撒き援護してくれる
Translation
Fires light orbs that attack independently from above. The orbs rise upwards in a spiral shape while scattering cover fire downwards.


Spell Card 9
日木符「フォトシンセシス」 (Nichimokufu "Fotoshinseshisu")
Sun Wood Sign "Photosynthesis"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
周囲の魔力を凝縮させた場を作り
中に入ると霊力の回復が促進
無尽蔵に射撃が使える状態になる
場は設置すると動かせず、他の
カードを使うと消えるので注意
Translation
Creates a spot filled with condensed magic; your rate of spirit recovery increases while inside, giving you an inexhaustible supply of firepower. Beware that the spot does not move from its initial position and disappears if another card is used.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Akiba is a god of stopping fires, and was once worshipped at the Akiha Shrine. Today, the shrine is dedicated to Kagutsuchi, a god associated with Akiba during the Edo period.
  2. ↑ The moon shines because its surface reflects sunlight.
  3. ↑ 'Doyou' (土用) refers to the eighteen-day transitional period between seasons. In Wu Xing, it is associated with the element of earth and considered to be a fifth season in itself. Mostly refers to the period before autumn, when it is generally the hottest of the year.
  4. ↑ St. Elmo's (pillar of) fire - fire-like electrical discharges on the mastheads of ships.
  5. ↑ Noachian - relating to Noah.
  6. ↑ Selene: a Greek goddess of the moon.
  7. ↑ Philosopher's Stone: mythical substance that could turn metal into gold.
  8. ↑ Himawari: a name of a Japanese weather satellite, means sunflower.
