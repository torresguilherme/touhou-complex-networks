Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
吐息「小鬼の深呼吸」 (Toiki "Shouki no Shinkokyuu")
Sigh "Small Oni's Deep Breath"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Patchouli Stage 6, Yukari Stage 5, Iku Stage 1, Tenshi Stage 2
Editor's Comment
Creates a black hole that sucks up bullets and pulls you towards it and then a white hole that blows away the bullets.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
火弾「地霊活性弾」 (Hidan "Chirei Kasseidan")
Fire Shot "Earth Spirit Activation Bullet"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Patchouli Stage 6, Yukari Stage 5, Iku Stage 1, Tenshi Stage 2
Editor's Comment
Creates a travelling series of fireballs in front of her (plus behind on Hard or higher.) The fireballs leap up from the ground and cause explosions upon landing back down.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
鬼神「ミッシングパープルパワー」 (Kishin "Misshingu Paapuru Pawaa")
Kishin "Missing Purple Power"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Patchouli Stage 6, Yukari Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Suika becomes huge in size, capable of blocking your way, stomps and periodically punches, which (on Normal difficulty or higher) creates an explosion in front of herself.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
鬼気「濛々迷霧」 (Kiki "Moumoumeimu")
Ghastly Air "Deep Fog Labyrinth" [1]
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Patchouli Stage 6, Yukari Stage 5
Editor's Comment
Suika turns into mist, which quickly travels around and leaves behind bullets.
Spell Card Story Mode 5
「百万同一鬼」 ("Hyakumandouikki")
"A Million Oni in One"
Owner
Suika Ibuki
Patchouli Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Shoots bullets in a spiral pattern (on Normal mode or higher), then throws an energy bomb that covers about half of the screen with a huge and highly damaging explosion.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
妖鬼-密- (Youki -Mitsu-)
Spectre -Dense-
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周囲の熱を拳に集めて発火させ
パンチと共に爆炎の塊を射出する
拳は打撃、爆炎は飛び道具という二つの属性を持つ技
Translation
Gathers surrounding heat into her fist, then punches and releases it as an explosive flame. A two-pronged move where the punch is a melee strike, and the explosion is a projectile.

{{{keycommand}}}

Earth Spirit -Dense-

1 orb
Comment

地面から岩が槍となり突き上がる
岩だけでなく落下パンチ部分にも
攻撃力がある
Translation
Gathers underground Qi veins to change the landscape, thrusting a rocky mass up from the ground like a spear. In addition to the spear, Suika's ground punch can also deal damage.

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
萃符「戸隠山投げ」 (Suifu "Tokugashiyama-nage")
Gather Sign "Mt. Togakushi Toss" [3]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
辺りの石や岩を萃めて巨岩にし
敵へ勢い良く投げつける技
射出は遅いがグレイズが出来ない
という珍しい特徴をもつ
Translation
Gathers all the stones and pebbles around to form a huge rock which she hurls at her opponent. It's slow, but on the other hand, you can't graze it.


Spell Card 2
酔神「鬼縛りの術」 (Suishin "Onishibari no Jutsu")
Drunk God "Art of Oni Binding"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
鎖で敵を縛りつけ霊力を散らし
食らった相手の霊珠を破壊する
ダメージは少ないが引き寄せての追撃が可能
Translation
Binds the enemy with a chain and disperses their spirit power, crushing one of their orbs. It doesn't do much damage, but it can pull enemies into range for additional attacks.


Spell Card 3
鬼符「ミッシングパワー」 (Kifu "Misshingu Pawaa")
Oni Sign "Missing Power"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
巨大化して周囲を吹き飛ばす
巨大化は一瞬で解除されるが
攻めて来る敵を追い払うには十分
Translation
Becomes huge and blows away everything around her. It wears off right away, but it's enough to scare off approaching enemies.


Spell Card 4
萃鬼「天手力男投げ」 (Suiki "Ame-no-Tajikarao-nage")
Gathering Oni "Ame-no-Tajikarao Toss" [4]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
完全無敵で掴みかかり敵を岩で
押し固めて投げ飛ばす
投げの様だが打撃なのでガードで
防がれてしまうのが欠点
無敵を利用した使い方がメイン
Translation
Grabs at the enemy while invincible, then crams them into a boulder and throws them. Despite resembling a grab, it's a regular melee move that can still be blocked. Mainly for use in situations that take advantage of the invincibility.


Spell Card 5
酔夢「施餓鬼縛りの術」 (Suimu "Segakishibari no Jutsu")
Drunken Dream "Art of Segaki Binding"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
敵を鎖で縛りつけ霊力漏れ状態に
陥らせる特殊な技
相手は満足に射撃も行えないため
流れを引き寄せる事が出来る
Translation
A special technique that binds an enemy and causes their spirit to drain over time. Since your opponent can't fire at will, you can really influence the game flow.


Spell Card 6
鬼神「ミッシングパープルパワー」 (Kishin "Misshingu Paapuru Pawaa")
Kishin "Missing Purple Power"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
巨大化した状態で暴れまわる
敵の攻撃をガードは出来ないが
一切のけぞることは無いため
一方的な展開に持って行きやすい
Translation
Turns huge so you can run amok. You can't block enemy attacks, but you can't be stunned, so it's easy to bulldoze on ahead.


Spell Card 7
霧符「雲集霧散」 (Mufu "Unshuu Musan")
Mist Sign "Gather Like Clouds, Vanish Like Mist"
Cost
1 card
Comment
鬼の霧で壁を作る防御技
霧は敵の弾幕を遮る壁になり
画面上に暫く停滞してくれる
Translation
A defensive shield of oni mist that blocks enemy projectiles and lingers on the screen for some time.


Spell Card 8
鬼火「超高密度燐禍術」 (Onibi "Choukoumitsudo Rinkajutsu")
Will-o'-Wisp "Superdense Phosphorus Disaster Art" [5]
Cost
4 cards
Comment
地面を叩き溶岩弾を噴出させる
溶岩は地面で数回分離し飛散
広範囲を焼き尽くす
Translation
Pounds the ground, causing a lava bullet to erupt upwards. The lava bounces and splits apart several times, burning a wide area to smithereens.


Spell Card 9
鬼符「大江山悉皆殺し」 (Kifu "Ooeyama Shikkaigoroshi")
Oni Sign "Complete Massacre on Mt.Ooe" [6]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
敵を掴み何度も地面に叩き付け
最後は大爆発で吹き飛ばす投げ
ガードで防ぐことは出来ないが
間合いが狭いので掴むには
上手く敵の懐に入る必要が有る
Translation
Grabs the opponent and knocks them into the ground over and over, culminating in a huge explosion. The grab is unblockable, but the timing requires you to be in extremely close.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 鬼気: refers to a ghastly atmosphere or the sight of something horrific. 迷霧: a deep, almost labyrinthine fog.
  2. ↑ A pun on the 元気玉 (Spirit Bomb) from the Dragon Ball series. Both are pronounced genkidama.
  3. ↑ Mt. Togakushi: a (real life) mountain said to have been created from the boulder flung from Ama-no-Iwato.
  4. ↑ Ame-no-Tajikarao: the god who hurled away the boulder blocking the entrance to Ama-no-Iwato.
  5. ↑ 燐禍 (rinka, lit. "phosphorus disaster"): a play on 燐火 (rinka, one of several JP terms for will-o'-wisps), as well as possibly 輪禍 (rinka, "car accident").
  6. ↑ Legends tell of a complete massacre of oni by Minamoto no Yorimitsu on their homeground Mt. Ooe.
