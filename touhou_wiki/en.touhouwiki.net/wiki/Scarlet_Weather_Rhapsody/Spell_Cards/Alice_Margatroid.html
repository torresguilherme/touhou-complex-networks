Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
足軽「スーサイドスクワッド」 (Ashigaru "Suusaido Sukuwaddo")
Foot Soldiers "Suicide Squad" [1]
Owner
Alice Margatroid
Reimu Stage 2, Marisa Stage 1, Remilia Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Summons dolls that follow you and explode on contact.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
剣符「ソルジャーオブクロス」 (Kenfu "Sorujaa Obu Kurosu")
Sword Sign "Soldier of Cross"
Owner
Alice Margatroid
Reimu Stage 2, Marisa Stage 1, Remilia Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Throws dolls with purple blades that spread and blue blades that are aimed at you.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
人形「魂のないフォークダンス」 (Ningyou "Tamashii no Nai Fooku Dansu")
Dolls "Soulless Folk Dance"
Owner
Alice Margatroid
Tenshi Stage 4
Editor's Comment
Summons dolls that fire laser bullets.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
人形操創 (Ningyou Sousou)
Doll Placement [2]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
人形を定位置に固定させ攻撃する
ボタンで設置位置を選択可能で
相手を縛る機雷となる基本技
Translation
Attacks with a doll that's fixed in position. Its position depends on the button used. A basic skill that serves as a floating mine to tie the opponent down.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
人形無操 (Ningyou Musou)
Doll Negation [3]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔力を込めた人形を発射し離脱
人形は衝撃を受けると爆発する
Translation
Leaps backwards while releasing magically-charged dolls. The dolls explode on impact.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
人形置操 (Ningyou Chisou)
Doll Arrangement [4]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
上空に複数の人形を待機させた後
それらに時間差で攻撃を行わせる
Translation
Places several dolls above in waiting, then makes them attack in sequence after a short delay.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
人形振起 (Ningyou Shinki)
Doll Activation
Cost
1 orb
Comment
強射撃で待機状態の人形を使い
周辺を攻撃させる設置系の技
大量に設置をしておけば大威力
広範囲に攻撃することが出来る
Translation
Activates the idle dolls from Alice's C projectile and makes them attack the surrounding area. If you set up a lot in advance, you can pull off a powerful wide-range attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
人形帰巣 (Ningyou Kisou)
Returning Dolls [5]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
投げた人形をブーメランのように
引き戻して攻撃する特殊な射撃
行きも攻撃力を持つためアリスに
珍しく直接攻撃能力も高い
Translation
A special projectile that throws a doll, then pulls it back like a boomerang. Since it does damage even on its way out, it's one of Alice's few moves that work well as a direct attack.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
人形火葬 (Ningyou Kasou)
Doll Cremation [6]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
魔力を込めた人形を投げつける
こちらは着弾までが遅い代わりに
本体との同時攻撃を可能にした
Translation
Hurls magically-charged dolls overhead. The projectiles now take longer to land, but this enables Alice to attack on her own at the same time.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
人形千槍 (Ningyou Chisou)
Thousand Spear Dolls [4]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周囲に武器を持った人形を集め
前方への突きを連射する近接攻撃
人形による攻撃だが打撃扱いで
グレイズでの回避は出来ない
Translation
A close-range attack that gathers dolls with weapons and has them jab forward repeatedly. Despite using dolls, it counts as a melee attack and so cannot be grazed.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
人形ＳＰ (Ningyou Esu-pii)
SP Doll [7]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
弱で人形を周囲にストックし
強でストック消費して攻撃を行う
最大三つまで待機する事が可能で
最大時に弱で全てを同時に放出できる
Translation
B version places a doll around you in orbit. C version expends a doll to attack. The maximum number of dolls in orbit is 3; when that happens, the B version releases all 3 dolls at once.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
大江戸爆薬からくり人形 (Ooedo Bakuyaku Karakuri Ningyou)
Ooedo Explosive Marionette
Cost
1 orb
Comment
自走能力の有る人形爆弾を投擲
人形は地上をゆっくりと歩き
接近、衝撃等を受けると爆発する
画面上に出せるのは一つまで
Translation
Throws a self-propelled doll grenade. The doll slowly walks along the ground, and explodes when touched or attacked. There can only be one on the screen at a time.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 10
シーカーワイヤー (Shiikaa Waiyaa)
Seeker Wire
Cost
1 orb
Comment
強射撃で待機中の人形で屈折する
反射光線を発射する技
光線は全ての人形を経由し最後は
相手へ向けて飛来する
Translation
Fires a reflecting beam that bends through Alice's idle C dolls. The beam passes through all the dolls, then finally goes flying toward the opponent.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
魔符「アーティフルサクリファイス」 (Mafu "Aatifuru Sakurifaisu")
Magic Sign "Artful Sacrifice"
Cost
1 card
Comment
魔力を込めた人形を投げ爆発
着弾が遅いため直撃は難しいが
消費に対し威力は高い
Translation
Throws a magically-charged doll that explodes. Since it's slow, it's hard to land a direct hit, but the power is high for the cost.


Spell Card 2
戦符「リトルレギオン」 (Senfu "Ritoru Region")
War Sign "Little Legion"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
人形が方陣を組んで敵を攻撃
方陣は時間で形を拡大していく
威力は控えめだが行動を制限する
効果は高い
Translation
Assembles dolls into formation and has them attack. The formation spreads out over time. It's not very powerful, but it's useful in restricting the opponent's movements.


Spell Card 3
咒符「上海人形」 (Jufu "Shanhai Ningyou")
Curse Sign "Shanghai Doll"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
人形から高出力のレーザーを照射
射出が早い上射程も無限なため
一般的な射撃として使いやすい
Translation
Shoots a high-output laser from a doll. The delay is short and the range is infinite, so it's easy to use it as a run-of-the-mill projectile.


Spell Card 4
魔操「リターンイナニメトネス」 (Masou "Ritaan Inanimetonesu")
Magipulation "Return Inanimateness"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
強力な爆発を起こす人形を投擲
爆発、威力、着弾速度が増加した
Translation
Throws a doll that causes a powerful explosion. The explosion, power, and speed are all increased.


Spell Card 5
戦操「ドールズウォー」 (Sensou "Dooruzu Woo")
War Command "Dolls' War"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
密度の高い人形の方陣を形成
方陣の中心に敵を捕らえれば威力
拘束時間で見返りが得られる
Translation
Creates a dense formation of dolls. If you can trap the opponent in the center of the formation, they'll take heavy damage and be stuck for a period of time.


Spell Card 6
咒詛「蓬莱人形」 (Juso "Hourai Ningyou")
Malediction "Hourai Doll"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
複数の人形からレーザーを照射
前方広範囲をカバーでき高威力
Translation
Shoots lasers from multiple dolls. Covers a wide area in front with high power.


Spell Card 7
偵符「シーカードールズ」 (Teifu "Shiikaa Dooruzu")
Spy Sign "Seeker Dolls"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
上空に大量の人形を配置した後
レーザーの網を下方向に作り出す
威力は当たり方次第で不安定だが
敵を燻し出す効果は高い
Translation
Throws a great number of dolls into the air, which create a net of lasers below them. Its power fluctuates depending on how the hits land, but it's a great way of smoking out the enemy.


Spell Card 8
紅符「和蘭人形」 (Koufu "Oranda Ningyou")
Scarlet Sign "Holland Doll"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
十字にレーザーを放つ人形を発射
本体は投げてすぐに動けるので
レーザーを盾に戦闘を有利に
進める一手として利用できる
Translation
Releases a doll that fires a cross-shaped laser. Alice can move again right after throwing it, so you can use the lasers as a shield to influence the battle to your advantage.


Spell Card 9
人形「未来文楽」 (Ningyou "Mirai Bunraku")
Doll "Futuristic Bunraku"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
人形一つを精密操作するモード
人形は本体のようにキーで動き
攻撃や移動を素早く行える
第四ボタンで操作を中断可能
Translation
Switches to a mode where you precisely control a single doll. The doll moves with the arrow keys, and responds quickly to attack and movement commands. You can end control with the D button.


Spell Card 10
注力「トリップワイヤー」 (Chuuryoku "Torippu Waiyaa")
Focus Power "Trip Wire"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
強射で設置済みの人形とその間の
紐が発光して攻撃力を持つ
位置関係を上手く操れば痒い所に
攻撃が届く便利な技
Translation
The strings between Alice and her idle C dolls all glow red, and obtain hitboxes. If you control their positions carefully, this can be an extremely useful attack that hits right where you want it to.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 足軽 (Ashigaru): Footsoldiers of medieval Japan
  2. ↑ 操創 (sousou, using kanji for 'control' and 'create'): possibly a pun on several words also pronounced 'sousou', such as 草創 ('inauguration') or 蹌蹌 ('staggeringly').
  3. ↑ 無操 (musou, using kanji for 'nothing' and 'control'): possibly a pun on several words also pronounced 'musou', such as 無想 ('unthinking').
  4. ↑ 4.0 4.1 Both of Alice's 214B/C skills can be pronounced ningyou chisou. 置操 uses kanji for 'placement' and 'control', while 千槍 means 'thousand spears'.
  5. ↑ Pronounced ningyou kisou, forming a pattern with Alice's other "Ningyou ___sou" skills.
  6. ↑ Pronounced ningyou kasou, forming a pattern with Alice's other "Ningyou ___sou" skills.
  7. ↑ SP stands for Security Police.
