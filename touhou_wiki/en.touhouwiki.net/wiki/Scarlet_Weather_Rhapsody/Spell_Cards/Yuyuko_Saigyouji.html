Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
幽符「冥界ミステリースポット」 (Yuufu "Meikai Misuterii Supotto")
Ghostly Sign "Netherworld Mystery Spot"
Owner
Yuyuko Saigyouji
Sakuya Stage 3, Youmu Stage 1, Reisen Stage 3, Iku Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 1
Editor's Comment
A ring of ghosts converges on Yuyuko, then moves toward the player.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
霊蝶「蝶の羽風生に暫く」 (Reichou "Chou no Hafuusei ni Shibaraku")
Spirit Butterfly "A Butterfly's Wingbeats, Brief in Life"
Owner
Yuyuko Saigyouji
Sakuya Stage 3, Youmu Stage 1, Reisen Stage 3, Iku Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 1
Editor's Comment
Releases a stream of ghosts that split into butterflies at the screen's edge.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
宴会「死して全て大団円」 (Enkai "Shi shite Subete Daidan'en")
Banquet "All Things Come Full Circle in Death"
Owner
Yuyuko Saigyouji
Sakuya Stage 3, Reisen Stage 3, Iku Stage 3
Editor's Comment
Multiple ghost rings appear while shrinking in size.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
幽胡蝶 (Yuukochou)
Ghostly Butterfly
Cost
1 orb
Comment
手元から幽霊の形をした弾を発射
画面端で蝶に姿を変え敵を追尾
敵を追い詰める弾幕となる
Translation
Releases ghost-shaped bullets from her hands that turn into homing butterflies when they reach the edge of the screen.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
未生の光 (Mishou no Hikari)
Lights of the Unborn
Cost
1 orb
Comment
五筋の光の形状をしたレーザー
放射状に放つ為距離が開くほど
カバーできる範囲が広くなる
Translation
A five-branched laser. Since it radiates outward, it can cover more area at greater distances.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
悉皆彷徨 (Shikkai Houkou)
All Things Wandering
Cost
1 orb
Comment
大量の幽霊を輪の形に呼び出す
輪は上方へ向けて進みながら攻撃
見た目通り上空の敵に対して
強力な牽制となる
Translation
Calls forth a number of spirits in a ring that floats diagonally upwards. As you might imagine, it's an effective deterrent against airborne opponents.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
胡蝶夢の舞 (Kochoumu no Mai)
Dance of the Butterfly Dream
Cost
1 orb
Comment
前方へ踏み込み両手の扇で攻撃
普段の緩慢な動きとうって変わり
鋭い突進技として機能する
強は大振りだがグレイズ能力有り
Translation
Steps forward and attacks with fans in both hands. Serves as an effective rush attack, markedly different from her usual slow movement. The C version has a much heavier swing to it, but grazes.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
好死の霊 (Koushi no Tama)
Spirits that Died Well [1]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
幽胡蝶の変形技
画面端に接触した幽霊が光に分離
より攻撃的に変化した
Translation
A more offensive variation of Ghostly Butterfly. A ghost sent to the edge of the screen splits into lasers.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
鳳蝶紋の槍 (Agehachoumon no Yari)
Lance of the Swallowtail Crest
Cost
1 orb
Comment
刃の様に収束した妖気を放出する
発射の際余剰の力が蝶になり
広い範囲にランダムに飛び散る
計算ずくとの事だが本当なのか
少々怪しいものがある
Translation
Shoots fey mist gathered into a sword shape. The excess power turns into butterflies on release that scatter randomly over a wide area. She says it's on purpose, but it sounds somewhat dubious.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
誘霊の甘蜜 (Yuurei no Kanmitsu)
Spirit-Luring Nectar
Cost
1 orb
Comment
幽霊を誘導する発光体とそれを
追いかける幽霊を同時発射する
発光体はキーで位置を操作でき
それで幽霊を制御する間接攻撃
ホールドで制御時間を延長可能
Translation
Simultaneously releases a spirit-attracting orb and spirits. You can control the orb's direction, making the spirits follow as an indirect attack. Holding extends the controllable time.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
逆さ屏風 (Sakasa Byoubu)
Reverse Screens [2]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
後ろへ身を翻して前方を払う打撃
無敵が有り威力も高いが判定が
極端に前方に短く、相手が攻撃を
していない限りほぼ当たらない
カウンター専用と言える技
Translation
A melee move that flips backwards, sweeping the area in front of her. It has invincibility and is strong, but the hitbox is very close-range and will usually miss if the enemy isn't attacking. Very counter-centric.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 9
スフィアブルーム (Sufia Buruumu)
Sphere Bloom
Cost
1 orb
Comment
光の粒を鞭の様にしならせて撒く
粒は時間差で桜に弾けて敵を攻撃
強で出すと彼女の文様を描く
Translation
Scatters beads of light like a whip. After a delay, the lights burst into cherry blossoms to attack the opponent. The C variation traces her spiral insignia.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
死符「ギャストリドリーム」 (Shifu "Gyasutori Doriimu")
Death Sign "Ghastly Dream"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
円状に大量の蝶弾を発射する
周囲をカバーし無敵もあるため
切り替えしの基本となるカード
Translation
Fires a wave of butterfly shots out in a circle. Since it's good at covering your surroundings and has an invincible startup, it's a basic card for reversals.


Spell Card 2
冥符「黄泉平坂行路」 (Meifu "Yomi-Hirasaka Kouro")
Nether Sign "Path to Yomotsu-Hirasaka" [3]
Cost
1 cards
Comment
手元に冥界への簡易門を形成
幽霊達がそこから前方へ殺到する
統率されてるとはいえ幽霊なので
軌道は毎回気まぐれ
Translation
Forms a simple gate to the Netherworld at her hands, from which many phantoms surge forward. Yuyuko may be commanding them, but they're still just simple phantoms, so their trajectories vary each time.


Spell Card 3
霊符「无寿の夢」 (Reifu "Tenju no Yume")
Spirit Sign "Ageless Dream" [4]
Cost
2 cards
Comment
幽霊を憑り付かせ体力を奪う
幽々子がダメージを受けるなど
制御が途切れた場合憑依が解除し
総ダメージが低くなるのに注意
Translation
Possesses the enemy with a phantom that drains their health. Be aware that if the effect is interrupted by Yuyuko taking damage or so on, the possession ends and total damage is lowered.


Spell Card 4
死蝶「華胥の永眠」 (Shichou "Kasho no Eimin")
Deadly Butterfly "Eternal Sleep in Dreamland" [5]
Cost
4 cards
Comment
周囲に蝶弾の嵐を巻き起こす
蝶弾は幽々子の周囲を旋回した後
画面全体へと散っていく
Translation
Whips up a storm of butterfly shots around Yuyuko. The butterflies spiral around her, then spread out across the whole screen.


Spell Card 5
再迷「幻想郷の黄泉還り」 (Saimei "Gensoukyou no Yomigaeri")
Lost Again "Gensokyo's Rise from Yomi" [3]
Cost
5 cards
Comment
地表に冥界への門を開く大技
門からは幽霊が殺到し敵を攻撃
門は敵を緩やかに追跡するため
敵の移動を大きく制限できる
Translation
A dramatic move that opens a Netherworld gate on the ground, from which phantoms surge up to attack the enemy. The gate follows the enemy somewhat, allowing you to greatly restrict their movement.


Spell Card 6
寿命「无寿国への約束手形」 (Jumyou "Tenjukoku he no Yakusoku Tegata")
Lifespan "Ticket to the Ageless Land" [4]
Cost
4 cards
Comment
幽霊を放ち敵へ憑り付かせる
この段階では無害だが幽霊が全て
消える瞬間に攻撃判定が出現する
カウント前に攻撃を受けると
憑依が解除されるので注意
Translation
Fires phantoms that possess the enemy. They're harmless at first, but once the last one vanishes, a huge explosion is triggered. Be aware that if Yuyuko is damaged before the countdown ends, possession stops.


Spell Card 7
霊蝶「蝶の羽風生に暫く」 (Reichou "Chou no Hafuusei ni Shibaraku")
Spirit Butterfly "A Butterfly's Wingbeats, Brief in Life"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
幽胡蝶の広域版。壁で蝶に変わる
幽霊を全方位に一斉発射
幽霊に攻撃力は無いが、時間差で
同時攻撃するには好都合
Translation
Wide-range version of Ghostly Butterfly. Releases a volley of ghosts in all directions, which split into butterflies once they reach the walls. The ghosts themselves do no damage, but the time delay gives you a good opportunity to set up more attacks at the same time.


Spell Card 8
蝶符「鳳蝶紋の死槍」 (Choufu "Agehachoumon no Shisou")
Butterfly Sign "Deadly Lance of the Swallowtail Butterfly Crest"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
背面に扇を展開し、ビームと
蝶弾を前方へ乱射する
Translation
Opens a fan behind her, and fires a barrage of beams and butterflies forwards.


Spell Card 9
幽雅「死出の誘蝶灯」 (Yuuga "Shide no Yuuchoutou")
Ghostly Elegance "Light Trap of Passing Away"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
幽霊が敵を取り囲み徐々に狭まる
直接当てるのは難しいが、敵の
自由を奪う効果は強力
Translation
Ghosts surround the enemy, slowly moving inwards. Difficult for scoring a direct hit, but the enemy's freedom is severely restricted.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ Possibly taken from the Chinese idiom "好死不如悪活" ('a bad life is better than a good death').
  2. ↑ One of several aspects to the practice of sakasa-goto, in which everyday objects and customs are inverted when mourning a death.
  3. ↑ 3.0 3.1 Yomi: the land of the dead in Shinto mythology. Yomotsu-Hirasaka: the slope leading down to Yomi.
  4. ↑ 4.0 4.1 A reference to the "Mandala of the Land of Heavenly Life" (天寿国繍帳, tenjukoku shūchō), commissioned after the death of Prince Shōtoku. "天寿" (heavenly life) may possibly be a misprint of "无寿" (roughly, non-life) which is the phrase used in this spellcard.
  5. ↑ 華胥 (Kasho in Japanese, Huaxu in Chinese) is a Chinese utopia described in the Liezi. The Yellow Emperor was said to have gone to Huaxu in his dreams. Once he woke up, he became enlightened.
