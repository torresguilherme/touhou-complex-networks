Story Mode Cards[edit]

Spell Card Story Mode 1
銀符「シルバーバウンド」 (Ginfu "Shirubaa Baundo")
Silver Sign "Silver Bound"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Alice Stage 4, Patchouli Stage 1, Remilia Stage 1, Aya Stage 3, Komachi Stage 2, Tenshi Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Also a player spellcard. Creates a barrage of bouncing knives.
Spell Card Story Mode 2
時符「咲夜特製ストップウォッチ」 (Jifu "Sakuya Tokusei Sutoppuwocchi")
Time Sign "Sakuya's Special Stopwatch"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Alice Stage 4, Patchouli Stage 1, Remilia Stage 1, Aya Stage 3, Komachi Stage 2, Tenshi Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Also a player spellcard, but without the knives. Creates stopwatches that keeps you from moving and then fires aimed knives.
Spell Card Story Mode 3
幻術「マイナイフリカージョン」 (Genjutsu "Mai Naifu Rikaajon")
Illusion "My Knife's Recursions"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Alice Stage 4, Aya Stage 3, Tenshi Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Stops time and fires knives. The knives fired can turn their direction whenever time has stopped.
Spell Card Story Mode 4
時符「シルバーアキュート360」 (Jifu "Shirubaa Akyuuto Sanbyakurokujuu")
Time Sign "Silver Acute 360"
Owner
Sakuya Izayoi
Tenshi Stage 6
Editor's Comment
Creates knives that surround you.

Skill Cards[edit]



Skill Card 1
クロスアップマジック (Kurosuappu Majikku)
Close-up Magic
Cost
1 orb
Comment
ナイフを周りに回転させ跳躍
触れた相手を切り裂く打撃技
飛び上がりにグレイズ判定が在り
上空からの弾幕攻撃に強い
Translation
Jumps as knives spin around her. The enemy will be cut if they touch them. You can graze while you're jumping, so it's good against danmaku attacks from above.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 2
バウンスノーバウンス (Baunsu Noo Baunsu)
Bounce No-Bounce
Cost
1 orb
Comment
壁と天井で跳ね返るナイフを発射
威力は微小だがナイフが壁を作り
投網の様に敵の行動を制限する
Translation
Releases knives that bounce against the walls and ceiling. Not particularly powerful, but good for walling off opponents.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 3
マジックスターソード (Majikku Sutaa Soodo)
Magic Star Sword
Cost
1 orb
Comment
時間加速で威力を重視したナイフ
高速で正面にまとめて飛ばす為
真っ向からの撃ち合いに優れる
Translation
Knives that have been accelerated in order to emphasize attack power. Since they fly straight forward at high speed, they're useful in face-to-face firefights.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 4
バニシングエブリシング (Banishingu Eburishingu)
Vanishing Everything
Cost
1 orb
Comment
時間を停止させて咲夜が移動
普通は移動中を認識できない為
一種のワープ技として機能する
立ち位置にカードを残しているが
彼女自身マジックのつもりらしい
Translation
Sakuya moves while time is stopped. Since you can't see her while she's moving, it essentially serves as a type of teleportation move. She leaves cards behind at her original position, apparently because she treats it as a magic trick.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 5
プロペリングシルバー (Puroperingu Shirubaa)
Propelling Silver
Cost
1 orb
Comment
周回軌道で飛ぶ変則ナイフ投げ
壁として設置する使い方も勿論
周回軌道上に相手を置ければ
強力な攻撃にもなる攻防一体
Translation
An atypical knife-throwing technique where the knives orbit around her. In addition to its obvious use as a shield, it also serves as a powerful attack if the enemy is in the orbit's path.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 6
スクウェアリコシェ (Sukuwea Rikoshe)
Square Ricochet
Cost
1 orb
Comment
質を重視した跳弾型のナイフ
速度、反射回数、命中回数が増加
アバウトに投げる使い方より
連携を意識した運用が要求される
Translation
A ricocheting knife that puts quality before quantity. Speed, bounce count, and hit count increase with level. Best used deliberately as a combo linker, rather than just tossing it out haphazardly.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 7
離剣の見 (Riken no Ken)
Sight of Distant Blade [1]
Cost
1 orb
Comment
ナイフを投げた後に時間を操作
空中で停止させ待機させる技
設置されたナイフは時間経過か
次の設置に反応して速度が戻る
Translation
Throws knives and then manipulates time to stop them in midair. The knives will resume motion after time passes, or after placing another set.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}


Skill Card 8
パーフェクトメイド (Paafekuto Meido)
Perfect Maid
Cost
1 orb
Comment
敵の攻撃を受ける直前に時間停止
背後へ回りつつナイフで取り囲む
打撃に反応を集中しているため
飛び道具は後頭部に直撃する
Translation
Stops time right before an enemy hit lands, then circles around to their back while surrounding them with knives. Since she's concentrating on countering physical attacks, projectiles will just hit her upside the head.
Move Command
{{{keycommand}}}

Spell Cards[edit]



Spell Card 1
幻符「殺人ドール」 (Genfu "Satsujin Dooru")
Illusion Sign "Killing Doll"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
広範囲にばら撒かれたナイフが
敵へと向きを変えて殺到
Translation
Knives scatter outwards, then rush in the direction of the enemy.


Spell Card 2
時符「プライベートスクウェア」 (Jifu "Puraibeeto Sukuwea")
Time Sign "Private Square"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
彼女の十八番の時間操作スペル
これは周囲の時間を遅延させる
相手がスローになり、攻防の面で
圧倒的優位に立つことが出来る
Translation
Her biggest specialty, time manipulation. Delays time around her to slow down the enemy, providing overwhelming offensive and defensive superiority.


Spell Card 3
傷符「インスクライブレッドソウル」 (Shoufu "Insukuraibu Reddo Souru")
Wound Sign "Inscribe Red Soul"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
両手のナイフで敵を滅多切り
発生が早く短射程だが範囲は広い
一見直接打撃のように見えるが
実は射撃扱いで有る事に注意
Translation
Hacks the enemy to pieces with a knife in each hand. Starts quickly and only extends for a short range, but covers a wide area. It may look like a melee attack, but do note that it actually counts as a projectile.


Spell Card 4
幻葬「夜霧の幻影殺人鬼」 (Gensou "Yogiri no Gen'ei Satsujinki")
Buriallusion "Phantomic Killer in Night Mist"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
殺人ドールの強化版スペル
ナイフが一度画面外に退避し
目で追う事も困難な速度で敵へと
襲い掛かる時間差攻撃
Translation
A stronger version of Killing Doll. A time-delayed attack where knives leave the screen, then bear down upon the enemy at speeds nearly impossible for the eye to follow.


Spell Card 5
「咲夜の世界」 ("Sakuya no Sekai")
"Sakuya's World"
Cost
5 cards
Comment
時間操作スペル最高峰の一つ
周囲の時間を完全に停止させ
一方的に攻撃を行う反則スペル
ただし消費、隙共に絶大
Translation
One of her strongest time manipulation spells. An unfair spell that completely stops time around her so she can attack uninhibited. Its card cost and punishability are both huge, though.


Spell Card 6
傷魂「ソウルスカルプチュア」 (Shoukon "Souru Sukarupuchua")
Scarred Soul "Soul Sculpture"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
傷符の強化版に当たるスペル
威力、範囲共に大幅に拡大
Translation
A stronger version of Wound Sign. Power and range are greatly increased.


Spell Card 7
銀符「シルバーバウンド」 (Ginfu "Shirubaa Baundo")
Silver Sign "Silver Bound"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
大量の反射ナイフを同時発射
ナイフ単体の威力は抑え目だが
攻撃範囲と弾数はかなりの物
Translation
Launches bouncing knives in all directions at once. Each individual knife is weak, but it makes up for it in quantity and range.


Spell Card 8
奇術「エターナルミーク」 (Kijutsu "Etaanaru Miiku")
Conjuring "Eternal Meek"
Cost
2 cards
Comment
前方へ向けてナイフを連射する
ヤケクソで投げているだけと言う
噂があるが真偽は定かではない
Translation
Sends knives forwards rapid-fire. There's a rumor that she's just flinging them around out of desperation, but nobody knows for sure.


Spell Card 9
速符「ルミネスリコシェ」 (Sokufu "Ruminesu Rikoshe")
Speed Sign "Luminous Ricochet"
Cost
3 cards
Comment
画面を何度も跳ね回る貫通ナイフ
ナイフは反射の度に攻撃力が復帰
結果相手を長時間拘束する
Translation
A piercing knife that repeatedly ricochets between the edges of the screen. Since its full attack power returns each time it ricochets, it can pin down the enemy for a long time.


Spell Card 10
時符「咲夜特製ストップウォッチ」 (Jifu "Sakuya Tokusei Sutoppuwocchi")
Time Sign "Sakuya's Special Stopwatch"
Cost
4 cards
Comment
時間停止フィールドを飛ばす技
捕まった相手はフィールドから
離れるまで動きを封じられる
Translation
A technique that throws out time suspension fields. Enemies caught in the field can't move until they leave it.

Notes[edit]

  1. ↑ 離剣の見 ('riken no ken'): a pun on the identically-pronounced "離見の見" ('sight of distant sight'), a Noh term referring to a performer's ability to see themselves from the audience's perspective.
