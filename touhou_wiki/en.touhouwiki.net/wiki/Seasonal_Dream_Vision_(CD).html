Commentary

Track 13
 六十年目の東方裁判 Eastern Judgement in the Sixtieth Year ♪ ZUN

アレンジャーの曲で十二ヶ月を一巡りしてきて、最後に幻想郷は元に戻る。どこ
か懐かしく、どこか可愛らしく、そして嫌な気分を残さない。私の曲の中でもここ
まで"東方"らしい曲は数少ないと思います。これは一年の外に置くのに相応し
い曲です。小説もあんなだし:-)

Twelve "months" have come and gone with the arrangers' songs, and finally we return to Gensokyo.
Somewhat nostalgic, somewhat cute, and leaving no unpleasant feelings behind.
Out of all my songs, I don't think there are many that are this "Touhou-like".
This is a song that is fitting for a one-year gap. The story is like that too. :-)
