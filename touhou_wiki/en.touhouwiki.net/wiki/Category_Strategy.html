Category:Strategy

From Touhou Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

With most shooting games, there are two goals that people play for. One is to beat the game, and the other is to beat the game with as high a score as possible. The Touhou games are not particularly difficult to clear compared to other games, so scoring becomes the focus of play after a while.

The Touhou series features the ability to save replays, which are a great resource to learn advanced strategy. However, it is also helpful to know why the players do the things they do. These pages will attempt to explain the ideas behind scoring technique, so that you can come up with your own methods, rather than just copying the replays.

If you are unfamiliar with how the game works, you can start with the specific Gameplay articles found in each game.

(By the way, the "not difficult to clear" part does not apply to Lunatic mode, which is beyond the scope of these articles.)
