Databases[edit]

  • Unofficial Gensokyo World Guide
A Japanese "unofficial" Touhou database.
  • 東方シリーズスコアボード
A Japanese scoreboard for Touhou 6 through 16 (excluding fighters).
  • 東方リプレイアップローダ＠chisa.dtdns.net
A Japanese replay archive with support for Touhou 6 through 14 (excluding fighters).
  • Touhou (ZUN) on VGMdb
A comprehensive database of videogame CDs and arangements.
  • 東方編曲録　〜 Arrangement Chronicle
A database of Touhou arrangements.
  • Maribel Hearn's GitHub Pages
A collection of useful webpages for Touhou, such as world records, Lunatic no miss no bomb clears, patches and tools, community jargon and a tier list creator.
  • LunarCast Replay Database
Moderated and searchable English replay database with support for Touhou 6 through 17 (excluding fighters).
  • Touhou Replay Archive (OUTDATED)
Abandoned English replay uploader for Touhou 6 through 13 (excluding fighters).
