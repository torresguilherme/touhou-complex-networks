Ruins of Torifune ~ Trojan Green Asteroid (鳥船遺跡　～ Trojan Green Asteroid, Torifune Iseki ~ Trojan Green Asteroid) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2012-04-30 (COMIC1☆6). It is part of "ZUN's Music Collection" and it is the sixth volume (vol.6).

Tracks

Dazzling and enthusiastic music collection performed by Shanghai Alice Illusionary Band, Part 6
