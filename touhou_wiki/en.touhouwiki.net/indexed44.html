Stock[edit]

Various Japanese resellers are now selling Touhou games through the Amazon.com marketplace. It's best to buy items marked with the phrase "Fulfilled by Amazon" because that indicates said item is in stock at a USA warehouse.

J-LIST/JBOX is a licensed reseller working with AKIBA-HOBBY and that is known to stock some Touhou games and merchandise from time to time. (Caution: J-LIST sells NSFW products as well)

If you find the item SOLD OUT, you can try requesting for the item by contacting the merchant of the site, but there will be no guarantees. You cannot request for items that have reached End-of-life.
