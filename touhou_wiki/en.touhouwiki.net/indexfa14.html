Touhou Bell Hermitage ~ Forbidden Scrollery (東方鈴奈庵　～Forbidden Scrollery, Touhou Suzunaan ~ Forbidden Scrollery) is an official doujin album by Team Shanghai Alice released on 2016-12-10. The CD accompanied the sixth volume of Forbidden Scrollery.

Tracks

  • 01. 判読眼のビブロフィリア (03:41)
    • Bibliophile with a Deciphering Eye
  • 02. 運河を行き交う人妖 (03:03)
    • Humans and Youkai Traversing the Canal
    • original title: 運河を行き交う人妖
    • source: 東方輝針城　～ Double Dealing Character
  • 03. 兎は舞い降りた (03:30)
    • The Rabbit Has Landed
    • original title: 兎は舞い降りた
    • source: 東方紺珠伝　～ Legacy of Lunatic Kingdom
